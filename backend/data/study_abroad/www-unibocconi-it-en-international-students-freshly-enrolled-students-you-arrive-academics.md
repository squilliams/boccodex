# Academics

Deep dive into the Bocconi University academics world. Find below all practical core details on deadlines, class and exam schedule according to your student profile.

Regular Students

Before the beginning of your academic year at Bocconi, check out:

- The [Guide to the University](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/guide-university "Guide to the University") with respect to your specific degree course
- [Timetable calendar](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/timetables-calendars-and-rooms/academic-calendar "Academic Calendar") with the key dates for the academic years
- [Course profiles](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/courses-offered-academic-programs-2025-2026-ay "Courses offered in Academic Programs 2025-2026 a.y. ") based on your specific program
- Surf your [yoU@B](https://youatb.unibocconi.it/) Student Diary! A reserved area online available to all Bocconi students. Through yoU@B, students can receive information from the University, use online services dedicated to teaching, administer some activities in their academic careers, register to exams, check dates, times and rooms for both exams and graduation sessions, check class timetables and so on.

Remember to log in and enter your student ID number as the username and your password.

All enrolled students will receive an e-mail granting them access to their yoU@B Diary.

Double Degree Students

Before the beginning of the academic year at Bocconi, Double Degree students receive a **tailored and individual** study plan, which is based on the Double Degree agreement between Bocconi University and Home Schools and it is approved by the Director of the Master of Science program in which students are enrolled at Bocconi.

It may include a mix of courses of the 1° and the 2° year of the Bocconi MSc program, according to the courses' matching between Bocconi and Home School. Bocconi credit system is ECTS equivalent.

Before the beginning of the academic year, you will be asked – if foreseen by your individual study plan – to choose your electives at Bocconi, with the possibility to change 2° semester electives during the “add/drop period” in January.

For all further information check the [Guide to the University](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/guide-university "Guide to the University")
