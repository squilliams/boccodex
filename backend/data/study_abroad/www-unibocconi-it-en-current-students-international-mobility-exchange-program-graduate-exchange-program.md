# Graduate Exchange Program

The Exchange Program is open to graduate students regularly **enrolled in their 1st year** of a Bocconi Master Degree.  Students will be able to attend a single term at one of the foreign universities **during their 2nd year**, after passing a selection, and just during the academic year the selection is related to.

**\\*\\* For more details, please refer to the Guide to the Exchange Program below\*\***
