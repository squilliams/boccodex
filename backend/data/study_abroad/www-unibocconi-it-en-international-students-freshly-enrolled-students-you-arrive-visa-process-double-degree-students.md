# For International Students

At Bocconi we welcome an incredibly diverse group of international students for our regular programs, exchange programs and single courses. Anyone relocating to a new country for study purposes needs to consider a series of procedures before and after arrival.

We aim to guide you throughout your journey at Bocconi University to make the most out of your experience.

Whether you are a prospective student, a freshly enrolled student or a current student, here you will find concrete information on immigration procedures, relocation and administrative steps, academics and tips on how to enjoy your stay at our Campus and in Milan.
