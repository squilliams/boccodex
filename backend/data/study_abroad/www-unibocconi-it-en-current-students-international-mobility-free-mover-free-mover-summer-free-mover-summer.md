# Free Mover Summer

Undergraduate and Bachelor in Global Law students (AY 2025-26)

Free Mover Summer

[Overview](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/international-mobility/free-mover/free-mover-summer)

#### Eligibility and Destinations

The Free Mover Summer is open to Bocconi students from all **Bachelor of Science Programs** and for the **Bachelor in Global Law** who meet the **requirements**, in the summer of their second year. Participation in a program in the summer of the first year or before will not be recognized, as well as the participation in a program in the summer of the third year.

The application to the Free Mover Summer will be on-hold and the recognition platform will not be available until the Bocconi requirements are met. **If an applicant does not meet the requirements** before the beginning of the Summer Program, the application to the Free Mover Summer will be **declined**.

Academic Requirements

**Enrollment year**: students regularly enrolled in the 2nd year of a BSc program or in the Bachelor of Global Law.

**Credits**: at least 60% of 1st year credits must be registered. The calculation includes all the exams officially registered in the academic career by February 3rd, 2026 (end of the ordinary session). If the applicant has changed the BSc program, exams which are not part of the 1st year of the NEW BSc study plan are not included in the calculation of the credits.

In addition, applicants **must meet the academic requirements** of the partner school (if any).

Language Requirements

**Level B2** or higher for English, proved by one of the options listed below:

- Enrolment in a BSc program taught in English;
- Bocconi English exam of level B2;
- English International language certificates of Level B2 or higher.

In addition, applicants **must meet the language requirements** of the partner school (if any).

Destinations

Bocconi University has selected a number of Partner schools from its international network which offer interesting and educationally valid Summer Programs. The following is the only list considered **valid** for the recognition of courses offered in the Summer Program through institutions abroad.

Students can submit applications for other alternatives that must be **examined** individually in advance by the Study Abroad office, **before** applying to the Free Mover Summer. The request, accompanied by a letter of motivation and information material on the University/Program, has to be submitted through **B in Touch,** available on the yoU@B Diary to the Study Abroad Office.

- Copenhagen Business School (International Summer University Programme)
- ESADE (ESADE Summer School)
- HEC Paris (HEC Paris Summer School)
- King's College London (Summer Programmes)
- University of Leeds (Leeds International Summer School)
- London School of Economics (LSE Summer School)
- Maastricht University (Summer School)
- SciencesPo (SciencesPo Summer School)
- University of California - Berkeley (Berkeley Summer Sessions)
- University of California - Los Angeles (UCLA Summer Sessions)
- Stanford University (Summer Sessions)\*
- University College London (UCL Summer School)
- The University of Warwick (Warwick Summer School)
- Hanyang University (International Summer School)
- Lady Margaret Hall, University of Oxford (Summer Program)
- The University of Melbourne (Short-Term Program)

\*The majority of courses offered by Stanford University during Summer Quarter are eight weeks, but Bocconi University will recognize one exam only.
