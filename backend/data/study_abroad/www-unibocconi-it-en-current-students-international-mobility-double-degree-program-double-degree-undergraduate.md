# Double Degree Undergraduate

_Bocconi University offers two Double Degree Programs - the **Double Degree with Peking University** and the **Bocconi-HEC Double Program** \- and a Triple Degree Program - the **World Bachelor in Business** \- with world-class universities at the undergraduate level. The participation to these unique programs allow students to broaden their international experience, take advantage of the academic expertise of the Partner Schools as well as to increment their career talent within the international job market._
