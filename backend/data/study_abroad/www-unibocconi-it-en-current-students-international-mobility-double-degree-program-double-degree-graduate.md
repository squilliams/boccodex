# Double Degree Graduate

_At the graduate level, Bocconi University offers students a unique portfolio of Double Degree Programs with outstanding universities from all over the world such as **London School of Economics, HEC Paris, ESADE, Sciences Po, ESSEC Business School, Fudan University** and many more. Through these Double Degree Programs, students will develop their academic, professional and cultural perspective. Furthermore, by obtaining **two Master's Degrees**, one from Bocconi University and one from the Partner School, the profile of Double Degree Students is highly valued by international employers._
