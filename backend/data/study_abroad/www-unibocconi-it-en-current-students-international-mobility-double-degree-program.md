# Double Degree Program

_Bocconi has developed an extended network of **Double Degree opportunities** in cooperation with several_ [_**prestigious universities worldwide**_](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/services/dd-network) _where participants will have the chance to live a unique academic and life experience._

_Through the DD program students will also be able to foster their career as global business leaders, able to master the complexity of future challenges for companies and institutions in a plural context._
