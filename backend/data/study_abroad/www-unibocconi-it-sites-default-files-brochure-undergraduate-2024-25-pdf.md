## Page 2

1. Before applying to the Exchange program, you are required to **carefully read** the following documents:

- ☑ Bocconi Students TRAVEL RISK POLICY
Available on yoU@B – International SOS
- ☑ Guide to the Exchange Program
Available on our website

More specifically, among others, we invite you to take note of the following:

- ☑ Preparing for Travel (par.2 - Bocconi Students TRAVEL RISK POLICY)
- ☑ Accommodation information (par.1 - Guide to the Exchange Program)
- ☑ Recognition of exams taken abroad (par. 6 - Guide to the Exchange Program)

2. Please also note that:

- ☑ This brochure has been released in **February 2024** and reflects the current situation to date. Slots available and teaching methods (physical, blended, online) at Partner Universities **may be subject to change**.
- ☑ During their participation in the Exchange Program, students **must comply with** the rules, regulations, and codes of conduct established by the Host Institution.
- ☑ Additionally, **students are responsible for covering living expenses**, including rent, food, healthcare, recreation, travel and transportation, health insurance, textbooks, school supplies, visa documents, and other related costs.
- ☑ Exchange Students **can enroll in** courses offered by the Host Institution, provided that these courses are not exclusively reserved for local students. Additionally, it's important to note -when planning studies at Bocconi and abroad- that **enrollment in specific courses at the host institution is never guaranteed** and depends on various factors, such as meeting prerequisites, seat availability, etc.
- ☑ Concerning slots available, requirements, and restrictions, only the information contained in this list will be applied to the **current selection** of the Exchange Program.
- ☑ In light of the general uncertainty and fluidity of the global situation, whether the overall conditions would not safeguard the well-being and security of students Bocconi University does not guarantee that students will be able to take part in the Exchange semester. Moreover, travel and medical risks can rapidly change and lead Bocconi University and host Institutions or Countries to increase travel and medical restrictions. Therefore, **the Exchange Program can be canceled**.
- ☑ International students or students with **dual citizenship** cannot apply for some Schools in the countries for which they hold current citizenship. Carefully check the note section for each destination. In case of doubt please contact the Study Abroad office through BinTouch on the yoU@B Diary.
