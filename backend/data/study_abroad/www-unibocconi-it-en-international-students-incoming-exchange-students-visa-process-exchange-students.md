# Visa Process for exchange students

All non-EU exchange students must apply for a **Student Visa** before coming to Italy.

To learn more about visa application deadlines and **requirements**, visit [http://vistoperitalia.esteri.it/home/en.](https://vistoperitalia.esteri.it/?lang=it_IT "this link") A guided procedure will help you find out whether you need to apply for a visa and where - based on your nationality, country of residence, reasons for visit and length of stay. Depending on your situation, you may be required to apply for a visa in your home country, in your country of residence or in the country where you are currently studying.

Visa requirements may vary. The official Acceptance Letter, issued by Bocconi University, is always required.

**Application Process**

Students must complete a 2-step process:

- Pre-register and submit the application on the Universitaly portal (mandatory) at [https://www.universitaly.it/](https://eur03.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.universitaly.it%2F&data=05%7C02%7Cvaleria.crincoli%40unibocconi.it%7C052b57df291447b9c2f708dd72c6de4c%7C6bf3b57a9fb447c29ada51156518f52f%7C1%7C0%7C638792920634676347%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJFbXB0eU1hcGkiOnRydWUsIlYiOiIwLjAuMDAwMCIsIlAiOiJXaW4zMiIsIkFOIjoiTWFpbCIsIldUIjoyfQ%3D%3D%7C0%7C%7C%7C&sdata=gAVv02%2BS2Xyk3NBAHk7mzdoSqAfnJ0N74ao2qQ8zOr0%3D&reserved=0 "Original URL: https://www.universitaly.it/. Click or tap if you trust this link."). This application is different and separate from the Bocconi Exchange Program online application. _Please note: the procedure to complete the Universitaly application for the Academic Year 2026–2027 is not yet open; a specific communication will follow with detailed instructions on this mandatory step._
- Apply for a Study Visa: procedures may vary from Consulate to Consulate. Students should follow the instructions provided on the website of the Consulate where they plan to apply. We recommend that students review visa procedures and start searching for a **visa appointment well in advance**.

Please note that, according to the Italian Authorities and ministries, acceptance into the exchange program does not guarantee the issuance of a visa. The decision is made solely by the relevant diplomatic mission, Consulate or Embassy.

Universitaly

Pre-register on the Universitaly portal at [https://www.universitaly.it/](https://eur03.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.universitaly.it%2F&data=05%7C02%7Cvaleria.crincoli%40unibocconi.it%7C052b57df291447b9c2f708dd72c6de4c%7C6bf3b57a9fb447c29ada51156518f52f%7C1%7C0%7C638792920634676347%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJFbXB0eU1hcGkiOnRydWUsIlYiOiIwLjAuMDAwMCIsIlAiOiJXaW4zMiIsIkFOIjoiTWFpbCIsIldUIjoyfQ%3D%3D%7C0%7C%7C%7C&sdata=gAVv02%2BS2Xyk3NBAHk7mzdoSqAfnJ0N74ao2qQ8zOr0%3D&reserved=0 "Original URL: https://www.universitaly.it/. Click or tap if you trust this link.")

To register, click on " **First steps in Italy**" box in the homepage and follow the registration guide. After that click on “ **Pre-enroll now**" that will appear in your personal area.

You will be asked to fill out a **3 step-form**:

**STEP A**:

- complete the form with your personal data: it must be entered exactly as shown on your passport.

**STEP B**:

- fill out the form with your passport number and expiration date, then upload it (the page with your personal data only)
- upload a passport-size picture of your face
- select the country and the Italian Embassy or Consulate where you will be requesting your visa; then in the section “specify the reason for the visa application” type in “I want to spend a period of mobility for study/traineeship (e.g. Erasmus)”
- select the University (Università Commerciale Luigi Bocconi), select Corsi Singoli in “course type” drop-down list
- in the “course name” tab, type in “Exchange Program”

**STEP C**:

- as an exchange student, in this section you must submit your Bocconi acceptance letter (downloadable from the Bocconi online Exchange Program application). In the “Qualifications and Language Certificates” drop-down list, select “Other”, then type in “Bocconi Acceptance Letter” in the “Qualification name” tab and upload the letter.

For **technical** assistance when completing the form, please click on “ [International Students Support](https://mipa.support.cineca.it/support?token=eyJ0eXAiOiJKV1QiLCJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiJ9.eyJmcm9tIjoid3d3LnVuaXZlcnNpdGFseS5pdCIsInNlc3Npb25faWQiOiIyNjQxZDdiNWE3YzIwMzBmMmNjNjMyMDBlNDFiMGYzYSIsInNlcnZpY2VEZXNrSWQiOiIxNzAiLCJjb21wb25lbnRJZCI6IjU5NjMzIiwiZmFsbGJhY2tBZGRyZXNzIjoiamlyYS5zZG11bml2aXRAY2luZWNhLml0IiwicmVmZXJlciI6Imh0dHBzOlwvXC93d3cudW5pdmVyc2l0YWx5Lml0In0.3zFKaNrzp4K0SE_Kp9FaDC9FzoQcWdHf-2sfnAtddBw)” at the bottom of the Universitaly webpage, or use the support link available in your application.

Permit of Stay

Once in Italy, all non-EU students will have to obtain a "Permesso di Soggiorno" (Permit of Stay). Further info is available at this [link](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/international-students/freshly-enrolled-students-when-you-arrive/permit-stay).
