# Academics for exchange students: Course selection

The **Course Selection** for the Fall semester of the AY 2026-2027 will run from **August 24-26, 2026,** through the yoU@B student diary at [**agenda.unibocconi.it**](https://agenda.unibocconi.it/ "agenda.unibocconi.it") **.** Courses will be assigned on a **first come-first served** basis and no waiting list will be available. To maximize your chances of securing classes, we recommend staying flexible and expanding your pre-approved tentative course list to include backup options, in case you are unable to register for your preferred classes.

Exchange students will also have the possibility to change their study plan during the **Add/Drop period** scheduled between **September 7 and 15**, 2026\. After the Add/Drop deadline **no changes** will be allowed on your study plan.

**All courses** included in the students' study plans **will appear** in their Official Bocconi final **transcripts**.

Since exchange students can choose from courses across all years and Degree programs, class schedule **overlaps** may occur. Class groups with the same course code may have different schedules and may be assigned a different number of credits.

In case you need assistance in building your study plan, you can contact the [**Academic Advisors**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/services/academic-advisors).

**STUDY PLAN GUIDELINES**

1. Exchange students must enroll in a **minimum of 1 and a maximum of 5 courses** per semester while at Bocconi and must ensure that they also satisfy their home university requirements.
2. **Undergraduate** students can select only courses at the undergraduate level (identified with code 30xxx). **Graduate** students are allowed to choose both undergraduate (30xxx) and graduate (20xxx/21xxx and 50xxx) courses.
3. Students who wish to complete all their exams during the first exam session must **ensure** there are no schedule conflicts among their exams. The exam schedule is available at the following link [www.unibocconi.eu/exams](https://didattica.unibocconi.eu/esami/index.php?&urlBack=/wps/wcm/connect/bocconi/sitopubblico_en/navigation+tree/info/current+students/timetables%2c+calendars+and+rooms/exams/).
4. If **two** final **exams** are scheduled on the **same** day and at the same **time**, students can select both courses and sit the exams in **different available exam sessions**. Bocconi University has multiple exam sessions, with Fall semester exams held in December, January and February and Spring semester exams held in May, June/July.

**Additional Course Selection Rules**:

· **Language courses**: exchange students **cannot** **add language courses** to their study plan

· **Attending vs. non-attending**: students may complete courses as either attending or non-attending. There is no need to specify this status when selecting courses, as it is managed directly with the instructor after the start of classes. Students must follow the instructions outlined in the course syllabi or explained by the instructor in class

**Course restrictions**:

- some courses have specific prerequisites: carefully review the **"Suggested Background Knowledge"** section in the [course profile](https://didattica.unibocconi.eu/ts/tsn_ord_ir.php) section.
- some courses might have overlapping contents/program: students should consult instructors to determine whether they can take both courses simultaneously.

**Special Programs Requirement** s

**CEMS** students are required to select their courses from the [**CEMS specific list**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/services/courses). For further information on CEMS requirements, please refer to [cems@unibocconi.it](mailto:cems@unibocconi.it)

**THEMIS students** can find further information about courses approved by the THEMIS network and program requirements on the [**Themis webpage**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/international-mobility/network-programs/themis-law-network "THEMIS Law Network"). For further information on Themis requirements, please refer to [themis@unibocconi.it](mailto:themis@unibocconi.it).

For more information on **CIVICA** requirements, students can reach out to [civica@unibocconi.it](mailto:civica@unibocconi.it)
