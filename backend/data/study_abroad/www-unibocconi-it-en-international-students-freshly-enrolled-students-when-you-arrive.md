# Freshly enrolled students - When you arrive

## NON-EU Students! Finalize your immigration status by getting your Permit of Stay

**IMPORTANT!** The **Visa** is the document that a **non-EU citizens** get from the Italian Embassy or Consulate in their country of residence and is **required for entry into Italy and the Schengen area**.

**Once** **in Italy**, it is necessary to **apply for the Permit of Stay for Study Reasons within 8 working days** of entry to stay legally in Italy.

[How to Apply for Your Permit of Stay](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/international-students/freshly-enrolled-students-when-you-arrive/permit-stay)

## Bocconi facilities

B in Touch

B in Touch can answer all your questions about the University. Visit the B in Touch Point, a welcome area on the Bocconi campus. You can also access the B in Touch service online from the

[yoU@B](https://agenda.unibocconi.it/ "mailto:you@b") student diary and search for the information you’re looking for by reading the always up-to-date FAQs or contact the various offices on campus!

Wi-Fi Connection

Wi-fi coverage is available throughout the entire Bocconi campus. Students can access the "bocconi-studenti" network using their ID number and password. Please note that you must approve terms and conditions of use of the service in your yoU@B Student Diary.

If you need assistance with your connection, you can refer to the **Wi-fi Help Desk**.

For problems, suggestions or clarifications, please send an email to the following address: [helpwifi@unibocconi.it](mailto:helpwifi@unibocconi.it)
