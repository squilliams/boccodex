# Estimated Costs

For further information about the cost of living in Milan, you can also visit this webpage on YesMilano's [website](https://www.yesmilano.it/en/study/cost-living-milano).

Housing

- Single room, on-campus dorm: 4000-4800€ per semester
- Private single room: 600 - 1100€ (per month)
- Private double room: 500 - 750€ (per month)
- Flat: 800 - 1800€ (per month)

Transports

- Electronic card: 10€ (first issue)
- Monthly pass for students up to 27: 22€
- Monthly pass for students over 27: 39€
- Single ride ticket: 2,20€
- Daily ticket: 7,60€
- Bike sharing: 4,50€ per day - 9€ per week - 36€ per year

Meals

- Italian breakfast: 2 - 4€ (espresso, coffee or cappuccino + croissant)
- Brunch: 20 - 30€
- One main course at the university canteen: 5€
- Lunch at cafes: 10-20€
- Ice cream: 2 - 5€
- Dinner at restaurant: 30 - 50€
- Dinner at pizzeria: 15 - 30€

Permit of Stay

- 100,46€ + 16€ for the revenue stamp

Health insurance (WAI ITALY)

- 6 months: 91€
- 1 year: 140€

Italian Language Courses

- In-person Italian Language Crash course: 250€
- In-person Italian Language Follow-up course: 200€

Entertainment

- Aperitivo/Happy hour: 7 - 15€
- Cinema: 7 - 13€
- Pub: 7 - 20€
- Concerts: 15 - 100€

Cell phone

- Sim card: 10€
