# International Networks

Bocconi University is committed to providing students with an educational experience that **goes beyond national borders**, preparing them to address challenges and explore opportunities on a global scale.

Through collaboration with academic institutions and networks worldwide, Bocconi offers students access to a truly international perspective. Our **exchange programs**, **Double Degrees** and **other study abroad opportunities** allow students to immerse themselves in diverse cultures, acquire intercultural skills and develop a deeper understanding of the geopolitical and economic dynamics shaping the global business landscape.

Since our first exchange agreements in 1974, our network has continuously expanded, providing students with increasing opportunities overseas. Our extensive global network includes **290** **partner schools** in **55 countries** across six continents.

Additionally, Bocconi proudly holds founding membership in prestigious networks such as the CEMS, PIM - International Management Network, the Themis Law Network and CIVICA, the European University of Social Sciences.
