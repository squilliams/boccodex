# How to apply

Undergraduate and Bachelor in Global Law students (AY 2025-26)

_**Latest update - April 16, 2026**_

Students wishing to attend a Summer Program in Summer 2026will have to formally apply at Punto Blu **starting from April 27, 2026 and no later July 17, 2026.**

**The relevant dates will be updated on this page once available.**

Entry requirements and travel restriction must be verified by students themselves prior to departure, in order to guarantee that the Program is carried out in compliance with applicable health requirements and visa law.

Students are responsible for the Host School tuition fees, travel, board and lodging expenses during the Summer Program. Participants are also required to be covered by a comprehensive international sickness and accident insurance policy while abroad.

Before the Application

- **check** if you meet the Bocconi the requirements (see [Eligibility](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/international-mobility/free-mover/free-mover-summer/free-mover-summer "Free Mover Summer") section);
- **check** if you meet the partner school requirements;
- **ensure** you can meet the partner school application deadlines and procedures;
- **check** if the course you want to attend and recognize is coherent with your Bocconi study plan;

How to Apply

**Apply** to the Free Mover Summer at Punto Blu, uploading:

1\. a valid English certificate - if not already registered in your career (see [Eligibility](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/international-mobility/free-mover/free-mover-summer/free-mover-summer "Free Mover Summer") section);

2\. the ‘Free Mover Summer 2026 - Student info' document (available below).

It is necessary to **save** the application receipt.

- [Free Mover Summer 2026 - Student info](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/Free_Mover%2BSummer%2B2026_Student_info.docx)

After the Application

The Study Abroad Office will **check** if you meet the Bocconi academic and language requirements and will **send** you a notification once the recognition platform is available in your yoU@B diary.

Through this tool you can send a recognition request to the Degree Program Director.

After the Bocconi recognition **approval**, you can carry out the **partner** school enrolment procedures. We allow our students to participate in Free Mover Summer Program, if they have the Bocconi academic and language requirements, but we do not select nor officially nominate them. It is ultimately up to the host schools to accept or not Bocconi students.

While applying to the Free Mover Summer, students can indicate **one Summer Program only.**
