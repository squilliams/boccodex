# Incoming Exchange Students

The **International Student Desk** (ISD) is the Bocconi office committed to helping **incoming exchange students** make the most of their stay on campus. Arrival in a foreign country and getting used to a new environment can be very challenging. The ISD offers exchange students a series of **tailored services** and **activities** to help them acclimate to their new surroundings during the beginning of their program and **supports** them throughout their stay.
