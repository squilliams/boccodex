# International Student Desk

**CONTACT US**

The International Student Desk at Bocconi University is the office in charge of incoming exchange students.

Our **On-campus Front Desk** and **Virtual Info Desk** are here to help answer any questions about your exchange.

**On-campus Front Desk:** B in Touch Point - box 002 ground floor (entrance from piazza Sraffa 13)

**January 19 to February 27**

- Monday: 10am-12.30pm
- Tuesday: 2.30pm-4.30pm
- Wednesday: 10am-12.30pm
- Thursday: 10am-12.30pm and 2.30pm-4.30pm
- Friday: 10am-12.30pm (virtual)

The On-campus Front Desk and Virtual Info Desk will be **closed** during the Welcome Week (January 26–30). You can find the ISD Team at the Welcome Desk, on floor -2 of the Roentgen building at Via Roentgen 1.

**March 3 to June 30**

- Tuesdays: 2.30pm-4.30pm
- Thursdays: 10am-12.30pm
