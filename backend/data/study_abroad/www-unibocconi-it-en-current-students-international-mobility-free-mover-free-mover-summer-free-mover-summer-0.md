# Free Mover Summer

Integrated Master of Arts in Law (AY 2025-26)

Free Mover Summer

[Overview](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/international-mobility/free-mover/free-mover-summer)

_**Latest update - December 22, 2025**_

**ELIGIBILITY & DESTINATIONS**

The Free Mover Summer is open to Bocconi students from the five-year **Integrated Master of Arts in Law** who meet the **requirements** and who can attend a Summer Program once, in the summer of their third or fourth year. Participation in a program in the summer of the first year, second year or before will not be recognized, as well as the participation in a program in the summer of the fifth year.

The application to the Free Mover Summer will be on-hold and the recognition platform will not be available until the Bocconi requirements are met. If an applicant does not meet the requirements before the beginning of the Summer Program, the application to the Free Mover Summer will be declined.

We allow our students to participate in Free Mover Summer Program, if they have the Bocconi academic and language requirements, but we do not select nor officially nominate them. It is ultimately up to the host schools to accept or not Bocconi students.

Academic Requirements

**3rd year students**

**Enrollment year:** Students regularly enrolled in the 3rd year of the five-year Integrated Master of Arts in Law.

**Credits:** At least 100% of 1st year, 60% of 2nd year credits registered. The calculation includes all the exams officially registered in the academic career by February 3rd, 2026 (end of the ordinary session).

In addition, applicants **must meet** the academic requirements of the partner school (if any).

**4th year students**

**Enrollment year**: Students regularly enrolled in the 4th of the five-year Integrated Master of Arts in Law.

**Credits**: At least 100% of 1st year, 60% of 2nd year credits and 60% of 3rd year credits registered. The calculation includes all the exams officially registered in the academic career by February 3rd, 2026 (end of the ordinary session).

In addition, applicants **must meet** the academic requirements of the partner school (if any).

Language Requirements

**3rd and 4th year students**

**Level B2 or higher** for English, proved by one of the options listed below:

- Bocconi English exam of level B2;
- University Legal Language Exam;
- English International language certificates of Level B2 or higher

In addition, applicants **must meet the language requirements of the partner school (if any)**

Destinations

Università Bocconi has selected a number of Partner schools from its international network that offer interesting and educationally valid Summer Programs. The following is the only list considered valid for the recognition of courses offered in the Summer Program through institutions abroad.

Students can submit applications for other alternatives that must be **examined** individually in advance by the Study Abroad office, **before** applying to the Free Mover Summer The request, accompanied by a letter of motivation and information material on the University/Program, has to be submitted through **B in Touch,** available on yoU@B Diary, to the Study Abroad Office.

- ESADE (ESADE Summer School)
- King’s College London (Summer Programmes)
- London School of Economics (LSE Summer School)
- SciencesPo (SciencesPo Summer School)
- University of California. Berkeley (Summer Sessions)
- Université de Genève (Université de Genève Summer Schools)
