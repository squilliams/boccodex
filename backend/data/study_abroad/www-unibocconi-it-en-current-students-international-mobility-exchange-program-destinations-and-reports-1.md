# Exchange Program

The Exchange Program offers students a unique international experience, allowing them to spend a term abroad at one of our 293 partner universities in 56 countries across five continents.

Through our bilateral agreements, students attending the program will carry out a study term, without paying tuition fees at the host school. However, students are responsible for covering their own travel expenses, accommodation, meals, and any other associated costs. Scholarships, such as those offered by ISU and Erasmus, are available. Courses completed at the partner institution can be recognized, subject to specific rules and procedures that students must adhere to.

Students can access the Exchange Program only once during a study program, through an application, upon possession of specific requirements. The Program entails a selection, with subsequent publication of the ranking, which takes place annually.

For more details, please refer to the sections below.

### Timing and deadlines

- Deadline for registering examinations (corresponding to the end of the ordinary winter exam period)\*: **February 3rd, 2026.**

- Publication of Brochures: **mid February 2026.**

- Application opens: **February 25th, 2026.**

- Deadline for application at Punto Blu and uploading of Language certificates\*\*: **March 3rd, 2026 at 3 pm (Italian time).**

- Results and confirmation: **end of March/beginning of April 2026.**

- For students selected for a destination on [sub iudice basis](https://bit.unibocconi.it/hc/en-us/articles/10798686905874-I-was-selected-sub-judice-for-a-destination-what-does-that-mean), deadline for passing the study plan second language oral exam: **by the June/July 2026 exam session, for all the programs. By the departure date, for BIG students.**

- For IM Global Experience and FIN Global Experience Track students, deadline for the submission of the Curriculum Vitae and the Video Interview: **March 3rd, 2026 at 3 pm (Italian time)**.

For the method for collecting the material please check [IM Global Experience and FIN Global Experience Track Selection Criteria.](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/international-mobility/im-global-experience-selection-criteria "https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/international-mobility/im-global-experience-selection-criteria")

\*To ensure that the **first language exam** is recorded in your study plan and considered in the **Exchange Program score calculation** through the **conversion of an international certificate**, it is essential to follow the timeline set by the Language Center. For additional details, please refer to the [dedicated page](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/language-center/certificates/language-certification-recognition) on the Bocconi Language Center's website.

\*\*If you need a certificate in order to comply with the language requirements, we strongly recommend that you take it as soon as possible. Please allow **enough time to receive the certificate**, always considering the above mentioned deadlines.
