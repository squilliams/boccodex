# Scholarships for internships abroad

**ATTENTION PLEASE! YOU'RE ACCESSING THIS PAGE VIA AN OLD LINK AND THE CONTENTS MAY BE NO LONGER VALID  (LAST UPDATE: APRIL 2024).**

**STARTING FROM MAY 2024, PLEASE REFER TO THE NEW CONTENTS AVAILABLE AT THE FOLLOWING LINKS FOR** [**STUDENTS**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/career-services/your-internship-start-credit-recognition "Your internship: from start to credit recognition") **AND** [**COMPANIES**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/employers-and-partners/activating-and-managing-internships "Activating and managing internships") **.**

\\*\\*\\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*

The pursuit of **an unpaid internship abroad** (involving no monetary grant) may entitle the student to a scholarship provided by the University. The chart below will help you identify the scholarship best suited for your internship.

**Remember that each scholarship may entail additional requirements: check your eligibility by following the links below.**

If your internship (for credits or without credits) takes place in an **Erasmus country**

[**Erasmus+ scholarship**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/services/erasmus-scholarships-ay-2023-2024) **(a.y. 2023/2024)**

If your internship is hosted by an **International Organization**

[**Grant for internships at International Organization and Chambers of Commerce**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/services/grant-internships-international-organizations-and-chambers-commerce-ay-2023-2024) **(a.y. 2023/2024)**

If you have been selected for **Banco MAECI — MIUR — FCRUI**

[**Grant for internships at the offices of the MAECI (Ministry of Foreign Affairs and International Cooperation)**](https://www.unibocconi.it/it/corsi-di-studio/area-studenti-iscritti/servizi/contributo-stage-presso-le-sedi-estere-del-maeci-ministero-degli-affari-esteri-e) _— **only in Italian**_

Moreover, check if you are eligible for funding from

[**Contributions for International Mobility Programs**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/services/isu-bocconi-scholarship-integrations-0)
