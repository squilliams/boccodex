# Health Insurance

Before their arrival, students should ensure they  are properly **covered** in case they require  **medical care during their stay in Italy**.Bocconi University does not provide students with an insurance policy.Students shouldcheckif the social security scheme in their  country of residence covers the cost of medical treatment in Italy.

**EU citizens**: be sure to bring the **European Health Insurance Card** ( **EHIC**), which entitles the holder to get necessary treatment at the same cost as  citizens of the host country. The card is valid only for **state-provided services**, asprivate healthcare is not covered. We recommend that , before departure, students verify with the social security administration of their home country how to obtain  the card and which services are covered.

EU citizens can also apply for **Form S1**. They should obtain their S1 form from the healthcare institution of their  home country **before  departure**. Students should confirm eligibility with the social security institution of their country of residence, as requirements may vary between EU member states.

**Non-EU citizens**: before leaving their home country these students are strongly advised to obtain **health** **insurance  with international coverage.** To apply for a Permit of Stay, students will be required to prove that they are covered for medical expenses  for the entire duration of their stay in Italy.
