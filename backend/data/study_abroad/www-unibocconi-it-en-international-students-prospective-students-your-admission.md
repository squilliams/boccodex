# Prospective students - Before your admission

Choosing a university degree program is a very important process. Bocconi University provides prospective students with important information to make a well-informed choice.

There is a wide variety of guidance activities to help you understand the content of our programs in depth, discover the campus and learn more about our teaching method.

**Check out your program of interest and learn more about your choice!**
