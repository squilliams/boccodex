_Double Degrees_

A Double Degree is a program that allows you to study at two top business and economic schools and receive two diplomas, one from each University. You will spend the first academic year at Bocconi and the second academic year at a prestigious partner.

- HEC École des Hautes Etudes Commerciales (Paris, France)
- Keio University (Tokyo, Japan)

_Joint Degree_

A Joint Degree is a joint program that allows you to study at two top business and economic schools and receive one joint diploma, signed by both universities. You will spend the first academic year at Bocconi and the second academic year at a prestigious partner:

- Université Catholique de Louvain (Louvain, Belgium)

_Exchange Programs_

A study abroad experience lasting one term at a top partner university. Our Exchange partners include:

- Yale University, Phd in Economics (New Haven, US) - 1-year Program
- The University of Wisconsin-Madison (US)
- Erasmus Universiteit – School of Economics (The Netherlands)
- Universite Toulouse I Capitole - Toulouse School of Economics (France)
- Singapore Management University, School of Economics (Singapore)
