# Housing

Bocconi offers you the chance to make the most of student life by staying in one of our 8 university residence halls. You’ll live in the heart of campus, in an international, intercultural and stimulating environment.

It is a life experience that will stay with you forever.

The University also offers students the chance to make the most of their university experience by staying in one of the 8 university residence halls.
