# Academics for exchange students: MBA courses

**MBA level** incoming **exchange** students at Bocconi will be eligible to take **graduate (MSc) courses** offered within the Bocconi exchange course catalogue.

For a **limited** number of students, however, Bocconi offers the opportunity to take some **MBA courses** belonging to the **SDA Bocconi** School of Management MBA curriculum. These courses are offered in English, and they might change every year, therefore additional information will be provided as the course start date approaches.

Students regularly enrolled in the SDA MBA program will have prioritized access, therefore enrollment into MBA classes **cannot be guaranteed** in advance. Places are **limited** and assigned on the basis of the student’s CV, which will be evaluated by the SDA Bocconi MBA office.

MBA students will directly be contacted before the course selection with detailed information.
