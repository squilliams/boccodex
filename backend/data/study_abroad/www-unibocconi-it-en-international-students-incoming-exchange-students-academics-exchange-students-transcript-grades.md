# Academics for exchange students: Transcript of Grades

Incoming exchange students who are enrolled in the exchange program at Bocconi University are provided with instructions on how to autonomously access their **official  Bocconi University digital transcript**.

After each exam session, the transcript will be updated with the exam results, as soon as these grades are officially registered in the Bocconi systems. This process is not automatic, and might require some time.

**Please note**: grades obtained during midterms will not appear on the transcript; only final exams grades will be listed.

An official PDF version of the digital transcript will be sent to each partner university Coordinator, at the end of the semester. **Hard copies** are sent to our partner universities **ONLY upon request**.

To ensure the security and authenticity of e-transcripts, these documents are released through the Bocconi system and can be verified at this [link](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/student-administration/certifications/verification-certificates-electronic-format).

The conversion of Bocconi grades is managed by the student’s home university, based on their grading conversion criteria.
