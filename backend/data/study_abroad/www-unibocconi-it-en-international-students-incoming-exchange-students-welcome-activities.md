# Welcome Activities

Just before the beginning of each semester, we organize an intensive calendar of **Welcome Activities**, designed to meet the needs of incoming exchange students. Our Welcome Activities are aimed at helping students **familiarize** with Bocconi, become better acquainted with the **academic** **environment**,comply with **administrative procedures** and make **new** **friends** in order to be ready for this once in a lifetime opportunity!
