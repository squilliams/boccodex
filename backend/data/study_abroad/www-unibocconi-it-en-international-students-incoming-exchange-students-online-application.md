# Online application

The Fall 2026 online application will be open from April 9 to May 20 at 3pm (Italian time), 2026.

To apply online you will need the USER ID and PASSWORD that will be sent to you and your Home University coordinator.

IMPORTANT: the online application will open on a rolling basis, based on the date your nomination is received from your Home University.

In the application you will be required to upload the most updated version of:

- CV (resume)
- Grade report
- Headshot photo (for the Bocconi ID card)
- Passport personal data page (EU citizens can submit their national ID)
- Disability certificate issued by the Home University (only for students in need of special accommodations)

For questions on the Exchange program, please refer to [inexchange@unibocconi.it](mailto:inexchange@unibocconi.it).
