The MSc in Finance offers students different study abroad options, both for short periods (such as Exchange programs) and for longer periods (Double Degree), depending on the track Finance Track or Global Experience Track you’re enrolled in.

_Double Degrees_

If you are attending the Finance Track, you can apply to participate in a Double Degree program. These programs allow you to study at two top business and economic schools and receive two diplomas, one from each University. You will spend the first academic year at Bocconi and the second academic year at a prestigious partner.

- **ESADE** Escuela Superior de Administracion y Direccion de Empresas (Barcelona, Spain)
- **HEC** École des Hautes Etudes Commerciales (Paris, France)
- **Keio University** (Tokyo, Japan)
- **RSM** Rotterdam School of Management, Erasmus Universiteit (Rotterdam, Netherlands)
- **Stockholm School of Economics** (Stockholm, Sweden)
- **NOVA School of Business & Economics** (Lisbon, Portugal)
- **HSG** University of St.Gallen (St.Gallen, Switzerland)

_Exchange Programs_

If you are attending the Global Experience Track or Finance Track you have the opportunity to participate in an Exchange program, which will allow you to study abroad for one term at a top partner university. A few of our most prestigious Exchange partners include:

- **University of Wisconsin-Madison**, Joint PhD Program in Finance and Economics (Madison, US) – 1-year program
- **EDHEC** Business School (France)
- **University of St. Gallen**(Switzerland)
- **Jiao Tong University**, Shanghai Advanced Institute of Finance (China)
- **Instituto Tecnológico Autónomo de México, ITAM** (Mexico)
- **The University of New South Wales** (Australia)
