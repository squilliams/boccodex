# Internships

Since the Exchange is a full time program, internships cannot be activated during the exchange teaching period.

Students can refer to the Career Services to look for internship opportunities and prepare for job interviews.

Full details on the support provided are available on the [Career Services webpage](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/career-services "Career Services").
