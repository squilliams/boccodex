# Medical Assistance

#### **Euromedica**

Health center located at **Viale Bligny 11**. After signing up for an annual membership card at the **cost of €12**, students, faculty and staff have a **10%** discount on specific outpatient services. For further information [click here](https://www.euromedica-assistance.com/).

#### **Individual Counseling**

A **Counseling and Self-Empowerment** desk is available to students, to support their psychological well-being and help them develop greater self-awareness of their personal resources. Individual counseling (offered in Italian, English or French) aims to help **identify** and **resolve** situations of **difficulty** and **discomfort**, while also strengtheningtheir personal resources, through a personalized pathway developed together. Further **information** available [**here**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/services/counseling).

#### **Health Services**

For further information about **health services** and the on-campus medical center during your stay in Italy visit [**this webpage**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/international-students/freshly-enrolled-students-you-arrive/health-services "Health Services").
