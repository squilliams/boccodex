# Free Mover Summer

_**Latest update - April 16, 2026**_

The information here available reflects the **current situation to date**.

Students can attend Summer Programs in **Summer 2026.** Nevertheless, whether the overall conditions would not safeguard the wellbeing and security of students, Bocconi University does not guarantee that students will be able to attend Summer Programs blended/in person. Before applying, you are required to carefully read the Bocconi Students **TRAVEL RISK POLICY**, available on yoU@B – International SOS and **the Travel Risk Policy Exceptions for A.Y. 2025/26:** in-person Summer Programs 2026 **will not be authorized** in the countries where the student Exchange is suspended.

**GENERAL OVERVIEW**

Bocconi University has selected a number of partner schools from its international network which offer interesting and educationally valid Summer Programs.

The **Free Mover Summer** allows students to spend a short period abroad in the summer months (around 2-3 weeks) to attend a Summer Program and take a curricular course for which they can receive credit. The course can be registered in the academic career at Bocconi **.**

Students wishing to attend a Summer Program in **Summer 2026** will have to formally apply at Punto Blu **starting from April 27, 2026 and no later than July 17, 2026**. Check the section 'How to apply' for details.

Click on the links below to discover the **undergraduate** and **law**  opportunities.

Please note that the Free Mover Summer **is not open to MSc students.**
