# Free Mover Summer - How to apply

Integrated Master of Arts in Law (AY 2025-26)

_**Latest update - April 16, 2026**_

Students wishing to attend a Summer Program in **Summer 2026** will have to formally apply at Punto Blu **starting from April 27, 2026 and no later than July 17, 2026**.

**The relevant dates will be updated on this page once available.**

Entry requirements and travel restriction to enter the Host Country must be verified by students themselves prior to departure, in order to guarantee that the Program is carried out in compliance with applicable health requirements and visa law.

Students are responsible for the Host School tuition fees, travel, board and lodging expenses during the Summer Program. Participants are also required to be covered by a comprehensive international sickness and accident insurance policy while abroad.

Before the Application

- **check** if you meet the Bocconi the requirements (see [Eligibility](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/international-mobility/free-mover/free-mover-summer/free-mover-summer-0 "Free Mover Summer Law") section);
- **check** if you meet the partner school requirements;
- **ensure** you can meet the partner school application deadlines and procedures;
- **check** if the course you want to attend and recognize is coherent with your Bocconi study plan;

How to Apply

**Apply** to the Free Mover Summer at Punto Blu, uploading:

1\. a valid English certificate - if not already registered in your career (see [Eligibility](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/international-mobility/free-mover/free-mover-summer/free-mover-summer-0 "Free Mover Summer Law") section);

2\. the ‘Free Mover Summer 2026 - Student info' document (available below).

It is necessary to **save** the application receipt.

- [Free Mover Summer 2026 - Student info](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/Free_Mover%2BSummer%2B2026_Student_info_0.docx)

After the Application

The Study Abroad Office will **check** if you meet the Bocconi academic and language requirements and will **send** you a notification once the recognition platform is available in your yoU@B diary.

Through this tool you can **send** a recognition request for the course you wish to attend during the Summer Program to the Director of the Integrated Master of Arts in Law for the course with or without correspondence, in order to obtain his/her preliminary approval. Please be aware that - during the request procedure - you are required to attach a detailed course syllabus or to provide the website link related to the course.

After the Bocconi recognition **approval**, you can carry out the **partner** school enrolment procedures. While applying to the Free Mover Summer, students can indicate **one Summer Program only.**
