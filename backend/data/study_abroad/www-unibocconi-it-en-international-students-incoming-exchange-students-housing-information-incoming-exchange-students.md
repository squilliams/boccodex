# Housing Information for Incoming Exchange Students

Incoming exchange students will have the chance to reserve a room in a student residence hall located on campus or in its immediate surroundings.

For the academic year 2026/2027 incoming exchange students will have a dedicated housing application. Housing at Bocconi is not guaranteed and rooms are assigned on a first come - first served basis.

In order to apply for housing at Bocconi, students are required to complete a **separate housing application**, managed directly by the Bocconi Housing Office.

The Housing Office will contact nominated students before the housing application opens, by providing them with all the relevant instructions on how to complete it.

Information on AY 26-27 housing is available on [this webpage](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/bachelor-and-law-programs/housing/exchange-incoming-housing-ay-2026-27).
