# Welcome Activities

Before the official start of classes, in late August and early September, Bocconi invites all first-year students to participate in our Welcome Days. A series of events and initiatives created to welcome you at Bocconi campus and introduce you to our course program, with special events for international students.

Your schedule is tailored to fit your specific group.
