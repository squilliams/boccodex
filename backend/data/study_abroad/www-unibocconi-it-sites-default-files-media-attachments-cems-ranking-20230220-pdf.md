Allocation

# CEMS semester abroad

## RANKING – RULES OF THE GAME

- The final allocation will take into consideration the average weighted on credits, according to the following formula: Student academic weighted credit average (WA) * [Number of credits earned (NC) divided by the maximum number of credits that can be earned (MAX NC)].
- The calculation of credits includes all the exams of the 1st semester of the 1st Master of Science year (study plan officially taken by February 4th, 2023 included, end of the ordinary exam session). Exams that are not part of the 1st semester of the 1st Master of Science year study plan (internship, languages, anticipated exams and anticipated curricular supplementary activities) are not included.
- Courses subdivided into modules: the credit points and the grades earned for the single modules passed will be taken into account and they can be viewed by printing the unofficial academic transcript at the Punto Blu.

# Internship

8 consecutive weeks full-time abroad. Abroad = outside the Home School Country (outside Italy)

Timeline: during Summer 2023 or after the end of academic activities of Term 2 23-24.
It cannot be overlapping with the CEMS year (Block Seminar, term dates).

# CEMS Graduation

1° CEMS Graduation available: December 2024
If you plan to Graduate from CEMS in December 2024, last graduation in Bocconi in October 2024.

2° CEMS Graduation available: December 2025
If you plan to Graduate from CEMS in December 2025, last graduation in Bocconi in October 2025.
