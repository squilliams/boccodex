# Application & Selection Timeline referred to mobility a.y. 2026/2027

|     |     |
| --- | --- |
| **APPLICATIONS** |
| **ALL MSc PROGRAMS** | from **November 12** to **November 27, 2025 (11:59pm)\***<br>Application and dossier have to be uploaded at Punto Blu by the deadline. Students can choose **from one and up to all the destinations** available for their MSc Program in order of preference.<br>The dossier must include the following documents:<br>- Curriculum vitae in English<br>\- Copy of ID/Passport<br>- Copy of Bachelor Degree and transcript of records (only for students that did not attend the Bachelor at Bocconi)<br>- Language Certificates and GMAT (if needed) <br>accepted until **January, 26 2026 (11:59pm)** at Punto Blu. |

|     |     |
| --- | --- |
| **ONLINE VIDEO INTERVIEWS** |
| **ALL MSc PROGRAMS** | By **January 8, 2026 (11:59pm)**<br>Only applicants meeting the minimum requirements will receive an ad hoc email with the instructions on how to access the online video interview platform. |

|     |     |
| --- | --- |
| **LANGUAGES/GMAT or GRE** |
| **ALL MSc PROGRAMS** | English/French/GMAT or GRE: students holding a valid certificate should upload it at Punto Blu by the application deadline ( **November 27, 2025 at 11:59pm)**. Otherwise, certificates will be accepted at Punto Blu until **January 26, 2026 (11:59pm)\*\***.<br>**Important!** TOEFL and IELTS certificates must have been awarded no more than 2 years prior to the Double Degree application deadline. All other certificates must have been awarded no more than 3 years prior to the Double Degree application deadline which is **November 27, 2025**. |

|     |     |
| --- | --- |
| **SELECTION RESULTS\*\*\*** |
| **ESS** **and IM Concentrations\*\*\*\*** | By the end of January, 2026 |
| **MM, FINANCE - Finance Track, AFM,**<br>**ACME, EMIT, GIO, PPA** | By the end of February, 2026 |

**IMPORTANT:**

\*Students failing to submit all requested documents by the deadlines will be excluded from the DD selections.

\*\*If students apply for a destination where the French proficiency is required (level B2 for ACME - HEC Paris and HEC Montréal; level C1 for GIO - Sciences Po Masters taught in French) and do not satisfy the requirement with a valid certificate, they will be automatically enrolled in the French test organized by the Global Alliances office. The date and time of the internal French test will be shared with the relevant candidates only, at a later stage.

_\\*\\*\\*_ Students may also apply for the Exchange Program; however, they must confirm their participation in the Double Degree before the Exchange Program rankings are published. Students' names will appear in the Exchange Program rankings only if they decline their place in the Double Degree Program, without paying the administrative contribution. Students selected for the Double Degree program will have a few days to confirm their participation by paying the required administrative contribution.

Students who do not accept the destination assigned are automatically excluded from the program. They will not be assigned any other destination.

A destination that was not included among the original application choices, cannot be added among the choices of the candidate after the publishing of the Double Degree rankings (even if spots for said destination are still available).

Once selected, please make sure to start the VISA application process on time.

\*\*\*\*ESS and IM Concentrations candidates will be admitted to the Double Degree Program sub iudice, provided they submit a valid language certificate by January 26, 2026 (if needed). Students who fail to provide a valid certificate by the deadline will be excluded from the Program, even ifpreviously selected.
