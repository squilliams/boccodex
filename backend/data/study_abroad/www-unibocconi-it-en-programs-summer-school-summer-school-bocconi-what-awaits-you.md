# Summer School at Bocconi: what awaits you

## Schedule & Activities

Here you can find all the details you need to know about our exciting **curricular** and **extracurricular activities**.

Explore our comprehensive **schedule**, stay updated with important updates, and access a variety of links to enhance your summer learning experience.

Make the most out of your summer with us!

**Welcome and opening ceremony**

**Monday 29 June, morning (via Roentgen 1, Aula Magna)**

Welcome to Bocconi Summer School!

After receiving your welcome kit at the reception desk, you will attend the opening ceremony and then join a campus tour.

You will receive an e-mail with the meeting time.

**Lectures and group work**

**Monday 29 June, afternoon**

The first lecture will take place on the first day, after lunch. All the details will be shared in the morning during the opening ceremony.

For all the following lectures and group work sessions you have to refer to your course syllabus, on [Blackboard](https://blackboard.unibocconi.it/) (Bocconi e-learning platform).

_Please note that Blackboard will be available starting from one week before the beginning of the program._

**Visits**

**During the program**

Besides the lectures and the group work, you will be involved in one or two visits.

Please refer to your course syllabus, on [Blackboard](https://blackboard.unibocconi.it/) (Bocconi e-learning platform) and follow the instructions given in class by your Professor.

_Please note that Blackboard will be available starting from one week before the beginning of the program._

**MSc Programs Presentation**

**Tuesday 14 July, afternoon (via Roentgen 1, Aula Magna)**

The Summer School can be a way to experience Bocconi before considering it for your Master. Find out about our MSc programs: offer, admissions procedures, financial aid, student and campus life. This is the perfect moment to ask all your questions!

**Exam**

**Friday 17 July, morning**

The final exam will take place on the last day of the program.

Only students who attend at least 80% of the academic activities and pass the exam, will receive the final official certificate and 6 credits.

**Cooking Class**

**First week of the program, after 5pm (FoodGenius Academy - Viale Col di Lana, 8)**

Make new friends while cooking with professional chefs... and don’t forget to come hungry!

Attention: to attend this activity it is mandatory to register.

**Bocconi Sport**

**During the program**

Enjoy our Sport Center! Summer School students have the possibility to access Bocconi sport facilities to make the most of their experience.

**Farewell Party**

**Wednesday 15 July, evening**

Celebrate your Summer School experience with your classmates during a farewell "aperitivo" - location and timing will be sent via e-mail.
