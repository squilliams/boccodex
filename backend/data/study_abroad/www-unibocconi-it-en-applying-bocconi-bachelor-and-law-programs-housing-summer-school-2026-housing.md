# Summer school 2026 housing

If you are enrolled in one of the Bocconi Summer School programs that will take place during summer 2026, you have the opportunity to make the most of your experience by choosing accommodation among one of the options offered by the University. Please check on this page all the details and how to apply!

Summer school for High School students

High school students will be offered accommodation in the Bocconi residence halls for the period **Sunday, July 5th - Friday, July 17th 2026.**

Bocconi will reserve 6 entire buildings to the high school students, namely the following residences: Bocconi, Javotte, Bligny, Castiglioni, Dubini, Spadolini. Please check all the details and pictures of each residence [here](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/housing/bocconi-residence-halls). The price for accommodation in one of our residence halls for the whole period will be **1600,00 euros**.

Rooms available will be all single bedrooms, with bathroom shared with only one other student of the same gender. All rooms are placed in apartments of 2 or 4 bedrooms, or alternatively in clusters of single rooms with shared bathroom. The Summer School participation does not include meal plan.

If you wish to stay at the same residence with another Summer school High school student, please apply at the same time together and select the "Bocconi residence". We will only be able to put you together in that residence.

In order to submit the **separate housing application**, after having completed your enrollment in the program, please check the information below. We suggest you to apply for housing as soon as possible after your enrollment, as availability cannot be guaranteed for all participants due to the overall limited number of rooms.

The link to apply for housing will be available on this webpage starting from 11 March 2026 and it will be available until rooms will be finished, and in any case until 21 April 2026 at 6 pm.

Before you apply for housing, it is necessary that you have completed the application for the Summer School program.

Attention!

Application is open to high school students in their third-to-last and second-to-last year in Italy or abroad.

Only students who will have already turned 15 years old by the start of the program are eligible to participate in Bocconi Summer School.

[**ONLINE APPLICATION - Summer school for high school students housing**](https://admissions.unibocconi.it/admissions/Students/OpenFromExtUrl?concourseId=14458&ordType=)

**no rooms left**

**How to apply**

- Access the link with the same userID/password already used for the program application

- Complete the "Residence selection" section by choosing the residence hall you prefer among those available.

- Select the room and click on "Save" at the end of the section

- Complete the "Contact details" section and click on "Save" at the end of it

- Click on "Confirm data": this will be the necessary step to secure the room

- Proceed with the payment of the accommodation through the link available in the subtitle of the "Payment&documents" section; then upload the receipt of payment in the dedicated upload space

- Print, sign (attention! handwritten signature is required and mandatory) and let your parent/legal guardian sign all the documents available in the application; then upload each of them in the correspondent upload space

- Click on "Submit application"

The office will get back to you by publishing the confirmation of your housing assignment and all the information for your check-in on the same portal around mid-May 2026.

Summer school in Law for High School students

Students of the Summer school in Law will be accommodated at the Bligny residence for the period **Sunday, July 5th - Friday, July 10th, 2026**.

Please check all the details and pictures of the Bligny residence [here](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/campus/buildings-and-classrooms/bligny-residence).

The price for accommodation at the Bligny residence for the whole period will be **800,00 euros**.

Rooms available will be all single bedrooms, with bathroom shared with only one other student of the same gender. All rooms are placed in apartments of 2 or 4 bedrooms, or alternatively in clusters of single rooms with shared bathroom. The Summer School participation does not include meal plan.

If you are interested in staying at the Bligny residence, you will have to submit a **separate housing application**, after having completed your enrollment in the program. When accessing the application, please select the option "Summer School in LAW - Bligny residence" as it will be the only one available for the Law program; the other residence halls will not be available. Please apply for housing as soon as possible after your enrollment, as availability cannot be guaranteed for all participants due to the overall limited number of rooms.

The link to apply for housing will be available on this webpage starting from 11 March 2026 and it will be available until rooms will be finished, and in any case until 21 April 2026 at 6 pm.

Before you apply for housing, it is necessary that you have completed the application for the Summer School program.

[**ONLINE APPLICATION - LAW STUDENTS Summer school accommodation**](https://admissions.unibocconi.it/admissions/Students/OpenFromExtUrl?concourseId=14458&ordType=)

**How to apply**

- Access the link with the same userID/password already used for the program application

- Complete the "Residence selection" section by choosing ONLY the the "Summer school in LAW - Bligny residence" option. Please do not select other options as they will NOT be available for LAW students.

- Select the room and click on "Save" at the end of the section

- Complete the "Contact details" section and click on "Save" at the end of it

- Click on "Confirm data": this will be the necessary step to secure the room

- Proceed with the payment of the accommodation through the link available in the subtitle of the "Payment&documents" section; the upload the receipt of payment in the dedicated upload space

- Print, sign (attention! handwritten signature is required and mandatory) and let your parent/legal guardian sign all the documents available in the application; then upload each of them in the correspondent upload space

- Click on "Submit application"

The office will get back to you by publishing the confirmation of your housing assignment and all the information for your check-in on the same portal around mid-May 2026.

Summer school for Bachelor and Master students

Students of the Summer School for Bachelor students or of the Summer School for Master students will be offered the opportunity to apply for a room for the period **Sunday, June 28th - Saturday, July 18th 2026** at Aparto Giovenale, an external residence located on campus with which Bocconi has a partnership and that already hosts numerous Bocconi students.

**From April 1st, 2026: please contact the office at [summerschool@unibocconi.it](mailto:summerschool@unibocconi.it) if you need accommodation**

ONLINE APPLICATION - BACHELOR and MASTER summer school accommodation - expired

How to apply

- Access the link with the same userID/password already used for the application for the program

- Complete the "Reserve your room" section by choosing the room type you prefer (ENSUITE room)

- Select the room and click on "Save" at the end of the section

- Click on "Submit data/Conferma dati" at the top right hand corner

- Now you see a new section of application ("Ensuite room reservation"): it means that your have secured a spot. The section contains the document "How to complete your reservation". Please follow the instructions contained in it, in order to complete the reservation of your room at Aparto Giovenale.

- Fill in your contact details and click on "Save" at the end of the section

- Click on "Submit application/Inoltra application"

**Important note**

Aparto reservations will NOT be considered valid unless the procedure described above is completed; i.e. Aparto will only manage reservations submitted through the Bocconi housing application, meaning that the student has accessed the Bocconi Housing portal, selected and confirmed one of the Aparto options, viewed the subsequent sections of the application, and only then completed the procedure on the Aparto portal. The Bocconi Housing Office will share the official reservation list with Aparto. Any reservations not included in this list and/or submitted through different channels will be automatically cancelled.
