# Must - Know

At Bocconi you will be located close to the city center in a safe and vibrant area. Our campus is woven into the urban fabric of Milan and is powered by a full range of facilities and services to help you make the most of your time here. Find out more about Housing, Health & Safety, Diversity and Inclusion and some useful tips on living in Milan here below.
