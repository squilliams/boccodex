# Academics for exchange students: Exams

Exchange students are allowed to take exams during the available **official exam sessions,** within their exchange period and **on Bocconi** **premises** only. Students who sit and pass all the exams of their study plan during the first available exam session are free to leave campus starting from the **Earliest Departure Date**. Check the Earliest Departure Date on the [**Academic Calendar**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/services/academic-calendar-exchange-students). Students are entitled to sit and re-sit exams during the second exam session as well. Exam re-sits are allowed **only in case of failure**.

**Registration** for each exam must be carried out within **4 working days** prior to the exam date (Saturdays, Sundays and public holidays are not included), through the yoU@B agenda, in order to have the grades registered. Exam dates and registration deadlines are available at [**this webpage**](https://didattica.unibocconi.eu/esami/index.php).

Exams can be either oral or written; some courses might foresee mid-terms. More than one exam may be scheduled on the same day. Exam **rooms** are posted on the student's yoU@B agenda the afternoon of the working day before the exam.

**Grades** are assigned on a scale of **30/30**:

- 0 to 17: failing grades
- 18 to 30: passing grades

Exams that award a passing grade cannot be re-sat, while failed exams can be attempted again in the next available session within the exchange period.
