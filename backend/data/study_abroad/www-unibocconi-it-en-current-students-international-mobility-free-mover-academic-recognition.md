# Academic recognition

Undergraduate Students (AY 2025-26)

**EXAMS RECOGNITION**

Students applying to the Free Mover Summer can recognize **only one course**, which will be registered the Bocconi academic career with a grade out of thirty. In case there is no indication of a grade, it will not be possible to recognize the exam (i.e.: pass/fail grading system is not valid).

Before the Summer Program

The partner school course you wish to attend must have a corresponding Bocconi course and it has to be approved by the Degree Program Director or delegate. The program must be approved **before** the Summer Program begins. It is the student's responsibility to verify that the recognized course is coherent with the Bocconi study plan.

Once the Recognition platform is available, you must send the recognition request to the Degree Program Director in order to obtain his/her preliminary approval.

In order to do so you need to **upload** to the platform an overall description of the Summer course you wish to attend. The Degree Program Director has to receive all the major information on the course (e.g. course description - objectives, contents, lectures and/or special workloads, written/oral exam, the number of credits assigned to the course and/or the number of classroom and lecture hours, the local instructor's contacts).

After The Summer Program

Upload the partner school’s official transcript of records (mentioning course, grade and credits) to the Recognition platform and proceed with the recognition.

Exams that **CANNOT** be recognized are:

- foreign language exams and computer tests;
- courses that are not coherent with the student's study plan;
- list of courses for which credits cannot be granted if taken abroad (please refer to the Guide to the University).
