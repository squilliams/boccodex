# BSc in International Government Selection Criteria

Exchange Program 2026-27

Selection is based on merit evaluation, taking into consideration the student's **academic weighted average** and the **number of credits**.

**Student WA + \[0.1 x (Student NC - MIN NC required)\]**

0.1 points will be attributed for each credit above the minimum number of credits required (40% of 1st year credits registered). These points will be added to the weighted credit average.

The calculation of credits thus includes all the exams (part of the 1st year study plan and 2nd year - 1st semester - study plan) officially taken by **February 3rd, 2026** (included), end of the ordinary exam session.

If the student wants to be considered eligible for a specific destination, **in case of additional language requirements** are specified, he/she **must have a certificate valid for both terms indicated on the brochure.**

When **rankings** are published, students will be notified the assigned destination.

**Please note:**

- Courses subdivided in moduleswill be considered as **separated courses** for the academic requirements and exchange score calculation: they can be viewed by printing the unofficial academic transcript at the Punto Blu.

- If applicants have equal final scores for a specific destination and there is one last slot available, the students' preferences linked to the destination will be used as criteria in order to set the ranking. In case of equal preferences, priority will be given to the student who applied earlier (considering hh:mm:ss).
