# Costs & Scholarships

Costs

Students selected for the Double Degree program will not be required to pay **any tuition fee to the host institution**, but should confirm their participation to the Double Degree by paying **to Bocconi an administrative contribution of 2.000 Euros.** This contribution will not be returned even if the student does not complete the Double Degree program.

Students will continue to pay fees at Bocconi while abroad.

Some partner institutions may require the payment of administrative fees for different reasons (application, graduation fee, visa costs, insurance, teaching material or other costs). Students are responsible for these expenses, and Bocconi cannot reimburse them.

For all the Double Degree programs with selection at the time of [Bocconi MSc programs' application (LSE, Sciences Po, China MIM, Essec-Bocconi DD, Yale SOM)](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/master-science/application-and-admission/master-science-programs-202425-ay) the administrative contribution is of 2.000 Euros and specific financial scheme applies.

Scholarships

The scholarships available to students are the following:

- — Erasmus+ scholarships (for destinations included in the [**Erasmus Program**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/services/erasmus-scholarship));
- — [**Contributions for International Programs.**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/funding)
