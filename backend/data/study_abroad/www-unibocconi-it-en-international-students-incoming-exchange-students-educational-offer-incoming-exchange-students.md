_The A.Y. 2026-2027 educational offer will be available in July 2026._

The 25-26 Educational Offer for incoming exchange students is also available on this [page](https://didattica.unibocconi.eu/ts/tsn_ord_ir.php). To search for courses offered in the Fall 25 semester select “I sem./Yearly” while for courses available in the Spring 26 semester select “II sem./Yearly” from the Semester drop-down menu. Additional filters can be applied on this page, such as course name, course level, Department and language of instruction.

**Important**: some courses may have specific restrictions: carefully check the "Suggested background knowledge" section available in the course profile [page.](https://didattica.unibocconi.eu/ts/tsn_ord_ir.php)
