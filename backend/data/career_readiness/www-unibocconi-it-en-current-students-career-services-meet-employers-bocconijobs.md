# Bocconi&Jobs

## 13-15 April, 2026

_**Bocconi&Jobs** is the University's main career fair. It is held twice a year and hosts employers from various countries and sectors, including Auditing, Consulting, Finance, Industrial & Consumer Goods, Services, Law and Tax Firms, Tech._

_A unique opportunity to **interact with recruiters** on-campus, **discover internship and job opportunities**, and **submit your application**._

## Latest edition

_The **spring edition** of Bocconi&Jobs was held in a hybrid format according to the following structure:_

- _**Preview days (10-12 April, 2026)**, to access the virtual booths of Employers, prepare for on-campus meetings, and view available internship and job offers;_
- _**Online webinars (13 April, 2026)**, consisting of a brief company presentation with a final Q&A session, to gather information directly from Employers in preparation for on-campus interactions;_
- _**Live days (14 and 15 April, 2026)**, dedicated to on-campus interaction with company representatives;_
- _**Extra application days (until 26 April, 2026)**, to continue applying online for available opportunities._

![](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/styles/container_100_lg/public/media/images/immagine_2026-02-09_120445_1.png.webp?itok=u2pYAgeW)

## Info

Bocconi&Jobs is dedicated exclusively to **students and graduates up to one year after graduation from the Undergraduate School, and students and graduates up to three years after graduation from the Graduate and Law School.**

**Registration is mandatory** to join the event and must be completed through the **dedicated Bocconi&Jobs website,** madeavailable closer to the event.

The list of participating employers can be found on the **landing page of the Bocconi&Jobs website,** made available closer to the event.
