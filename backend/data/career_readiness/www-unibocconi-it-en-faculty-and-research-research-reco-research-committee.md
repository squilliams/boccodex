# ReCo – Research Committee

**ReCo – Research Committee**

The Research Committee (ReCo), with regard to research activities, is a consultative and preparatory body, and also a decision-making body, but only within the scope outlined in letter b) of the following paragraph and any other areas identified by the Academic Council.

In particular, the Research Committee is assigned the following functions and tasks:

a. to provide opinions and make proposals for the evaluation of research activities;

b. to propose and implement programs to support and promote research activities.

The Committee is currently composed as follows:

**President**

Dean for Research - Prof. Elena Carletti

**Department Representatives**

Department of Accounting - Prof. Wanli Zhao

Department of Computing Sciences - Prof. Francesca Buffa

Department of Decision Sciences - Prof. Igor Pruenster

Department of Economics "Ettore Bocconi" - Prof. Nenad Kos

Department of Finance - Prof. Nicolas Serrano-Velarde

Department of Legal Studies “Angelo Sraffa” - Prof. Giovanni Strampelli

Department of Management and Technology - Prof. Paola Cillo

Department of Marketing - Prof. Chiara Longoni

Department of Social and Political Science - Prof. Alessia Melegaro

**CODICE**

The Committee of Directors of Research Centers (CODICE) is assigned the role of providing opinions and making proposals regarding the Research Centers.

CODICE is composed of the Dean for Research, who serves as its chair, and the Directors of the Research Centers.
