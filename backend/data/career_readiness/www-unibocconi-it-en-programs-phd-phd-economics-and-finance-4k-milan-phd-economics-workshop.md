**12-13 May 2026** · Bocconi University · Milan, Italy

**Economics, Finance, and Accounting**

**Overview**

The **Milan PhD Economics Workshop** is an annual academic conference that brings together doctoral researchers from leading global institutions to present and discuss cutting-edge research in economics, finance, and accounting.

Hosted by **Bocconi University** in May 2026, the Workshop aims to provide friendly and stimulating enviroment in which PhD students can share ideas, receive constructive feedback and engage with peers from a wide range of institutions.

**Keynote Speakers**

[**Víctor Sancibrián**](https://sancibrian-v.github.io/)

Assistant Professor of Economics, Bocconi University · Affiliate at IGIER

PhD, CEMFI · Research interests: econometrics, panel data, and heterogeneity in micro–macro analysis.

[**Amalia Repele**](https://sites.google.com/view/amalia-repele)

Assistant Professor, Institute for International Economic Studies (IIES), Stockholm University · Visiting Scholar at Riksbank

PhD in Economics & Finance, Bocconi University (2025) · Research interests: macroeconomics, labor markets, and heterogeneous agents.

**Program**

The 2026 Workshop will feature two days of paper sessions and keynote addresses.

**Call for Papers**

The Organizing Committee warmly invites submissions from PhD students and early-career researchers working in

all areas of economics, finance, and accounting. We welcome both theoretical and empirical contributions, including

work at an early stage of development.

Submitted papers will be reviewed by the Organizing Committee, and selected contributors will be invited to present

their work in dedicated paper sessions. The Workshop is designed to encourage open discussion and constructive

feedback, with the goal of helping participants improve their research and presentation skills.

Both single-authored and co-authored papers are eligible for submission.

[**Submission form**](https://bocconi.eu.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_0qs46wyjJaH1CMS)

[**Attendance form**](https://bocconi.eu.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_3rR0SzgjmJlZcvs)

There is no participation fee. Coffee breaks and lunches will be provided for all conference participants. Participants

are responsible for their own travel and accommodation costs. An informal social dinner will also be organized for

those who wish to attend, at their own expense.

**Paper submission deadline: February 28th, 2026**

Attendance registration deadline: April 15th, 2026

**About**

The Milan PhD Economics Workshop is jointly organized by doctoral students from Bocconi University, Università

Cattolica del Sacro Cuore, the University of Milan, the University of Milan–Bicocca, and the University of Pavia. It

serves as a forum for emerging scholars to exchange ideas, refine research, and engage with peers and faculty from a

broad range of international programs.
