# Concentration in International Organizations

Explore the Program

[Overview](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/master-science/economics-and-management-government-and-international-organizations) [Study Plan](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/master-science/economics-and-management-government-and-international-organizations/study-plan) [Study Abroad Programs](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/master-science/economics-and-management-government-and-international-organizations/study-abroad-programs) [Program Placement](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/master-science/economics-and-management-government-and-international-organizations/program-placement)

The concentration structure includes:

a. at least 1 course chosen from among the following:

- ECONOMICS OF EUROPEAN INTEGRATION, in English
- PROJECT MANAGEMENT AND FUNDING OF INTERNATIONAL PROGRAMS, in English

b. at least 2 courses chosen from among the following ones plus those not chosen from point a) above:

- PUBLIC ECONOMICS, in English
- DEVELOPMENT ECONOMICS, in English
- COMPARATIVE POLITICS: INSTITUTIONS AND INSTITUTIONAL DEVELOPMENT, in English
- PUBLIC MANAGEMENT FOR COMPETITIVENESS, in English
- EUROPEAN POLITICS: BETWEEN TECHNOCRACY AND POPULISM, in English
- CITIES AND REGIONS: MANAGING GROWTH AND CHANGE, in English
- GEOPOLITICS FOR BUSINESS, in English
- POLITICS OF CONFLICT, in English
- INTERNATIONAL AND COMPARATIVE TAXATION, in English

c. The possibility to choose 1 course freely chosen from among the [offered electives](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/master-science/economics-and-management-government-and-international-organizations/electives).

Please note that the courses listed above are subject to review every year.
