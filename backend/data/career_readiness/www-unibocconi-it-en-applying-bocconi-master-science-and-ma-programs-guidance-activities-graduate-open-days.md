# Graduate Open Days

_**Take part in our Graduate Open Days, dedicated to students attending universities in Italy and abroad, and Bocconi BSc students.**_

_Participate and discover what it's like to be a Bocconi student. Attend our program presentations, interact with faculty, graduates and students to make the best choice for your future studies!_

## Activities

- **Bocconi and the academic offer**, introduction to Bocconi with the Rector and the Dean of the Graduate School
- **MSc Programs**, presentations and Q&A with Program Directors
- **Admissions**, dedicated info desk and chat with Bocconi staff
- **Fees, Funding and housing**, dedicated chat with Bocconi staff
- **Specialized Master Programs**, dedicated chat with Bocconi staff
- **Info desk** on the services available at Bocconi
- **Student Lounge**, to meet Bocconi students and talk about courses, exams, housing and much more about university life
- **Virtual tour** of the campus
