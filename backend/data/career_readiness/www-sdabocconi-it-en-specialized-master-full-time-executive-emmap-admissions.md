# Selection and admission process

The selection process is based on the comprehensive analysis of the documentation received and other elements and aims to have a highly qualified, motivated class group capable of contributing to a dynamic and stimulating learning environment. We assess not only whether you are the right profile for EMMAP, but also whether EMMAP meets your expectations.

Admission is through a rigorous selection process that evaluates the professional background, motivation and personal aptitude of those who apply. Each year, EMMAP welcomes outstanding figures in PA: professionals, consultants, people in leadership and managerial roles, who choose to embark on the EMMAP journey with strong motivation and a cultural vision oriented towards change and innovation.

Selection thus ensures the formation of a class group in which talented individuals can not only pursue their professional and personal goals but also enrich the on campus learning experience.

## Admissions results

As soon as the selection process is completed you will receive a formal letter from the Admissions team, notifying you of the admission decision. Selection results are never communicated by telephone. All decisions made by the Admissions committee are final.

The selection process can result in one of the following outcomes:

**Admission:**

You will receive the official admission notice along with the participation contract. To confirm your participation, you must complete the registration process and payment of the first installment by the deadline specified by the committee. If all available spots have been filled, the school may place you on a waiting list or offer admission for the following edition. Admission to the program is valid for the year the application is submitted. However, you can request deferment to the following year, but approval of deferment is on a case-by-case decision by the Admissions committee.

**Non-admission:**

the selection process evaluates not only the individual profile but also the overall composition of the EMMAP class. While we acknowledge the significant effort all candidates invest in the application and the selection process, we do not provide personalized feedback to non-admitted candidates.

## How to apply

The Application for EMMAP 2026-2027 is available **exclusively online** and must be completed with accurate and precise information regarding your personal and professional experiences, your reasons for attending the program, and other aspects of your personality.

**Please note that the online application portal is a separate platform, so your SDA Bocconi website (www.sdabocconi.it) credentials cannot be used. Please proceed with a new registration.**

- All required documents must be uploaded to the relevant section of the Application for admission portal and submitted to the Admissions team
- Applicants may track the receipt of their application and monitor their application status directly through the online application
- Additional documents can be uploaded after the initial application submission

Before submitting your application, you should read the brochure carefully.

## We look for highly motivated candidates with well-defined goals

The selection process for EMMAP 2026-2027 is **rolling** and runs from July 2025 to 15 April 2026.

The outcome of admissions will be announced within a few weeks after the application is submitted and the selection process is completed. Due to the limited availability of places, we recommend that you start the admission process well in advance of the Master program start date.

**To increase your chances of admission, please submit your application as soon as possible.**
