# Marketing and business development

Marketing and business development are strategic levers to create value and build long-lasting relationships with customers. For companies and managers, it is essential to recognize diverse consumer needs and understand market changes in order to design personalized, innovative, and effective commercial and marketing strategies.

In this section, you will find the School’s full educational portfolio dedicated to this topic, along with events, news, research activities, and in-depth content.
