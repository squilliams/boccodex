# Bachelor of Science Programs

#### **Dean of the Undergraduate School**: Chiara Fumagalli

Bocconi offers a variety of **Bachelor Degree** programs **in the Social Sciences,** coordinated by the Undergraduate School, **in the fields of Economics, Management, Finance, Political Science, Data Science and Artificial Intelligence**.

The **teaching model** adopted for all Bachelor programs emphasizes constant interaction between students and faculty through group projects, simulations and case history analysis. You will also be encouraged to broaden your horizons with a choice of **study abroad opportunities** at leading partner universities around the world, and take advantage of our contacts with **top employers** throughout your time on our **vibrant urban campus**.

After graduation, you can choose to either **continue your education** with a graduate program or **join the workforce** with the skills and knowledge sought after by employers.

Undergraduate programs usually last **three years**: only the World Bachelor in Business – jointly developed in partnership with the Marshall School of Business-University of Southern California and the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology – is a **four-year program**.

Bocconi also offers two flagship programs in the field of Law open to high school students: the [**Bachelor in Global Law**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/law/bachelor-global-law "Bachelor in Global Law"), – taught in English – and the [**Integrated Master of Science in Law**](https://www.unibocconi.it/it/corsi-di-studio/giurisprudenza/laurea-magistrale-giurisprudenza "Laurea magistrale in Giurisprudenza")– **which lasts 5 years and is taught in Italian**.

Student Life

_Bocconi is about more than just classes_

Your years on campus are about more than just the classes you attend: Bocconi and Milan also promote activities focused on culture, sports, art and sharing ideas. They help make your Bocconi experience an exciting time that is key to personal and professional growth.

Our Campus

_A Sustainable Campus_

Our campus is a safe, multicultural and lively place, located in downtown Milan. Both its historic buildings and new spaces are integrated into the urban fabric to create an active environment, reverberating with university life. The campus includes a recreational sports center, a large park and a new student residence hall.

Living in Milan

_A European and cosmopolitan city_

Milan is an open, international and multicultural city, a European metropolis full of events and public services with an unparalleled nightlife. It is a renowned symbol of fashion and design, as well as an important financial, economic and business hub.

An Open and Inclusive Community

_Once a Bocconian, always a Bocconian!_

You become a member of the Bocconi community when you start your program, joining other students, faculty, staff, Alumnae and Alumni. But Bocconi is not only a real community where you spend your days on campus, but also – and increasingly more so – a community you can get to know through our online social networks.
