# Network PARI

Public Affairs e relazioni istituzionali

##### In collaboration with Cattaneo, Zanetto, Pomposo & Co., a leading Italian company in the public affairs sector

Public management

The **PARI (Public Affairs and Institutional Relations) Network** was created as a response to the almost complete absence in Italy of spaces for reflection on the **management of the public affairs function**. It is designed for companies, institutions, trade associations, and nonprofit organizations interested in creating a space for dialogue and skill development in the field of institutional relations. The Network is evolving into a true community of professionals. Its goal is to explore and compare the effectiveness of models and tools for managing the corporate function responsible for relations with policymakers and regulators.

While there are various occasions for discussing the role, competencies, and professional paths, there is a lack—unlike in other countries—of specific debate on the corporate dimension of this function and the management issues related to it, such as:

- The role of the public affairs function in strategic planning
- Criteria for choosing between make (in-house professionals) and buy (external specialists)
- The choice between internalizing expertise and outsourcing to specialists.
- Organizational structure and placement within the company.
- **Budget-setting criteria**.
- Metrics for monitoring and evaluating results
- Issues related to recruiting and developing personnel in the public affairs function
- Incentives for public affairs personnel
- Differences in decision-making processes and institutional culture between Italy and other countries and the implications for designing the public affairs function
- Coordination of public affairs in sectors with multi-level governance (State and Regions, or European Union and Member States)
- Principles for coalition building and role distribution among stakeholders with converging interests
- Models for effective collaboration between companies and trade associations in public affairs activities

Despite significant progress in the professionalism of the public affairs function, conscious management of the choices that influence its effectiveness—particularly in terms of alignment with corporate strategic priorities—remains overlooked. It is therefore essential to promote the dissemination of best practices and the identification of significant innovations.

The PARI Network – Public Affairs and Institutional Relations – offers an opportunity for dialogue and in-depth exploration, supported by SDA Bocconi research, aimed at developing relevant knowledge and skills in the field.

## The PARI Network offers:

- ### 3 THEMATIC WORKSHOPS

Participate in 3 half-day thematic workshops, designed to foster dialogue between participants and SDA Bocconi researchers on the managerial issues of the public affairs function and to identify actionable solutions.

- ### BENCHMARKING STUDY

on the management models adopted by participating organizations. The study compares the benefits, limitations, and overall effectiveness of the adopted solutions, providing concrete insights for improvement.

- ### Access to the PARI KNOWLEDGE BANK

A dedicated repository collecting materials from all Network initiatives and useful documents to optimize the management of the public affairs function.

- ### Personalized COACHING sessions

Upon request, it is possible to organize a half-day meeting with SDA Bocconi researchers to analyze the management solutions adopted by the organization and discuss improvement initiatives.

- ### CUSTOM

The annual program is defined in close collaboration with an advisory committee composed of representatives from the organizations enrolled in the Network. This approach ensures that activities are fully aligned with the community’s priorities and emerging challenges.

## Program Advisory

Learning is never a linear journey—it is a path built together, combining specific training needs with practical tools tailored to individual challenges. Contact our team of Program Advisors, and together we will design the ideal learning path for you or your team, ensuring alignment with your goals.

**Your benefits:**

A dedicated orientation meeting, either in-person or remotely, to identify learning needs and create a customized plan. Throughout the course, you will experience a tailored welcome to introduce you to the program and foster networking between participants and instructors. Upon completion, your evaluation will become a valuable contribution for continuous improvement of the offering.

**How your organization benefits:**

Training consultation with HR managers to analyze organizational needs and develop personalized projects. Ongoing progress monitoring and a final follow-up to share results and gather feedback, ensuring constant alignment with pre-set goals.
