# Intended Learning Outcomes

Data Science and Business Analytics

[Overview](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/master-science-program/data-science-and-business-analytics) [Governance](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/master-science-program/data-science-and-business-analytics/governance) [Placement and Program Performance](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/master-science-program/data-science-and-business-analytics/placement-and-program-performance) [Program structure](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/master-science-program/data-science-and-business-analytics/program-structure)

During the Master of Science program, students acquire specific knowledge and skills related to design and management of big databases, Data Science techniques and management (strategic and marketing issues).

Through the teaching activities that characterize the course, they develop skills in

- making judgements autonomously
- improving their written and oral communication
- acquiring lifelong learning skills useful for both continuing their studies and their professional futures.
