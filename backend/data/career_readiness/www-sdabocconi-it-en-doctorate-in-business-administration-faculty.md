# Faculty

In addition to some of the most experienced educators of our faculty, the program has invited **internationally acclaimed faculty and industry experts**.

Participants in the SDA Bocconi DBA program can count on **one-to-one support** and advice from faculty with expertise covering the entire spectrum from pure academic to mostly applied research.
