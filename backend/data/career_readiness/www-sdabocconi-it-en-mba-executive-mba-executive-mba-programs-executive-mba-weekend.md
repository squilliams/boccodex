# Executive MBA Weekend

## Transforming perspectives, fostering impact.

General management and strategy

_Start_ May 2026

_Duration_ 18 months

_Where_ Milano / Roma

_Format_ Weekend

_Language_ Italian, English

_Avg. working experience_ 13 years

SDA Bocconi’s Executive MBA is more than a master’s program—it is a pivotal step in personal and professional transformation. Guided by the claim "Transforming Perspectives. Fostering Impact," the program empowers executives to redefine their outlook, embrace change and create lasting impact within their organizations and beyond.

Designed for ambitious professionals, the master equips students with leadership and management skills, while enhancing their ability to adapt, prioritize and strategize in today’s dynamic business environment.

For executives seeking flexibility without compromising career growth, the **Weekend format** provides an ideal balance between professional, academic and personal commitments. **Available in Milano and Roma**, this **bilingual format integrates sessions in Italian with selected modules in English**, fostering an inclusive and dynamic learning environment.

Structured around weekend sessions, it allows participants to apply new knowledge immediately in their professional roles while building a strong network across industries. This format supports those who want to advance their leadership skills and strategic vision while maintaining their current responsibilities.

## Who is it for

The Executive MBA – Weekend Format is designed for high-potential professionals and executives with solid work experience who aim to pursue their aspirations and significantly expand their growth opportunities.

It is ideal for those seeking to balance an investment in their skills and competencies with their professional commitments: individuals looking to gain knowledge that can be immediately applied in a professional context, fostering a productive synergy between study and work.

It is also for those aspiring to build an active network with fellow participants, faculty and alumni, enhancing both personal growth and the contributions they make to society.

## Why Executive MBA

- ### Gain theoretical knowledge, specific skills, and action-oriented methods

The EMBA is a unique experience for your managerial development. It allows you to enhance and expand your knowledge base, consolidate theoretical approaches and operational intervention methods, and strengthen your motivations.

- ### Balance professional ambitions with personal needs

By fostering a dynamic learning experience that integrates seamlessly with work and personal commitments, the program enables participants to enhance their leadership skills, broaden their perspectives and drive meaningful impact—all while maintaining the balance essential to long-term success.

- ### Engage with a prestigious faculty

The faculty at SDA Bocconi School of Management forms the cornerstone of the EMBA. Throughout the program, their expertise is enriched by contributions from managers, entrepreneurs and professionals from the Italian and international business landscape.

- ### Join a highly qualified international network

The Executive MBA is a gateway to a high-profile professional network, that extends well beyond the program's completion, connecting you to the broader Bocconi University and SDA Bocconi Alumni community. It is an invaluable resource for building relationships, exchanging ideas and accessing professional opportunities.

- ### Deliver lasting benefits to your organization

The Executive MBA is a high-return investment for companies as well, driving organizational innovation and enhancing employee motivation.

## A comprehensive learning experience

- ### Learning environment

The Executive MBA integrates an intensive face-to-face teaching approach, essential for engaging with faculty and guest speakers, fostering participant interaction and ensuring effective group dynamics. The program also includes distance learning activities and self-directed learning moments such as case studies, group projects, virtual teamwork, simulations and experiential learning.

- ### A highly effective learning mix

The Executive MBA offers a differentiated and flexible learning model enriched with theoretical frameworks and best practices. It provides an action-oriented experience that can be directly applied and tested in the workplace. Additionally, it connects you with professionals from diverse industries and backgrounds, fostering continuous exchange and mutual growth.

- ### Learning through application

The program employs a training process focused on applying models and interpretive frameworks that are validated through real business cases. These cases illustrate the evolving dynamics of industries and markets on an international scale.

- ### A flexible approach with diverse tools

The program offers flexible schedules and learning spaces combined with a rigorous, innovative curriculum. This curriculum integrates theoretical frameworks and action-oriented learning that is directly applicable in the workplace. Participants also benefit from networking with professionals across various sectors, creating a dynamic and enriching peer group.

- ### Faculty and industry experts

The program is anchored by the faculty of SDA Bocconi School of Management and Bocconi University. Their contributions are enhanced by insights from managers, entrepreneurs and leaders from Italian and international businesses. These collaborations add valuable professional and personal perspectives to the learning experience.

- ### Methods and tools

A distinctive feature of the Executive MBA is the advanced technological resources provided by SDA Bocconi, including a dedicated device and an innovative e-learning platform. This versatile platform serves as a repository for shared materials, assessments and a robust knowledge management tool.

- ### Continuous assessment

To maintain the program’s high quality and provide participants with regular feedback, the evaluation process is ongoing. This ensures that each participant achieves the competencies expected at the Executive MBA level. At the end of each academic cycle, the Program Director, in collaboration with the faculty, reviews individual progress.

## Admissions

We seek highly motivated candidates with diverse backgrounds, strong professional experience and well-defined personal qualities. Our rigorous selection process assesses motivation and personal strengths, ensuring a dynamic and stimulating EMBA class each year.

**Requirements:**

- Degree certificate (in any discipline), transcript of the grades
- Application form
- Professional experience (at least 6 years), detailed CV
- Proficiency in English
- SDA test or GMAT
- Sponsoring company application
- Interview
- Leadership skills, interpersonal abilities and a team-oriented mindset.
