# Faculty

**A Faculty of researchers and professionals**

The MiMS faculty is multidisciplinary and composed of Bocconi professors and researchers involved in institutions dedicated to healthcare management:

- [**CERGAS**](https://cergas.unibocconi.eu/), the Centre for Research on Health and Social Care Management, which has been developing applied research to support healthcare management since 1978
- The GHNP Division (Government, Health and Not-for-Profit) at SDA Bocconi, which, over the past two decades, has built extensive experience in executive education programs for the healthcare sector (such as [**EMMAS**](https://www.sdabocconi.it/en/specialized-master-full-time-executive/emmas/), [**Executive Management Training Courses**](https://www.sdabocconi.it/en/on-campus-executive-education/), and [**Custom Management Training Programs**](https://www.sdabocconi.it/en/executive-custom-programs/)), as well as in the development of the Master of International Healthcare Management, Economics and Policy ( [**MIHMEP**](https://www.sdabocconi.it/en/specialized-master-full-time-executive/mihmep/)).
