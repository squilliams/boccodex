Participation in the EMF program requires a substantial financial commitment. However, it is a strategic investment that delivers high future returns—both personally and professionally.

## Fees and expenses

The tuition fee for the EMF 2027-2028 program is €40,000 (outside the scope of VAT), payable in four installments.

- First installment

**€ 10,000**

upon enrollment

- Second installment

**€ 10,000**

by 31 March 2027

- Third installment

**€ 10,000**

by 30 June 2027

- Fourth installment

**€ 10,000**

by 30 September 2027

**The fee includes:**

  - Teaching materials
  - Leader series activities
  - HR series
  - Coaching
  - Media Experience Lab
  - EMF C-suite forum
  - Refinitiv platform (EMF's training partner)
  - Applications provided by the teaching project (JMP, Crystal Ball, MATLAB...
  - School equipment, including a light dinner offered on Friday evening and during full week when necessary for teaching purposes.

Participants must have a personal laptop. For those who request it, the use of a PC Laptop provided by SDA Bocconi will be possible by paying a security deposit of €600 before the start of the program. At the end, the PC must be returned and the security deposit will be refunded.

Participants in [**EPFIRE - Executive Program in Finanza Immobiliare e Real Estate**](https://www.sdabocconi.it/en/on-campus-executive-education/epfire-executive-program-in-finanza-immobiliare-e-real-estate-272938/) of SDA Bocconi interested in the Executive Master in Finance (EMF) - Corporate Finance & Real Estate track will have the opportunity to enroll in the Master’s program with a 10% tuition reduction.

## Scholarships and tuition waivers

_1st round_
14 May 2026

_2nd round_
15 July 2026

_3rd round_
15 September 2026

_4th round_
15 December 2026

SDA Bocconi provides special pricing on the Executive Master in Finance fee in the form of partial waivers, aimed at outstanding profiles that will help promote the program's various objectives.

Applications must be submitted at the application stage by the rounds indicated in the competition notices.

Timely submission of the online application for admission offers a better chance of being awarded a tuition waiver.

The allocation of tuition waivers averages 25% of the registration fee. Those admitted may receive a waiver of up to 50% of the Executive Master in Finance fee.

**To apply for a tuition waiver you must:**

- have completed and submitted the Application for Admission to EMF, with all required documents;
- submit the application in response to the notice, attaching the specified documentation, through the Application portal;
- meet the deadlines and carefully check the requirements, without which the application cannot be accepted.

**The waiver request can be submitted at the selection stage. Applications will be evaluated on the basis of:**

- academic and professional curriculum;
- requirements stated in the notice;
- results of selection tests at EMF;
- interview with EMF management.

Admission to EMF is independent of whether a scholarship or tuition waiver is awarded.

The tuition waiver will be awarded during the selection process within the round dates indicated in the announcement.

## Loans and funding

SDA Bocconi assists candidates and applicants in their search for funding by providing the necessary documentation to finalize applications for financial support. For more information you can consult the section on funding on the Bocconi University website (for [Italian](https://www.unibocconi.it/it/studenti-iscritti/agevolazioni/finanziamenti-master-universitari-e-sda), for [international applicants](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/student-loans)). The application for funding must be received by the School by the end of the month following the course start date.

The University also makes information available to [international students](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/funding-country) regarding possible sources of funding in different countries of the world.
