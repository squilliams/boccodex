3 courses chosen from:

- GLOBAL FINANCIAL MARKETS, in English
- STRUCTURED AND PROJECT FINANCE, in English
- PRIVATE EQUITY AND VENTURE CAPITAL, in English
- FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT (ADVANCED TOPICS), in Italian
- ENTERPRISE AND FINANCIAL RISK MANAGEMENT, in Italian
- MERGERS AND ACQUISITIONS, in Italian and in English
- RIORGANIZZAZIONI FINANZIARIE E DISTRESSED VALUE INVESTING, in Italian
- INTERNATIONAL CORPORATE FINANCE, in English
- BIG DATA FOR BUSINESS DECISIONS, in English
- FINANCIAL STATEMENTS ANALYSIS (ADVANCED COURSE), in English
- FINANCE AND DEVELOPMENT, in English
- INVESTING IN ALTERNATIVE – ILLIQUID ASSETS, in English

1 course freely chosen from among the electives offered by the Graduate School.
