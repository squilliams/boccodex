The LLM in European Business and Social Law (EBSL) is a full-time, 12-month Specialized Master program taught entirely in English.

Students will develop a thorough comprehension of EU law and institutions, as well as an overview of international law — combining theory with a practical, hands-on approach. This global dimension is essential for success in today’s increasingly interconnected and competitive market.

The program is divided into four legal areas of specialization: Digital Law; Foundations of Law; Public and Social Law; and Business Law.

**Dates:** September 2026–September 2027

**Duration:** 12 months

**Mode and language:** full-time in person, in English

## Employment

This program prepares participants for **diverse professional paths.** Graduates with a law degree may join legal teams within **companies operating in the European internal market,** while those with other academic backgrounds can apply their legal expertise across departments such as **data analysis, risk assessment, marketing, communications** or **finance**.

Alumni are also well-positioned for roles in **European research centers and institutions,** contributing to policy-oriented research and impact assessments, or in **NGOs and civil society organizations** engaged in advocacy. Recent graduates have interned at **prestigious institutions and organizations**, including the European Commission, International Criminal Court and International Bar Association, as well as in major firms such as Deloitte, PWC and IBM.
