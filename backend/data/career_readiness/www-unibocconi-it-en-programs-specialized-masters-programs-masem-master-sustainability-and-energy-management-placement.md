# Placement

The **Master in Sustainability and Energy Management** equips students with the knowhow, skills and competencies to pursue a career in sustainability, energy and innovation within leading companies in both the public and private sectors, consulting firms, utilities, international organizations and NGOs, public institutions:

![](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/media/images/career.jpg)

**Support services**

The Bocconi Career Service, in cooperation with MASEM staff, helps students enter the job market by providing:

- on-campus presentations and the Bocconi&Jobs career events
- JobGate, a web-based area with internship and job offers
- a Placement Library offering information and reference documents on the international and Italian job markets
- CV Book, an annual publication collecting the CVs of MASEM graduates, shared among the corporate partners
