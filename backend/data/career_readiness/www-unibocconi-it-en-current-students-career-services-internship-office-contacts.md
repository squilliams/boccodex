**Have you already found an internship?**

Do you need to know how to activate, carry out or recognize it?

The Internship office is at your disposal; choose one of the following contact points.

_For any question regarding internship and placement opportunities, please refer to the_ [_Career Service Infopoint_](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/services/career-services-infopoint) _(Bachelor of Science programs) or the_ [_account of their Master of Science_](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/services/distance-personalized-advice-1)

[_program_](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/services/distance-personalized-advice-1) _within the Career Service._

**B in Touch**

Mailing service available on yoU@B, by selecting B in Touch, “Internships" topic.

Take a look at our brand new FAQs; if you do not find an answer, send us a request. You will receive our support via email on your Bocconi account.

**Who:** **all students and graduates** who have found an internship and need to start, carry out or end it.

**When**: always on, from Monday to Friday.

**ONE-TO-ONE SESSION (remote via Teams)**

Before booking a meeting with the internship office, please read the module “Internship... let’s go!” available on Bboard (you may find there answers to all your questions). While booking, remember to indicate the reason for the appointment.

**Who:** students and graduates whose **internship is about to start, it’s being carried out or it already ended**.

**When:** Monday (afternoon) Tuesday (morning), Thursday (afternoon) and Friday (morning).

**Where to book:** Appointments. Access your yoU@B Diary, left menu Internship> Internship activation and recognition, in the activities calendar select **Internship — One-to-One Session**. On the day and time of the appointment click on the link of Teams you will find in Appointments.

**DEDICATED PHONE NUMBER**

You may contact the Internship Office on the dedicated number +39.02.5836.2478.

**Who:** **all students and graduates** who have found an internshipand need to activate, carry out or conclude it.

**When:** **Mondays, Wednesdays and Thursdays (from 9.30 to 10.30); Tuesdays and Fridays (from 14.30 to 15.30).**

**Wednesday 17 January:** _**suspended**_

_For urgent matters please get in touch with us through B in touch > Internship_

_Due to the high volume of calls, you might find the phone line busy (if so, the phone will not ring). We remind you that you can always reach us on B in touch._
