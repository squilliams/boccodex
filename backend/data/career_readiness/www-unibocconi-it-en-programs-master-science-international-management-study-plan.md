The first part of the study plan includes educational activities that are common to all programs. The second part of each program includes educational activities allowing you to personalize your study plan.

For details on the various activities of the IM programs, see the dedicated pages:

- [**IM Concentrations**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/master-science/international-management/im-concentrations "IM Concentrations")
- [**IM CEMS MIM**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/master-science/international-management/im-cems-mim "IM CEMS MIM")
- [**IM Asia - China MIM**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/master-science/international-management/im-asia-china-mim "IM Asia - China MIM") Double Degree program with the Fudan School of Management
- [**IM Asia - DD ESSEC**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/master-science/international-management/im-asia-essec-bocconi-double-degree "IM Asia - ESSEC-Bocconi Double Degree") Double Degree program with ESSEC in Paris and Singapore
- [**IM Concentration – DD ESSEC Luxury Management**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/master-science/international-management/im-concentration-dd-essec-luxury-management "IM Concentration – DD ESSEC Luxury Management")

- **Behavioral skills seminar**
- **Managerial applications (business game)**

The first part of the study plan (1st year) includes educational activities that are common to all programs. These activities include core courses, a business game, and a behavioral skills seminar, with slight differences across programs.

**IM Concentrations** includes:

- one compulsory concentration course
- one concentration elective on data analysis
- one concentration elective
- one free elective

**IM Global Experience** includes:

- one course on supply chain management
- one elective course in the area of management
- two free electives

All programs include:

- professional English seminars
- second language
- supplementary curricular activities
- internship
- thesis

The second part of each program (2nd year) includes educational activities allowing you to personalize your study plan through electives. In addition, seminars, an internship and two EU languages enrich your CV and your thesis will complete your curriculum.
