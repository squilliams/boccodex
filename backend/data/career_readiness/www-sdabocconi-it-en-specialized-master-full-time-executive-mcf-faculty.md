# Faculty

**A pool of dedicated experts**

The MCF faculty includes over 20 teaching and research staff at the SDA Bocconi School of Management, that have strong links with the financial community. In addition, 12 senior executives from financial institutions and several companies’ financial departments make up the Advisory Board. We also host professional faculty and visiting professors from other top international universities and institutions that actively cooperate with SDA Bocconi.

## MCF advisory board

The Master in Corporate Finance launched its Advisory Board.

This brand-new entity comprises the Program Director Federico Pippo, the Community Manager Andrea Benocci and 10 Alumnae/i, whom their own peers elected in early December 2021. The Community of MCF alumni cast more than 600 votes in total, spreading them across more than 40 candidates coming from all MCF editions.
