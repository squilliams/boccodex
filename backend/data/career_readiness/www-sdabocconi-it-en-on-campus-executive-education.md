# On campus executive education

## Let’s manage the future

Choose the program that best suits your needs

SDA Bocconi’s Executive Education Programs are designed to meet the growth needs of individuals and organizations across all sectors and sizes.

Whether you represent a large corporation, an SME, work in the public or healthcare sector, or run your own business, here you’ll find the ideal learning path to strengthen your managerial skills and create value for your organization and society.

With the **SDA Bocconi Executive Education Certificate**, you can design a personalized learning journey, developed together with our Program Advisors and based on the programs in your areas of interest. By completing at least 11 days of education—combining on-campus courses and, if you wish, an online live program—within two years, you’ll earn formal recognition of your commitment and join the **Bocconi Alumni Community**, a global network of professionals with whom to share experiences and opportunities.

## SDA Bocconi Executive Education Certificate

To navigate today’s evolving macroeconomic landscape and organizational change, it is essential to broaden **managerial capabilities**. **Learning** plays a central role in this journey.

An initial conversation with our **Program Advisors** is a required step to design a coherent learning path, clarify objectives and participation options, and to be eligible to earn the Certificate. From there, participants can choose the learning format that best supports their **professional development**.
