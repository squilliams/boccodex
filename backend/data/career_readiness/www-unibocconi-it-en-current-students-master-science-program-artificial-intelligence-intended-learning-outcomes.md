During the Master of Science program, students acquire interdisciplinary skills to transition companies toward a sustainable business and management approach.

Through the teaching activities that characterize the course, they develop skills in:

- making judgements autonomously
- improve their written and oral communication
- acquire lifelong learning skills useful for both continuing their studies and their professional futures.
