# Why Choose a Bocconi BSc?

Bocconi is recognized internationally for its academics and research in the social sciences, in the fields of economics, management, finance, political science, data science and legal studies.

You will learn from expert faculty, attending programs at the forefront of modern higher learning. A Bocconi education is a springboard to improved professional prospects and enables our graduates to make a significant contribution to society: This is the meaning behind our motto, “Knowledge that Matters.”

Reputation

Bocconi is a leading European university of international standing in economics, management and legal studies. We have a long tradition of excellence in teaching, and have built a strong reputation for research in areas that also include finance, political science, data science and artificial intelligence, as demonstrated by improving placements in top international rankings.

Innovation and Research

Cutting-edge research constantly updates the content of our Bachelor programs, creating innovative curricula arranged within flexible study plans. Students are thus guaranteed to receive solid preparation in the wide range of disciplines offered - from management and economics to political science, finance and data science.

Global Network

Our extensive network is made up of:

- **290 partner schools**
- **4,000 employers**
- **146,000+ alumni** worldwide

Students can take advantage of these contacts for a unique educational experience and remarkable career opportunities.

A Multicultural Community

We are a truly international university: representing **more than 100 countries worldwide** our students and faculty pursue a policy of tolerance and openness.

Bocconi’s diverse and open community makes our campus an inclusive place where languages, experiences, cultures and ideas are exchanged on a daily basis, resulting in professional but also personal growth.

Programs at the Leading Edge

Our teaching methods are founded on extensive use of the latest technologies and continuous interaction between students and instructors, along with group work, business cases, testimonials and discussions. A programming course aims to teach students how to think through algorithms and face complex problems, and emphasis on relational and communication skills prepares them for today’s collaborative work environments.

A Dynamic Campus in Downtown Milan

Students can experience an active and exciting time on campus, participating in sports, cultural activities, entertainment, student associations, student-run media and volunteering.

In recent years, the campus has evolved to increasingly focus on sustainable issues and the safety and wellbeing of our community: the newest area of the campus includes a student dorm, a new location for the business school, plus a Sport Center with an Olympic-size pool that is open to the local community.

Our campus is located in downtown Milan, a European business, financial and cultural hub, where history merges with modernity, tradition with innovation. Located just a 10-minute walk from the city center, the campus is sustainable, innovative and safe.

Careers

Bocconi provides graduates with strong support when entering the job market, through its deep connections with top employers worldwide. Dedicated activities and personnel help graduates exploit their creativity and entrepreneurship in innovative sectors and startups.
