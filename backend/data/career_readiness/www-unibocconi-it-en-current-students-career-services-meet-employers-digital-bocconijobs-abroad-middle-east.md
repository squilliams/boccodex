# Digital Bocconi&Jobs abroad Middle East

## 31 October 2025 (12pm-4pm Dubai time), online

_The event is a great opportunity to meet online with employers offering internship and job opportunities in Middle East._

_During the event, participants will be able to:_

- _access employer’s digital booth;_
- _interact directly with recruiters;_
- _read corporate profiles and watch videos;_
- _check the open positions and apply._

### 27 - 30 October: Preview Days

Explore company profiles of attending employers, read their corporate info, watch their videos, browse their LinkedIn page and review their job opportunities.

### 31 October: Digital Career Fair

Digitally engage with company representatives, interact with recruiters, and gain valuable insights into the hiring processes for applying to the available opportunities.

### 31 October - 4 November: Early Birds Application

Send online the applications for internship and job opportunities (reserved exclusively for students who participated in the event).

## Participation requirements and deadlines

_Students and graduates who meet the requirements (please see the boxes on the right) can **apply You@B > Career Service menu > Events Calendar from 8 September 2025 at 1 pm to 3 October 2025 at 11.59 pm OR upon reaching the maximum number of participants**(if necessary, a waiting list will be available)._

_**Students and graduates meeting the requirements will receive a confirmation email.**_

- _**Bocconi Bachelor Programs:**_

_Students regularly enrolled in their 2nd and 3rd year in the a.y. 2025/26; World Bachelor in Business students regularly enrolled in their 3rd and 4th year in the a.y. 2025/26._
- _**Bocconi Master of Science Programs:** Students regularly enrolled in their 1st and 2nd year in the a.y. 2025/26; Graduates within 3 years from graduation._
- _**Bocconi Specialized Master programs:**_

_Students regularly enrolled in the a.y. 2025/26; Graduates within 3 years from graduation._

_English language proficiency is required._
