HEC-Bocconi Double Degree

[International Opportunities](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/bachelor-science/international-politics-and-government/international-opportunities) [After Graduation](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/bachelor-science/international-politics-and-government/after-graduation)

The **Data, Society and Organisations track** is a **double degree** in the BSc in International Politics and Government offered in collaboration **with HEC Paris**. This new program uniquely merges social science critical thinking with problem solving and data analytical skills, featuring in-depth training in quantitative methods and data science.

After spending a total of 3 semesters at Bocconi and 3 semesters at HEC Paris, **you will earn two Bachelor Degrees, one from Bocconi and one from HEC**.

Exposing you to different international learning environments through courses taught on two campuses, the track is for students who want to work at the **intersection of data science and the social sciences** to understand human behavior and provide effective solutions to the main challenges of society and organizations.

It aims to **prepare students for graduate programs** in data science and the social sciences, management and public policy.

Graduates are also **trained to cover the most in-demand jobs** that require hybrid knowledge in these areas, with the entrepreneurial skills needed to have an impact on society and within organizations.
