# Fees and scholarships

**A challenging but high-return investment**

Participation in the EMMS program requires a substantial financial commitment. However, it is a strategic investment that delivers high future returns—both personally and professionally.

## Fees and expenses

The tuition fee for the 2027-2028 EMMS program is € 42,000, payable in four installments \*

- First installment

**€10,500**

upon acceptance of admission (non-refundable deposit fee)

- Second installment

**€10,500**

by 31 March 2027

- Third installment

**€10,500**

by 31 May 2027

- Fourth installment

**€10,500 (€4,500 \*\*)**

by 4 January 2028

\\* An “early bird fee” of € 36,000 will be granted to candidates that have enrolled and have paid the deposit fee before 10 November 2026.

\\*\\* For "Early Birds"

**Application fee: €120**

The fees include:

  - All teaching material used during the program
  - Access to both schools’ facilities
  - Catering (coffee breaks and lunches) during face-to-face modules

The fees do not include:

  - Travel and accommodation expenses.

Participants must provide their own laptop.

## Scholarships and tuition waivers

_1st round:_
30 Jun 2026

_2nd round:_
30 Sep 2026

_3rd round:_
15 Dec 2026

_Early bird:_
10 Nov 2026

Each year, SDA Bocconi School of Management makes multiple merit-based scholarships and tuition waivers available for outstanding candidates, thanks to the ongoing support of companies, organizations, alums, and current students.

Applications for tuition waivers are evaluated according to the following criteria:

- Analysis of the requirements indicated in the specific notice of competition
- Analysis of academic and professional records
- Results of the EMMS selection process

To be eligible for a tuition waiver, applicants must:

- Submit their application for the EMMS 2027-2028 program
- Review and meet the pre-requisites for each tuition waiver
- Prepare a motivation letter explaining why you should be awarded a tuition waiver
- Flag the tuition waiver option through the online application portal by the indicated deadline

**Important**:

- All candidates who meet the requirements can apply for the tuition waivers. Applicants will only be considered eligible for a tuition waiver after completing the EMMS application (including the online form and all supporting documents).
- You can apply for scholarships and tuition waivers even if your selection process is still in progress. However, only admitted candidates will be evaluated for the awarding of a tuition waiver or scholarship.
- The tuition waiver is not compatible with the "early bird fee". Candidates enrolled by 10 November 2026 who are subsequently awarded a tuition waiver are no longer eligible for the early bird fee.

## Loans and funding

The School assists candidates with their search for funding by supplying the necessary documentation and encouraging them to explore all scholarship and loan possibilities. SDA Bocconi provides opportunities for low-interest loans to admitted students ( [International students](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/student-loans), [Italian students](https://www.unibocconi.it/it/studenti-iscritti/agevolazioni/finanziamenti-master-universitari-e-sda)), thanks to agreements with partner banks. Loan applications must be submitted by the end of month following the starting date of your Master.

Furthermore, the University provides [international students](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/funding-country) with information about possible sources for financing in several countries. For further information, please visit the Università Bocconi website.
