# Brain Health Policy and Management Nexus

Changing the skin of neuroscience means intervening with innovative approaches and actively involving all stakeholders: neurologists, psychiatrists, and health professionals. The goal is to make this area more functional, productive and patient-centered.

![Health & Life Sciences Knowledge Platform](https://www.sdabocconi.it/_next/image/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fsda-website-prod-sdawebsiteprodbucketpublic693d05b-w3mwb4fo9x9p.s3.eu-south-1.amazonaws.com%2Fs3fs-public%2F2025-02%2Fhealth-life-sciences-card.jpg&w=3840&q=90&dpl=dpl_EgyAUeGYW1L1ERS2WGuDkM3HLa8o)

## What it is and why it was started

The Brain Health Policy and Management Nexus is a research and training platform created by SDA Bocconi with the non-conditional support of Lundbeck Italia.

It aims to consolidate the knowledge and skills needed to address brain health challenges.

Neurological and psychiatric diseases, which affect more than 1.5 billion people each year, need solutions based on innovative policies, exchanging of knowledge, and advanced training for professionals.

## Main goals

To generate and disseminate new skills in the field of brain health.

To promote research and transfer of knowledge to policy makers and practitioners.

To strengthen the capacity of professionals through training courses.

To support the development of evidence-based policies.
