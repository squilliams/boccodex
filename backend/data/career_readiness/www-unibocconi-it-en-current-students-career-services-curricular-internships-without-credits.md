# Curricular internships without credits

**ATTENTION PLEASE! YOU'RE ACCESSING THIS PAGE VIA AN OLD LINK AND THE CONTENTS MAY BE NO LONGER VALID  (LAST UPDATE: APRIL 2024).**

**STARTING FROM MAY 2024, PLEASE REFER TO THE NEW CONTENTS AVAILABLE AT THE FOLLOWING LINKS FOR** [**STUDENTS**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/career-services/your-internship-start-credit-recognition "Your internship: from start to credit recognition") **AND** [**COMPANIES**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/employers-and-partners/activating-and-managing-internships "Activating and managing internships") **.**

\\*\\*\\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*

These are other internships pursued by students in addition to the curricular internship for credits (even if satisfying the requirements for credit recognition), or internships which do not satisfy the requirements for registration in the curriculum of studies.

|     |     |
| --- | --- |
| **CURRICULAR INTERNSHIP WITHOUT CREDITS** |
| **Purpose** | To enhance the learning process. |
| **Timing** | The internship is carried out during is situated the course of studies and may begin at any time of the year.<br>It may be concluded even after the award of a degree: in this case it changes nature, becoming **extracurricular**. In such a case, the internship period after the graduation cannot exceed **6 months**. |
| **Duration** | **Bachelor** (either in Italy or abroad)<br>— Minimum 1 month<br>— Maximum 6 months<br>**Master of Science/Law**(either in Italy or abroad)<br>— Minimum 2 months<br>— Maximum 6 months |
| **Benefits** | **Italy**: compensation (monetary contribution) and any benefits are at the discretion of the employer, not being prescribed by law.<br>**Abroad**:  compensation (monetary contribution) and any benfits depend on respective national laws.<br>In case of an unpaid internship you may ascertain your eligibility for one of the [scholarships](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/services/scholarships-internships-abroad) made available by the University.<br>_The compensation is defined as “allowance” or “scholarship”. Terms as “salary” or “remuneration” are not correct if referred to an internship, since this latter is not an employment relationship._<br>_However, fiscally, such allowances may be equated to employment incomes (cfr. art. 50 co. 1, lett. c), DPR n. 917/1986 TUIR - ris. A.E. n. 95/E/2002)._<br>_For the purpose of tax return, the intern is advised to contact his/her own accountant._<br>_Reimbursement of expenses (namely, costs incurred by the intern, agreed with and on behalf of the employer, that shall be justified, such as train tickets) are not fiscally relevant, neither for the employer nor for the intern._ |
| **Hosting institution** | May be carried out **in Italy or abroad**, in any organized workplace as in: public or private companies, industrial, commercial or service companies, banks or financial services firms, professional firms or traders’ associations, public bodies, institutions or cultural and artistic associations, non-profit organizations, organizations or companies operating in the tourism, communication or media sectors, international organizations, diplomatic representations, cultural institutions or other organizations.<br>The University reserves the right to evaluate the eligibility of the internship location and any blended (in-person/remote) or remote (distance) modes, according to the regulations. |
