# Cyber Risk Partners Forum

Explore the Program

[Overview](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/master-science/cyber-risk-strategy-and-governance) [Study Plan](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/master-science/cyber-risk-strategy-and-governance/study-plan) [Program Placement](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/master-science/cyber-risk-strategy-and-governance/program-placement)

The MSc in Cyber Risk Strategy and Governance is supported by the **Cyber Risk Partners Forum**, which includes the following Cyber Risk Partners:

- **Cisco**
- **Fincantieri**
- **Intesa Sanpaolo**
- **KPMG**
- **NTT Data**
- **Spike Reply**
- **Vodafone**

This Forum brings together Partner company representatives, Program Directors, and Bocconi and Politecnico di Milano Rectors, Deans and Faculty members. The Forum meets periodically in order to share knowledge and discuss areas of cooperation and research.

Thanks to the support of **Cyber Risk Partners**, students may be eligible for **merit-based scholarships**. Partners cover three full tuition waivers for students admitted to the MSc in Cyber Risk Strategy and Governance.

Students also have several opportunities for interacting with Partners throughout the program, including:

- Meeting them during **Welcome Days**
- Seeing **testimonials and business cases** from Partners in selected courses and field projects
- Choosing **thesis topics** based on Partner suggestions
- Participating in **recruiting activities** with Partners

Cyber Risk Partners are recognized and listed as **Donors of Bocconi University**.
