### European FS (Financial Services) Tech Hub

ICE SDA Bocconi intends to consolidate its role as innovation hub at the service of companies.

ICE has identified the area of Financial Services on which to position its role of innovation hub, and the FS Tech Hub of Milan wants to develop a path together with some partners.

Within this path the FS Tech Hub wants to implement several initiatives with a modular approach that ensures the solidity and relevance of the overall program maximizing financial inclusiveness and internationality.

### Editorial

Annual Report of the Hub, research Papers (in the FS tech area) and a book over the FS tech sector

The goal of the Hub is to study the industry - in  great ferment - of financial tech services,  combining all together the demands of market  operators, the evolution of regulatory issues and the  innovations proposed by the world of start-ups, with an international cutting-hedge approach and  oriented to the  value creation for the overall  ecosystem. The final result will give participants  access to studies, reports and insights with  the latest state of the art in financial tech service.

### Ecosystem

ICE SDA Bocconi aims to establish an ecosystem where it plays a pivotal role, with a specific focus on the latest advancements in innovation, valuation, and governance within the FinServ (FS) tech industries.

The FS tech hub will:

- **Address research and study initiatives** based on the needs expressed by the participants, aligning with market demands
- **Enhance participants' growth** by introducing them to the latest technological innovations through a virtual connection with selected start-ups
- **Provide** participants with regular **research findings**, workshops, and educational programs

### Manifesto

The main targets for **ICE SDA BOCCONI European FS Tech Hub** are described in our **Manifesto**.

- ###### COOPERATION FOR VALUE CREATION

Creation of value based on the culture of collaboration and the exchange of knowledge between the main players of innovation in financial services, that represent a source of data and inspiration for research, also enabling the development of relationships within the ecosystem

- ###### VALUE RESEARCH

Enhancing the work of ICE SDA Bocconi researchers, placing them in contact with the subjects of their research and creating new sources of funding for research activities (output: scientific report)

- ###### SHARE KNOWLEDGE

Establish a place for sharing data and information, creating a bridge between supply and demand companies, innovative startups, researchers and the market at international level

- ###### BUILD SKILLS AND COMPETENCES

Offer a wide range of training opportunities, such specialized training courses and classes on the topic of "valuation in fintech, start-ups and tech innovation

- If you are a fintech startup interested in joining the FS Tech Hub, write an email to [**ice@sdabocconi.it**](mailto:ice@sdabocconi.it) and we will contact you back.

### Why European FS (Financial Services) Tech Hub?

Hub partners can benefit greatly from the research reports produced within the hub, which are validated by academia, industry, and regulators participating in the hub.

Here are the main ways in which partners can benefit from these research reports.

Partners will gain a competitive advantage in the \*tech services industry, staying ahead of the curve in a rapidly evolving industry and market dynamics. Datasets and validated insights from the hub will support their strategic decision making, product development, risk mitigation strategies, and innovation initiatives.
