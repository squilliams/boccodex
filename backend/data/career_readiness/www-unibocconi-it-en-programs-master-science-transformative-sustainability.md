_Joint Degree with Politecnico di Milano_

In an era marked by growing geopolitical uncertainty and increasing resistance to sustainability commitments, the imperative to **lead environmental and social transformation** remains a structural necessity - one that must be addressed with urgency, innovation and interdisciplinary expertise.

**Bocconi University and Politecnico di Milano** have joined forces to offer a pioneering, interdisciplinary **joint degree program**: the MSc in Transformative Sustainability.

This program responds to the pressing need faced by governments, institutions and businesses across jurisdictions - from Europe to the Americas, from Africa to Asia, and beyond. The aim is to **tackle the complex, multidimensional challenges** posed by climate change, social equity and responsible innovation. Addressing it requires a **new generation of professionals** equipped with both strategic vision and technical expertise, capable of navigating the intersection of innovation, policy and business.

Drawing on the complementary strengths of two leading institutions, this unique program integrates:

- **Technical disciplines** such as environmental technologies, energy systems, mobility and circularity
- **Social sciences** including management, economics, data science and law

The MSc in Transformative Sustainability is designed to prepare students to:

- **Lead the transition of companies** toward sustainable business models through innovation in products, processes, and systems
- **Understand and apply ESG criteria** in governance, management and investment decisions, evaluating impact
- **Leverage technology** to generate value for both business and society

What sets this program apart is its holistic approach: it combines the “why” of sustainability (strategic vision), the “what” (scientific and technological tools), and the “how” (innovation and design methodologies). Students are trained to manage the full innovation pathway - from strategic planning to implementation.

Graduates will be uniquely positioned to:

- Identify and interpret the **transformations required by sustainability** across core business functions
- Design and lead **systemic innovation processes** at the organizational level, bridging strategy and technology
- Collaborate across sectors and with **diverse stakeholders** to address complex environmental and social challenges
- Balance **environmental imperatives with social and economic considerations** in the transition process

At a time when sustainability is not only a moral duty and strategic necessity, but also increasingly subject to scrutiny and challenges, this program provides the tools, mindset, and network to navigate such complexity. It also empowers our graduates to drive meaningful, balanced impact through an innovative and inclusive approach that cements sustainability as a lasting strategy.

Program Director

### Miriam Allena

![foto Prof. Miriam Allena](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/styles/scale_64x64/public/media/images/Allena850.jpg.webp?itok=QNqH7-I2)

Transformative sustainability is one of the defining challenges of our time. Across the globe, businesses are being called upon to rethink their models in light of urgent environmental and social obligations. From global climate agreements to national regulations, sustainability is gaining momentum despite a complex and fragmented geopolitical landscape. Yet sustainability also faces political backlash, economic skepticism and regulatory uncertainty. Amid these challenges, building a future based on integrity and holistic sustainability calls for creativity, resilience and clear purpose - along with the ability to balance competing interests and think ahead.

The joint Bocconi-Politecnico di Milano MSc in Transformative Sustainability is designed to meet this global demand. It offers an integrated, interdisciplinary approach that reflects the evolving needs of the market, where sustainability is a core strategic priority and no longer a niche concern.

Our goal is to train a new generation of sustainability leaders and professionals who can combine strategic thinking with strong technical expertise. The MSc in Transformative Sustainability prepares two main professional profiles: **Sustainability Innovation Strategist**, capable of leading the transformation of companies and institutions by integrating sustainability into operations, governance, and innovation processes, with the goal of creating long-term value; and **Sustainability-Focused Analyst or Consultant**, equipped to understand enabling technologies and environmental and social systems, as well as to apply emerging economic and management tools to design and evaluate sustainability and impact strategies - particularly in consulting, investment and policy contexts.

Student Life

Your years on campus are about more than just the classes you attend: Bocconi and Milan also promote activities focused on culture, sports, art and sharing ideas. They help make your Bocconi experience an exciting time that is key to personal and professional growth.

Our Campus

_A Sustainable Campus_

Our campus is a safe, multicultural and lively place, located in downtown Milan. Both its historic buildings and new spaces are integrated into the urban fabric to create an active environment, reverberating with university life. The campus includes a recreational sports center, a large park and a new student residence hall.

Living in Milan

_A European and cosmopolitan city_

Milan is an open, international and multicultural city, a European metropolis full of events and public services with an unparalleled nightlife. It is a renowned symbol of fashion and design, as well as an important financial, economic and business hub.
