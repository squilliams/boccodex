## **One year after graduation**\*

- **98.2%** of graduates are employed
- **51.9%** of employed graduates work abroad

**\*** Data refers to a.y. 2021-2022 graduates

## **On graduation day\*\***

- **59.4%** of graduates are employed
- **68.4%** of employed graduates work abroad
- They have been called to an average of **2.6** job interviews
- They have received on average **1.6** job offers

**\*\*** Survey - graduation sessions a.y. 2022-2023

Our **recruiters** include, among others:

- Cattaneo Zanetto & Co.
- European Central Bank
- European Commission
- European Investment Bank
- EY
- Intellera Consulting
- KPMG
- OECD

## **Graduating students - satisfaction with the Study Program**

**84.6**% of students who graduated in the 2023-24 academic year declared they were satisfied with their Study Program
