# Enhancing Experience - Curricular Supplementary Activities

Enhancing Experience - Curricular Supplementary Activities aim to develop broad-spectrum culture and are considered useful activities for entering the job market. The study plans of students enrolled in MSc programs include 2 compulsory credits dedicated to these activities (with the exception of the DSBA, CYBER, TS and AI programs, whose students can attend the Enhancing Experience and record the 2 credit points earned as extra activities).

Enhancing Experiences are assigned through participation in dedicated Calls for Applications that open at different times of the year. A few days before the opening of each Call, a message is sent through the yoU@B student diary specifying the opening and closing dates. **The procedure for applying is available in the yoU@B student diary under Enhancing Experience > Call - Choice of Activity** starting from the opening day of the Call.

The achievement of the 2 credits is subject to meeting the participation requirements and **passing an assessment test** **(pass or fail), which, even in the case of students registered in online activities,**

**will always take place in person on campus**. The options for carrying out the tests (oral, written, multiple choice, open answer) may vary according to the activity and will be communicated at the beginning of each course.

The Enhancing Experience offer is provided by the **Campus Life**, **ITEC** and **Language Center** services and by the Graduate School, and is integrated with the **Broaden Your Frame** seminar cycles and the **Media Experience** initiative.
