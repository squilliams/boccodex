# Intended Learning Outcomes

Accounting and Financial Management

[Overview](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/master-science-program/accounting-and-financial-management) [Governance](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/master-science-program/accounting-and-financial-management/governance) [Placement and Program Performance](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/master-science-program/accounting-and-financial-management/placement-and-program-performance) [Program structure](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/master-science-program/accounting-and-financial-management/program-structure)

During the Master of Science program, students acquire specific knowledge and skills related to administration (accounting and financial statements), corporate finance, planning and management control, and auditing.

Through the teaching activities that characterize the course, they develop skills in

- making judgements autonomously
- improving their written and oral communication
- acquiring lifelong learning skills useful for both continuing their studies and their professional futures.
