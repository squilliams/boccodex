## Research, growth, and global impact

SDA Bocconi School of Management is an international school that empowers people to drive change and make a meaningful impact on individuals, organizations, and society.

In addition to our urban campus in Milan – the most sustainable business school campus in the world – we have a campus in Rome and a pan-Asian hub in Mumbai.

Our educational offering is built on rigorous, original research and includes MBAs, specialized and executive masters, open-enrollment executive education, and custom programs for companies. According to Bloomberg Businessweek, SDA Bocconi is the third-best business school in Europe. The latest Financial Times rankings place our Full-Time MBA 4th in the world and the School 6th in Europe.

### Value and values: our idea of a Business School

> We live in a time where opinions are abundant, but critical thinking – grounded in skills and evidence – is increasingly rare. At SDA Bocconi, we believe management is, above all, an act of responsibility: it means leading people, resources, and organizations with awareness and vision.
>
> Being a business school means creating and spreading knowledge that makes a difference. It’s not just about acquiring tools, but about developing a broad, interdisciplinary mindset – one that embraces complexity rather than avoiding it.
>
> For us, value and values are inseparable. Every program, every initiative, every research effort is designed to generate positive change for individuals, organizations, and communities. If this resonates with you, we invite you to explore our School – and become part of it.

**Stefano Caselli, Dean SDA Bocconi**

The SDA Bocconi community is part of the broader Bocconi Alumni network – a growing ecosystem of people, ideas, and opportunities.
