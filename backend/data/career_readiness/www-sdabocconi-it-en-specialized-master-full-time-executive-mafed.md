# Master in Fashion, Experience & Design Management

## Add more value to creativity.

Fashion, design and luxury

_Start_ 18 January 2027

_Duration_ 12 months

_Where_ Milano

_Format_ Full-time

_Language_ English

_Avg. working experience_ 3 years

The **Master in Fashion, Experience & Design Management**(MAFED) is an intensive 1-year full-time program offering 70 ECTS/ CFU credits. It provides you with solid management skills tailored for highly creative industries.

MAFED empowers you by combining the power of imagination with enhanced methodologies and analytical capabilities. It fosters consistent strategies and carefully targeted marketing initiatives, enabling you to create lasting, engaging, and imaginative brands that stand the test of time.

## Who is it for

MAFED is the right Master for you if:

- You want a managerial role in a fashion, luxury, or design company.
- You want to work in organizations that create unique customer experiences.
- You want to launch your own venture in these symbol-intensive industries.
- You want to engage with the power of industry.
- You want to prepare for all this in Milan, an international and challenging environment, and the well-recognized symbol of Italian style and excellence worldwide.

## Why MAFED

- ### Build a solid career in a fast-moving business world

The program provides **specific training for creative managers, bridging the gap between managerial and creative roles**. You will gain a comprehensive understanding of new managerial approaches, enhanced analytics, and digital technologies, while recognizing the unique DNA of fashion and design companies, often rooted in their successful, well-established histories.

- ### A powerful network of industry leaders and alums

MAFED is supported by SDA Bocconi, Università Bocconi, and Fondazione Altagamma, a network of top-tier Italian brands. This ecosystem provides **exclusive access to business leaders, top scholars, and a strong network of alumni and alumnae**, ensuring lifelong professional connections and opportunities.

- ### World-class faculty and top guest speakers

The program is led by **globally recognized facult** y and enriched by insights from **top managers and industry leaders**, who share real-world business cases, providing a dynamic and hands-on learning experience.

- ### Experiential learning: study tours, corporate visits, and field projects

You will engage in **study tours to major fashion capitals** like Paris and New York, depending on your concentration. Additionally, **corporate visits and field projects** will immerse you in leading brands' strategies, allowing you to apply your classroom knowledge in real-world settings.

- ### Italian excellence and an international learning environment

Milan, the **heart of international fashion and design**, provides a unique setting where **tradition meets innovation**. You will experience firsthand how brands leverage cultural heritage while staying contemporary and globally competitive. You will also be part of a diverse, international environment, enriched by global events such as Milan Fashion Weeks and Design Week.

- ### Elective concentrations in fashion or hospitality

In the final phase of the program, you can tailor your learning experience by choosing between **fashion or hospitality concentrations**, allowing you to specialize in the area that best aligns with your career ambitions.

- ### We care about your professional future

Throughout the program, you will benefit from **a series of activities designed to help shape your career**, developing the skills and insights necessary to navigate the professional world with confidence.

#### Study Tours

**Study tours** offer a unique opportunity to deepen your understanding by experiencing industry insights firsthand. As a class, you will travel to Paris for the **Haute Couture study tour** and to another destination for a second Tour.

For the " **Go to Market in a Fashion and Design Global Landscape**" concentration, you will join the Retail New York Study Tour, while those in " **Food, Experience & Hospitality Management**" will travel to Tuscany.

**Study tours are optional and not included in the program fee**.

October 2027November 2027

#### Field projects: bridging theory and practice

To ensure effective learning, MAFED students apply classroom theories to real-world business challenges through **in-company group projects**. Over eight weeks, teams of two to five students tackle key corporate aspects such as brand positioning, pricing, and market entry, under the guidance of an SDA Bocconi professor and a company executive.

These projects provide companies with innovative solutions, access to highly motivated and skilled students, and a chance to assess potential future hires. At the conclusion, teams present their findings through a final report and presentation, delivering valuable insights to the company.

## A unique international program, made in Milano, Italy

- ### What makes MAFED stand out?

Understand the unique DNA of fashion and design companies and enrich it with innovative managerial approaches, advanced analytics, and cutting-edge technologies—driving results-oriented creativity.

- ### Learn Italian with us

For a career in Italy’s fashion industry, speaking Italian is a valuable asset. That’s why we offer an on-campus language course, included in the program fee. Learn how to shape the future of tradition in Italy—create new experiences and lay the foundation for your success.

- ### Mentoring program with LVMH & Loro Piana

A truly exceptional opportunity, the MAFED Mentoring Program helps students develop key skills, expand their network, gain career guidance, and define personal priorities.

This exclusive initiative is made possible through our collaboration with Loro Piana and LVMH, featuring mentors from various Maisons and the Loro Piana HR team, who actively support and shape this program.

- ### A full-time (and full-mind) commitment

MAFED is an intensive, full-time program with a dynamic mix of lectures, exercises, and guest speaker sessions. Mornings consist of structured classes, with some days extending into the afternoon.

A highly interactive learning approach—combining case studies, group work, and real-world business insights—equips you with the practical tools to analyze challenges and make strategic decisions in fashion and design.

Assessments are based on coursework and exams, with successful completion of all program components required to earn the Master in Fashion, Experience & Design Management.
