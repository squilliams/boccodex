# Internship and admission to the master of science programs

**ATTENTION PLEASE! YOU'RE ACCESSING THIS PAGE VIA AN OLD LINK AND THE CONTENTS MAY BE NO LONGER VALID  (LAST UPDATE: APRIL 2024).**

**STARTING FROM MAY 2024, PLEASE REFER TO THE NEW CONTENTS AVAILABLE AT THE FOLLOWING LINKS FOR** [**STUDENTS**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/career-services/your-internship-start-credit-recognition "Your internship: from start to credit recognition") **AND** [**COMPANIES**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/employers-and-partners/activating-and-managing-internships "Activating and managing internships") **.**

\\*\\*\\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*

Your **internships credits** can be included in the amount of credits required for the selection process of Bocconi applicants 2024/2025 a.y. if **recorded in the academic career** by:

**\- July 31, 2023** for the first admission round;

**\- April 30, 2024** for the second round;

II round

In order to be registered in your study plan by the deadline of **April 30, 2024** (If it’s not already registered in your study plan) **the internship** must satisfy the following criteria:

**1)** to be properly activated before its start and obtained the **prior authorization for credit recognition** by the Internship Office ( [check here](https://www.unibocconi.eu/wps/wcm/connect/bocconi/sitopubblico_en/navigation+tree/home/programs/current+students/services/career+services/students+and+graduates/internship/credit+recognition/requirements+of+curricular+internships+for+credit/requirements+of+curricular+internships+for+credit+home) the requirements of a curricular internship for credits).

ATTENTION: the prior authorization for credit recognition (at the beginning of the experience) it’s mandatory to obtain the credits also for all the experiences formalized without the Bocconi Internship agreement (internship outside of Italy [formalized through a bilateral contract made by the company](https://www.unibocconi.eu/wps/wcm/connect/bocconi/sitopubblico_en/navigation+tree/home/programs/current+students/services/career+services/students+and+graduates/internship/activating+an+internship/activation+through+foreign+contract));

**2)** it must **be completed by April 30** or it must have **reached the minimum duration**(6 weeks full-time/8 weeks part-time)\* required for curricular recognition **by April 30** (in the case of an internship still running after that date);

**3)** it must have **by April 30 all the necessary documentation** for final recognition (we remind you that it is your responsibility to ensure that all the required documents are there).

For this to happen, we have set up timescales that will help you to achieve the final credit recognition by April 30. Strictly follow the steps below, (we remind you that it is your responsibility to ensure that all the required documents are there).

Please note that the internship must be included in your study plan (in line with deadlines of modification of the study plan).

Curricular internship completed by April 30, 2024

**1) by April 16** check that the following documents are present in the **yoU@B diary > Internship menu > My internships**:

\- the final evaluation completed by you and the final evaluation (or final certificate) completed by the company supervisor;

\- only for research field projects (report written by you on the activity carried out).

ATTENTION: if some issues occur in completing or finding the documents, promptly contact the Internship Office (via B in touch > Internship section).

**2)** Following the final recognition of the internship, the credits are registered in the study plan.

ATTENTION: the internship must be included in your study plan to replace an elective course in line with deadlines of modification of the study plan. For additional information you can contact the Academic Services selecting Study Plan on B in touch.

If this is your second curricular internship, with extra credits, check the correct registration of the credits in your study plan.

Curricular internship still running after April 30, 2024 (minimum duration of 6 weeks full-time/8 weeks part-time reached by April 30, 2024)

**1)** **from April 8 to April 10**, **write to the Internship Office** (B in touch > Internship section) and ask for advance recognition of the internship for admission to the master's degree;

**2)** **starting from April 11**, you and your company supervisor fill in the final evaluation form. You can find it in the **yoU@B diary > Internship menu > My internships > Evaluations**. Your supervisor will receive an email with a link to fill in the evaluation form.

**3)** **by April 16** check that the following documents are present in the yoU@B diary > Internship menu > My internships:

\- the final evaluation completed by you and the final evaluation (or final certificate) completed by the company supervisor;

\- only for research field projects (report written by you on the activity carried out).

ATTENTION: if some issues occur in completing or finding the documents, promptly contact the Internship Office (via B in touch > Internship section).

**4)** Following the final recognition of the internship, the **credits are registered in the study plan**.

Attention: the internship must be included in your study plan to replace an elective course in line with deadlines of modification of the study plan. For additional information you can contact the Academic Services selecting Study Plan on B in touch.

If this is your second curricular internship, with extra credits, check the correct registration of the credits in your study plan.

\*More information on the [minimum duration](https://www.unibocconi.eu/wps/wcm/connect/bocconi/sitopubblico_en/navigation+tree/home/programs/current+students/services/career+services/students+and+graduates/internship/credit+recognition/requirements+of+curricular+internships+for+credit/requirements+of+curricular+internships+for+credit+home) of full-time and part-time internships is available here. Beware of any [suspensions](https://www.unibocconi.eu/wps/wcm/connect/bocconi/sitopubblico_en/navigation+tree/home/programs/current+students/services/career+services/students+and+graduates/internship/during+the+internship/change+of+duration+extension+suspension+interruption/change+of+duration+of+the+internship_extension%2C+suspension+and+interruption+home) during the internship which will be subtracted from the total duration and will have to be recovered with an extension of your experience if they do not allow the minimum duration to be reached.
