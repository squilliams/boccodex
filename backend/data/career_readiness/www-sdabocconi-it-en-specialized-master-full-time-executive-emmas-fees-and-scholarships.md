## Fees and expenses

The tuition fee for the EMMAS 2026-2027 program is €25,000 (excluding VAT), payable in five installments.

**€ 5.000**

upon enrollment

- Second installment

**€ 5.000**

by 30 May 2025

- Third installment

**€ 5.000**

by 30 September 2025

- Fourth installment

**€ 5.000**

by 31 December 2025

- Fifth installment

**€ 5.000**

by 28 February 2026

The participation fee includes teaching materials, use of the School's equipment, and the cost of accommodation for the Study Tour.

Participants must provide their own laptop.

## Scholarships and tuition waivers

SDA Bocconi provides scholarships and waivers for EMMAS applications through the publication of calls for applications. Special payment terms are awarded on the basis of merit criteria and any additional recognition modalities that are expressed by notice. Competition notices, if available, are posted on this page.

The tuition waiver/scholarship request can also be submitted before the conclusion of the master selection process.

If you meet more than one requirement among those listed in the notices, you can submit multiple requests for tuition waivers/fellowships.

In addition, the School also participates in notices proposed by public administrations for scholarships/tuition waivers for EMMAS applicants.

### EMMAS 25 - Tuition Waivers

To apply for a scholarship or tuition waiver, applicants must read the official call for applications (“bando di concorso”) to learn about the eligibility requirements and application procedures. Applications will be considered only from candidates who, by the deadlines stated in the call for applications, have:

- submitted the admission application to the **Executive Master in Management delle Aziende Sanitarie e Socio-Assistenziali (EMMAS 25)**, duly completed and accompanied by all required documentation;
- submitted the **Application for the Call**;
- sent the application following the procedures and deadlines specified, and carefully verified that all eligibility criteria listed in the call are met—otherwise the application will not be accepted.

If, at the time of publication of the call, the online admission application has already been submitted, candidates may access it again and complete the scholarship or tuition waiver request, provided it is within the deadline indicated in the call.

### To be eligible for a tuition waiver, you must:

- Have completed and submitted the Application for Admission to EMMAS, with all the required documents
- Submit the application in response to the notice, attaching the documentation indicated, via the Application portal
- Meet the deadlines and carefully check the requirements, without which the application cannot be accepted

If at the publication of a notice the online application is already submitted, the candidate may access it again and make the waiver request respecting the deadline stipulated in the notice.

### Applications will be evaluated on the basis of:

- Academic and professional curriculum
- Motivation letter
- Requirements stated in the notice
- Test results for selection for EMMAS

The allocation of tuition waivers will take place during the selection process, by the dates indicated in the announcement. It is possible to apply even before the conclusion of the selection process.

Admission to EMMAS is independent of the awarding of any tuition waivers.

## Loans and funding

SDA Bocconi assists candidates with their search for funding by supplying the necessary documentation to finalize applications for financial support. For more information you can consult the section on funding of the Bocconi University website (for [Italian](https://www.unibocconi.it/it/studenti-iscritti/agevolazioni/finanziamenti-master-universitari-e-sda), for [international applicants](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/student-loans)). The application for funding must be received by the School by the end of the month following the course start date.

Furthermore, the University provides international students with information about possible sources for financing in several countries.
