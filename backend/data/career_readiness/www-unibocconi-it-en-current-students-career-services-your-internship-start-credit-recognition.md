# Your internship: from start to credit recognition

_You successfully managed the interviews, and now an Employer is offering you an internship position: don’t know what happens next? Need to understand how to manage the internship documents? You want to get academic credits for your internship, but are unsure about rules and processes?_

_The **Bocconi Internship Office is your point of reference during the whole internship experience**, being in charge of:_

- _Guiding you through the **procedures to obtain internship documents**, both in Italy and abroad;_
- _Supporting you in understanding the **rules to properly carry out the internship;**_
- _Helping you with the **recognition phase to obtain credits** in your study plan._
