Fees and Financial Aid

Tuition and fees for the 2026-2027 edition amount to **€ 16,000**.

Fees shall be paid in instalments. The structure of the tuition and fees instalments will be updated on this website soon.

- **I installment - advance**: € 2,500 on enrollment. Please be aware that while settling the I instalment you will be asked to pay an extra €32,00 as Italian tax on University fees.

- **I installment - adjustment:** € 3,900 -  15 October, 2026

- **II installment**: € 6,400 - 15 December, 2026

- **III installment**: € 3,200 - 15 March, 2027

Please note that the tuition fees paid are non-refundable.

## **Loans**

To cover the Specialized Master Programs tuition and fees, students with EU and UK citizenship can ask for low-interest loans to credit institutes with which Bocconi University has special agreements.

In the Bocconi online application, the maximum amount students can request cannot exceed the tuition and fees of the chosen master program.

Find out more about getting a loan at [**www.unibocconi.eu/specializedmasterloans**](http://www.unibocconi.eu/specializedmasterloans "http://www.unibocconi.eu/specializedmasterloans")
