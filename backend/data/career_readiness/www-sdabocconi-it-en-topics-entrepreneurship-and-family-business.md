# Entrepreneurship and family business

Being an entrepreneur does not only mean having a clear vision, but also knowing how to turn it into an action plan. It is therefore essential to identify new opportunities, manage risks, and value people, creating impact for the business, the family, and the wider community.

In this section, you will find the School’s full educational portfolio dedicated to this topic, along with events, news, research activities, and in-depth content.
