# Accessibility Statement

SDA Bocconi School of Management promotes an equitable, inclusive and accessible digital environment for all its community, spontaneously adhering to the digital accessibility standards in force, including Law 4/2004 and the AgID Guidelines on the accessibility of IT tools.

![Accessibility](https://www.sdabocconi.it/_next/image/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fsda-website-prod-sdawebsiteprodbucketpublic693d05b-w3mwb4fo9x9p.s3.eu-south-1.amazonaws.com%2Fs3fs-public%2F2025-11%2Faccessibility.jpg&w=3840&q=90&dpl=dpl_EgyAUeGYW1L1ERS2WGuDkM3HLa8o)

SDA Bocconi is committed to ensuring that its digital content is constantly updated and improved, with an ongoing review of the institutional website and online resources so that they are accessible to all people.

Any feedback is fundamental in order to continue to improve digital services and to respond to different accessibility needs. Reports and suggestions can be sent to the email address: [info@sdabocconi.it](mailto:info@sdabocconi.it)
