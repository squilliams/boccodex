# An Open and Inclusive Community

Bocconi is a truly international community, with students and faculty representing more than 100 countries around the world. Together, they make our campus an inclusive place where languages, experiences, cultures and ideas are exchanged on a daily basis, resulting in professional but also personal growth.

- **15,900** total students enrolled at Bocconi
- **5,100** international students
- **8,200** students enrolled in Bachelor programs
- **53%** international students enrolled in Bachelor programs in class groups taught in English
