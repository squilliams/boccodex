## Document summary

Here is the extracted information from the fees document for the academic year 2025-26 at Università Bocconi:

**Total Amount per Academic Year by Program**
- **Bachelor (Laurea Triennale and Giurisprudenza)**: €16,700

**ISEE-Based Contribution Brackets**
The document does not specify ISEE-based contribution brackets. However, it mentions a system of agevolazioni (scholarships and tuition waivers) based on merit and economic conditions. The specific brackets are not detailed in the provided text.

**Installment Schedules**
- **Ordinary Contribution**:
  - 1st installment (Acconto): €3,340 (due by 15/10/2025)
  - 2nd installment: €3,340 (due by 30/01/2026)
  - 3rd installment: €6,680 (due by 30/04/2026)
  - Conguaglio: €3,340 (due by 30/01/2026)
- **Agevolazioni (Scholarships and Tuition Waivers)**:
  - 80% Partial tuition waivers: €3,340 (total)
  - 60% Partial tuition waivers: €6,680 (total)
  - 40% Partial tuition waivers: €10,020 (total)
  - 20% Partial tuition waivers: €13,360 (total)

**Exemptions**
The document mentions that agevolazioni are available based on merit and economic conditions. Specific exemptions are not detailed in the provided text.

**Deadlines**
- 1st installment (Acconto): 15/10/2025
- 2nd installment: 30/01/2026
- 3rd installment: 30/04/2026
- Conguaglio: 30/01/2026

For more detailed information, it is advised to refer to the official regulations published on the university's website: [www.unibocconi.it/contributi](http://www.unibocconi.it/contributi) and [www.unibocconi.it/agevolazioni](http://www.unibocconi.it/agevolazioni).
