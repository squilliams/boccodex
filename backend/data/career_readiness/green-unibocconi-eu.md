Our Mission

GREEN aims to conduct and promote research at the intersection of the spatial of socio-economic phenomena and of climate change, transportation, environmental policy and energy markets.

AN ECOLOGICAL HISTORY OF EUROPE\\
\\
An ecological history of Europe is an academic workshop that examines Europe’s past through the lens of environmental change and human–nature interactions. The workshop explores how ecosystems, climate, land use, and resource exploitation have shaped European societies over long time periods. Topics include agriculture and forests, urbanization, energy use, biodiversity loss, and environmental governance from antiquity to the present.](https://green.unibocconi.eu/ecological-history-europe)

[![Image of Prof. Miriam Allena interviewed on PNNR](https://green.unibocconi.eu/sites/default/files/styles/badge_xl/public/media/images/bilancia%2Bmani%2Bunite%2Bconcept%2Blaw.jpg?itok=ipACtF-F)\\

**Observatory on Public Procurement and Sustainability**\\

\\
**Renewable Gas Observatory**\\
\\
