In accordance with ministerial regulations, the Faculty Board is composed of at least sixteen members (of whom at least 12 Bocconi full professors and associate professors).

The Faculty Board plans and manages the PhD program. The Faculty Board coordinates curricular activities and supervises research activities aimed at contributing to the advancement of research methodology and knowledge in a specific field.
