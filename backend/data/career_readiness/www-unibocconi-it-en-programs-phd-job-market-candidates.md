The international Job Placement of our PhDs is a central strategic goal of the PhD School. Recent employers of our students include both Academic and Non-Academic Institutions.

**Academic Institutions**:

Harvard University, University of Warwick, University of Texas, HEC, Universidad Carlos III Madrid, Cass Business School, Penn State, Imperial College Business School, Aston Business School, Tilburg University, Michigan State, University College London, ESSEC International Business School, Concordia University, University of Bristol, University of Dublin, University of Technology Sydney, Queen Mary's College, London School of Economics and many others.

**Other Non-Academic Institutions**:

European Commission, Eurostat, Banco Santander, HSBC London, McKinsey & Co., ENI, European Central Bank, Central Bank of England, Central Bank of Italy, Goldman Sachs, OECD and many others.
