### Supporting Institutions

Fondazione Altagamma

![Altagamma](https://www.sdabocconi.it/_next/image/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fsda-website-prod-sdawebsiteprodbucketpublic693d05b-w3mwb4fo9x9p.s3.eu-south-1.amazonaws.com%2Fs3fs-public%2Finline-images%2Faltagamma_logo_0.png&w=640&q=90&dpl=dpl_EgyAUeGYW1L1ERS2WGuDkM3HLa8o)Fondazione Altagamma is an internationally renowned **Italian association operating in the high-end market**. Its members reflect Italian style and culture in their own company management and products. They stand out for their innovation, quality, service, design, and prestige. Today, Altagamma has **76 member companies** in the world of fashion, design, jewelry, automobiles, and gourmet food specialties, along with a prestigious network of around 200 leading global luxury brands, which form the Altagamma Honorary Council.

Altagamma is the official sponsor of the MAMA and contributes to the Master not only financially – by funding scholarships and financing field projects – but also with business testimonials and seminars on key issues, facilitating access to companies to allow program participants to visit them, and to let faculty compile case studies. In addition, Altagamma assists in setting up activities specifically to promote the career development of participants. Lastly, Altagamma provides the opportunity to organize special events that give the Master high visibility among its numerous stakeholders.
