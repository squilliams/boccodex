# Facts and Figures

Bocconi is among the world's top research institutions in economics, having obtained several grants from international funders.

Specifically, Bocconi has won 72 European Research Council (ERC) grants and currently **ranks 1st in panel SH1**.

For details of all funded projects, please see the Research Centers websites.
