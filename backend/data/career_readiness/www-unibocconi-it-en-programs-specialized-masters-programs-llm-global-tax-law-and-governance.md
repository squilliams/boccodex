# LLM in Global Tax Law and Governance

LLM in Global Tax Law and Governance

Overview

[Presentation](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/specialized-masters-programs/llm-global-tax-law-and-governance/presentation) [Program Structure](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/specialized-masters-programs/llm-global-tax-law-and-governance/program-structure) [Admission](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/specialized-masters-programs/llm-global-tax-law-and-governance/admission) [Fees and Financial Aid](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/specialized-masters-programs/llm-global-tax-law-and-governance/fees-and-financial-aid)

The LLM in Global Tax Law and Governance (GTLG) is a 10-month program that provides legal tools, critical analysis of case law and practices, and a systematic understanding of the relationship between tax authorities and taxpayers.

As taxation and its financial and managerial aspects gain prominence in professional settings, the need for experts capable of managing complex corporate strategies and interactions with tax authorities — both nationally and internationally — has become increasingly evident.

The GTLG program is designed to address these needs, equipping students with the comprehensive technical and interpersonal skills necessary to become innovative leaders in the world of global taxation

## Program Snapshot

**Dates:** September 2026–July 2027

**Duration:** 10 months (classes conclude in May, exams/thesis preparation May–July)

**Mode and language:** full-time

- Global Track, in English: designed for both non-Italian and Italian students seeking international careers in tax law
- Domestic Track, partly in English: combines an English and Italian learning experience, tailored for students who wish to pursue opportunities in Italian tax legislation

**Credits:** 62 CFU

[Learn more about the program](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/specialized-masters-programs/llm-global-tax-law-and-governance/program-structure)

The ideal candidate possesses the following:

- Bachelor or Master degree in law, economics, management or political science
- limited working experience
- fluency in English

[Discover the requirements](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/specialized-masters-programs/llm-global-tax-law-and-governance/presentation)

**Application deadlines**

- 18 March 2026
- 21 May 2026

[Learn how to apply](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/specialized-masters-programs/llm-global-tax-law-and-governance/admission)

To cover tuition and fees, students with EU and UK citizenship can ask for low-interest loans to credit institutes with which Bocconi University has special agreements.

## Employment

Taxation is now a **strategic priority with global impact,** and this LLM provides the innovative knowledge and skills needed to excel in the evolving field. The program offers advanced specialization for future careers in **major law and accounting firms**, **consulting firms**, and **multinational corporations** with in-house tax departments.

Opportunities also extend to **public sector institutions** like national tax authorities and financial regulators, where professionals support **policy implementation and enforcement**. Graduates may also work in tax policy and economic developmentfor **international or EU organizations**, pursue **academic or research** roles, or launch ventures in **tax technology and advisory services.**
