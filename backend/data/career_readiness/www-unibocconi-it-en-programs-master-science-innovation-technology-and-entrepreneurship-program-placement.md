#### Graduate Job Statistics

#### One year after graduation

- _**94.3%** of graduates are employed_
- _**53%** of employed graduates work abroad_

Data refers to a.y. 2022-2023 Economics and Management of Innovation and Technology graduates

#### On graduation day

- _**83.5%** of graduates are employed_
- _**53.5%** of employed graduates work abroad_
- _They have been called to an average of **4.8** job interviews_
- _They have received on average **1.8** job offers_

Survey - Economics and Management of Innovation and Technology graduation sessions a.y. 2023-2024
