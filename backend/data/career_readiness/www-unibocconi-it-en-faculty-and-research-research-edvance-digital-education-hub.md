# Edvance – Digital Education Hub

_The first Digital Education Hub in Italy, created to foster digital culture and innovation in higher education_

Coordinated by Politecnico di Milano and funded as part of _Mission 4 “Education and Research"_ of the National Recovery and Resilience Plan (NRRP), this project **brings together several universities and Higher Education in Art, Music and Dance (AFAM) institutions in Italy.** Their ambitious goal is to develop, disseminate and consolidate advanced digital skills, while promoting the adoption of innovative and inclusive teaching methods.

With more than **200 online courses (MOOCs),** Edvance offers high-quality opportunities for continuous learning and professional development.Participants can also certify their acquired skills through micro-credentials and open badges.

The project is aimed at **students, faculty and professionals** interested in exploring topics such as artificial intelligence, data literacy and digital sustainability.

**Participation in the courses is free of charge**: users can access the Edvance platform through their university credentials (for institutions participating in the project) or via SPID or CIE digital identification.
