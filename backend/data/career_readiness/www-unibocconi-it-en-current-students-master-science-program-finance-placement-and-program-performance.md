## **One year after graduation\***

- **93%** of graduates are employed
- **63.9%** of employed graduates work abroad

**\*** Data refers to a.y. 2021-2022 graduates of the English class groups

## **On graduation day\*\***

- **82.2%** of graduates are employed
- **61.2%** of employed graduates work abroad
- They have been called to an average of **5.9** job interviews
- They have received on average **2.1** job offers

**\*\*** Survey - graduation sessions a.y. 2022-2023 (English class group)

## **Graduating students - satisfaction with the Study Program**

**86.4%** of students who graduated in the 2023-24 academic year declared they were satisfied with their Study Program

**83.1%** of students who attended courses offered in 2024-25 academic year are satisfied with the way the courses were carried out (grades from 6 to 10 on a 1-10 scale)
