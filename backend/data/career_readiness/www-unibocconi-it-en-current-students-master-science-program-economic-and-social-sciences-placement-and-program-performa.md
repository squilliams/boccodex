## **One year after graduation\***

- **94.4%** of graduates non continuing their studies are employed
- **52.9%** of employed graduates work abroad
- **39.4%** of our graduates are continuing their studies or are employed in Higher Education Institutions

**\\*** Data refers to a.y. 2021-2022 graduates

## **On graduation day\*\***

- **80.2%** of graduates are employed
- **60.4%** of employed graduates work abroad
- They have been called to an average of **3.6** job interviews
- They have received on average **1.6** job offers

**\\*\\*** Survey - graduation sessions a.y. 2022-2023

## **Graduating students - satisfaction with the Study Program**

**87.4%** of students who graduated in the 2023-24 academic year declared they were satisfied with their Study Program

## **Attending students - satisfaction with teaching**

**86.1%** of students who attended courses offered in 2024-25 academic year are satisfied with the way the courses were carried out (grades from 6 to 10 on a 1-10 scale)
