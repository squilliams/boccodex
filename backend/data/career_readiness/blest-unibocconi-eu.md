## Research Streams

[![Image of Democracy, Solidarity and Governance](https://blest.unibocconi.eu/sites/default/files/styles/badge_xl/public/media/images/future.png?itok=dp2RY_nS)\\
\\
**Democracy, Solidarity and Governance**\\
\\
The DSG stream investigates the inextricable link between Democracy, Solidarity and Governance within the European Union context both at the macro and the micro level.](https://blest.unibocconi.eu/research-streams/democracy-solidarity-and-governance-in-europe)

[![Image of Europe and the World  (EATW)](https://blest.unibocconi.eu/sites/default/files/styles/badge_xl/public/media/images/eu-and-world_2.png?itok=rk7RjPht)\\
\\
**Europe and the World**\\
\\
The EW stream looks at the changes occurring, also in the light of recent events, at the role of the European Union in the world.](https://blest.unibocconi.eu/research-streams/europe-and-the-world)

[![Image of Transparency and Accountability in Europe  ](https://blest.unibocconi.eu/sites/default/files/styles/badge_xl/public/media/images/transparency.png?itok=02ItseIQ)\\
\\
**Transparency and Accountability in Europe**\\
\\
The Transparency and Accountability stream focuses on oversight both in relation to private and public sectors, with a particular focus on the stability and efficiency of EU financial markets, the protection of investors and the safeguarding of stakeholders’ interests.](https://blest.unibocconi.eu/research-streams/transparency-and-accountability-in-europe)

[![Image of Economic and Monetary Integration (EMI)](https://blest.unibocconi.eu/sites/default/files/styles/badge_xl/public/media/images/Economic-and-Monetary-Integration-i.png?itok=sLD_4uWm)\\
\\
**Economic and Monetary Integration**\\
\\
The EMI stream investigates two key aspects of Europe’s Economic and Monetary Union (EMU): the single market and the common currency. In particular, it studies how the balance between the gains and the pains from European integration depends on the design of coordinated, coherent policy approaches, or the lack thereof, in both the real and the monetary domains.](https://blest.unibocconi.eu/research-streams/economic-and-monetary-integration)

[![Image of Digital and Sustainable Europe](https://blest.unibocconi.eu/sites/default/files/styles/badge_xl/public/media/images/digital.png?itok=XogW01kh)\\
\\
**Digital and Sustainable Europe**\\
\\
The Digital and Sustainable Europe stream investigates the digital revolution and its important implications in terms of industrial dynamics and companies’ strategies, as well as in terms of enabling sustainability.](https://blest.unibocconi.eu/research-streams/digital-and-sustainable-europe)

[![Image of Banking and Financial Integration](https://blest.unibocconi.eu/sites/default/files/styles/badge_xl/public/media/images/banking.png?itok=-Sj3tHzy)\\
\\
**Banking and Financial Integration**\\
\\
The banking and financial integration stream investigates the integration process in financial markets, banks and other financial institutions in Europe. Promoting financial integration within and across countries is a desirable task as in a truly integrated financial system funds flow smoothly from lenders to borrowers and from an economy to another, this supporting economic growth. Yet, it is a challenging task, with potentially severe unintended consequences.](https://blest.unibocconi.eu/research-streams/banking-and-financial-integration)
