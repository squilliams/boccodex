# Faculty

**A team of experts dedicated to the public sector**

The EMMAP Faculty is a team that has been consistently committed for over fifty years to cutting-edge **research** and **high-quality training** in the field of **Public Management & Policy**. Its mission is to generate a positive impact and drive real change within public administration (ministries, regional governments, agencies, local authorities), healthcare (agencies, local health authorities, hospitals, research institutes), and the Third Sector (non-profit organizations, cooperatives, etc.), through the people it works with in the Master’s program.
