**Transformation rarely starts with certainty.**

**It starts with a doubt, an ambition, a quiet awareness that you are ready for more.**

**At the heart of our MBAs lies transformation** — first and foremost of your competencies, but also of how you think, act, and lead.

The SDA Bocconi **MBAs Open Day is designed around your questions** — offering the space to explore your aspirations and understand which path can shape your next move.

Throughout the day, you will explore our portfolio of MBA programs — the **Full-Time MBA**, the **Executive MBA (EMBA)**, and **the Global Executive MBA (GEMBA)** — gain a clear understanding of each program’s structure, distinctive features, relevance, and learning philosophy, while **engaging with the people who enable this transformation**: **Directors**, the **professionals of the Guidance & Recruitment Team** and the **Career Development Center**, and **Alumnae and Alumni** sharing their journeys.

Get an overview of the day:

- [**MBAs Programs presentation**](https://www.sdabocconi.it/en/events/mbas-open-day-20260411/#agenda)
- [**Orientation meetings with the Guidance & Recruitment Team**](https://www.sdabocconi.it/en/events/mbas-open-day-20260411/#orientationmeetings)
- [**Guided Campus Tour**](https://www.sdabocconi.it/en/events/mbas-open-day-20260411/#campustour)

We look forward to welcoming you — **whether on campus in Milan** for a fully immersive experience, **or online** from wherever you are — and supporting you as you move **from questions to decision** and take the next step in your professional journey.

## Orientation Meetings

After registering for the event and selecting the Orientation Meeting, you will receive a confirmation email with a link to **reserve your preferred time slot**.

The Orientation Meeting is a dedicated one-to-one conversation with our Guidance & Recruitment Team a meaningful moment of dialogue where _your goals truly meet our guidance_. It is designed to help you reflect on your aspirations, clarify your priorities, and explore which program best aligns with your professional background, ambitions, and desired transformation.

This personalized exchange goes beyond information: it’s an opportunity to receive tailored insights, ask candid questions, and gain a clearer perspective on the path that can best support your next step.

## Campus Tour

Do you want to experience campus life like a real student? During the Open Day, you’ll have the opportunity to attend live classes, explore areas dedicated to the different programs, and discover the brand-new Bocconi Sport Center.

A guided campus tour will allow you to experience firsthand the environment where your transformation could begin immersing yourself in the spaces, the atmosphere, and the vibrant community that define life at SDA Bocconi.
