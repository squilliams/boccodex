# Faculty

**International Luxury Experts**

The strength of the EMiLUX faculty lies in a very high level academic preparation and in the close and uninterrupted contact with the business world. The work of the EMiLUX professors is grounded in research, publishing, teaching, and consultancy with some of the most famous luxury companies in the word.

_From left to right: Stefano Pogutz, MBA Director; Cyrille Vigneron, Chairman of Cartier Culture and Philanthropy; Gabriella Lojacono, EMiLUX Director; Lorenzo Bertelli, Marketing Director & Head of Corporate Social Responsibility, Prada Group; Stefano Rosso, CEO, BVX, part of OTB Group; Stefania Carraro, EMiLUX Coordinator_
