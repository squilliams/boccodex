# Network LOPS

Logistica e operations del pronto soccorso

Healthcare

Established in 2023, the **LOPS Network** serves as a platform for dialogue and professional development for those who lead, manage, and design Emergency Department (ED) processes.

Through a carefully designed combination of classroom teaching, experiential learning activities, and structured dialogue around national and international best practices, the Network supports ED leadership teams and coordinators in understanding the evolving healthcare landscape and advancing innovative managerial methods and tools to effectively govern and optimize Emergency Department operations.

![lops](https://www.sdabocconi.it/_next/image/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fsda-website-prod-sdawebsiteprodbucketpublic693d05b-w3mwb4fo9x9p.s3.eu-south-1.amazonaws.com%2Fs3fs-public%2F2026-01%2Flogo_network_lops.png%3Fv%3D1769444289&w=3840&q=90&dpl=dpl_EgyAUeGYW1L1ERS2WGuDkM3HLa8o)

### Why the LOPS Network

The LOPS Network is a structured platform for professional dialogue and development designed for those who aim to:

- Understand and anticipate the evolving trajectories of Emergency Departments within a rapidly changing regulatory and institutional landscape (DM77, 116117, PNRR, ministerial reforms, etc.)
- Acquire, apply, and further innovate Operations Management methodologies and tools to address the core challenges facing Emergency Departments
- Engage in peer exchange and test new managerial approaches

## The LOPS Network offers:

- ### 5 workshop

The format includes **5 meetings** at SDA Bocconi (10:00 a.m. – 4:00 p.m.) combining peer exchange and experiential learning.

- ### Managerial innovation

tools and strategies to enhance Emergency Department logistics and operations

- ### System scenarios

dialogue with key stakeholders to analyze ongoing system transformations (e.g., 116117, hospital system reforms, etc.) and assess their impact on ED management

- ### Interactive methodology

workshop-based sessions designed to foster peer benchmarking and hands-on learning

## 5 discussion workshops

The LOPS Network is designed for **professionals and organizational teams** involved in **Emergency Department leadership and coordination, Nursing and Medical Leadership, Operations Management, Management Control, and Regional 118 Emergency Service management**.

The format includes 5 workshops designed with a hands-on, lab-based approach, aimed at fostering interaction between participants and SDA Bocconi faculty, with a focus on industry dynamics and the promotion of executive innovation in the fields of logistics and operations.

Each workshop combines theory, practice and exchange moments in an effective way and is divided into 6 steps:

**Theoretical framework introduction and definition** by the course faculty

**Group work**

**Networking lunch**

**Open discussion** on the topics addressed

**Debriefing** and take home by the faculty

**Post-event material sharing** and working document prepared by SDA Bocconi faculty.

### Redesigning Emergency Department Processes for Long-Term Sustainability

Now in its third edition, LOPS aims to further explore and develop—together with its Community—the innovation of emergency department processes, building on two key drivers. On the one hand, the opportunities enabled by technology (such as indoor navigation, telemedicine, and virtual assistants); on the other, the challenges arising from socio-demographic change (including population aging and growing social fragility).

Each theme will be addressed across multiple sessions, allowing the Community to learn, experiment, and exchange insights on the application of methods and practices, in the spirit of a truly collaborative learning and transformation network.

## Program Advisory

Learning is never a linear journey—it is a path built together, combining specific training needs with practical tools tailored to individual challenges. Contact our team of Program Advisors, and together we will design the ideal learning path for you or your team, ensuring alignment with your goals.

**Your benefits:**

A dedicated orientation meeting, either in-person or remotely, to identify learning needs and create a customized plan. Throughout the course, you will experience a tailored welcome to introduce you to the program and foster networking between participants and instructors. Upon completion, your evaluation will become a valuable contribution for continuous improvement of the offering.

**How your organization benefits:**

Training consultation with HR managers to analyze organizational needs and develop personalized projects. Ongoing progress monitoring and a final follow-up to share results and gather feedback, ensuring constant alignment with pre-set goals.
