Economic and Social Sciences

[Overview](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/master-science-program/economic-and-social-sciences) [Governance](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/master-science-program/economic-and-social-sciences/governance) [Placement and Program Performance](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/master-science-program/economic-and-social-sciences/placement-and-program-performance) [Program structure](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/master-science-program/economic-and-social-sciences/program-structure) [Guest Lectures](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/master-science-program/economic-and-social-sciences/guest-lectures)

During the Master of Science program, students acquire specific knowledge and skills related to economics, quantitative subjects and the social sciences.

Through the teaching activities that characterize the course, they develop skills in

- making judgements autonomously
- improving their written and oral communication
- acquiring lifelong learning skills useful for both continuing their studies and their professional futures.
