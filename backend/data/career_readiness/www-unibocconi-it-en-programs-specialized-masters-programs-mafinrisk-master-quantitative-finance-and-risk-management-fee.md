# Fees and Financial Aid

MAFINRISK - Master of Quantitative Finance and Risk Management

[Overview](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/specialized-masters-programs/mafinrisk-master-quantitative-finance-and-risk-management) [Program Structure](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/specialized-masters-programs/mafinrisk-master-quantitative-finance-and-risk-management/program-structure) [Admissions](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/specialized-masters-programs/mafinrisk-master-quantitative-finance-and-risk-management/admissions) [Contact us](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/specialized-masters-programs/mafinrisk-master-quantitative-finance-and-risk-management/contact-us)

**TUITION AND FEES**

Tuition fees for the 2026-2027 Master of Quantitative Finance and Risk Management are €16,000 (in addition to fixed stamp duties totaling €32). Payment can be made in three separate installments (specific amounts and deadlines to be confirmed).

**Academic Year 2025-2026**

Academic tuition and fees for the 2025-2026 Mafinrisk edition amount to €16,000 (+ fixed stamp duties €32,00) to be paid in 3 installments as below:

|     |     |     |
| --- | --- | --- |
|  | **Amount** | **Deadline** |
| 1st installment - advance | €2,500 (+ fixed stamp duties €32,00) | on enrollment |
| 1st installment - adjustment | €3,900 | by 15 October 2025 |
| 2ndinstallment | €6,400 | by 15 December 2025 |
| 3rd installment | €3,200 | by 13 March 2026 |

The tuition and fees include attendance to classes, course materials, use of Bocconi facilities, access to the Library and the Career Services.

Tuition and fees are due in their full amount from the enrollment and for the whole academic year.

In case of withdrawal from the program, the installments paid cannot be refunded and the tuition and fees due at the time of submitting the withdrawal request must be paid.

**The full Regulation of tuition and fees for Specialized Master Programs is available at** [**this link**](https://www.unibocconi.eu/wps/wcm/connect/bocconi/sitopubblico_en/navigation+tree/home/programs/current+students/services/fees/specialized+masters/).

**MERIT-BASED TUITION WAIVERS**

Thanks to a contribution from [**Intesa Sanpaolo**](https://www.intesasanpaolo.com/) (the program's main corporate partner since 2011) offered **in memory of Roberta Lento**, a company employee who passed away prematurely, Mafinrisk has been able to award **one or two partial tuition waivers** each year to deserving students, selected on the basis of **merit criteria**.

Starting from the **2025–2026 edition**, the contribution will fund two partial tuition waivers of €4,000 each, awarded to the two most deserving female students on the basis of merit criteria.

For the 2026–2027 edition, thanks to the renewed contribution from Intesa Sanpaolo, two partial tuition waivers of €4,000 are available, which will be awarded to the two most deserving female students.

**STUDENT LOANS**

To cover the Specialized Master Programs tuition and fees, students with EU and UK citizenship can ask for low-interest loans to credit institutes with which Bocconi University has special agreements.

The online application to make your loan request **is open from July 2026 until October 2026.**

In the Bocconi online application, the maximum amount students can request cannot exceed the tuition and fees of the chosen master program.

All information about loans available and how to apply can be found at this [link](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/services/student-loans-0)

**BOCCONI SPORTS SCHOLARSHIPS**

" **Bocconi Sport Scholarships"** aim to support students with **sporting talent**, removing, where necessary, any socio-economic barriers that could prevent them from accessing university education.

The initiative stems from the University's belief that supporting high-profile young athletes who are eager to commit to their professional growth creates value for the community and for the country as a whole.

[Here](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/master-science-and-ma-programs/funding/bocconi-sport-scholarship-ay-2026-27-master-science-programs "https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/master-science-and-ma-programs/funding/bocconi-sport-scholarship-ay-2026-27-master-science-programs") you can find detailed information about the scholarships, general rules, eligible recipients and requirements.

**Very important note**: **Students enrolled in Specialized Master programs** who are interested in this opportunity **are kindly asked to first contact the assistant of their respective Master’s program to receive instructions on the application procedures and timeline**.

**OTHER FUNDING OPPORTUNITIES FOR STUDENTS WITH  ITALIAN CITIZENSHIP**

**INPS Scholarships**

INPS offers scholarships to finance your Specialized Master Program. Candidates who are interested in this opportunity should personally verify all information on the INPS webpage and follow the procedure specified therein.

**Scholarships offered by Regions**

Some Regions may offer scholarships to their residents for attending post-graduate programs. Candidates interested in these opportunities should personally contact the Regional Offices.
