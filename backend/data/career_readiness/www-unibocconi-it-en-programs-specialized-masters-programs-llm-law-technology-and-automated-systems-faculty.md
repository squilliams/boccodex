The faculty of the LLM in Law of Technology and Automated Systems is drawn from worldwide-renowned academic institutions, law firms, public authorities, think-tanks and intergovernmental organizations.

Each LLM module and sub-module will be taught by top-tier experts and specialists of each subject.
