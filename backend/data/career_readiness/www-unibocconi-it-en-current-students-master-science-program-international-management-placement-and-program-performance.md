## **One year after graduation\***

- **95.7%** of graduates are employed
- **60.3%** of employed graduates work abroad

**\*** Data refers to a.y. 2021-2022 graduates

## **On graduation day \*\***

- **82.7%** of graduates are employed
- **58.9%** of employed graduates work abroad
- They have been called to an average of **5.6** job interviews
- They have received on average **1.9** job offers

**\*\*** Survey - graduation sessions a.y. 2022-2023

## **Graduating students - satisfaction with the Study Program**

**90.6%** of students who graduated in the 2023-24 academic year declared they were satisfied with their Study Program
