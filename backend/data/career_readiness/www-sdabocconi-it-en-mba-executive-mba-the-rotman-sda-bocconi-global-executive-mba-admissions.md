# Selection and admissions process

At the Rotman-SDA Bocconi GEMBA, we seek talented professionals with diverse backgrounds, strong experience, and a global mindset. Our admissions process is designed not only to assess your qualifications but also to ensure that the program aligns with your aspirations and professional goals.

The selection process begins with a pre-evaluation of the candidate’s basic requirements.

**Before submitting an application, we encourage prospective students to connect with our Admissions team for an initial assessment.** Once the application is received, a thorough review of submitted documents and test results follows. Final admission decisions are based on a holistic evaluation of each candidate’s potential to contribute to and benefit from the program.

Admission to the GEMBA represents a strategic investment in your future. The program enhances leadership abilities, strengthens decision-making skills, and provides the tools to navigate global business challenges—all while expanding your international network and business fundamentals.

## Admissions results

As soon as the selection process is completed, you will receive a formal letter from the Admissions Committee notifying you of the decision. Selection results are never communicated by telephone. All decisions made by the Admissions Committee are final.

The selection process will result in one of the following outcomes:

- **Admission:** you will receive the official admission notice along with the participation contract. If all available spots have been filled, the school may place you on a waiting list or offer admission for the following edition.

Candidates who receive an offer must confirm their participation within the deadlines set by the Admissions committee.

Admission to the program is valid for the year the application is submitted. However, you can request deferment for the following year, but approval of deferment is on a case-by-case decision by the Admissions committee.

- **Non-admission:** Admission to the program is highly selective. Our selection process evaluates each candidate’s individual profile while also considering the overall composition of the Rotman-SDA Bocconi GEMBA class, which must be consistent with the quality and diversity the school requires. While we acknowledge the significant effort all candidates invest in the application and selection process, we do not provide personalized feedback to non-admitted candidates.

## How to apply

- All required documents must be uploaded to the relevant section of the Application portal and submitted to the Admissions team.
- Applicants may track the receipt of their application and monitor their application status directly through the online application.
- Additional documents can be uploaded after the initial application submission.

We encourage you to apply early to maximize your chances for admission.

## We look for highly motivated candidates with well-defined goals

The Rotman-SDA Bocconi GEMBA admission process is rigorous, ensuring that selected candidates meet specific criteria set by the school. These criteria guarantee the highest-quality educational experience for both participants and companies.

The selection process evaluates each candidate’s academic and professional background, motivations, and individual skills. It is designed not only to determine whether a candidate is a good fit for the program but also to ensure that the GEMBA aligns with their expectations and career goals. We dedicate significant time and effort to admissions because the uniqueness of the Rotman-SDA Bocconi GEMBA comes from the diverse talents of its students, who bring extraordinary value and energy to the class.

We encourage candidates to start the process by submitting their application to the Admissions team and sending in all required documents and test scores (GMAT, TOEFL, etc.) as soon as possible. Selection is based on both the candidate’s profile and the submission date of their application while ensuring a diverse and globally representative class, in line with the school’s commitment to diversity and international reach for the Rotman-SDA Bocconi GEMBA.
