# In-Company Training abroad

_Career programs organized at **company premises** and structured in a **theoretical part** (company presentations, speeches) and a **practical one** (business cases, group work, networking, etc.)._

_**What’s in it for you?** You will gain better understanding of employers and the job market in different Countries, join hands-on projects, engage with company representatives to dive deep into their functional areas, career possibilities, and internship opportunities._

_In-Company Training abroad programs have different timings, locations and tracks, and may vary in application deadlines and eligibility criteria._

_A **sine qua non** **condition** for being eligible and selected is having **student status** at the time of application and maintaining it **for the entire duration** of the Program._

_Here you can find more info on eligibility:_

- _**1st, 2nd-year students** that will be **regularly enrolled** in a Bocconi **Master of Science** Degree Course in the **current academic year**;_
- _**2nd-year students** that will be **regularly enrolled** in a Bocconi **Bachelor of Science** Degree Course in the **current academic year** ( **only for specific Programs**);_
- _**3rd-year students** that will be **regularly enrolled** in a Bocconi **Bachelor of Science** Degree Course in the **current academic year**;_
- _**3rd, 4th-year students** that will be **regularly enrolled** in the Bocconi **World Bachelor in Business** in the **current academic year**;_
- _**4th and 5th-year Master of Science in Law** ( **only for specific Programs**);_
- _**GPA: minimum 22/30**(average weighted on credits)._
- _For programs taking place in the Fall semester, please note that all **prospective first-year Master of Science students** admitted to the In-Company Training Abroad program **must complete their enrollment** at the University before the start of the program._
- _For programs offering multiple tracks taking place on the same dates, candidates may **apply to only one track.** Multiple applications for different tracks within the same program will be automatically excluded._

_Please check the **calendar below** for an overview of all the **Programs of the semester**._

_In order **to find more info** related to each In-Company Training Program abroad, please refer to:_

_You@B > Career Service menu > Events Calendar_

_Then, **to apply for specific Programs**, please refer to:_

_You@B > Punto Blu > Menù > In-Company Training: upload your CV in PDF format and record a short video interview_

_**Incomplete applications will not be considered**._

_Should the number of applications exceed the maximum capacity of students admitted, they will be ranked according to:_

- _Evaluation of **students' dossier** (GPA, internship and work experiences, study abroad experiences, extracurricular activities)._
- _Soon after, Career Services will evaluate the **video interview** or invite the most suitable applicants for a **one-to-one interview video/individual interviews** (motivation, communication and relational skills, fluency)._

_In case, the candidate is not considered suitable for the Program, he/she will not be selected, regardless of his/her academic performance._

Important info

Please find below important info on exceptional circumstances and student responsibilities (costs, visa, Bocconi Students Travel Risk Policy, travel recommendations of Ministry for Foreign Affairs and International SOS).

Costs

_**Participants are responsible** for the following costs:_

- _**visa fees** to enter the Country of destination (when required);_
- _**health insurance** (when required);_
- _**meals** and **extra costs**(when not included)._

_**For EU Programs**:_

_Participants have to reach the destination on their own. **Travel and accommodation expenses shall be borne by participants** responsible for their arrangements._

_**For extra-EU Programs (all US destinations and Dubai)**:_

_Participants have to reach the destination on their own. **Travel expenses shall be borne by participants**, who are responsible for their own travel arrangements. **Accommodation expenses will be covered by the University**._

Students responsibilities

_Before applying, please note that it is the students' responsibility to:_

- _Verify personally, with the authorities of the hosting Country, the suitable procedures for obtaining the appropriate visa, if required, and any other necessary documentation in full compliance with local laws. **All formalities relating to visas and other documents required to enter the Country are the responsibility of the student**._
- _Read the **“Bocconi Students Travel Risk Policy”** (available on yoU@B), and comply with the risk mitigation measures suggested._
- _Access, through yoU@B diary, the **Travel Risk Awareness Elearning**, and the **International SOS Member’s portal**, which includes Country Guides and Travel Security and Medical Risk Ratings._
- _Check and **follow all the travel recommendations** of your Country’s Ministry for Foreign Affairs and International SOS._

Exceptional circumstances

_Please, be aware that **Bocconi University reserves the right to cancel the Program** if exceptional circumstances occur before or during the carrying out of the Program._

_Travel and health risks can rapidly change and lead Bocconi University and hosting Institutions/Companies or Countries to increase travel and health restrictions. In any case, **Bocconi University does not guarantee that the In-Company Training will take place if the general conditions do not safeguard the well-being and safety of students**._
