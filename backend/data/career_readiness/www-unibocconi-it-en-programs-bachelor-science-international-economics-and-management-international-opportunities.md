Studying abroad can be a great opportunity for growth both academically and personally, allowing you to challenge yourself in a multicultural environment.

Students in this program can participate in different study abroad options, both for short periods (such as Exchange Programs) and for longer periods (such as Double Degrees).

_Double Degrees_

A Double Degree is a special program that allows you to study at two top business schools and earn two degrees, one from each University.

Bocconi University offers an exciting Double Degree program in partnership with Peking University, one of China's most prestigious institutions. As a student participating in this unique opportunity, you will spend your first two years in Milan, attending the BSc in International Economics and Management at Bocconi. Then you will head to Beijing for two more years in the renowned “Future Leaders” Program at Peking University’s Guanghua School of Management. By the end of the four years, you will graduate with two internationally recognized diplomas — one from Bocconi and one from Peking University — setting you up for a truly global career.

_Exchange Programs_

The Exchange Program gives you the chance to spend one semester abroad at a partner university, starting in your Bachelor of Science program.

One-semester programs with prestigious partner universities, including:

- University of Pennsylvania, Wharton School (US)
- New York University, Stern School of Business (US)
- HEC Paris (France)
- National University of Singapore (Singapore)
- University of Sydney (Australia)
