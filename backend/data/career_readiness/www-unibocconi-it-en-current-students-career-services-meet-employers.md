# Meet with employers

_Heard about career events and want to join? Curious about networking with recruiters and presenting your cv in person? Not ready to apply yet, but already interested in making informed decisions about potential employers?_

_Join the **events organized by Bocconi Career Services to meet with top-tier firms**, and you will have the chance to:_

- _**Approach recruiters** to ask questions_
- _**Gain insights** about companies and their corporate culture_
- _Discover **internship and job opportunities** and apply for open positions_
