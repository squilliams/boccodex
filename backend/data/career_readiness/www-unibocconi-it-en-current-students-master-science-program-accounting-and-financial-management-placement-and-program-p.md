## **One year after graduation\***

- **96.3%** of graduates are employed
- **43.6%** of employed graduates work abroad

**\\*** Data refers to a.y. 2021-2022 graduates of the English class group

## **On graduation day\*\***

- **82.5%** of graduates are employed
- **39.4%** of employed graduates work abroad
- They have been called to an average of **5.8** job interviews
- They have received on average **1.9** job offers

**\\*\\*** Survey - graduation sessions a.y. 2022-2023 (English class group)
