# JobGate: applying online

_**JobGate is Bocconi’ online portal to find an internship or a job**. The platform is **available 24/7** for applications, and every day new opportunities are published, both in Italy and abroad, dedicated to profiles with up to three years of work experience._

_You can apply for as many opportunities as you wish, and you can also sort the listings using **advanced filters** (starting date, business field, function, and much more), to carefully select offers that align with your professional goals._

## Discover more

How to access

_From yoU@B > menu on the left, Career Services > button “Internship and placement Offers”._

_JobGate is open to **Bocconi University students and graduates**:_

- _If you are a student: access with the same login and password used for yoU@B._
- _If you are a graduate: you need to register on_ [_http://www.alumnibocconi.it_](http://www.alumnibocconi.it/ "http://www.alumnibocconi.it/") _to obtain new login and password._

Special collections of offers

_On JobGate you can also browse the offers according to pre-filtered boxes showcasing different clusters of opportunities: by **geographical area**, by **business field**, or **linked to specific internship programs**. When available, you can access these collections from the JobGate homepage or following the path Home/Jobs/More filters/Jobs Source._

- _**Focus on**: Internship and job offers are grouped according to specific “topic-based” features, such as **geographical area** (e.g. Companies in the Americas, Companies in Asia etc.) and the **employer’s industry** (e.g. International Organizations, Law firms for legal profiles, Chambers of Commerce etc.)_
- [_**MAECI – MUR – FCRUI**_](https://www.unibocconi.it/it/studenti-iscritti/career-services/opportunita-di-stage-presso-le-sedi-maeci-allestero "Opportunità di stage presso le sedi MAECI all'estero") _**:** This box collects internship opportunities at **Embassies, Permanent Representations of the OO.II, Consulates, Italian Institutes of Culture**. This program is only open to **Master of Science students with Italian citizenship**. You can find more info at the dedicated pages._
- [_**Curricular Internship Special**_](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/curricular-internship-special "Curricular Internship Special") _: Twice a year, this publication is exclusively aimed at **Master of Science students who have not yet activated a curricular internship** and offers internship opportunities provided by Companies which are part of Bocconi's partnership programs. You can find more info at the dedicated pages._
