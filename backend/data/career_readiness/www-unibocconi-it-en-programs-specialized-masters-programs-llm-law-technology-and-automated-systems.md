The LLM in Law of Technology and Automated Systems is a Specialized Master program taught entirely in English, offered both full-time (12 months) and part-time (2 years). Students may also pursue a dual program with King’s College London.

The program focuses on all key areas of interest for lawyers and legal practitioners managing the impact of digital transformation and implementation of disruptive technologies.

The objective is to equip tomorrow’s professionals with the cutting-edge solutions to face critical issues emerging in recent years related to privacy, AI, blockchain, cybersecurity, intellectual property, e-commerce, data protection and more.

## Why Choose LATAS?

![](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/styles/container_100_lg/public/media/images/_mg_0189_1.jpg.webp?itok=VT669aNS)

Legal skills for the digital age

_Training future lawyers to understand, shape, and regulate digital transformation. Students learn how to apply traditional legal principles to emerging technologies such as AI, blockchain, and data-driven innovation, developing practical skills to design legal solutions for a rapidly evolving digital economy._

Knowledge from field experts

_Courses are taught by leading academics and practitioners from top law firms, international organizations, and the tech industry. Their expertise provides students with both conceptual grounding and hands-on understanding of how technology is reshaping law and regulation._

International network

_The program attracts participants from diverse legal and cultural backgrounds, building a truly global classroom. Graduates join an active community of Bocconi and LL.M. alumni working in international firms, regulatory bodies, and technology companies around the world._

Dynamic location

_Located in the heart of Milan, Bocconi offers access to conferences, workshops and partnerships with major tech and law players, creating an immersive professional environment._

Dual program possibility

_There is the opportunity to apply for the Dual Program with King’s College London, earning two degrees and studying in two global centres of law and technology. This exclusive track deepens expertise and broadens international career prospects by completing one semester in Milan and one in London._

## Program at Bocconi University

Bocconi offers both a full-time option (12 months) and a part-time option (2 years). Both programs have the same structure: compulsory and electives courses, in addition to an internship, clinical work, or an individual research paper to complete the program.

[Learn more about the program](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/specialized-masters-programs/llm-law-technology-and-automated-systems/program-structure)

## Dual Program with King’s College London

This prestigious program tailored for legal professionals allows students to spend one semester each at Bocconi and King's College London. At the program's conclusion, students will obtain a 2nd-level Specialized Master in Law of Internet Technologies and Automated Systems in Italy, as well as an LLM in Law, Innovation and Technology issued in the UK.

[Learn more about the program](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/specialized-masters-programs/llm-law-technology-and-automated-systems/dual-programme-kings-college-london)
