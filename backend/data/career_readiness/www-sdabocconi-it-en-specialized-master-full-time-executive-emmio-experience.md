## A world of perspectives: the value of diversity

Diversity at SDA Bocconi is more than a value—it’s the driving force behind a transformative learning experience. Our classrooms bring together bright minds from across the globe, each contributing their unique perspectives, backgrounds, and expertise. This exchange fosters innovation, broadens horizons, and prepares our participants to navigate the complexities of a global business environment.

As part of this commitment, our [**Class profile**](https://www.sdabocconi.it/en/specialized-master-full-time-executive/emmio/class-profile/) reflects an extraordinary mix of nationalities, industries, and professional experiences, ensuring that every discussion is enriched by a variety of viewpoints. But we don’t stop there.

**International Student Care** is our way of ensuring that every participant, no matter where they’re from, feels at home at SDA Bocconi. Moving to a new country is exciting, but we know it comes with challenges. That’s why **Alice Dealessi** and **Valentina Grassi** are here to support you every step of the way, from visas and permits to stay, to cultural integration and daily life tips.

Beyond this, our **Program Management team** plays a fundamental role in ensuring that every student is guided and supported throughout their on-campus journey. Acting as dedicated points of reference, our Program Managers provide personalized assistance, fostering an environment where students feel heard, valued, and cared for. We’re not just shaping your education - we’re shaping an experience that feels like home.

Need more details? Reach out to Alice and Valentina at [studentcare@sdabocconi.it](mailto:studentcare@sdabocconi.it)

## Inside-out learning: an experience without borders

At SDA Bocconi, learning extends far beyond the classroom. We believe that the most impactful experiences happen when knowledge is applied, connections are made, and perspectives are broadened. That’s why our **extracurricular activities** are designed to bring the business world to life and help you build lasting relationships along the way.

The **architecture of our campus** also reflects this philosophy. Designed with **transparency, accessibility, and openness** in mind, our buildings feature vast glass facades, open spaces, and seamless indoor-outdoor integration. This unique structure symbolizes our commitment to breaking down barriers, creating a continuous exchange of ideas and knowledge that extends far beyond the classroom.

## Alumni network: your forever community

Graduation is not the end of your SDA Bocconi journey—it’s just the beginning of a lifelong connection. As part of our global Alumni Community, you’ll have continuous access to networking opportunities, exclusive events and a thriving professional ecosystem that supports you at every stage of your career.

Through the [Bocconi Alumni Community (BAC)](https://www.bocconialumni.it/), you can engage with a network of industry leaders, participate in regional chapter events, and benefit from mentorship opportunities. Our global Alumni Chapters play a key role in spreading the word and values of SDA Bocconi worldwide, fostering connections and creating opportunities in major business hubs across the globe. Stay connected, leverage your SDA Bocconi experience, and continue to be part of a community that extends far beyond the classroom.

**EMMIO Alumni Reunion 2025**

**INNOVATING MULTILATERALISM: TRUST, TRANSFORMATION AND DIPLOMACY**

The 2025 edition of the EMMIO Alumni Reunion explored the shifting dynamics of international cooperation in an increasingly complex global environment. Participants had the opportunity to engage in timely discussions on nuclear diplomacy, global risks, digital transformation, and innovative approaches to resource mobilization.

Highlights included the keynote address by **Rafael Mariano Grossi, Director General of the IAEA**, who emphasized the role of diplomacy and trust in navigating nuclear challenges. The agenda also featured expert-led panels and interactive group activities that encouraged reflection on the future of multilateralism. The event fostered meaningful exchange among alumni and practitioners, reaffirming the EMMIO community’s commitment to shaping responsive and resilient international institutions.

**EMMIO Alumni Reunion 2023**

**EMERGING DIGITAL TECHNOLOGIES FOR INTERNATIONAL ORGANIZATIONS**

![Alumni Reunion 2023](https://www.sdabocconi.it/_next/image/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fsda-website-prod-sdawebsiteprodbucketpublic693d05b-w3mwb4fo9x9p.s3.eu-south-1.amazonaws.com%2Fs3fs-public%2Finline-images%2Femmio_event_2024.jpg&w=1080&q=90&dpl=dpl_EgyAUeGYW1L1ERS2WGuDkM3HLa8o)

The reunion’s theme delved into the realms of technology that significantly impact the landscape of international organizations. Participants were provided with a valuable opportunity to grasp evolving trends and applications, emphasizing how these advancements can be harnessed positively to address global challenges.

The event featured an intriguing talk titled “Space Solutions to Achieve the 2030 Agenda” by Simonetta di Pippo, Director of the Space Economy Evolution Lab and a Professor at SDA Bocconi. She discussed the importance of cooperation in space and highlighted the role of cross-cultural interactions within the space domain as a platform for fostering peace and diplomacy.

The presentation not only enriched participants’ understanding of space-related solutions but also underscored the broader implications of collaborative efforts in addressing the goals set for 2030.
