# Faculty

EMMS professors are **experts in the fields of marketing and sales**, combining rigorous academic research with deep industry expertise. They bring **real-world insights directly into the classroom** through cutting-edge teaching methods, **interactive sessions and practical experience**. Beyond academia, our faculty actively engage in industry through **consultancy and executive roles**, ensuring that every lesson is relevant, impactful, and immediately applicable. The program’s professors carry out **non-academic work** in their respective fields and have significant professional experience in areas related to the subjects addressed in the program.

- **Research**, challenging boundaries and exploring new ideas
- **Publishing**, sharing influential insights widely
- **Teaching**, preparing the next generation of marketing and sales leaders
- **Consultancy**, continuously validating theories in real-world contexts.
