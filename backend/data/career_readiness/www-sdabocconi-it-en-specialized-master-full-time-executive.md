# Specialized masters

## Let’s master the future

SDA Bocconi’s Specialized Master programs offer a personalized learning journey where you can nurture your ambitions and drive meaningful change.

Designed for young professionals – in the full-time format - and experienced managers – in the executive/part time format - our programs, offered in English or Italian, provide comprehensive managerial training with focused insights into specialized topics and industries, fostering leadership and personal transformation in a dynamic environment.

Lead change with competence and passion. Choose the program that best fits your growth path.
