# Public Management

Public institutions are navigating a period of unprecedented complexity, where renewal and innovation are essential. Senior executives, public managers, and professionals need advanced leadership and management capabilities to steer change and deliver long-lasting value to society. Drawing on more than 50 years of experience, the School collaborates with national and international institutions to design impactful, practice-oriented solutions.

In this section, you will find the School’s full educational portfolio dedicated to this topic, along with events, news, research activities, and in-depth content.
