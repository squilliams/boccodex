Bocconi&Jobs was held on 11 April 2017, where 129 companies — both Italian and non-Italian — met with Bocconi students, alumni and Specialized Master graduates. The event is an “extraordinary occasion in which we seek to create a market between supply and demand,” as described by former rector, Gianmario Verona, to Buongiorno Regione Lombardia. It took place as the university launched its first Specialized Master program in Law of Internet Technology, which focuses on “intellectual property rights and copyright up to e-commerce without forfeiting privacy and fundamental freedoms on the Internet,” as explained by former coordinator, Gabriele Gagliani.
