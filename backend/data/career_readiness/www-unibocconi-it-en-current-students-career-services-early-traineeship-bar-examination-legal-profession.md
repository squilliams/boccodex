# Early traineeship for Bar Examination (legal profession)

**As of 20 February 2018, during the final year of**

**studies**, it is possible **to carry out 6 of the total of 18 months**

**of training** prescribed by the aw to access the **Bar Exam**.

**REQUIREMENTS FOR ADMISSION TO**

**THE ADVANCE TRAINEESHIP**

Students meeting

the following requirements may be admitted to the advance traineeship:

- enrollment

in the fifth year (not an out-of-program year) of the Integrated Master of Arts

Degree in Law;
- possession

of credits in the following disciplinary sectors: Private Law (IUS/01), Civil

Procedural Law (IUS/15), Criminal Law (IUS/17), Criminal Procedural Law

(IUS/16), Administrative Law (IUS/10), Constitutional Law (IUS/08), European

Union Law (IUS 14);
- Possession

and registration in curriculum of studies of the credits for all 1st,

2nd and 3rd year instructional activities.

Moreover:

- for

students not having effected a period of study abroad in the 4th

year: possession and registration in curriculum of studies of the credits for

all 4th year instructional activities.
- for

students having effected a “lengthy” period of study abroad (at least one term)

in the 4th year: possession and registration in curriculum of

studies of at least 60 credits for all 4th or 5th year

instructional activities.

**CONDITIONS OF VALIDITY**

In order for the

advance traineeship to be valid, the following conditions must be met:

- obtaining

of initial authorization;
- duration

of at least 6 calendar months, and of at least 288 hours, with the student’s

attendance at a minimum of 12 court hearings. If the student graduates before the end of such 6

month period, the            advance

traineeship will be valid if it has been of a duration of at least 5 months

and, in any event, of at least 288 hours;
- obtaining

of final validation;
- graduation

not later than 12 months from the end of the advance traineeship;
- confirmation

of recording in the register of trainee attorneys within ninety days from

graduation (the degree certificate or an equivalent document must be presented

to the Bar association – _Ordine di_

_avvocati_).

Furthermore, the traineeship must always be [formalized as an internship](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/services/activation-through-bocconi-documents).

All detailed information on the rules and procedures

to be followed for the advance traineeship (admission requirements, conditions,

roles, responsibilities, procedures for applying for and formalizing the

traineeship, documentation to be presented to the Bar association for

authorization and requirements for validation) is provided on the site pages

dedicated to the [Bar Exam](https://www.unibocconi.it/it/corsi-di-studio/esami-di-stato/esame-avvocato-tirocinio-anticipato) _(only in Italian)_.
