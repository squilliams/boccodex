| **SUSPENSION** |
| **When** | Subject to agreement with the University, a period of temporary suspension of the experience (e.g. for company closures or vacations) is permitted.<br>Recovery of the originally planned duration<br>ŌĆö **curricular internship for credits**: suspensions must in any case be recovered by the scheduled end of the internship through extension, if they affect the internshipŌĆÖs minimum duration.<br>ŌĆö **curricular without credits or extracurricular internship**: recovery is to be effected at the discretion of the employer.<br>In the event that the intern must be absent in order to take an **exam**, the day thus lost **need not be recovered**. |
| **How long** | The sum of suspensions must not exceed one month in the course of a given internship. |
| **How** | The employer | Enters the suspension period on the JobGate portal. |
| The Internship Office | Verifies the reasons and confirms the extension, making it effective. |
| You | In the case of a credited curricular internship, verify that the suspension has not reduced the duration of the internship to less than the minimum required for recognition. |

|     |     |     |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **WITHDRAWAL** |
| **When** | Prior to beginning of the internship.<br>Unless there is proper justification (grave reasons), withdrawal entails a **penalty** that precludes activation of any new internship for two weeks. |
| **How** | You | Enter the withdrawal and the reasons for it on JobGate in the "My internships" area, "Changes in duration" section in the yoU@B Agenda. |
| The Internship Office | Verifies the reasons and confirms the withdrawal, making it effective.<br>If there are no grave reasons justifying the withdrawal, activates the blockage precluding activation of any new internship for two weeks (penalty). |

|     |     |     |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **INTERRUPTION** |
| **When** | During the internship.<br>Unless there is proper justification (grave reasons), interruption entails a **penalty** that precludes activation of any new internship for two weeks.<br>Moreover, the interruption of a curricular internship for credit may result in the non-recognition of the experience in the study plan, even if the minimum required duration is reached. |
| **How** | The employer | Enters the interruption date and fill in the "motivation" box, on the JobGate portal. |
| You | Confirm the interruption on JobGate in the "My internships" area, "Changes in duration" section in the yoU@B Agenda.<br>In the case of a curricular internship for credits, verify that the interruption has not reduced the duration of the internship to less than the minimum required for recognition. |
