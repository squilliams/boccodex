ABOUT US\\
\\
The AXA Research Lab on Gender Equality, funded with the contribution of AXA Research Fund and AXA Italy, promotes research in social sciences related to gender. We carry out theoretical and empirical high-quality research with a strong policy impact. Research includes all areas of gender economics, gender policies, and gender diversity management. The Lab aims to promote gender equality in the economic and social spheres through rigorous scientific methodologies.](https://genderlab.unibocconi.eu/about-us)
