# Rankings & accreditation

## Rankings: a signal of value

International rankings are one of the ways business schools are assessed and compared globally. They offer a useful perspective across multiple dimensions and are best understood through a multi-year view rather than as a single-year result. For everyone who chooses SDA Bocconi, they are a meaningful signal of the strength and consistency of our academic model over time.

Studying at a globally recognized institution means entering a vibrant learning environment, accessing an international network, and unlocking long-term opportunities for current students and alumni alike.

Academic rigor, relevance of research, and innovation are among the driving forces behind our impact.

## Accreditations: a mark of excellence

SDA Bocconi is among a select group of business schools worldwide to hold the prestigious “triple crown” – the three most important international accreditations: AACSB, EQUIS, and AMBA.

As the first school in Italy to achieve this distinction, our programs are also certified by other global institutions, placing us among the most accredited schools in Europe.

![AACSB](https://www.sdabocconi.it/_next/image/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fsda-website-prod-sdawebsiteprodbucketpublic693d05b-w3mwb4fo9x9p.s3.eu-south-1.amazonaws.com%2Fs3fs-public%2F2025-04%2Faacsb.jpg%3Fv%3D1744202336&w=384&q=90&dpl=dpl_EgyAUeGYW1L1ERS2WGuDkM3HLa8o)

### AACSB

[**International - The Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business**](http://www.aacsb.edu/)

Only 5% of business schools worldwide have earned the prestigious AACSB accreditation. SDA Bocconi is proud to be one of them.

![EQUIS](https://www.sdabocconi.it/_next/image/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fsda-website-prod-sdawebsiteprodbucketpublic693d05b-w3mwb4fo9x9p.s3.eu-south-1.amazonaws.com%2Fs3fs-public%2F2025-04%2Fefmd.jpg%3Fv%3D1744203006&w=384&q=90&dpl=dpl_EgyAUeGYW1L1ERS2WGuDkM3HLa8o)

### EQUIS

[**European Quality Improvement System**](https://efmdglobal.org/accreditations/business-schools/equis/)

In 1998, SDA Bocconi was the first school in Italy to be awarded this prestigious accreditation, and is currently the only school to have been re-accredited five consecutive times.
