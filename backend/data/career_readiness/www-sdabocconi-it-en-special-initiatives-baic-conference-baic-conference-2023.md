# BAIC Conference 2023

Technological Challenges to Established Orders

14-15th December 2023

[Program 2023](https://sda-website-prod-sdawebsiteprodbucketpublic693d05b-w3mwb4fo9x9p.s3.eu-south-1.amazonaws.com/s3fs-public/file/agenda_baic_14_dicembre_2023.pdf)

## About: The Third Bocconi Assembly for Innovation and Cooperation

Technological Challenges to Established Orders

In the face of geopolitical changes, societal and environmental concerns, shifting industry boundaries, evolving technological ecosystems, increasing uncertainty, and competitive pressures, the aim of the Assembly is to serve as a forum for discussing and exchanging new ideas and developing a research agenda on innovation and cooperation. A changing reality demands new insights. With speeches and panels involving leading scholars in the field of management, competitive paper sessions, and open assembly deliberation, we aspire to advance this agenda and promote research projects that facilitate our understanding of these two fundamental drivers of economic growth and prosperity.

The Assembly will take place on December 14th and 15th 2023, on the new campus of Bocconi University, a leading European institution committed to generating knowledge that matters and delivering cutting-edge business research. SDA Bocconi School of Management will host the Conference on its brand new Campus, an innovative urban space, a meeting point for excellence in research, education, and architecture.

We gratefully acknowledge the support to BAIC of Romeo and Enrica Invernizzi Foundation, devoted to inspiring the future of basic research, fostering progress, contamination, and innovation and contribute to social progress.

## ICRIOS

Funded and supported by a donation from Milan’s Romeo e Enrica Invernizzi Foundation, ICRIOS is a research center at Bocconi University created to be a reference point for cutting-edge, multi-disciplinary and collaborative research on innovation, organization, strategy and entrepreneurship, with the explicit objective to influence in a significant way both the academic debate and management practice.

ICRIOS promotes collaboration and knowledge sharing at any stage of the research process, especially among scholars in diverse and complementary fields, based across nations, and working in different research traditions. ICRIOS fellows are selected among recognized experts in their field of study.
