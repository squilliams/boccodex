# In-Company Training abroad Dubai (On-site)

Career Services is pleased to inform you that we have set up an In-Company Training in Dubai, in collaboration with AFC, Pemo, Silkhaus, and Moody's.

BCG, Whiteshield, and Chalhoub (TBD).

The participating Companies' list is not final and will be updated regularly

The program will include:

- Company presentations
- Workshops / Panels with Junior and Senior professionals
- Q&A sessions and career opportunities

The students will spend half a day and/or a full day in each of the companies previously mentioned.

The event will be held **on-site in Dubai from March 6th to March 10th, 2023.**

Participants have to reach Dubai on their own.

Accommodation expenses will be covered by University.

Travel expenses shall be borne by participants, who are responsible for their own travel arrangements.

Participants are also responsible for the following costs:

- Visa fees to enter the country of destination (when required)

- Health insurance (when required)

- Meals and extra costs (when not included)

**Application details:**

The program is dedicated to 16 students with a strong interest in **Dubai job market**, fluent in English, curious, and open-minded.

Eligibility:

- 1st and 2nd-year students that will be regularly enrolled in a Bocconi Master of Science Degree Course in the a.y. 2022-2023

- 2nd-year students that are regularly enrolled in a Bocconi Master of Science Degree Course in the a.y. 2021-2022, graduating in March 2023
- 3rd year students that will be regularly enrolled in a Bocconi Bachelor Degree Course in the a.y. 2022-2023.
- World Bachelor in Business students regularly enrolled in their 3rd and 4th year in the a.y. 2022/23

GPA: minimum 22/30 (average weighted on credits).

**Language requirements:**

Applicants whose native tongue is not English have to prove a good knowledge of the English language through one of the following options:

- Enrolment in a program taught in English

- Bocconi English exam of Level C1

- Other certificates accepted by Bocconi Language Center at the C1 level (for the list of the certificates accepted and correspondent level, refer to the [table](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/services/language-certification-recognition-2))

Applications will open on Wednesday, 08 February 2023 at 01:00 pm (CET)

The **deadline** for applications is on **Sunday, 12 February 2023 at 11:59 pm (CET)**

**To Apply:**

**You@B > Punto Blu > Menù > In-Company Training:**

- Upload your CV with a picture in English in PDF format.

**You@B > Career Services > Events Calendar > on March 6 > In-Company Training abroad Dubai:**

- Record a short video interview (you will find all the info on yoU@B)

Incomplete applications will not be considered.

The results of the selection will be communicated via e-mail by Friday, 17 February 2023.

_**Students' responsibility**_

Before applying, please note that it is the students' responsibility to:

- Verify personally, with the authorities of the host country, the suitable procedures for obtaining the appropriate visa, if required, and any other necessary documentation in full compliance with local laws. All formalities relating to visas and other documents required to enter the country are the responsibility of the student.

- Read the “Bocconi Students Travel Risk Policy” (available on yoU@B), and comply with the risk mitigation measures suggested.

- Access, through yoU@B diary, the Travel Risk Awareness Elearning, and the International SOS Member’s portal, which includes Country Guides and Travel Security and Medical Risk Ratings.

- Check and follow all the travel recommendations of your Country’s Ministry for Foreign Affairs and International SOS.

- Check all the Covid-19 Procedures to enter the host country and re-enter your home country.

Please, be also aware that Bocconi University reserves the right to cancel the program if exceptional circumstances occur before or during the carrying out of the program.

Travel and health risks can rapidly change and lead Bocconi University and hosting Institutions/Companies or Countries to increase travel and health restrictions. In any case, Bocconi University does not guarantee that the In-Company Training will take place if the general conditions do not safeguard the well-being and safety of students.
