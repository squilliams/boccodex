SDA Bocconi provides scholarships and tuition waivers for EMMAP applications through the publication of competition notices. Special payment terms are awarded on the basis of merit criteria and any additional forms of recognition that are expressed in the notice. Competition notices, if available, are posted on this page.

The tuition waiver/scholarship request can also be submitted before the conclusion of the master selection process.

If you meet more than one requirement among those listed in the notices, you can submit multiple requests for tuition waivers/scholarships.

In addition, the School also participates in notices proposed by public administrations for scholarships/tuition waivers for EMMAP applicants.

**To apply for a tuition waiver, you must:**

- Have completed and submitted the Application for Admission to EMMAP, with all required documents
- Submit the application in response to the notice, attaching the specified documentation, through the Application portal
- Meet the deadlines and carefully check the requirements, without which the application cannot be accepted

If at the publication of a notice the online application has already submitted, the candidate may access it again and make the tuition waiver request respecting the deadline stipulated in the notice.

**Applications will be evaluated on the basis of:**

- Academic and professional curriculum
- Motivation letter
- Requirements stated in the notice
- Selection test results at EMMAP

The allocation of waivers will take place during the selection process, by the dates indicated in the announcement. It is possible to apply even before the conclusion of the selection process.

Admission to EMMAP is independent of any waiver or scholarship award.

### Tuition waivers EMMAP 2026

**SDA Bocconi offers tuition fee reductions for EMMAP in the form of partial exemptions, for a total value of €185,000,** with the goal of attracting outstanding candidates whose background, professional experience, and/or personal profile will contribute to the unique learning experience of the EMMAP program at SDA Bocconi.

Applications must be submitted within the application phase and according to the rounds indicated in the specific calls for applications. The exemption allocation process is structured in several phases (Rounds), corresponding to the deadlines established in each call.

Submitting your admission application early increases your chances of receiving a fee waiver.

On average, the waivers cover around 40% of the tuition fee. Selected candidates may receive a waiver of up to 70% of the EMMAP tuition fee.

**We invite applicants to carefully read the eligibility requirements and participation procedures outlined in the official call for applications.**

## Loans and funding

SDA Bocconi assists candidates and applicants in their search for funding by providing the necessary documentation to finalize applications for financial support. For more information you can consult the section on funding on the Bocconi University website (for [Italian](https://www.unibocconi.it/it/studenti-iscritti/agevolazioni/finanziamenti-master-universitari-e-sda), for [international applicants](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/student-loans)). The application for funding must be received by the School by the end of the month following the course start date.
