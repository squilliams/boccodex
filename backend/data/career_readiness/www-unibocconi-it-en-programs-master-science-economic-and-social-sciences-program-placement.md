#### Graduate Job Statistics

#### One year after graduation

- _**89.9%** of graduates non continuing their studies are employed_
- _**55%** of employed graduates work abroad_
- _**43%** of our graduates are continuing their studies or are employed in Higher Education Institutions_

Data refers to a.y. 2022-2023 graduates of the English class group

#### On graduation day

- _**68.7%** of graduates are employed_
- _**64.1%** of employed graduates work abroad_
- _They have been called to an average of **3.9** job interviews_
- _They have received on average **1.5** job offers_

Survey - graduation sessions a.y. 2023-2024
