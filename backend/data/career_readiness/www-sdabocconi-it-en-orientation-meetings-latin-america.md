# Orientation meetings - Latin America

Ready to enjoy a new vision of your career? Eager to dive into an enriching and transformative learning experience at one of Europe's premier Business Schools?

**Lima** 03 February 2026

**Santiago De Chile** 05 February 2026

**Bogotá** 08 February 2026

Explore SDA Bocconi's [MBA](https://www.sdabocconi.it/en/mba-executive-mba/) and [Master](https://www.sdabocconi.it/en/specialized-master-full-time-executive/) Programs with us and request a personalized meeting with our recruiters. Simply select the date and location that work best for you and register to book your **30-minute one-on-one session**. Once you register, we will follow up to confirm the exact meeting time. Alternatively, we can arrange an online chat if that is more convenient for you.

_Please note: Due to high demand, the number of requests may exceed the available meeting slots. Consequently, some candidates may be placed on a waiting list, and a remote chat may be arranged as an alternative._

For any questions, please get in contact with **Fabrice Charlot**: [fabrice.charlot@sdabocconi.it](mailto:fabrice.charlot@sdabocconi.it)
