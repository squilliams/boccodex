# FEL - Financial Education Lab

FEL - Financial Education Lab aims to promote financial education, in coordination with existing private and public initiatives. It seeks to involve various communities (schools, companies, associations and Bocconi students) in the creation of easily accessible dissemination materials for the general public according to different needs.

An important goal of the Financial Education Lab is to support schools in financial education by facilitating faculty training and empowering them to provide financial literacy content independently.
