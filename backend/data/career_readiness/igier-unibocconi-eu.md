About IGIER

Launched in 1990 as a joint initiative of Bocconi University, CEPR, and NBER, today IGIER is a dynamic and international research environment that fosters a community of scholars and a wide array of initiatives with the overarching goal of inspiring, nurturing, and disseminating rigorous empirical and theoretical research in all fields of economics.
