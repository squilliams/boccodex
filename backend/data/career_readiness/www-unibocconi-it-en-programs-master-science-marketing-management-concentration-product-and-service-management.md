# Concentration in Product and Service Management

Explore the Program

[Overview](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/master-science/marketing-management) [Study Plan](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/master-science/marketing-management/study-plan) [Study Abroad Programs](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/master-science/marketing-management/study-abroad-programs) [Program Placement](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/master-science/marketing-management/program-placement)

The concentration structure includes:

a. 2 courses chosen from among the following:

- INDUSTRIAL MARKETING, in English
- INTERNATIONAL MARKETING, in Italian and in English
- MARKETING DEI SERVIZI, in Italian
- PRICING ANALYTICS, in English

b. 1 course chosen from among the following:

- ADVERTISING MANAGEMENT, in Italian
- MARKETING ESPERIENZIALE, in Italian
- TRADE MARKETING AND CATEGORY MANAGEMENT, in Italian
- SALES MANAGEMENT, in English
- SOCIAL MEDIA MARKETING, in English
- INNOVATIVE RETAIL DESIGN, in English
- EVENT AND MEGA EVENT MANAGEMENT AND CREATIVE INDUSTRIES, in English
- VALUE CHAINS AND BUSINESS MODELS IN FASHION & LUXURY, in English
- PROJECT MANAGEMENT, in English
- SEMIOTICA APPLICATA AL MARKETING, in Italian
- SUPPLY CHAIN MANAGEMENT, in English
- MANAGERIAL ISSUES IN MADE IN ITALY INDUSTRIES, in English
- MANAGEMENT OF FASHION AND LIFESTYLE COMPANIES, in English
- PREDICTIVE AND GENERATIVE AI FOR BUSINESS DECISIONS, in English
- SPORTS BUSINESS AND MANAGEMENT, in English
- DATA ANALYSIS FOR MANAGERIAL DECISION MAKING, in English
- COLLABORATIVE STRATEGIES FOR INNOVATION, in English
- INNOVATION IN THE SHARING ECONOMY: THE ROLE OF PLATFORMS AND BUSINESS MODELS, in English
- LUXURY COMPANIES IN THE GLOBAL LANDSCAPE, in English
- DIGITAL COMMUNICATION, in English
- ECOMMERCE, in English
- SUSTAINABLE COMMUNICATION AND RETAILING, in English

c. 1 course freely chosen from among the [offered electives](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/master-science/marketing-management/electives).

Please note that the list might be subject to changes
