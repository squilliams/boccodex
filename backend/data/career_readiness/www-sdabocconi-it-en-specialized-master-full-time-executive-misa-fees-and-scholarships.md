# Fees and scholarships

**A challenging but high-return investment**

Participation in the MISA program requires a substantial financial commitment. However, it is a strategic investment that delivers high future returns—both personally and professionally.

## Fees and expenses

The tuition fee for the MISA 2027 is €30,000 (outside the scope of VAT), payable in four installments

- First installment

**€ 8,000**

upon acceptance of admission

- Second installment

**€ 8,000**

by 26 March 2027

- Third installment

**€ 7,000**

by 28 May 2027

- Fourth installment

**€7.000**

by 30 July 2027

The fee includes:

  - Enrollment in the Master's Program;
  - All teaching materials;
  - Use of the infrastructure and services of SDA Bocconi.

Participants must provide their own laptop.

## Scholarships and tuition waivers

_1st round_
23 Apr 2026

_2nd round_
15 Jul 2026

_3rd round_
12 Nov 2026

To apply for a waiver you must:

- have completed and submitted the MISA Application for Admission, with all required documents;
- submit the application in response to the notice, attaching the indicated documents, through the Application for Admission portal;
- meet the deadlines and carefully check the requirements, without which the application cannot be accepted.

Applications will be evaluated on the basis of:

- academic and professional resume;
- letter of motivation;
- requirements stated in the announcement;
- selection test results at MISA.

The tuition waivers will be awarded during the selection process, by the dates indicated in the announcement. It is possible to apply even before the conclusion of the selection process.

Admission to MISA is independent of any waiver or scholarship award.

## Loans and funding

SDA Bocconi assists candidates and applicants in their search for funding by providing the necessary documentation to finalize applications for financial support. For more information you can consult the funding section of the Bocconi University website (for [Italian](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/student-loans) and for [international applicants](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/student-loans)). The application for funding must be received by the School by the end of the month following the course start date.

The University also makes information available to international students regarding possible sources of funding in different countries of the world.
