# MIHMEP: a crossroads of talent

MIHMEP graduates have gone on to rewarding careers in many healthcare fields. The main recruiting companies are public and private healthcare organizations, consulting firms, private companies, companies from the bio-pharmaceutical and medical device sectors, universities and research centers, Ministries of Health, and international organizations.

## Develop the 5 fundamental skills for your professional growth

Designed by SDA Bocconi’s Career Development Center in collaboration with the program Director, the Career Curriculum is a learning path that helps you develop the 5 fundamental skills needed for your professional growth. It includes a variety of activities, at individual and class levels, scheduled during the academic year.

You will acquire concrete tools to equip yourself with skills and competencies to enhance your professional development to the fullest.
