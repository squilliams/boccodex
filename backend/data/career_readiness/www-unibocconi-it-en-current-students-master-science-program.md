# Master of Science Program

In this section, necessary information is provided for students enrolled in an Graduate program. Included is information regarding the Graduate School's educational offer as well as the wide variety of services that accompany students throughout their Graduate careers.

- [**University Guide 2025-26**](https://didattica.unibocconi.eu/tsg/guide.php?volume=R2&edizione=2026&Invia=ENTRA)
- [**Courses Offered in Academic Programs 2025-26**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/courses-offered-academic-programs-2025-2026-ay)
