About us

The Bayesian Learning Laboratory (BayesLab) is a research unit of the Bocconi Institute for Data Science and Analytics (BIDSA) that aims to foster and develop modern and innovative research in Bayesian Statistics with emphasis on Machine Learning and Nonparametrics.
