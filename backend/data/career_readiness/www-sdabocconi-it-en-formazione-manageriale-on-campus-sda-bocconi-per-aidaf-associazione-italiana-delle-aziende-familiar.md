# SDA Bocconi per AIdAF - Associazione Italiana delle Aziende Familiari

The executive education programs of SDA Bocconi support business leaders by providing tools and knowledge on key topics to address today’s challenges and build the future.

![governance-program-per-imprese-familiari-e-non-quotate.jpg](https://www.sdabocconi.it/_next/image/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fsda-website-prod-sdawebsiteprodbucketpublic693d05b-w3mwb4fo9x9p.s3.eu-south-1.amazonaws.com%2Fs3fs-public%2F2025-03%2Fgovernance-program-per-imprese-familiari-e-non-quotate.jpg&w=3840&q=90&dpl=dpl_EgyAUeGYW1L1ERS2WGuDkM3HLa8o)

Thanks to the agreement between SDA Bocconi School of Management and AIdAF, SDA Bocconi is committed to offering AIdAF members the following benefits:

- **15% discount** on the individual participation fee for each member, by entering the code “AIdAF-152026” in the notes field at the time of registration;
- **20% discount**, if an AIdAF member company enrolls **three or more participants** in the same course;
- **20% discount** for all women enrolling in SDA Bocconi executive education programs, valid until **March 8, 2027**.

The above discounts cannot be combined.

Enrollment in the programs must be completed through the respective course web pages.
