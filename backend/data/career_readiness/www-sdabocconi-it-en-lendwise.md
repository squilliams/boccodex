# Lendwise

Students who have been admitted into one the following programs: MBA, EMBA, GEMBA, MAMA and MCF are eligible to apply for a Lendwise loan.

![MBA](https://www.sdabocconi.it/_next/image/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fsda-website-prod-sdawebsiteprodbucketpublic693d05b-w3mwb4fo9x9p.s3.eu-south-1.amazonaws.com%2Fs3fs-public%2F2024-11%2Fhero-mba_0.jpg&w=3840&q=90&dpl=dpl_EgyAUeGYW1L1ERS2WGuDkM3HLa8o)

Students are eligible for a loan only if they reside outside Italy at the time of application. Any application for a loan made from within the Italian territory and/or by students currently residing in Italy will be rejected by Lendwise.

Interest rates are personalized to the applicants’ profile and are fixed for the entire duration of the loan. In addition, there are no fees for making early repayments or settling your loan early. See more details about the terms and conditions, and how to apply for a loan on [Lendwise’s website](https://cta.lendwise.com/bocconi/apply-now).

Main features of Lendwise loans:

- Competitive and fixed interest rate
- Loans can cover tuition fees and living costs, although amounts will be adjusted according to the profile of each individual applicant. Please note that loans cannot cover the deposit or first instalment paid by the student upon enrollment
- No collateral or guarantees required
- Easy online process
- No payments during studies for those enrolled in a full-time course
- 10 years repayment period

Apply directly through [Lendwise’s website](https://cta.lendwise.com/bocconi/apply-now).

For further information please refer to the FAQ section on Lendwise’s website.
