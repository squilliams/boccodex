# Connections

![](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/media/images/ragazzo-online.jpg)

Check Out Our Quiz Game!

Take a look at new topics, see what sparks your interest and maybe even get a different perspective.

**Connections** will help you open new paths and build links to your future!

The Game

Connections is an **online game** created by Bocconi University **f** **or high school students** worldwide.

As you browse the main subjects taught in our BSc programs, you can stop and get a taste of what they offer.

It’s a great way to **figure out which program is the one for you**, and you might be surprised at what grabs your attention!

It’s **very simple**. Move freely around the map and take a break at the stop that interests you most.

A click will show you a problem to solve that is specific to that topic. Try to figure it out, then move on to try as many others as you like.

Just hop on board and enjoy your trip towards the Bocconi program that suits you best.
