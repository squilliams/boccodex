# General management and strategy

Being a manager today means developing leadership skills while also guiding a team and steering the organization. The challenge lies in building companies that are both economically strong and fair, transforming a vision into a concrete strategy that ensures sustainable economic growth.

In this section, you will find the School’s full educational portfolio dedicated to this topic, along with events, news, research activities, and in-depth content.
