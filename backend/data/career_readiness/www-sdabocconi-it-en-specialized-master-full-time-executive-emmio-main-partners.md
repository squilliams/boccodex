### AHRMIO

SDA Bocconi is a proud academic partner of the Association for Human Resources Management in International Organizations (AHRMIO); the association is dedicated to furthering the management of people and human talent in not-for-profit international organizations in the governmental and non-governmental sectors. It does this through the generation and sharing of knowledge, the provision of professional development opportunities, and the organization of events to discuss and advance the state-of-the-practice in HR management.

### Global Food Security Cluster

SDA Bocconi is a proud partner of the Global Food Security Cluster (GFSC), established to coordinate the food security response during a humanitarian crisis, addressing issues of food availability, access, and utilization. The GFSC is an innovative inter-agency, across-the-board mechanism continuously experimenting with innovative ways to boost preparedness, fight vulnerability of national systems, increase humanitarian assistance effectiveness, and increase UN and non-UN cooperation. The EMMIO participates in the intense knowledge exchange and generation of the cluster, and brings the valuable lessons it learned to the classroom.

### Career Development Roundtable

SDA Bocconi is proud to partner with the CDR to develop original content and to advance knowledge on human resources management, aiming to contribute to continuously improve management practices in international organizations.

The CDR does so by creating a network dedicated to sharing best practices, and generating and spreading knowledge on human capital topics and issues.

### Global Careers Fair
