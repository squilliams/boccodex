# Funding

#### **Bocconi4Access to Education a.y. 2026-27**

Bocconi has always been committed to making higher education available to all motivated and talented students through a financial aid system, based on both merit and need. The " **Bocconi4Access to Education**" support scheme aims at promoting access to a plural environment, to an open and accessible university for students who share progress, plurality and inclusion as fundamental values for their personal, cultural and professional growth.

Opportunities may vary from year to year based in part on the funds made available by donors to the University.

Please find below the **funding opportunities available for a.y. 2026-27**.

These opportunities offer a different kind of support to families and, in most cases, take into consideration the economic and asset situation of students' families.

Each opportunity consists of a full or partial tuition waiver, based on the student’s and family's economic needs. We suggest that each family, who considers it appropriate, applies through the single “ **Bocconi4Access to Education a.y. 2026-27**” application.

All applications will be evaluated, and benefits will be allocated until all the available budget has been exhausted.

Please visit our dedicated [webpage](https://www.unibocconi.it/wps/wcm/connect/bocconi/sitopubblico_it/albero+di+navigazione/home/corsi+di+studio/lauree+triennali/agevolazioni/agevolazioni_prospective_hp) if you are an Italian candidate.

Bocconi Access to Education Scholarships

Bocconi aims to support deserving students who are willing to invest in their future, even in challenging and potentially constraining economic family conditions.

These funding opportunities guarantee a wide coverage of the student's needs, depending on the circumstances:

- [100% Full tuition waiver](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/bachelor-and-law-programs/funding/100-full-tuition-waivers-ay-2026-27 "100% Full tuition waivers a.y. 2026-27")
- [80% Partial tuition waiver](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/bachelor-and-law-programs/funding/80-60-partial-tuition-waivers-ay-2026-27 "80-60% Partial tuition waivers a.y. 2026-27")
- [60% Partial tuition waiver](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/bachelor-and-law-programs/funding/80-60-partial-tuition-waivers-ay-2026-27 "80-60% Partial tuition waivers a.y. 2026-27")

Boost Your Future Scholarships

Through these Scholarships, Bocconi supports students who, even with their family support, still require significant assistance in addition to their family's investment, that could enable them to pursue their studies without concerns:

- [40% Partial tuition waiver](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/bachelor-and-law-programs/funding/40-20-partial-tuition-waivers-ay-2026-27 "40-20% Partial tuition waivers a.y. 2026-27")
- [20% Partial tuition waiver](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/bachelor-and-law-programs/funding/40-20-partial-tuition-waivers-ay-2026-27 "40-20% Partial tuition waivers a.y. 2026-27")

Benefits foreseen for years subsequent to the first

Starting from the second academic year, a number of benefits will be made available exclusively to students enrolled in years subsequent to the first.

For details, please check our future publications in the ‘enrolled students’ area; below are some of the main possibilities offered so far:

- [**ISU Bocconi Scholarship application a.y. 2026-27**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/bachelor-and-law-programs/funding/introduction-isu-bocconi-scholarship-ay-2026-27-timeline "Introduction to ISU Bocconi Scholarship a.y. 2026-27 - Timeline");
- [**Excellence awards.**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/funding/funding-bachelor-science-and-law-programs/excellence-awards-ay-2025-26 "Excellence awards a.y. 2025-26") They refer to full tuition exemptions offered by Bocconi to the most deserving students.
- [**International mobility grant**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/funding/international-mobility-grant-ay-2025-26 "International mobility grant a.y. 2025-26"). Students who decide to undertake a mobility experience for study abroad will be considered for the assignment of the International mobility grant, which can be awarded thanks to a mix of resources between Erasmus+ funds, ministerial funds and Bocconi's own funds.

Tuition waivers based on specific student features

Tuition waivers for students with specific features, regardless of the family's financial situation:

- [Women award](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/bachelor-and-law-programs/funding/women-award-ay-2026-27 "Women Award A.Y. 2026-27")
- [Tuition waiver for students with disabilities equal or greater than 66%](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/bachelor-and-law-programs/funding/tuition-waiver-students-disabilities-equal-or-greater-66-ay-2026-27 "Tuition waiver for students with disabilities equal or greater than 66% - A.Y. 2026-27")
- [Partial tuition waiver for simultaneously enrolled siblings](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/bachelor-and-law-programs/funding/partial-tuition-waiver-simultaneously-enrolled-siblings-ay-2026-27 "Partial tuition waiver for simultaneously enrolled siblings - A.Y. 2026-27")
- [Support programs for student athletes](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/bachelor-and-law-programs/funding/bocconi-sport-scholarship-ay-2026-27 "Bocconi Sport Scholarship a.y. 2026-27").

Loans

- [Direct Loans for US citizens](https://www.unibocconi.it/wps/wcm/connect/bocconi/sitopubblico_en/navigation+tree/home/programs/current+students/services/funding/us+direct+loans_hp2%2803082020%29);
- [Funding by country](https://www.unibocconi.it/wps/wcm/connect/bocconi/sitopubblico_en/navigation+tree/home/programs/current+students/services/funding/bachelor+of+science+and+law+programs/loans+and+other+opportunities/funding+by+country/funding+by+country_hp).
