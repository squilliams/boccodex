## Overview

2 yrs

English

120 CFU

This **highly ranked program** allows students to **master multiple areas of finance**, from investment banking to risk management, from corporate finance to asset pricing and derivatives, from financial advisory to asset management. This – combined with a **thorough methodological preparation covering both traditional and innovative artificial intelligence techniques** – is the program’s competitive advantage when compared to the one-year graduate programs widely available.

The MSc in Finance is divided into two main tracks: the Finance Track and the Finance Global Experience Track. Each is intense and challenging, exposing participants to different international learning environments.

**Students must select the track of interest as a separate program choice when submitting the application for admission.**

The Master of Science in Finance at Bocconi:

- is regularly evaluated as **one of the best Finance Master programs** worldwide by the most prestigious international **rankings**
- provides a complete and high-quality education, ensuring a **solid multidisciplinary foundation and targeted specialized skills**
- relies on an outstanding **international faculty**, as well as “in-the-field” courses with **expert practitioners**
- takes advantage of the **international environment on campus**, with students and professors from various countries, and many opportunities to enrich the academic curriculum with **experiences abroad**
- offers access to study abroad programs at prestigious **partner schools all over the world**
- provides outstanding opportunities for **relations with top employers** in Italy and abroad
- benefits from the advantages that our location in the center of **Milan** provides, in terms of quality of life and **many professional opportunities**

Both tracks of the two-year program balance solid theoretical training and in-the-field application: with new computational labs, updated contents in various courses and a new Climate Finance Lab, you will be provided with all the skills required to meet the needs of today’s job market.

The program presents **many career opportunities** as managers, analysts and economists at banks, insurance companies and other financial institutions, as well as consultants in either corporate finance advisory or finance divisions of large consulting companies and financial authorities and regulators.

Program Director

### Anna Battauz

![](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/styles/scale_64x64/public/media/images/battauz_0.jpg.webp?itok=FDy0qInU)

The field of finance is constantly evolving and subject to continuous innovation. It is increasingly global and complex. Therefore, graduates with a solid, extensive and advanced skill set in all areas of finance can benefit from extraordinary prospects for employment and exceptional career opportunities at financial institutions, investment funds, private equity firms, fintech and insurance companies, corporations, consulting firms and government agencies.

Our program trains precisely this type of graduate. We prepare our students to embark on competitive, well-structured and fulfilling career paths. In the rigorous mandatory courses of the first year, we equip them with broad and robust theoretical knowledge and methodologies across all areas of finance. The second year offers a diverse selection of elective courses, a focus on personal skill development, and a variety of professional internship opportunities that directly facilitate their entry into the job market.

In summary, our program is intense and challenging, which is why it is highly valued by the most prestigious global recruiters.

Student Life

_Bocconi is about more than just classes_

Your years on campus are about more than just the classes you attend: Bocconi and Milan also promote activities focused on culture, sports, art and sharing ideas. They help make your Bocconi experience an exciting time that is key to personal and professional growth.

Our Campus

_A Sustainable Campus_

Our campus is a safe, multicultural and lively place, located in downtown Milan. Both its historic buildings and new spaces are integrated into the urban fabric to create an active environment, reverberating with university life. The campus includes a recreational sports center, a large park and a new student residence hall.

Living in Milan

_A European and cosmopolitan city_

Milan is an open, international and multicultural city, a European metropolis full of events and public services with an unparalleled nightlife. It is a renowned symbol of fashion and design, as well as an important financial, economic and business hub.
