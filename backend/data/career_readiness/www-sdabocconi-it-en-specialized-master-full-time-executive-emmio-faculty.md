# Faculty

**A long experience of international public management**

SDA Bocconi has invested in the EMMIO all the strength of its four-decade commitment for shaping public management reforms. The strength of its faculty lies in the extraordinary network that unites **internationally renowned** **scholars** with **senior managers of UN system organizations**, innovators, and change agents. In our programs, we integrate academic research and management practices to provide strong methodological and scientific training plans.

## Guest Speakers

The EMMIO leverages on the experience and added value of a number of guest lecturers among **senior practitioners and executives** operating in international organizations.

**Giovanni Pio**

Head of Global Change Management at United Nation World Food Programme. Clinical Faculty at SDA Bocconi Master of Public Administration (MPA) and Executive Master in Management of International Organizations (EMMIO).

**Javier Chavarria**

Head of Internal Audit and Deputy Director of the Office of Internal Oversight Services at the advisory services for the Organization for the Security and Cooperation in Europe (OSCE). Certified internal auditor (CIA), Certified Risk Management Assurer (CRMA) and Certified Fraud Examiner (CFE).

**Michael Hathorn**

Professional executive coach, change management expert, partner of OPTIMIS.

**Advit Nath**

Controller and Director at the International Fund for Agricultural Development (IFAD) based in Rome, and in charge of managing the integrity, transparency, and control of IFAD’s resources.

**Stefano Olmeti**

Ph.D., coach and leadership development practitioner. Works with leaders from around the world to help them achieve breakthroughs for themselves and their organizations.

Former Head of Executive Coaching at the World Bank. Executive coach and leadership development practitioner working at the intersection of executive learning and international development. MPM, Master of Public Management (SDA Bocconi) Founder.

**Carlo Scaramella**

Deputy Regional Director, Regional Bureau for North Africa, Middle East, Central Asia and Eastern Europe, RBC United Nations World Food Programme (WFP),Cairo, Egypt.

**Stephane Timpano**

Managing Partner, Lagos and West Africa Bain & Co Office Head, EMEA Government and Public Sector Practice Lead.

**Nils Van Wassenhove**

Supply Chain Officer, United Nations World Food Programme (WFP).
