#### Graduate Job Statistics

#### One year after graduation

- _**100%** of graduates are employed_
- _**23.5%** of employed graduates work abroad_

Data refers to a.y. 2022-2023 graduates interviewed

#### On graduation day

- _**90%** of graduates are employed_
- _**30.6%** of employed graduates work abroad_
- _They have been called to an average of **3.6** job interviews_
- _They have received on average **2.8** job offers_

Survey - graduation sessions a.y. 2023-2024
