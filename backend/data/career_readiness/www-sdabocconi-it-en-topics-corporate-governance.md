# Corporate governance

Companies, from multinationals to family businesses, must rethink their strategies and operations by placing sustainability and transparent governance at the core. This shift demands new skills and greater accountability in corporate governance roles, with board members and auditors responsible for ensuring the effectiveness of systems and guiding a fair value creation process.

In this section, you will find the School’s full educational portfolio dedicated to this topic, along with events, news, research activities, and in-depth content.
