## Fees and expenses

The tuition fee for MiMS 2027-28 is €24,000 (excluding VAT), payable in four installments.

- First installment

**€ 6,000**

upon acceptance of admission

- Second installment

**€ 6,000**

by 31 March 2027

- Third installment

**€ 6,000**

by 31 May 2027

- Fourth installment

**€ 6,000**

by 30 July 2027

The fee includes:

  - in-house teaching materials, use of the School's facilities
  - Study Tour costs (travel and lodging).

Participants must have a personal laptop.

## Scholarships and tuition waivers

Based on exclusive merit criteria, SDA Bocconi makes available concessions on the fee for the Master in Healthcare Management by offering partial waivers for outstanding profiles.

Applications must be submitted at the application stage by the deadlines specified in the competition notices.

**To apply for a waiver, you must:**

- Have completed and submitted the Application for Admission to MiMS, with all the documents required.
- Submit the application in response to the notice, attaching the documentation indicated, via the Application portal.
- Meet the deadlines and carefully check the requirements, without which the application cannot be accepted.

If, when a notice is published, the online application has already been submitted, the candidate may access it again and make the waiver request by the deadline stipulated in the notice.

**Applications will be evaluated on the basis of:**

- Academic and professional curriculum.
- Requirements stated in the notice.
- Selection test results at MiMS.

The allocation of waivers will take place during the selection process, by the dates indicated in the announcement. It is possible to apply even before the conclusion of the selection process.

Admission to MiMS is independent of any waiver or scholarship award.

## Loans and funding

SDA Bocconi assists candidates and applicants in their search for funding by providing the necessary documentation to finalize applications for financial support. For more information you can consult the section on funding on the Bocconi University website (for [Italian](https://www.unibocconi.it/it/studenti-iscritti/agevolazioni/finanziamenti-master-universitari-e-sda), for [international applicants](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/student-loans)). The application for funding must be received by the School by the end of the month following the course start date.

The University also makes information available to international students regarding [possible sources of funding](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/funding-country) in different countries of the world.
