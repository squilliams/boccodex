## Who is it for

For approximately 130-140 students from nearly 40 countries and diverse backgrounds, willing to invest in high-return management education, our intensive program spans from 12 to 15 months, effectively equipping them to tackle the challenges of the market.

Our program is designed for professionals ready to make the famed ‘triple jump’ in their career - a change in industry sector, function, or country - or for those who want to take one step further towards a full-fledged career in general management.

It is a transformational learning experience for highly motivated goal-achievers, empowering them to become drivers of organizational change.

## Why Full-Time MBA

A Full-Time MBA offers intensive learning, networking, and personal growth opportunities. With a comprehensive curriculum, real-world experience, and faculty access, it provides a supportive environment for leadership development.

A diverse student body and global exposure enhance business acumen, while networking and collaboration opportunities build a strong professional network. It’s a strategic career investment, accelerating professional growth and opening doors to leadership roles.

- ### Comprehensive Business Education

  - Combines core business disciplines - finance, marketing, strategy, and operations - with contemporary and forward-looking perspectives
  - Includes five concentrations: Innovation and Entrepreneurship, Private Investment, Marketing strategy for growth, Luxury Business Management, Digital Transformation & AI
  - Adopts a multidisciplinary approach to develop a diverse skill set and an interconnected understanding of business

- ### Networking Opportunities

  - Provides ample networking opportunities
  - Connects students with candidates, alumni, faculty, and industry experts
  - Aids in future career growth, business partnerships, and job access
  - Boasts a strong alumni network of over 146,000 professionals worldwide

- ### Career Development Center

  - Dedicated Career Development Center for extensive career support
  - Key partner for MBA candidates seeking career growth
  - Invites recruiting companies to campus to enrich international talent pipeline

- ### Career Advancement

  - Boosts career prospects significantly
  - Equips participants with knowledge and skills for leadership roles
  - Aimed at excelling in management positions

- ### Prestige and Reputation

Recognized for its commitment to academic excellence, critical thinking, and a human-centered approach to leadership, the School fosters a learning environment where cultural awareness and managerial insight come together to shape forward-looking professionals.

## A comprehensive learning experience

Alongside the activities that make up its main structure, the SDA Bocconi Full-time MBA offers you other initiatives to support and enrich your overall preparation and give you an alternative look at some aspects of business, the economy, and our society.

- ### Optional MBA Online Pre-Courses

Starting from April the School provides **online pre-courses** that include Introduction to Financial Accounting, Quantitative Methods, Macroeconomics and Microeconomics. These courses are not compulsory for all students, but may be required for those without a background in economics and management. This is an **early bird enrolment advantage** that allows candidates to access their MBA personalized training, with dedicated faculty, much earlier than the actual MBA start.

- ### Italian Language and Digital Upskilling

You do not need to know the Italian language to attend our MBA, but you probably do not want to miss the opportunity to improve your language skills, particularly if you want to look for a job in Italy. From September to June we offer an italian course for students to improve their language skills. Basic knowledge in the digital age goes beyond learning a spoken and written language and some basic computing tools. This is why, in the first few months of our MBA program, we also provide courses in Microsoft Excel (online) as well as the Bloomberg package for Finance and Quantitative Analysis, PYTHON Coding and Application course, etc.

- ### Leadership Series

This is an initiative organized to expose our students to knowledge and experience from **top leaders** of international companies. A unique opportunity to learn from their hands-on experience and talk with them.

- ### Special Seminars

**Special Seminars** tackle cutting-edge, impactful topics by informally meeting **influential experts**. On these occasions, external speakers as well as SDA Bocconi and Bocconi faculty members come for talks on topics of interest. MBA students can discuss current issues in a timely fashion and naturally develop a habit to promptly compare the concepts seen in class with real-life cases.

- ### Industry Days - Unlocking New Horizons!

Industry Days offer students a unique platform to network, essential for enhancing opportunities in job hunting and career advancement. These events provide an unparalleled opportunity to immerse oneself in diverse industries, gaining firsthand insights into their intricacies and vast potential.

During these sessions, students gain valuable knowledge about how various industries operate, stay updated on the latest trends shaping their landscapes, and understand the challenges they face. Moreover, the program incorporates a Sport Day, providing a dynamic environment for students to compete in sports activities while fostering collaboration with the Master FIFA and other Master programs at SDA Bocconi.

- ### Learning Model

The structure of the program is demanding and requires you to be fully committed, but in turn provides you with the necessary edge to learn and develop the right skills, tools, and command over processes.

The program employs a variety of teaching methods, ranging from traditional lesson to case analysis, business games, simulations, workshops, seminars, online learning, group tutoring, action-learning experiences, bootcamp, out-of-the-classroom field trip and experiments. And much more.
