# Intended Learning Outcomes

Cyber Risk Strategy and Governance

[Overview](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/master-science-program/cyber-risk-strategy-and-governance) [Governance](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/master-science-program/cyber-risk-strategy-and-governance/governance) [Placement and Program Performance](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/master-science-program/cyber-risk-strategy-and-governance/placement-and-program-performance) [Program structure](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/master-science-program/cyber-risk-strategy-and-governance/program-structure)

During the Master of Science program, students acquire specific knowledge and skills related to computer science and social sciences for cyber risk .

Through the teaching activities that characterize the course, they develop skills in

- making judgements autonomously
- improve their written and oral communication
- acquire lifelong learning skills useful for both continuing their studies and their professional futures.
