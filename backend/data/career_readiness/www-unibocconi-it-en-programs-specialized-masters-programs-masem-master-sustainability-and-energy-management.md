# MASEM - Master in Sustainability and Energy Management

The Specialized Master Program in Sustainability and Energy Management (MASEM) is no longer offered by Bocconi University.

For this subject area, the **MSM Master in Sustainability Management**, offered by the SDA Bocconi School of Management, is now available.

[**MSM Master in Sustainability Management**](https://www.sdabocconi.it/en/specialized-master-full-time-executive/msm)

**Sustainability, climate change and energy** are globally recognized challenges for the 21st century, calling for a strong commitment for change by governments, companies and civil society.

The Bocconi Master in Sustainability and Energy Management (MASEM) addressed tomorrow’s challenges related to sustainable development, energy and the natural environment. MASEM created unique opportunities for graduates interested in building a professional career in a multi-disciplinary topic characterized by growing momentum. Its purpose was to create specialized managers and professionals, provide students with solid preparation and skills tailored to the evolution of the job market, and prepare them for industrial and service companies, international agencies, nonprofit organizations and public authorities.

MASEM was a program rooted in the tradition of Bocconi in sustainable management teaching.

The quality of our faculty, both in terms of teaching and research, has built a strong reputation around the world, as demonstrated by improving placements in top international rankings. MASEM graduates are highly regarded by our network of employers. The results we have achieved over the years with the MASEM program are a legacy for our graduates.

**FAQ**

**How to request a certificate:**

Certificate requests must be submitted exclusively through the dedicated _cert@B_ procedure available in the _Student Diary yoU@B_, under the _Certificates_ menu.

Bocconi University provides some self-declaration templates that can be printed through the _Certificates - Printable Documents_ function in the Student Diary yoU@B.

**How to activate an extracurricular internship:**

[Here](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/career-services/your-internship-start-recognition/guide-extracurricular-internships) you can find all the useful information for activating an extracurricular internship through Bocconi, which can act as the sponsoring institution within 12 months from the date of graduation.
