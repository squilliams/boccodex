Bocconi is a selective university with a fixed number of places available for each program offered.

We are committed to educating students to be competent and responsible, able to work in increasingly complex professional areas. With this goal in mind, programs are always cutting-edge and taught by a highly qualified faculty. Bocconi’s modern urban campus has excellent facilities, technology and people to provide many opportunities to enrich your all-around university experience.

Admissions are open for the **2026-2027 academic year.**

Please refer to the program you are interested in (i.e. LLM Full-Time or LLM Part-Time) for further details.

**These are the requirements to apply for the LLM LATAS:**

- **A degree in Law or in a closely related field, typically:**

a) a 5-year Integrated Master of Arts in Law or 3+2 years Master of Arts in Law from an Italian university, or

b) equivalent Law degree from a university abroad.

- **English knowledge, at least at the B2 level.**
- The selection process is based on the evaluation of a dossier including:
  - Academic curriculum (i.e. University transcript of records)
  - Curriculum vitae
  - Personal statement
  - Reference letters (up to 2)
  - Copy of an English language certificate (at least B2 level)
  - Interview (if needed)
  - Video interview (in English).

**Please note that we have additional requirements for the Dual Program with King’s:**

Additional admission requirements for the dual program with King’s College London:

- English proficiency: at least level C1 (advanced) – King’s band B;
- If UK degree, UK level 7 master’s degree, with at least a ‘merit’ as final grade.

Applications must be sent over by clicking on [Apply2B](https://apply.unibocconi.it/) and by completing the form.
