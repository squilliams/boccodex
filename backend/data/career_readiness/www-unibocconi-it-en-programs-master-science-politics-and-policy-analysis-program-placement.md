#### Graduate Job Statistics

#### One year after graduation

- _**91.1%** of graduates are employed_
- _**52.9%** of employed graduates work abroad_

Data refers to a.y. 2022-2023 graduates

#### On graduation day

- _**71%** of graduates are employed_
- _**54.5%** of employed graduates work abroad_
- _They have been called to an average of **3.8** job interviews_
- _They have received on average **1.7** job offers_

Survey - graduation sessions a.y. 2023-2024

**Our recruiters** include, among others:

- Cattaneo Zanetto & Co.
- European Central Bank
- European Commission
- European Investment Bank
- EY
- Intellera Consulting
- KPMG
- OECD
