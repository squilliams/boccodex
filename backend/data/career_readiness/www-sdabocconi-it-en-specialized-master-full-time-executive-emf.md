# Executive Master in Finance

## Trasforma le sfide in grandi opportunità.

Finance and accounting

_Start_ 8 February 2027

_Duration_ 12 months

_Where_ Milano

_Format_ Executive

_Language_ Italian

_Avg. working experience_ 10 years

_Credits_ 62 ECTS/CFU

The **Executive Master in Finance (EMF)** gives you all the tools you need to manage the challenges and take advantage of the opportunities in the world of finance. EMF is **a 12 month program**, in Italian, **alternating weekends**(62 CFU/ECTS) aimed at executive professionals who want to accelerate their careers while reconciling their professional activities. The educational project develops executive and leadership skills in the world of finance, while providing a high level of specialization in one of the **four different areas** characterizing the tracks:

- [Asset/Wealth Management](https://www.sdabocconi.it/en/specialized-master-full-time-executive/emf/track/#track-asset-wealth-management)
- [Banking & Insurance](https://www.sdabocconi.it/en/specialized-master-full-time-executive/emf/track/#track-in-banking-insurance)
- [Corporate Finance & Control](https://www.sdabocconi.it/en/specialized-master-full-time-executive/emf/track/#track-in-corporate-finance-control)
- [Corporate Finance & Real Estate](https://www.sdabocconi.it/en/specialized-master-full-time-executive/emf/track/#track-corporate-finance-real-estate)

## Why EMF

- ### To reconcile profession and work (part-time formula)

The part-time format makes EMF compatible with your work schedule. The 60 days of classroom training spread over 22 weekends and two full-time weeks allow you to invest in your training while continuing your normal professional activity. You will then be able to gain an important competitive advantage in terms of knowledge, operational tools and relationships, expendable right away in your daily life.

- ### To learn with "EMF flex-learning model"

EMF provides an opportunity to manage the uncertainty associated with the inability to reach the SDA Bocconi Campus in physical presence. For proven and justified professional or medical reasons, EMF flex-learning is activated, which includes the ability to connect remotely without losing the opportunity to interact with Faculty and the EMF class group.

- ### To develop the strategic perspective in finance

EMF combines the rigor of learning the tools used in international best practices of professionals with methodologies firmly linked to economic and financial theory, and executive content that enables them to improve their performance in the company through experiential learning on issues of strategy (Strategic Finance Capstone), financial communication (Media Experience Capstone), negotiation, leadership & team building (Negotiation & Leadership Capstone). Leadership coaching (one-on-one activities) complements the educational project with the development of awareness of leadership style, the impact you have on others, and personal and professional strengths.

- ### To enable "tailor-made" specialization

EMF offers training tailored to your needs and aspirations with four different specialization tracks and the option to select one elective course. It combines SDA Bocconi university research and education with the best practice of companies, banks, investors and advisors, constantly drawing on professional operations.

- ### To engage with frontier issues and innovative research

Finance is a world that appears increasingly complex in structure, dynamics and interconnections. Understanding and managing this complexity requires specialized skills but also new conceptual models and professional approaches

- ### To undertake career-switching

A comprehensive training approach that pays attention to basic preparation as well as to specialized and leadership skills is EMF's trump card that you can play in your career in finance "tout court" (Investment Banking, Retail and Commercial Banking, Private Equity, Venture Capital, Insurance), in the corporate sectors (Telecommunications, Media, Technology, Energy, Real Estate) and in consulting firms (Risk Consulting, Financial Advisory, Management Consulting).

- ### To have academic excellence and global experience

A large faculty composed of world-class academics and practitioners in close contact with leading companies, banks and investors, as well as a roster of leading guest speakers are a guarantee of the great value of a course like the Executive Master in Finance for a real change in professional perspective.

- ### Being glocal: local community with global vision

Access a very wide network of people around the world. Proximity and discussion with colleagues, faculty and guests form the basis of a network that offers valuable opportunities even after the Master through online activities and events of EMF CampUS the Community debating forum.

- ### SDA Bocconi is an academic partner of the CAIA Association.

The CAIA (Chartered Alternative Investment Analyst) Association offers 4 scholarships reserved for students of the Executive Master in Finance to support the CAIA certification exam.

CAIA is the leading professional designation for those who wish to develop their careers in the field of alternative investments.

Alternative investments include a wide range of emerging sectors in finance, such as Private Equity and Venture Capital, Hedge Funds, real estate investments, and Real Assets. The CAIA charter is awarded upon passing both levels of the exam and meeting the required professional experience.

For more information: [http://www.caia.org/](http://www.caia.org/)

## A comprehensive learning experience

- ### EMF C-suite Forum

The EMF C-Suite Forum is an opportunity for dialogue and interaction with the top management of leading companies and financial institutions that represent EMF’s areas of specialization, and it is an integral part of the Master’s project work.

[Discover more](https://www.sdabocconi.it/en/specialized-master-full-time-executive/emf/c-suite-forum/)

- ### Leader series

A time for discussion with top leaders from national and international organizations. A unique opportunity to meet prominent personalities also in informal moments such as the post-event aperitif.

[Discover more](https://www.sdabocconi.it/en/specialized-master-full-time-executive/emf/leader-series/)

- ### EMF CEO & CXO Forum

The EMF CEO & CXO Forum is an integral part of the Strategic Finance Capstone, which is dedicated to understanding the strategic levers of Finance for guiding Business Transformation in corporations and financial institutions. The EMF class has the opportunity to engage with the panel of Chief Executives featured at the event

[Discover more](https://www.sdabocconi.it/en/specialized-master-full-time-executive/emf/ceo-cxo-forum/)

- ### People & Culture series

A time for discussion with HR officers from important national and international organizations. An opportunity to meet with the labor market to understand current trends and the implications for human capital. The business lunch that follows is an informal time to share reflections and expectations.

- ### Special seminars

These are seminars on specific and topical issues with members of the financial and academic worlds who have high visibility in the national and international markets.

- ### Entrepreneur Series

A time for discussion with entrepreneurs who have created success stories in national and international organizations.

A unique opportunity to meet visionary personalities to get inspired and understand how to implement and manage extraordinary business economic activities even in informal moments such as the post-event aperitif.

- ### CFO Series

A time for meeting and discussion with Chief Financial Officers from domestic and international corporations and financial institutions. An opportunity to discuss the role of finance in business, how it is managed and the characteristics of the frontier CFO through "closed door" meetings including informal direct interaction modes.

- ### EMF Media Experience Lab

The ability to communicate effectively with the media is one of the most important skills for executives, but also among the least considered in traditional educational pathways. This module offers you the basic principles of proper communication and media relations management and provides you with customized training that simulates on-camera a real TV interview. The module is led by Andrea Cabrini, director of ClassCnbc and an expert in media training and public speaking.

- ### EMF Strategic Finance Capstone

EMF Strategic Finance Capstone is an applied lab where experts develop a tool kit of content/approaches to work in analyzing and solving complex problems, through the levers of strategy and finance to direct the business transformation of companies. The Capstone is preparatory to the Specialization tracks.

- ### LSEG Training

[LSEG](https://unibocconi.libguides.com/refinitiv), a global leader in professional financial information and an EMF Training Partner, is offering a Financial & Risk training program. All EMF participants are granted free access to the platform, which provides an extensive economic and financial database with historical archives, real-time data, instant messaging, news, and financial analysis.

[Discover more](https://unibocconi.libguides.com/refinitiv)

- ### Learning Model

EMF has a differentiated teaching model that is based on knowledge and interpretive logic applied to real cases. Distinguished faculty expertise, continuous interaction between faculty and participants, and multiple opportunities to engage with top-notch guest speakers ensure maximum learning effectiveness.
