# Tax & Legal Networking Event

## 19 February 2026 (2.30 to 6.00pm), Roentgen building

_The Tax&Legal Networking Event is a chance to meet a selected group of employers in this field and discover the career paths they offer._

_During the **networking afternoon on campus**, that will be held on **19 February 2026 from 2.30 to 6pm**, you will be able to:_

- _**meet Law & Tax Firms professionals** in person and **interact** directly with recruiters;_
- _**get useful information** about the hiring processes;_
- _**apply** for available internship and job opportunities._

## Registration info and deadlines

_To join the event, **registration is mandatory (starting on 9 February 2026, 1.00pm) and will close on 17 February 2026 at midnight or upon reaching the maximum number of participants**: students and graduates can **access the yoU@B diary, click on "Career Service" and then on “Events Calendar”.**_

_More info on how to participate will be **shared with registered students and graduates on 18 February, via email.**_

_Please note that the Tax & Legal Networking Event is open to students and graduates from the following Bocconi programs: Bachelor in Global Law (BGL), Corso di laurea Magistrale in Giurisprudenza (CLMG), LLM in Law of Technology and Automated Systems, LLM in European Business and Social Law and LLM in Global Tax Law and Governance, up to three years after completing their studies._
