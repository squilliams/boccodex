An internship that begins before graduation is considered curricular (with or without credits). **From the graduation day** onwards, such internship becomes extracurricular.

If your internship is **carried out in Lombardy**, due to a Bocconi policy, it remains curricular if it is¬†completed by 59 calendar days after the awarding of the degree.

Due to this policy:

The company can then activate a new internship that will start as an extracurricular one. For this new extracurricular internship you shall commit to meet [all the requirements](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/services/activating-extracurricular-internship) set out by the relevant law on extracurricular internships.

If the internship **is not carried out in Lombardy,** due to the relevant regional laws‚Äô peculiarities, please, contact the Internship Office in order to make sure that the University can act as promoting body. The University does not activate extracurricular internships in Latium and Emilia-Romagna.

If the internship **is carried out outside Italy,** please, do inform in due time your Company about your graduation.
