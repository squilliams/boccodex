#### Graduate Job Statistics

#### One year after graduation

- _**94.9%** of graduates are employed_
- _**60.8%** of employed graduates work abroad_

Data refers to a.y. 2022-2023 graduates of the Finance English class

#### On graduation day

- _**82.7%** of graduates are employed_
- _**61.5%** of employed graduates work abroad_
- _They have been called to an average of **6.9** job interviews_
- _They have received on average **2** job offers_

Data refers to a.y. 2023-2024 graduates of the Finance English class
