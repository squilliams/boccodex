# Recruiting Date Banking, Financial Services & Fintech

## Digital recruiting campaign: 4-8 March 2026

## On campus event: 4 March 2026 (1 to 6pm) in Roentgen Bulding

_The Recruiting Date Banking, Financial Services & Fintech is a chance to meet a selected group of employers in the industries, discover the career paths they offer, apply for available internship and job opportunities._

_Discover the agenda here below!_

### 2 and 3 March 2026: Preview Days

Discover company profiles of attending employers, read their corporate info, watch their videos, browse their LinkedIn page.

### 4 March 2026, from 1 to 6pm: Networking Afternoon on Campus

Meet company representatives in person, interact directly with recruiters, get useful information about the hiring processes and apply.

### 4-8 March 2026: Application Days

Send online the applications for internship and job offers.

## Registration info and deadlines

_To join the event, **registration is mandatory (starting on**_ _**21 February 2026**_ _**, 1pm) and will close on 1 March 2026 at midnight or upon reaching the maximum number of participants**: students and graduates can **access the yoU@B diary, click on "Career Service" and then on “Events Calendar”.**_

_More info on how to participate will be **shared with registered students and graduates, on**_ _**2 March 2026**_ _**.**_

_Please note that the Recruiting Date is open **only to students from Bocconi Master of Science Programs, 4th and 5th year Integrated Master of Arts in Law, WBB and Pre-experience Masters, and respective graduates up to three years after graduation.**_
