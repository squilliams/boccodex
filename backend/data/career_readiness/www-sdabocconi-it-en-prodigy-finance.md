# Prodigy Finance

Students who have been admitted into one the following programs: MBA, MCF, MAMA, GEMBA are eligible to apply for the **Prodigy Finance Loan Program**, which offers an innovative way to finance your studies.

Prodigy Finance offers loans funded by the School itself and other investors. These loans are available to international students from over 150 nationalities.

![MBA](https://www.sdabocconi.it/_next/image/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fsda-website-prod-sdawebsiteprodbucketpublic693d05b-w3mwb4fo9x9p.s3.eu-south-1.amazonaws.com%2Fs3fs-public%2F2024-11%2Fhero-mba_0.jpg&w=3840&q=90&dpl=dpl_EgyAUeGYW1L1ERS2WGuDkM3HLa8o)

Students are eligible for a loan only if they reside outside Italy at the time of application. Any application for a loan made from within the Italian territory and/or by students currently residing in Italy will be rejected by Prodigy.

Interest rates vary depending on individual applicants. See more details about the terms and conditions, and how to apply for a loan on [Prodigy’s website](https://prodigyfinance.com/get-a-loan/sda-bocconi-school-of-management/loan).

Main features of the Prodigy Finance - SDA Bocconi Loan Program:

- 150 nationalities covered
- Competitive interest rates
- Maximum loan size up to 100% of the cost of the program, although loans will be adjusted according to the profile of each individual applicant
- No collateral or guarantees required
- Easy online process
- No payment due until six months after your graduation. The repayment period can be either 7, 10 years or 15 years, depending on your program and application.

Apply directly through [Prodigy online platform](https://prodigyfinance.com/get-a-loan/sda-bocconi-school-of-management/loan).

For further information please refer to the [FAQ section](https://support.prodigyfinance.com/hc/en-us/) on Prodigy Finance’s website.
