Program Placement

The Master of Science in Transformative Sustainability has been offered starting in the 2022–2023 academic year. Placement information for this program is not yet available.

Thanks to an extended network of relations with companies and institutions, MSc graduates have gained professional experience in the following organizations:

- A2A
- Accenture
- Amazon
- Amplifon
- Bain & Company
- Boston Consulting Group
- Crédit Agricole CIB
- Deloitte
- European Commission
- EY
- GE Vernova
- Generali
- Kering
- KPMG
- Protiviti
- PwC
- The European House – Ambrosetti
- United Nations Industrial Development Organization (UNIDO)
