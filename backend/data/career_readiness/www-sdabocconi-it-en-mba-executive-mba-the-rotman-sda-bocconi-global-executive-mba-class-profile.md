# Class profile

A typical GEMBA student is **an intuitive, inquisitive, and enthusiastic person** who knows how to listen, concentrate, and get involved. A person with a good academic background, several years of work experience in any field, and advanced knowledge of English. She/he is willing to grow as a manager and as a person, to make a mark in their professional life and in society through new opportunities, responsibilities, and ethical values.
