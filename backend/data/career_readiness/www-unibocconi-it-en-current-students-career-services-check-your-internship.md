The **CHECK YOUR INTERNSHIP widget allows you to monitor** your internships independently and in real time. It guides you with dynamic and personalized lists through **your internship activation and recognition process.**

You will have a clear idea of the actions you need to perform and the activities carried out by the other actors involved in the internship process (Internship Office, degree program Director or delegate, company supervisor).

The widget **will appear in the INTERNSHIP section of your yoU@B diary as soon as your internship is registered on JobGate.** In the widget you will find the different steps which characterize every internship and that will follow its evolution (activation, credit recognition, changes of duration, etc.).
