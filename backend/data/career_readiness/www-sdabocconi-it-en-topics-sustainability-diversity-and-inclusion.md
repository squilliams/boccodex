# Sustainability, diversity and inclusion

For companies today, environmental sustainability cannot exist without a solid economic foundation, and vice versa. ESG factors must be integrated into business activities, starting from governance, developing a related strategy, and adapting human resources organization. It is therefore necessary to rethink the skills required to integrate social sustainability, ensuring fair treatment, equal opportunities, and representation at all levels.

In this section, you will find the School’s full educational portfolio dedicated to this topic, along with events, news, research activities, and in-depth content.
