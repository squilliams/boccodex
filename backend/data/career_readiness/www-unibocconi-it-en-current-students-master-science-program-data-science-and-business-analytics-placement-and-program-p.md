## **One year after graduation\***

- **93.2%** of graduates are employed
- **39%** of employed graduates work abroad
- **12%** are continuing their studies

**\*** Data refers to a.y. 2021-2022 graduates

## **On graduation day\*\***

- **82.4%** of graduates are employed
- **42.9%** of employed graduates work abroad
- They have been called to an average of **4.5** job interviews
- They have received on average **2.1** job offers

**\*\*** Survey - graduation sessions a.y. 2022-2023

## **Graduating students - satisfaction with the Study Program**

**86.4%** of students who graduated in the 2023-24 academic year declared they were satisfied with their Study Program

[**Outcomes of graduating students’ evaluation on the Study Program (in Italian only)**](https://www.unibocconi.it/it/chi-siamo/assicurazione-qualita/esiti-valutazioni)

## **Attending students - satisfaction with teaching**

**77.6%** of students who attended courses offered in 2024-25 academic year are satisfied with the way the courses were carried out (grades from 6 to 10 on a 1-10 scale)
