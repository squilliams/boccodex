To do for international students

In this area you can find some basic information to help you register and settle in Italy once you have been admitted in one of our PhD programs.

**EU CITIZENS**

Since January 2007 EU citizens living in Italy are no longer required to apply for a permit of stay. If they stay in Italy for a period exceeding 90 days, they must register with the Registry Office of the Municipality where they intend to reside. They must register within 3 months of their arrival in Italy.

Information on registration in the Municipality is available at the link [Residence: forms and documents for foreign people coming from abroad - Municipality of Milan](https://www.comune.milano.it/en/aree-tematiche/anagrafe/residenza-modulistica-persone-straniere)

**NON ‐ EU CITIZENS**

If you are a non - EU citizen, you need to apply for a student visa at the Italian diplomatic authority of the country you reside in. Please note that it is your responsibility to apply for the appropriate visa.

Once you hold your student visa and you cross the Italian border, you must apply for a Permit of Stay for study purposes ( _Permesso di soggiorno per motivi di studio_).

Non-EU students enrolled in the first year of the program who do not send the copy of permit of stay to the [PhD School Office](mailto:phdadministrativecenter@unibocconi.it) by the end of June, will not be admitted to the second year of the program.

[HOW TO APPLY FOR A STUDENT VISA](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/media/attachments/HOW%20TO%20APPLY%20FOR%20A%20STUDENT%20VISA_2026.pdf "PhD_how to obtain a VISA_2026")

[HOW TO APPLY FOR A PERMIT OF STAY](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/media/attachments/HOW%20TO%20RENEW%20YOUR%20PERMIT%20OF%20STAY_2026.pdf "How to renew the permit of stay 2026")

**NOTE:** Once you hold your Permit of Stay, you also need to register at the Municipality of the town you intend to reside in.

Information on registration in the Municipality is available at the link [Residence: forms and documents for foreign people coming from abroad - Municipality of Milan](https://www.comune.milano.it/en/aree-tematiche/anagrafe/residenza-modulistica-persone-straniere)
