# Faculty

**A pool of dedicated experts**

The global value of MIHMEP is also guaranteed by a **faculty** made up of leading figures in the international academic scene, by an **advisory** board that includes top leaders of healthcare organizations, institutions and industries, and a **scientific board** that consists of prominent worldwide experts in the areas of healthcare management.

## Guest Speakers

In addition to permanent and visiting faculty, you have the chance to attend numerous testimonials from guest speakers that continuously enrich and innovate the contents of the program. Over the years, more than 60 international guest speakers have contributed to MIHMEP.

Guest speakers are engaged in three series of seminars:

- **“From the shop floor”**: insights from those who make things happen;
- **“From the board”**: inspiration from those who lead;
- **“Facts matter”**: evidence from research on management and policy in health sector.
