PhD in Social and Political Science

[Overview](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/phd/phd-social-and-political-science) [Courses and Requirements](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/phd/phd-social-and-political-science/courses-and-requirements) [Faculty](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/phd/phd-social-and-political-science/faculty) [PhD Students in Social and Political Science](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/phd/phd-social-and-political-science/phd-students-social-and-political-science) [Job Market Candidates in Public Policy and Administration](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/phd/phd-social-and-political-science/job-market-candidates-public-policy-and-administration) [Info for Current Students](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/phd/phd-social-and-political-science/info-current-students) [Placement](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/phd/phd-social-and-political-science/placement)

In accordance with ministerial regulations, the Faculty Board is composed of at least twelve members (of whom at least 50% are full professors and associate professors).

The Faculty Board coordinates and supervises study and research activities carried out by PhD students; it monitors and assesses the performance of the program as a whole, and proposes possible improvement actions.
