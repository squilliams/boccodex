## Who is it for

The EMMAP participant, a new figure of "public servant".

EMMAP is the ideal meeting place for new public administration professionals, people who are highly motivated and culturally oriented toward the renewal of the public system. Managers with strong public ethics, aware of and respectful of the specificities and constraints of their role but far from the old bureaucratic mentality and capable of taking responsibility. Individuals committed to making public administration a place for building the country's welfare and development.

## Why EMMAP

- ### Work and learning in the field formula

In its sixteenth edition, EMMAP offers a part-time and blended format that allows you to reconcile participation in the Master program with your professional and personal commitments and apply the learning developed on campus to your professional challenges right away. Alongside the irreplaceable on campus sessions, which allow, among other things, the exchange of experiences with other participants, several activities will be developed in distance learning.

- ### Customized learning model

Experience, interpretation, debate and implementation - these are the four dimensions that make up our learning model, developed over years of discussion with hundreds of public managers. YourSELF (Self Empowerment Leadership Program) is the customized public leadership skill-building course that we provide to meet your specific training needs, starting with an individual assessment and ending with a coaching course.

- ### Concrete impact on organizations

During the Master program, you will carry out project work and a concrete executive innovation project, identified on the basis of the needs of your home institution (or company) and the strategic challenges that it faces. The project is developed with the support of a faculty member who will individually follow you and your institution through the process of planning, implementing and evaluating the project.

- ### \#SFIDE

#SFIDE is a series of webinars dedicated to Alumni and professionals who work for and with the Public Administration, aimed at discussing current and future challenges and exploring possible tools to address them.

## A comprehensive learning experience

- ### The format

From May 2025 for a 13-month period mix of **face-to-face** and **distance learning sessions**

  - **38 days** in modules of 3 or 4 days in Milano, on the [SDA Bocconi Campus](https://www.sdabocconi.it/en/who-we-are/campus-and-culture/milano/)
  - **8 days** over 2 modules on the new [campus in Roma](https://www.sdabocconi.it/en/who-we-are/campus-and-culture/roma/)
  - **4 days** abroad in April 2026 - International Learning Visit
  - **15%** distance learning teaching (synchronous sessions) and activities aimed at the implementation of the Action Learning Projects (ALPro) and the Innovation Project.

Daily timetable for in-person teaching, approximate: 8:30 a.m. to 5:45 p.m. (the first day of the module starting at 12:00 p.m. and the last day of the module ending at 4:00 p.m.).

- ### International learning visit

The module is designed to offer participants a different perspective from Italian public administration, develop comparative public management skills and learn from the concrete application in complex, multi-level governance contexts of the tools and approaches seen in the EMMAP course. It provides a mix of traditional lectures, testimonials and face-to-face time with practitioners and public managers from international institutions, agencies and organizations at one or more European cities.

- ### Action Learning Projects (ALPro)

**Action Learning Projects** (ALPro) are an integral part of EMMAP's curricular activities. Working **at institutions or private companies** other than your own on case studies selected by the Master coordinator and in groups with other participants, you will be able to:

  - deal with a problematic situation,
  - identify possible future scenarios for action,
  - propose a solution or several intervention alternatives.

The activity is carried out with the **tutorship** of a Bocconi Professor who proposes the project theme in close contact with the host institution's contact persons.

- ### Innovation Projects

EMMAP offers you a unique opportunity: **to carry out a managerial change project within your organization**, supported by a Faculty member who will guide you throughout the implementation period. The project may involve the participation of your colleagues, collaborators, and internal stakeholders.

- ### YourSELF - Self Empowerment Leadership Focus

**YourSELF (Self Empowerment Leadership Focus)** is a leadership development program tailored to your specific needs.

**Leadership** can be learned and trained. It involves personal and professional skills that can be developed starting from an awareness of one’s strengths and weaknesses. It grows through reflection on the relationship between initial intentions, actual behaviors, and the impacts generated.

To support this process, SDA Bocconi provides you with two key tools: **assessment and coaching**.

## Fees and scholarships

Our EMMAP is a significant personal and financial investment, but the return in professional growth and advancement is real.

The fee for participation in EMMAP 2026-2027 is €25,000 (outside the scope of VAT). The fee is to be paid in five installments.

Based on exclusive merit criteria, each year SDA Bocconi makes several scholarships and tuition waivers available to candidates and applicants.

In addition, the School also participates in notices proposed by public administrations for scholarships/exemptions for EMMAP applicants.
