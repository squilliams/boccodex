# Fashion, design and luxury

Creative vision and specialized managerial capabilities across the value chain—positioning, product development, merchandising and pricing, retail and omnichannel, clienteling and experience—become competitive levers alongside sustainability, traceability, digital innovation and responsible supply chains, creating new managerial opportunities across global creative industries, with Milan as the reference ecosystem.

In this section, you will find the School’s full educational portfolio dedicated to this topic, along with events, news, research activities, and in-depth content.
