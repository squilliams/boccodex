# Bocconi and Employers

Based on the quality of our programs, Bocconi graduates have a strong standing with top employers.

Thanks to a worldwide network of **companies, international organizations, chambers of commerce and institutions** in more than **70 countries**, every year **over** **1,700 students** and recent graduates have the opportunity to take part in an internship abroad.

### Placement Facts & Figures

_**94.5%**_ of MSc graduates are **employed one year after graduation**\*

_**36.2%**_ **work abroad**\*

_**24 days**_ is the **average time needed for job placement**\*

_**77.4%**_ of MSc graduates are **employed on graduation day**\*\*

#### Preparing for the Job Market

Through our **Career Services**, you will receive unparalleled and personalized support throughout the career orientation and job search processes. You will also have access to customized tools to successfully enter the job market and meet employers in Italy and abroad.

Recruiting Activities

Throughout the year, the Bocconi Career Service team organizes various initiatives providing you with excellent opportunities to meet prospective employers, and with easy access to a wide variety of internship and placement opportunities both online and on campus.

- **Bocconi&Jobs**

Bocconi&Jobs is our career event, organized twice a year every spring and fall. It is an exclusive opportunity to meet Italian and international companies, organizations and professional firms, attend employer presentations, leave your application and get interviewed.
- **Bocconi&Jobs Abroad**

Bocconi&Jobs goes abroad each year to a variety of locations (BENELUX, France, Germany & Switzerland, UK&Ireland, Spain & Portugal, Middle East). You can attend the fair for a unique opportunity to network with top local employers.
- **Investment Banking Days**

Every year you’ll have the opportunity to meet the leading investment banks on campus and find out about their recruiting programs through company presentations and informal meetings with company representatives.
- **Company Presentations and Networking Events**

Learn about company development programs and recruitment processes, hand in your application, learn about and compare different professional organizations and establish informal and direct contacts.
- **JobGate**

This online platform is reserved to Bocconi students and graduates, and is updated daily by employers promoting internship and placement opportunities in Italy and abroad.
- **Internship Programs**

A special section of the website that offers internships abroad.
- **Recruiting Date**

These events are informal networking sessions that allow you to submit your application in person and get useful information about companies and their recruiting processes and career paths.

Getting Ready to Enter the Workforce

The Career Service team supports you with information on different employment sectors, ways of planning your career, understanding and marketing your skills and aptitudes. Companies actively support Bocconi in this process by participating in University events and activities.

You can reserve your time slot for personalized advice about your application and your professional goals.

- **Career Service Infopoint**

This can be used to organize your job search and access information for your internship or job search. A reference service is also available, offering personalized consultations and assistance.
- **In-Company Training**

Training experiences that are short in duration, dedicated to small groups of students. Participants are hosted directly by a company and have the chance to get to know the various departments and the recruitment process. Opportunities are offered in different fields in Italy and in major EU countries.
- **Mock Interviews**

One-on-one selection interview simulations aimed at experiencing a job interview to discover your strengths and the areas for improvement you should work on.
- **E-learning Courses**

Tutorials and materials used for in-class seminars can be found in the e-learning area of the “Extracurricular Courses” section, along with a “Career Starter Pack.” It includes a powerful set of tools, exercises and tips to effectively enter the job market.
- **Get the Job**

In-class and online sessions covering topics related to the most effective tools when looking for employment or internship positions, and how to apply and approach the selection process.
- **Training Seminars**

Training classroom activities focusing on the soft skills that are indispensable in the workplace. They are held by company representatives.
- **A Career In...**

Seminars dedicated to exploring typical career paths. These meetings are a chance for direct contact with professionals working in a specific area or a particular sector of the job market. You can find out what these jobs entail, the skills required and their career prospects.
- **Careers in International Organizations**

Presentations targeted at students and graduates interested in building a career at an international organization. Representatives from organizations such as the World Bank, the EIB and the European Commission talk about the institutions where they work, hiring and internship opportunities and career paths.
- **POL (Personal Orientation Lab) Your Professional Aptitudes**

A service specifically created for students and recent graduates in order to support them in achieving a higher level of self-awareness about professional aptitudes.

Internships

Bocconi University and employers share a common view on the educational aim of internships: practical training, orientation to future careers and the first step into the job market. This is why an internship is part of the academic curriculum, and is mandatory for all Master of Science students.

Internships act as the gateway to the professional world: they are concrete and educational on-the-job experiences which aim to develop skills and help with future career choices. Graduate students can only begin upon completion of the curricular coursework of their first year of study. Internships can take place in Italy or abroad.

**JobGate**

This online platform is reserved to Bocconi students and graduates, and can be accessed through the yoU@B student diary.

It is updated daily with **internship and placement offers** in Italy and abroad, including a **Focus On** box with opportunities grouped by geographic area (Companies in the Americas and Companies in Asia, Pacific, Africa & Middle East) and by type of institution (International Organizations and NGOs, Chambers of Commerce and Foreign Offices).

JobGate also contains a **Curricular Internship Special section**, reserved to Master of Science and Law students, as well as opportunities at Embassies, Consulates and Italian cultural institutions, which is open to Italian citizens.
