# Candidate profile

The **Master Sustainability and Energy Management (MASEM)** is a 12-month full-time program, entirely in English, that proposes a multi-disciplinary educational approach to train the next-generation of managers and professionals interested in leading the change towards a sustainable future.

The MASEM program has been designed to attract talented students and young professionals, with **diverse backgrounds**, interested in acquiring skills and competencies in the economic, regulatory and managerial aspects of environmental and energy issues, through a balanced combination of theory and practice.

The program is particularly relevant to pursue a career in:

- **Sustainability, energy** and **innovation** within leading companies in both the public and private sectors
- **Consulting firms**
- **Utilities**
- **International organizations and NGOs**
- **Public institutions**

**The MASEM program is designed for:**

- graduates with an undergraduate degree (i.e. Italian Diploma di laurea, American Bachelor of Arts or Bachelor of Science, French Licence, Russian Bakalaur) preferably in economics, management, engineering and scientific disciplines;
- applicants who have not yet graduated must receive their degrees within 90 days of the beginning of the Specialized Master program;
- young professionals with limited professional experience (2-3 years)
