# Career fairs

_Career fairs are **interactive events** where you can **meet with a range of employers under one roof**. Bocconi career fairs may be dedicated to specific industries or open to all professional sectors, and can be organized in Italy or abroad, in presence or online. Regardless of the format, **companies will showcase their internship and placement offers**, and you can engage with recruiters to **gather information about specific roles, share your CV and make connections** for immediate or future internship and job opportunities._

## Available activities

Bocconi&Jobs

Two editions per year, it’s **the University’s main career event**, where you can meet companies from any business field and from different Countries. Check out the [dedicated page](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/career-services/meet-employers/bocconijobs "Bocconi&Jobs") to find out all the info about the event.

Bocconi&Jobs abroad

**Eight editions dedicated to specific regions abroad**:

- [Benelux](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/career-services/meet-employers/hybrid-bocconijobs-abroad-benelux "Bocconi&Jobs abroad Benelux")
- [France](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/career-services/meet-employers/bocconijobs-abroad-france "Bocconi&Jobs abroad France/Paris")
- [Germany and Switzerland](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/career-services/meet-employers/digital-bocconijobs-abroad-germany-and-switzerland "Digital Bocconi&Jobs abroad Germany and Switzerland")
- [Luxembourg](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/bocconijobs-abroad-luxembourg "Bocconi&Jobs abroad Luxembourg")
- [Middle East](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/career-services/meet-employers/digital-bocconijobs-abroad-middle-east "Digital Bocconi&Jobs abroad Middle East")
- [Spain and Portugal](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/career-services/meet-employers/digital-bocconijobs-abroad-spain-and-portugal "Digital Bocconi&Jobs abroad Spain and Portugal")
- [UK and Ireland](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/career-services/meet-employers/bocconijobs-abroad-uk-and-ireland "Bocconi&Jobs abroad UK and Ireland")(2 editions per year)

Participating employers offer internships and jobs available only in the regions for which the event is hosted.

Investment Banking Days

One edition per year, dedicated to **firms operating in the Investment Banking world**. Check out the [event page](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/career-services/meet-employers/investment-banking-days "Investment Banking Days") to find out all the info.

- [Fashion, Luxury & Beauty](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/career-services/meet-employers/recruiting-date-fashion-luxury-beauty " Recruiting Date Fashion, Luxury & Beauty")
- [Government, Public Services & International Organizations](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/career-services/meet-employers/recruiting-date-government-public-services-international-organizations "Recruiting Date Government, Public Services & International Organizations")
- [Web & IT Companies](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/career-services/meet-employers/recruiting-date-web-it-companies "Recruiting Date Web Companies")

Tax&Legal Networking Event

One edition per year, it is the **career event dedicated to Law and Tax Firms.** You can find more info [here](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/career-services/meet-employers/tax-legal-networking-event "Tax & Legal Networking Event").

_**How to join**_

_These initiatives are held throughout the whole academic year and participation is upon-registration only._

_**To register, please refer to You@B > Career Service menu > Events Calendar**, where you will also find the specific contact to ask for more info about the event of your interest, if needed._

_In some cases registration follows a specific process, and sometimes is limited to ad hoc categories of students and graduates: you will find information in the dedicated pages when needed._
