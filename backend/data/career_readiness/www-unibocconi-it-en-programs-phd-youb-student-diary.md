The yoU@B student Diary is a tailor-made planner accessible via the web and offered to all Bocconi students to help them organizing their agenda, receive information from various University Services,  connect their devices to the Bocconi wifi network, check dates, and so on.

**How to access yoU@B:** The yoU@B student diary can be accessed from the Bocconi website at: agenda.unibocconi.it or at [https://agenda.unibocconi.it/](https://agenda.unibocconi.it/)

**How to login:** To login, type your student number (matricola) in the appropriate ‘username’ space and your password in the corresponding ‘password’ space (same credentials as for your PhD email account).

This tool is available both in Italian and English: you can change languages at any time. We strongly suggest that you **check** your agenda on a **regular basis.** **For further information**: [yoU@B](mailto:yoU@B)
