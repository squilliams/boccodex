## Career Development Center

The **Career Development Center (CDC)** is a key partner to MBA candidates seeking to achieve their career growth and to invite recruiting companies who need to feed their international talent pipeline to Campus.

Each CDC team member's mission is to support you throughout the program, providing you with the right training, tools and knowledge to plan and execute an effective career strategy, hosting a large variety of employers and telling the experience of Alumni, helping you to successfully re-enter the job market after graduation.

## Empowering career success with tailored guidance

The CDC's mission is to equip students with the necessary tools to achieve their renewed career goals through a personalized approach, which includes a modern, up-to-date training plan and consistent, one-on-one career advising.

Design and develop your personalized career path

Boost your skillset – MBA specific training plan

Nurture your network

## Empowering companies with tailored talent strategies

Regardless of industry, size, location, or growth phase, the CDC collaborates with companies to enhance their **talent acquisition** and **employer branding** strategies, promote internship and job opportunities year-round, or design engaging activities within our community.
