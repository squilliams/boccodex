### Structure

- ![community](https://sda-website-prod-sdawebsiteprodbucketpublic693d05b-w3mwb4fo9x9p.s3.eu-south-1.amazonaws.com/s3fs-public/2025-06/community.svg?v=1750751023)

### Advisory Council

Composed of authorities, regulators and industry associations, will address the vision and long-term strategy of the Hub, answering to the need expressed by the market.

- ![community](https://sda-website-prod-sdawebsiteprodbucketpublic693d05b-w3mwb4fo9x9p.s3.eu-south-1.amazonaws.com/s3fs-public/2025-06/community.svg?v=1750751023)

### Founding Sponsors Advisory Board

Composed of members with high prestige and professionalism coming from leading Italian and international financial realities, that identifies the main topics of the research and interacts with the Advisory council.

- ![docs_ico](https://sda-website-prod-sdawebsiteprodbucketpublic693d05b-w3mwb4fo9x9p.s3.eu-south-1.amazonaws.com/s3fs-public/2025-06/docs_ico_0.svg?v=1750752352)

### Committee of Executive Sponsors

It is characterized by “experience, vision, engagement, flexibility and complementarity” and is composed of hub’s founding and premium sponsors. The plenary Committee addresses the operations, drives the strategies for the academic researches and brings in the Hub the real day-by-day market’s needs.

- ![servizi_ico](https://sda-website-prod-sdawebsiteprodbucketpublic693d05b-w3mwb4fo9x9p.s3.eu-south-1.amazonaws.com/s3fs-public/2025-06/servizi_ico.svg?v=1750751214)

### Instructors & Researchers

Responsible for directing and managing the FS Tech Hub’s scientific activities on the basis of the guidelines and objectives defined by the Advisory Council and the Founding Sponsor Advisory Board.

- ![president_ico](https://sda-website-prod-sdawebsiteprodbucketpublic693d05b-w3mwb4fo9x9p.s3.eu-south-1.amazonaws.com/s3fs-public/2025-06/president_ico.svg?v=1750751264)

### Supporters & Experts

They can be both internal to ICE SDA Bocconi (student associations etc.) and from the external ecosystem. Their aim is to provide a pool of talent, skills and resources useful for the success of the initiative.

### Steps and Objects

FS tech hub will be the reference point for the participants, and eventually also for external industry experts and students, interested in developing expertise in financial valuation and governance topic in the fast-growing world of tech applied to financial services. The Key phases are the following ones.

[Discover more](https://sda-website-prod-sdawebsiteprodbucketpublic693d05b-w3mwb4fo9x9p.s3.eu-south-1.amazonaws.com/s3fs-public/file/fs_tech_hub_background_objectives.pdf)

### Projectual Framework

- Definition of the vision and long-term strategy of the Hub, in response to the needs and demands expressed by the market.
- Identification of the primary research topics and engagement with the Advisory Council to represent the needs of the market.
- Implementation of academic research implementation and exploration of market trends and emerging challenges for participants of the Hub.

![projectual_framework_ice_2023](https://www.sdabocconi.it/_next/image/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fsda-website-prod-sdawebsiteprodbucketpublic693d05b-w3mwb4fo9x9p.s3.eu-south-1.amazonaws.com%2Fs3fs-public%2F2025-06%2Fprojectual_framework_ice_2023.jpg%3Fv%3D1750751463&w=3840&q=90&dpl=dpl_EgyAUeGYW1L1ERS2WGuDkM3HLa8o)

### Deliverables

- SHORT TERM (0-6 months)

Launch Event, Induction Advisory Council, Workshop Kick-off, Committee Board, 1° Workshop Trend

- MID TERM (6-12 months)

Experience Day, Private Report For Sponsors, Public Event (Report), Innovative Start Ups Competition

- LONG TERM (12-24 months)

Research Main Output - Book

### Key Success Factors

- CLEAR AND SHARED TARGETS FOR CUSTOMERS

  - EXECUTIVE SPONSORSHIP

    ICE SDA will address the research and provide outputs for Sponsors
  - RULES FOR ACTORS

    Clear roles and responsibilities (creation of a Board) that lead a constant growth

- PARTICIPANTS ENGAGEMENT

  - WIN-WIN MODE

    Shared view and actions that bring results to the whole community

- VALUE CREATION FOR ICE SDA BOCCONI

  - CONTENT PROGRAMMING

    Communication plan and scheduled contents production
  - ONLINE PLATFORM

    A channel for content sharing and value creation
