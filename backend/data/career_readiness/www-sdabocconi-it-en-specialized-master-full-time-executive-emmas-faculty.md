# Faculty

**A Faculty of researchers and professionals**

The EMMAS faculty is multidisciplinary and composed of Bocconi professors and researchers involved in institutions dedicated to healthcare management:

- il [**CERGAS**](https://www.sdabocconi.it/en/node/15792/) \- the Centre for Research on Health and Social Care Management – which since 1978 has been engaged in developing action-research to support healthcare management.
- The **Public Management and Policy Area** of SDA Bocconi, which over the past two decades has built extensive experience in executive education programs for healthcare (such as CORGESAN, COGEMSAN, Ippocrate, etc.), as well as in the development of the Master of International Healthcare Management, Economics and Policy ( [MIHMEP](https://www.sdabocconi.it/en/node/2148/)) and the Master in Healthcare Management ( [MiMS](https://www.sdabocconi.it/en/node/2137/)).
