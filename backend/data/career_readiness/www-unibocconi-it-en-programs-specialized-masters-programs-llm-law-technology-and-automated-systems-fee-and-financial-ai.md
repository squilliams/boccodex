**Full-Time Program**

Tuition and fees for the 2026-2027 edition is € 16,000 and include course materials and access to Bocconi facilities.

**More information about methods of payment see here (** [**link**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/services/fees-specialized-master-programs) **).**

**For more information on how use the PagoPa online system here (** [**link**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/services/fees-specialized-master-programs) **).**

Fees are payable in four installments as follows:

- **I installment - advance**: € 2,500 on enrollment (this sum is not refundable if, after having completed the registration to the program, students decide not to attend the program anymore). Please be aware that while settling the I instalment you will be asked to pay an extra €32,00 as Italian tax on University fees.

- **I installment - adjustment:** € 3,900 -  15 October, 2026

- **II installment**: € 6,400 - 15 December, 2026

- **III installment**: € 3,200 - 15 March, 2027

Please note that the tuition fees paid are non-refundable.

**Part-Time Program**

Tuition and fees for the **2-year program** is € 19,500 **(€ 9,750 per year)** and include course materials and access to Bocconi facilities.

Fees are payable in four installments as follows:

- **I installment - advance**: € 2,500 on enrollment (this sum is not refundable if, after having completed the registration to the program, students decide not to attend the program anymore). Please be aware that while settling the I instalment you will be asked to pay an extra €32,00 as Italian tax on University fees.

- **I installment - adjustment:** € 1,400 - 15 October, 2026

- **II installment**: € 3,900 - 15 December, 2026

- **III installment**: € 1,950 - 13 March, 2027

Please note that the tuition fees paid are non-refundable.

**Student loans**

To cover the Specialized Master Programs tuition and fees, students with EU and UK citizenship can ask for low-interest loans to credit institutes with which Bocconi University has special agreements.

The online application to make your loan request is open  **from 30 June 2026 to 18 October 2026**.

In the Bocconi online application, the maximum amount students can request cannot exceed the tuition and fees of the chosen master program.

All information about loans available and how to apply can be found at this [link](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/services/student-loans-0).

**Grants and Tuition waivers**

A number of tuition waivers are offered by the LLM in Law of Internet Technology corporate partners. Tuition waivers cover fully or partially the annual tuition fees of the LLM program and are assigned exclusively based on merit after  positively selected applicants of selection rounds have confirmed their enrollment by paying the first Instalment of the tuition fees.

Since 2017, tuition waivers have been kindly offered by: **4IP Council**, **BNP Paribas France, Emlex**, **Google**, **Osborne Clarke**, **Parola Associati**, **Portolano Cavallo Law Firm**, **Trevisan&Cuonzo.**
