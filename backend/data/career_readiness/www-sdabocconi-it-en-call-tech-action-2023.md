(Teleborsa) – The Call Tech Action was presented in Milan: a joint initiative by Assofintech, the Italian Insurtech Association (IIA), ICE SDA Bocconi, and the School of Management of Politecnico di Milano, under the patronage of the Municipality of Milan. A synergy between industry players, educational institutions, and public bodies aimed at promoting education, training, and employment.
