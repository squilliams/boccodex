# Requirements of curricular internships for credit

**ATTENTION PLEASE! YOU'RE ACCESSING THIS PAGE VIA AN OLD LINK AND THE CONTENTS MAY BE NO LONGER VALID  (LAST UPDATE: APRIL 2024).**

**STARTING FROM MAY 2024, PLEASE REFER TO THE NEW CONTENTS AVAILABLE AT THE FOLLOWING LINKS FOR** [**STUDENTS**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/career-services/your-internship-start-credit-recognition "Your internship: from start to credit recognition") **AND** [**COMPANIES**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/employers-and-partners/activating-and-managing-internships "Activating and managing internships") **.**

\\*\\*\\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*

On this page you will find the requirements for recognition of credited curricular internships _._

|     |     |
| --- | --- |
| **BACHELOR** |
| **Minimum duration** | **Full-time** 6 weeks (either in Italy or abroad)<br>**Part-time** 8 weeks (either in Italy or abroad) |
| **Timing** | **From the end of the II year teaching period**.<br>_Previous internship periods do not count toward the minimum duration for recognition._<br>It may, exceptionally, be possible to pursue the curricular internship between the end of the first year teaching period and the beginning of the second year teaching period, subject to the condition that the experience must not overlap with classroom teaching activity.<br>See the [_Guide to the University_](https://didattica.unibocconi.eu/tsg/testo.php?idr=22381&comando=Base&edizione=2024&volume=N3&idAnt=22381&strperc=) for additional requirements for recognition of these experiences. |
| **Content** | Must be in line with the course of study pursued by the student. For further information consult the [_Guide to the University_](https://didattica.unibocconi.eu/tsg/testo.php?idr=22381&comando=Base&edizione=2024&volume=N3&idAnt=22381&strperc=) or the section [_Types of Internship_](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/services/types-internship). |
| **Hosting Institution** | Internships at family-owned companies are not permitted. |

|     |     |
| --- | --- |
| **MASTER OF SCIENCE** |
| **Minimum duration** | **Full-time**: 8 weeks (either in Italy or abroad) <br>**Part time**: 11 weeks (either in Italy or abroad) |
| **Time allocation** | **From the end of the I year teaching period**.<br>_Previous internship periods do not count toward the minimum duration for recognition._<br>It may, exceptionally, be possible to pursue the curricular internship after the undergraduate degree and before the beginning of the teaching period of the master of science, subject to the condition that the experience must not overlap with teaching activity.<br>See the [_appropriate section_](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/services/recognition-internships-done-between-bachelor-and-master-science-programs) and [_Guide to the University_](https://didattica.unibocconi.eu/tsg/testo.php?idr=22680&comando=Base&edizione=2024&volume=R2&idAnt=22680&strperc=) for additional requirements for recognition of these experiences. |
| **Content** | Must be in line with the course of study pursued by the student. For further information consult the [_Guide to the University_](https://didattica.unibocconi.eu/tsg/testo.php?idr=22680&comando=Base&edizione=2024&volume=R2&idAnt=22680&strperc=) or the section [_Types of Internship_](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/services/types-internship). |
| **Hosting Institution** | Internships at family-owned companies are not permitted. |

|     |     |
| --- | --- |
| **LAW** |
| **Minimum duration** | **Full time**: 8 weeks (either in Italy or abroad)<br>**Part time**: 11 weeks (either in Italy or abroad) |
| **Time allocation** | **From the end of the IV year teaching period**.<br>_Previous internship periods do not count toward the minimum duration for recognition._ |
| **Content** | Must be in line with the course of study pursued by the student. For further information consult the [_Guide to the University_](https://didattica.unibocconi.eu/tsg/testo.php?idr=23718&comando=Base&edizione=2024&volume=R5&idAnt=23718&strperc=) or the section [_Types of Internship_](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/services/types-internship). |
| **Hosting Institution** | Internships at family-owned companies are not permitted. |
