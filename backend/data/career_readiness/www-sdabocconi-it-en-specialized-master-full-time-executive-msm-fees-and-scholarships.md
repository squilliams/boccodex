## Fees and expenses

The tuition fee for the MSM 2027 program is € 30,000, payable in four installments

- First installment

**€ 8,000**

upon acceptance of admission

- Second installment

**€ 8,000**

by 25 February 2027

- Third installment

**€ 7,000**

by 29 April 2027

- Fourth installment

**€ 7,000**

by 30 June 2027

**Application fee: €120**

**The fees include:**

  - Most teaching material (although some texts must be purchased separately)

  - Access to SDA Bocconi’s facilities and database

  - Courses in Italian language, with the possibility of attaining the CILS qualification of Italian language proficiency

Participants must provide their own laptop.

**Other expenses:**

Personal expenses will vary significantly depending on each individual's lifestyle. In general, the cost of living in Milan is comparable to that of other major European cities. We aim to provide you with an accurate expectation of monthly expenses based on information provided by our full-time master students in recent years:

  - Housing: € 800-1500
  - Meals: € 450
  - Transport: € 100
  - Misc. Expenses: € 350

Total € 1,700-2,400

## Scholarships and tuition waivers

_1st round_
23 April 2026

_2nd round_
08 July 2026

_3rd round_
28 October 2026

Each year, SDA Bocconi School of Management makes multiple merit-based scholarships and tuition waivers available for outstanding candidates.

**Applications are evaluated according to the following criteria:**

- Analysis of the requirements indicated in the specific notice of competition
- Analysis of academic and professional records
- Results of the MSM selection process

**To be eligible for a tuition waiver, applicants must:**

- Submit their application for the MSM 2027 program
- Review the pre-requisites for the tuition waiverl
- Flag the tuition waiver option through the online application portal by the indicated deadline

**Important:**

- Applicants will be considered eligible for a tuition waiver only after completing the MSM application, including the online form and all supporting documents
- You can apply for scholarships and tuition waivers even if your selection process is still in progress. However, only admitted candidates will be evaluated for the awarding of a tuition waiver or scholarship
- If you meet multiple requirements, you may apply for more than one scholarship or tuition waiver

The tuition waivers will be awarded during the selection process and no later than the starting date of the MSM 2027 Program.

Participants are encouraged to explore various funding opportunities available in different countries, such as employer sponsorships or scholarships provided by private foundations, international organizations, or local governments.

### Tuition waivers MSM 2027

For the 2027 MSM edition, multiple tuition waivers (covering up to 50% of the total tuition fee) are available to outstanding candidates.

You can apply to the scholarship/ tuition waivers even though your selection process is still in progress. Admission to the program is independent of the awarding of any scholarship or tuition waiver.

\\* A candidate may not cumulatively receive more than one tuition waiver awarded by SDA Bocconi or by MSM sponsors.

## Loans and funding

The School assists candidates with their search for funding by supplying the necessary documentation and encouraging them to explore all scholarship and loan possibilities.

SDA Bocconi provides opportunities for low-interest loans to admitted students ( [International students](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/student-loans?_gl=1*w8zbv*_ga*NTAzNzc4Mzk5LjE3NDU4NDc4NDc.*_ga_50JZS9XDXR*czE3NTY4MDc5ODYkbzM5OSRnMSR0MTc1NjgwNzk4OCRqNTgkbDAkaDA.), [Italian students](https://www.unibocconi.it/it/corsi-di-studio/area-studenti-iscritti/finanziamenti-master-universitari-e-sda)), thanks to agreements with partner banks. Loan applications must be submitted by the end of month that follows your Master starting date. Furthermore, the University provides [international students](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/funding-country) with information about possible sources for financing in several countries.

For further information, please visit the Università Bocconi website.
