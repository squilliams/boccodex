# International Mobility

Immersing yourself in an international mobility program isn't just about academics - it's about expanding your cultural horizons, cultivating adaptability, and sharpening your language skills. At Bocconi, students have the opportunity to pursue academic and personal growth on a global scale. With a network of 293 Partner Schools in 56 countries, you'll find unparalleled opportunities for cultural exchange.

By becoming part of our international mobility community, you'll connect with a global network of scholars, innovators, and changemakers. Your journey starts here at Bocconi University, where the world is yours to explore!
