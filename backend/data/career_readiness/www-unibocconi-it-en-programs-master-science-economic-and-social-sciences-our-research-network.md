Our Research Network

Students in the MSc in Economic and Social Sciences have the opportunity to participate in Bocconi research center activities, including seminars organized by IGIER, DONDENA and other research centers in the areas of economics and social sciences. The IGIER-BIDSA **Visiting Students Initiative** is a selective program that allows students to get involved in one of the research centers and present a paper to the reading group with the support of a mentor. Under certain conditions, students can also access certain PhD courses in Economics and Statistics.

During the MSc in Economic and Social Sciences, students have successfully applied for internships at major international institutions, such as the European Commission (various DGs), United Nations (various bodies), the European Central Bank, the European Investment Bank, the OECD, as well as leading private firms, such as Accenture, Citibank, McKinsey & Co, HSBC Bank plc, Shell, Telecom Italia, UBS, and various NGOs.
