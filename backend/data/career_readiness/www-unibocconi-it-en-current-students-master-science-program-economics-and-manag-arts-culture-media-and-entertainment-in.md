# Intended Learning Outcomes

Economics and Management in Arts, Culture, Media and Entertainment

[Overview](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/master-science-program/economics-and-management-arts-culture-media-and-entertainment) [Governance](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/master-science-program/economics-and-manag-arts-culture-media-and-entertainment/governance) [Placement and Program Performance](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/master-science-program/economics-and-manag-arts-culture-media-and-entertainment/placement-and-program-performance) [Program structure](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/master-science-program/economics-and-manag-arts-culture-media-and-entertainment/program-structure)

During the Master of Science program, students acquire specific knowledge and skills related to economic and management subjects focused on the creative sectors of the economy.

Through the teaching activities that characterize the course, they develop skills in

- making judgements autonomously
- improving their written and oral communication
- acquiring lifelong learning skills useful for both continuing their studies and their professional futures.
