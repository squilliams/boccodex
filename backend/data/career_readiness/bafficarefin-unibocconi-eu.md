## Research units

[![Image of Rules - Research Unit Law and Economics Studies](https://baffi.unibocconi.eu/sites/default/files/styles/badge_xl/public/media/images/iStock-820844568.jpg?itok=vreUVI0y)\\
\\
**Rules - Research Unit Law and Economics Studies**\\
\\
This unit has been created with the aim of using original foundations to redefine the relationship between law and economics in the field of legal science.](https://baffi.unibocconi.eu/research-units/rules)

[![Image of SFIB - Investment Banking and Structured Finance](https://baffi.unibocconi.eu/sites/default/files/styles/badge_xl/public/media/images/iStock-611868428.jpg?itok=wFQTc-W9)\\
\\
**SFIB - Investment Banking and Structured Finance**\\
\\
This unit produces applied research with a significant impact on the community of practitioners, policymakers, and scholars in all the fields of activity related to investment banking and structured finance.](https://baffi.unibocconi.eu/research-units/sfib)

[![Image of SIL - Sovereign Investment Lab](https://baffi.unibocconi.eu/sites/default/files/styles/badge_xl/public/media/images/iStock-173022254.jpg?itok=m1qyv3_l)\\
\\
**SIL - Sovereign Investment Lab**\\
\\
This unit supports a very broad reflection on the new role of the State in economic activity.](https://baffi.unibocconi.eu/research-units/sil)

[![Image of APE - Analysis in Pension Economics](https://baffi.unibocconi.eu/sites/default/files/styles/badge_xl/public/media/images/ape.jpg?itok=Zg93E0qn)\\
\\
**APE - Analysis in Pension Economics**\\
\\

[![Image of ASSET - Asset and Risk Management](https://baffi.unibocconi.eu/sites/default/files/styles/badge_xl/public/media/images/iStock-155392965.jpg?itok=JT9lJucY)\\
\\
**ASSET - Asset and Risk Management**\\
\\
This unit focuses on pricing factors in the commodities market, the dynamic relationship between sovereign bond base and CDS premia, cross-asset contagion, and the true risk-adjusted benefit of hedge funds.](https://baffi.unibocconi.eu/research-units/asset)

[![Image of BAFIR - Banking, Finance and Regulation](https://baffi.unibocconi.eu/sites/default/files/styles/badge_xl/public/media/images/iStock-639666654.jpg?itok=eU792LVT)\\
\\
**BAFIR - Banking, Finance and Regulation**\\
\\
This unit focuses on issues related to financial intermediation, financial stability and regulation, and sovereign debt.](https://baffi.unibocconi.eu/research-units/bafir)

[![CCR](https://baffi.unibocconi.eu/sites/default/files/styles/badge_xl/public/media/images/CCR.jpg?itok=0N5FFd0b)\\
\\
**CCR - Competiveness, Competition and Regulation**\\
\\
This unit focuses on the study of competition and economic regulation in strategic sectors](https://baffi.unibocconi.eu/research-units/bafir)

[![Image of CLEAN - Crime: Law and Economic Analysis](https://baffi.unibocconi.eu/sites/default/files/styles/badge_xl/public/media/images/iStock-609931412.jpg?itok=Qbm18hsq)\\
