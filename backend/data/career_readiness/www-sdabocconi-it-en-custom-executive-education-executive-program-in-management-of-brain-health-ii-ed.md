### Objective

This unique training course is dedicated to the community of neuroscience professionals, a field that is constantly innovating and evolving and central to the future of modern medicine.

The program includes five training sessions and aims to foster the consolidation of a set of executive skills in participants, functional to their professional development along with the search for an innovative strategic-organizational positioning for neuroscience, with a special focus devoted to the issue of governance of chronic care-giving.

- Strategizing for the development of services in the field of neuroscience, identifying levers of change for a new role in NHS arrangements with reference to the organizational and service "frontiers" of the field
- Repositioning and reconfiguring the field of neurosciences, within the evolution of models of integrated management of chronicity and taking care of patients
- Governing the cost dynamics and effectiveness of chronicity caretaking services within a logic of value creation
- Effectively managing services in the neuroscience field, in relation to the challenges of employee motivation, work organization, skill mix, building corporate and inter-agency networks, quality, and a renewed clinical role.

## Who should apply

Admission is reserved to **40 medical executives working in the neuroscience context** in the public and private sectors who hold or will hold managerial and/or executive positions in the near future, through the advanced course.

##### Selection process

The program will accept a maximum of 40 participants, selected by a Scientific Committee of referees from SDA Bocconi.

All participants must be in possession of a bachelor's degree, with work experience of a minimum of 4 years and an age not exceeding 55 years.

To participate in selection, interested parties will have to access the website (sdabocconi.it/epmabh) and fill out an application for admission, which includes the following documentation:

- Curriculum Vitae
- Identification Document (ID Card / Passport)
- Passport photo
- Copy of Degree Certificate / Self-certification of Degree (if obtained in Italy
- Letter of motivation
