# Credit recognition procedure

**ATTENTION PLEASE! YOU'RE ACCESSING THIS PAGE VIA AN OLD LINK AND THE CONTENTS MAY BE NO LONGER VALID  (LAST UPDATE: APRIL 2024).**

**STARTING FROM MAY 2024, PLEASE REFER TO THE NEW CONTENTS AVAILABLE AT THE FOLLOWING LINKS FOR** [**STUDENTS**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/career-services/your-internship-start-credit-recognition "Your internship: from start to credit recognition") **AND** [**COMPANIES**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/employers-and-partners/activating-and-managing-internships "Activating and managing internships") **.**

\\*\\*\\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*

Recognition of an internship in the study plan requires two mandatory steps:

- before internship: advance authorization
- at conclusion of internship: final validation

An internship can be recognized only if it has been properly activated. Check that the correct activation procedure has been followed in the [appropriate section](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/services/internship) (Activating an internship).

Below are the instructions to be followed in order to ensure recognition of an internship in your study plan.

You can get a feedback on the evolution of the internship recognition process through the [**CHECK YOUR INTERNSHIP widget**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/services/check-your-internship) available in the INTERNSHIP section of your You@B diary.

|     |     |     |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **WHO** |  | **WHAT** |
| **The Internship Office** | 1 | Evaluates the internship and makes available the “Request for credits recognition” button (advance authorization) on JobGate. |
| **You** | 2 | Access JobGate in the Agenda and request prior authorization in the area "My internships", section "Training project", with a click on the "Request credits" button. |
| **The Director of the degree course or his/her delegate** | 3 | Evaluates the internship and may grant or refuse **advance authorization** or, in case of need of further information, invite you either for a personal interview or a conversation via Help&Contact.<br>You may view the outcome of your request in the Calendar in the "My internships" area of JobGate, in the section "Training project", at the foot of the page (“Recognition of credits”) or directly in the Punto Blu in the "Career" section. |
| **The employer / You** | 4 | On the first day of the internship, sends/send **the original of the internship document**, signed by all parties, to the Internships Office.<br>The internship document, bearing all required signatures, is made available in electronic format in the "Attachment Area" of JOBGATE (in "My internships") and is a fundamental element for recognition (this does not apply for internships abroad activated through a contract provided by the employer). |
| **You** | 5 | Make sure that any suspensions or interruptions will not affect the **minimum duration** required for recognition of the internship (to check the requirements, [click here](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/services/requirements-curricular-internships-credit)). |
| **The employer** | 6 | Completes its final evaluation of the internship (the tutor will receive a dedicated message via e-mail). |
| **You** | 7 | Complete your final evaluation of the internship (you will receive a dedicated message in the Calendar).<br>_In case of a field project, upload in the "Attachment Area" of JobGate (in "My internships") a report on the research activity or any paper/material prepared during the project._ |
| **The Internship Office** | 8 | Checks the documentation (the internship document, the respective evaluations by the intern and the company tutor) and submits the internship dossier to the Director of the degree course for final validation. |
| **The Director of the degree course or his/her delegate** | 9 | Evaluates the internship and may grant or refuse **final validation** or, in the case of need of further information, invite you either for a personal interview or a dialog via Help & Contact. |
| **The Academic Affairs Division** | 10 | Registers the internship on your study plan.<br>_For undergraduates and law students that don't have a mandatory internship in their degree course, they must modify their study plan so as to include it._<br>_Once you have obtained final recognition of the internship, you will be able to check the proper registration of the formative credits, also through the Calendar in the "My internships" area of JobGate, or in the Punto Blu in the "Career" section._ |

_Recognition of a curricular internship for credits involves several actors: the time required to record credits depends on how rapid they all are. To keep this time to a minimum, make sure that both evaluation forms (of the intern and tutor) are filled out and that the internship document (in the case of activation through Bocconi documents) has been returned to the Internships Office._

Credits are usually registered in the study plan **after the end of an internship**. Exceptions for **advance recognition of an internship**– when it has not ended but meets the requirements for minimum duration – can be made in the following cases:

\- graduation before the end of the internship;

\- admission in a Bocconi Master of Science program;

\- application for a scholarship.

You will need to make a request for advance recognition to the Internship Office through B in touch. They will then make the final evaluation forms available in advance.

In general, all requirements – including the minimum duration – must be met by the deadline for the study plan completion for graduation or the other deadlines related to the cases in indicated above.
