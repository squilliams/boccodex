### Empowering female leadership

SDA Bocconi is committed to encouraging management training for women.

The educational dimension is one of the concrete levers that we can give men, women and our organizations to really change things, have impact. Providing training means offering a concrete tool for social change and transformation.

To foster and contribute to women’s professional development, SDA Bocconi School of Management offers them **until 8 March 2027: a 20% discount** on the participation fee for all Executive Open Programs and Executive Online Programs.

This promotion is valid only for SDA Bocconi's Executive Education and Online Executive Education programs starting in the above period, and cannot be combined with any other discount.

[Executive Open Programs](https://www.sdabocconi.it/en/on-campus-executive-education/) [Executive Online Programs](https://www.sdabocconi.it/en/online-executive-education/)

### 30% Club “Growth through Diversity"

SDA Bocconi demonstrates its commitment to diversity and inclusion through partnerships with leading associations in this area around the world. The [30% Club through Diversity](https://30percentclub.org/) is a global association that SDA Bocconi [partners](https://30percentclub.org/business-school/) with; their goal is to achieve a 30% representation of women on boards and in top positions at companies in more than 20 countries.

## Student Clubs

Clubs are a key part of the SDA Bocconi experience, fostering connections and sparking new ideas through exchanges among students, Faculty, Alums, and industry professionals. Led by students, they require strong management skills to secure funding, develop business plans, and deal with stakeholders.

Through the variety of their focuses and the people who animate them, Clubs provide a space for expression, dialogue and inclusion, helping to make our community more open, equitable and representative.
