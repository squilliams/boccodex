# Executive Master in Business of Events

## Your event that will leave a legacy.

Arts, sports and events

_Start_ Passed

_Duration_ 16 months

_Where_ Lausanne / Milano / Paris / Riyadh / Roma / Online

_Format_ Executive

_Language_ English

The **Executive Master in Business of Events (EMBE)** is designed for Sports, Entertainment and Exhibitions Industries. It is a global experience taught over 16 months across 5 cities throughout Europe and the Middle East.

EMBE is a unique program that offers participants with profound **exploration of the ecosystems** that scaffold the **events industry**, combining the managerial and entrepreneurial dimensions.

The Advisory Council, global leaders with unique experiences in the field, and the Faculty will discuss theoretical models and empirical examples from leading companies and organizations to unfold and examine different dimensions of the complexity of the industry.

## Why EMBE

- ### Develop a business event acumen

The business of events is more than organizing the events. It is about having the right approach for an entrepreneurial and proactive mindset so that you can create additional values for the events you manage.

- ### Access to international, high-profile networks

Four countries. Five cities. Endless contacts. The program will connect you to established and growing markets of events and will interconnect you with the network of the key leaders of the industry.

- ### Get a practical, hands-on perspective on events

The program framework integrates managerial and entrepreneurial skills, offering a pragmatic, applied approach across various industry sectors.

- ### Embrace the event mindset

The event mindset is not only a business mindset. It involves a wide range of perspectives. You will develop a set of essential skills, tools and techniques to navigate the complex world of events.

- ### Adopt a long-term vision of events

Events should go beyond a moment in time. You will develop specific core competencies and soft skills that will leave a positive impact: a legacy that will last much longer than the event itself.

## A comprehensive learning experience

- ### Learning environment

EMBE integrates in-class learning, field experiences, and online sessions. You’ll explore the business of events through lectures, real-world projects, and faculty guidance—gaining a dynamic, hands-on perspective across formats and locations, with continuity that strengthens your growth.

- ### Face-to-face modules

Deepen your understanding of the event industry through a mix of theory and practice. Interact with executives, connect with the SDA Bocconi network, and take part in field visits and observation activities for a hands-on, international learning experience.

- ### Synchronous distance learning

Online lectures and group work focus on real-world event topics, supported by dedicated faculty advising on your Event Project. The remote sessions complement in-person learning, ensuring flexibility while maintaining continuity and a strong learning journey.

- ### Event project

Apply what you've learned by working on a real Event Project—either within your own company or a key partner organization. With the guidance of a SDA Bocconi tutor, you’ll transform insights from the program into concrete, value-driven outcomes.

- ### Observation Programs

Get behind the scenes of major events through exclusive Observation Programs. Organized with top industry players, these oprionale and extracurricular experiences offer first-hand insights into the complexity of event planning and execution.

## EMBE: a crossroads of talent

The Executive Master in Business of Events is a launchpad for your managerial career across the events industry.

You’ll gain the strategic know-how and leadership skills to excel in:

- Event management agencies
- Corporate event departments
- Trade fair and expo organizations
- Specialized consulting and service providers
- Marketing and communications firms focused on events
- Nonprofit and government bodies that host large-scale initiatives

By completing EMBE, you’ll position yourself for a diverse range of opportunities, steering high-impact projects and shaping the future of event experiences.
