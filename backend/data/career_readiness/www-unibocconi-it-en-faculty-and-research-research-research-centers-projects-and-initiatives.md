# Projects and Initiatives

Bocconi Student Associations

Dedicated to raising awareness on sustainability issues within the Bocconi community, the student association Green Light for Business collaborates with the MUSA-PNRR project by organizing joint events and preparing materials with a focus on finance for sustainability.

[https://greenlight4business.com/](https://greenlight4business.com/)

Bachelor of Science Courses

**Financial Investments Course**

On a voluntary basis, participants are encouraged to choose a topic and present, in an extremely simplified yet meticulous way, concepts useful for financial decisions. The best outputs are included on this page.

Financial Education Podcasts

" [Finanza in ascolto](https://omny.fm/shows/egea/playlists/finanza-in-ascolto)" is a financial education podcast produced by Bocconi University's Financial Education Lab in collaboration with Egea. It is part of Spoke 4 activities within the MUSA (Multilayered Urban Sustainability Action) project, funded by Italy's National Recovery and Resilience Plan (PNRR)
