# Master in Imprenditorialità e Strategia Aziendale

## Allenati a innovare.

Entrepreneurship and family business

_Start_ January 2027

_Duration_ 12 months

_Where_ Milano

_Format_ Full-time

_Language_ Italian

_Avg. working experience_ 3 years

**Sustainability, digital transformation, technological revolution:** in an ever-changing environment, interpreting change and turning it into opportunities requires creativity, strategic vision and entrepreneurial skills. The speed with which new trends develop makes it necessary to be able to read these changes and create innovative solutions, putting innovation at the center of one's work.

To achieve this goal, the **Master in Imprenditorialità e Strategia Aziendale** offers an integrated approach that combines academic knowledge with practical application through individual projects. During the course, students will gain in-depth knowledge in business administration and entrepreneurship/innovation.

These two pillars form the foundation of the academic pathway, providing students with the skills needed to excel in various facets of the corporate world. This combination of academic knowledge and practical application will best prepare them to become the change agents in tomorrow's corporate world.

With a **'learning by doing’** teaching methodology, characterized by **integrated project works** which allow the theoretical concepts learned in the classroom to be put into practice, MISA aims to train a new generation of leaders capable of successfully meeting the challenges of the ever-changing corporate world.

## Why MISA

- ### For effective learning

We offer you a pragmatic and hands-on learning method that engages you from the very beginning with real innovation and business development projects — the best way to test your skills and fully express your potential.

- ### For the educational experience

We offer you high-level management content and professional skills through high-impact teaching tools, direct and engaging experiences, and a practical edge that is always measured against the real dynamics of the working world.

- ### For the opportunity to work on your project

During the course, you will have the unique opportunity to work on your own personal project, whether it is an ambitious start-up, an improvement in your family business, or a research project in a company.

- ### For international openness

We have designed a learning journey that is rooted in Italian entrepreneurship of excellence, but is also designed for those who will increasingly have to deal with the international economic scenario and job market

Thanks to our Faculty, guest speakers, fellow participants, and a community of over 146,000 Alumni, we welcome you into a network of relationships with strong professional and personal value — a network that will accompany you throughout your life.

- ### For the location

Classes are held at the SDA Bocconi Campus, located in the heart of Milan — a vibrant European city and the entrepreneurial capital of Italy, that offers the very best of Italian culture, design, fashion, art, and lifestyle.

Timeline

Calendar

#### Step 1 - Warm up

Warm-up is a preparatory phase devoted to basic topics, addressed either through distance learning or through self-study of materials selected by Faculty. At the end, there is a follow-up test with evaluation.

January 2027April 2027

#### Step 2 - Foundations

This phase aims to consolidate a common knowledge base on a number of key topics throughout the training, including competitive strategies, analysis of budget results, and skills in organization, marketing, finance, and entrepreneurship. There are also courses dedicated to developing teamwork skills, effective communication in public, and the use of databases.

May 2027July 2027

#### Step 3 - Elaboration and integration

This phase is devoted to developing knowledge of key management practices through three interdisciplinary modules:

**Operational excellence:** related to processes and control systems for continuous improvement of business activities in production and service areas.

**Growth Strategy:** the basics of building the company's growth strategies (internationalization, acquisitions and M&A, private equity and IPOs) and their evaluation.

**Beyond the core:** approaches and methodologies to explore new opportunities for launching innovative products and services, changing business models and innovating processes.

September 2027October 2027

#### Step 4 - Exploration and experimentation

This phase represents the application core of the Master. This is a key time to confront real projects, face concrete business innovation challenges, and touch on the processes and strategies that can lead a business to change, grow and develop.

In particular, this session of MISA is devoted to the practical application of three key themes:

**Creativity and design thinking**

It stimulates entrepreneurial attitude and allows you to experience firsthand the launch of a start-up, developing all stages of its creation, with final presentation in front of potential investors.

**Evolving scenarios**

Through a theoretical approach, the course provides an overview on the major trends that are shaping the future of business, stimulating reflection on the opportunities and challenges arising from them.

**Digital transformation**

Offers in-depth and applied knowledge of business models and digital innovation within traditional enterprises, showing their implications for all business functions.

November 2027December 2027

## A comprehensive learning experience

- ### A balance between theory and practice

The program combines face-to-face lectures on Monday through Wednesday, half a day on campus and half a day devoted to extra-campus activities (case-study analysis, individual and/or group exercises, learning and preparation for exams), with sessions devoted to project work on Thursday and Friday, offering a balance of theory and practical application.

The program is taught mainly in Italian but also includes teaching materials and activities in English (international series, international camp).

- ### Learning excellence

MISA has achieved the AACSB international certification, which is awarded to the most important business schools in the world. In addition to constituting a guarantee of program quality, this accreditation certifies the educational objectives that are achieved through the Master's program.

The certified objectives of MISA cover the development of the following skills:

Strategic Thinking and Problem Solving Skills, Entrepreneurial Mindset and Creativity, Management Knowledge, Team Work and Leadership Skills, Project & Process Management Skills, Diversity and Global Business Issues, Ethics and Integrity.

## Admissions

We are looking for participants with strong motivation, diverse profiles and well-defined personal qualities. Admission is through a rigorous selection process that evaluates individual aptitudes and aspirations, ensuring a challenging and dynamic MISA class group is created each year.

**Requirements**:

- Bachelor of Science or four-year degree in any discipline or equivalent foreign degree
- Those who will graduate within three months of the start of teaching activities (by the end of April 2026) are also eligible for selection. Such candidates (conditionally enrolled) will finalize their enrollment in the Master program after obtaining the degree.
- Application for admission
- Brief professional experience
- Knowledge of the English language, both written and spoken
- SDA test
- Interview
- Basic computer skills

The following are considered preferential factors for admission: TOEFL / IELTS / PTE / CAE and GMAT / GRE scores.
