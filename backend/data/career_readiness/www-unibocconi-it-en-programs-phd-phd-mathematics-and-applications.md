The PhD Program in Mathematics and Applications is a four-year doctoral program taught in English. It is fully funded by the **Tech Europe Foundation (TEF)**. **First cohort: a.y. 2026–2027.**

Based in the Department of Decision Sciences, it couples rigorous foundations in **Mathematical Analysis**— including the opportunity to tackle some of today’s most fascinating problems — with interdisciplinary links to economics, finance, decision theory and computer science. Students join a vibrant, international research environment supported by a faculty with a strong record of excellence, including several ERC grantees.

## The Program

_**Why a PhD in Mathematics?**_

Mathematics underpins breakthroughs across science and society. A PhD builds the depth and creativity to **tackle challenging problems**, model complex systems and design algorithms with real-world impact — preparing graduates for leadership in research-intensive settings.

_**Why Bocconi for Mathematics?**_

Bocconi offers a strong mathematical community embedded in the social, data and decision sciences, with close interaction with the computing sciences **,** optimization and machine learning. This setting bridges foundational theory and high-impact applications.

## **Curriculum and Timeline**

**First year.** Core advanced training in **Mathematical Analysis** (PDEs, variational methods, geometric analysis) that brings students quickly to the research frontier; electives from mathematics or, depending on interests, **economics, finance, statistics or computer science**.

**Second year.** This preparation — with a **view toward applications**— is completed through research-oriented courses (including visiting professors) while deepening the individual research started in the first year.

**Third and fourth years.** Fully devoted to research and thesis writing, with opportunities for international research visits and exchanges.

## **Faculty and Visiting Scholars**

Our [faculty](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/phd/phd-mathematics-and-applications/faculty "Faculty") includes internationally acknowledged researchers in **Mathematical Analysis and Applied Mathematics**, **Decision Sciences,** and **Mathematical Finance**. The program is supplemented by visiting professors who deliver short monographic courses on frontier topics.

## **Who We Are Looking For**

We welcome highly motivated applicants with a strong analytical background (MSc in Mathematics, Computer Science, Physics, Economics, Data Science, Engineering or related fields). **Two pathways:** a fully analysis-centered track, or an **interdisciplinary** track spanning economics, finance and machine learning via targeted electives and projects.

## **Careers**

Graduates may pursue careers in:

- academic and institutional research
- quantitative roles in industry and finance
- strategy consulting, operation research, optimization and AI/ML labs.
