# Career Services

Bocconi Career Services **support students and graduates as they enter the job market**, providing tools and advice to help them reach their professional goals. Close ties with the business community and organizations have enabled **successful partnerships with employers**, allowing the **recruitment of qualified talents**.

With the goal of fostering contacts between students and employers worldwide, **Bocconi Career Services organize a portfolio of activities** **related to recruiting, networking and career counseling**, available throughout the academic year.
