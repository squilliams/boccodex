# Innovation, digital transformation and AI

Artificial intelligence, advanced predictive models, and business intelligence are revolutionizing work and pushing companies to rethink their activities. It is essential to build a “data culture” across all management functions and develop the skills needed to evolve practices, service models, and strategies, maximizing impact on growth and future development.

In this section, you will find the School’s full educational portfolio dedicated to this topic, along with events, news, research activities, and in-depth content.
