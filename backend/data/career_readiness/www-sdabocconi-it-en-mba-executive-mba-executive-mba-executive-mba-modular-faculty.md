# A distinguished faculty

The faculty of SDA Bocconi School of Management serve as the cornerstone of the Executive MBA. Throughout the program, their teaching is complemented by contributions from managers, entrepreneurs, and representatives of the Italian and international business community. These engagements bring valuable perspectives and experiences, enhancing the program with meaningful insights.
