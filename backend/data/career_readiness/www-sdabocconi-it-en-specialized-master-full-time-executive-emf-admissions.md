## The EMF Admission Application

To apply for the EMF program, you must complete and submit the online admission application after registering on the admissions portal, through which you will receive your personal PIN and password.

The selection process for the Executive Master in Finance – EMF XI (2027–2028 edition) will take place between **March 2026 and January 2027.**

Given the limited number of places available, starting the admission process early increases your chances of success and of obtaining scholarships awarded to outstanding profiles within the deadlines set for each selection round. You may submit the application by the deadline of the relevant round and complete it later with the required documents. Submitting the application within the deadlines and applying for scholarships and tuition waivers ensures your eligibility for consideration.

The Admissions portal is a platform separate from the website _www.sdabocconi.it_; therefore, you will need to complete a new registration.

To submit your application to the EMF program, you must complete the online admission form. Please fill it out carefully to provide all necessary information regarding your personal and professional experience, as well as your motivations for choosing this Master.

You will be asked to select your specialization track at the time of the application, and you may also indicate additional tracks of interest in order of preference. Please read the general information contained within the application carefully.

## Admissions results

During the 4 rounds of selection, upon completion of the admission process you will receive a formal letter from the Admissions committee, notifying you of the admission decision. Selection results are never communicated by telephone. All decisions made by the Admissions committee are final.

The selection process can result in one of the following outcomes:

- **Admission:** you will receive the official letter of admission and contract for participation in EMF, which you must confirm by the deadline specified by the committee. If no more places are available for your Track of specialization, the school may offer you an alternative among other Tracks, place you on a waiting list, or offer you admission to the next edition.

Admission to the program is valid for the year the application is submitted. However, you can request deferment to the following year, but approval of deferment is on a case-by-case decision by the Admissions committee.

- **Non-admission:** the selection process evaluates not only the individual profile but also the overall composition and specialty of Track of the EMF class group. While we acknowledge the significant effort all candidates invest in the application and the selection process, we do not provide personalized feedback to non-admitted candidates.

## How to apply

The **Application** for EMF 2027-2028 is available **exclusively online** and must be completed with accurate and precise information regarding your personal and professional experiences, your reasons for attending the program, and other aspects of your personality.

At the time of application you must indicate the specialization track you have chosen, possibly indicating additional tracks of interest in order of preference. Please read carefully the general information within the application itself.

**Please note that the online application portal is a separate platform, so your SDA Bocconi website (** _**www.sdabocconi.it**_ **) credentials cannot be used. Please proceed with a new registration:**

- All required documents must be uploaded to the relevant section of the **Online application** portal and submitted to the Admissions team.
- Applicants can check the receipt of their application and monitor its status directly through the online platform.
- Additional documents can be uploaded after the initial application submission
