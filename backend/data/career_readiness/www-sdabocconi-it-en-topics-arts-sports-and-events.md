# Arts, sports and events

Effective management of cultural institutions, sports clubs and leagues, and live festivals and events requires a blended skill set: strategy, marketing and sponsorship combined with audience & fan engagement; facility & venue management aligned with safety and sustainability. The result is memorable experiences and demonstrable impact on cities and communities.

In this section, you will find the School’s full educational portfolio dedicated to this topic, along with events, news, research activities, and in-depth content.
