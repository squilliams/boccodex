# Recognition of internships done between the Bachelor and the Master of Science programs

**ATTENTION PLEASE! YOU'RE ACCESSING THIS PAGE VIA AN OLD LINK AND THE CONTENTS MAY BE NO LONGER VALID  (LAST UPDATE: APRIL 2024).**

**STARTING FROM MAY 2024, PLEASE REFER TO THE NEW CONTENTS AVAILABLE AT THE FOLLOWING LINKS FOR** [**STUDENTS**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/career-services/your-internship-start-credit-recognition "Your internship: from start to credit recognition") **AND** [**COMPANIES**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/employers-and-partners/activating-and-managing-internships "Activating and managing internships") **.**

\\*\\*\\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*

The University may recognize as a curricular internship an **internship pursued after obtaining of the Bachelor of Science degree and before the beginning of the Master of science program.**

You can request the Internships Office to authorize recognition of such experience through B in touch (the Office will discuss the matter with the Director of the degree course) before the end of first year teaching term (end of second semester).

Below, the instructions to be followed in order to ensure recognition in your curriculum of studies of an internship pursued between the Bachelor of Science and Master programs.

|     |     |     |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **WHO** |  | **WHAT** |
| **You** | 1 | Request authorization for recognition of the experience at the Internship Office via B in touch.<br>At the same time, send in attachment through B in touch:<br>— the document underlying the internship relationship with the employer that hosted you, e.g. a contract (if the internship was not activated through Bocconi University) in Italian or English;<br>— an internship certificate printed on the company’s letterhead and signed by a company representative in Italian or English;<br>— a three-year degree diploma (if obtained at a university other than Bocconi);<br>— a 2-page report written by yourself analysing activities done, goals achieved and consistency with your current study path in English. |
| **The Internship Office** | 2 | Evaluates the experience and/or the documentation provided, registers it on the JobGate portal and enables the request for credits (advance authorization).<br>_At this point the internship will appear in the "My internships" area of JobGate in the yoU@B Agenda._ |
| **You** | 3 | Access JobGate in the Calendar, in the area "My internships", section "Training project", **confirm** the internship with a click on the appropriate button and request **advance authorization** with a click on the "Request for recognition of credits" button on the same page. |
| **The Director of the degree course or his/her delegate** | 4 | Evaluates the internship and may grant or refuse **final validation** or, in the case of need of further information, invite you either for a personal interview or a conversation via B in touch. |
| **The Academic Affairs Division** | 5 | Registers the internship in your study plan.<br>_Once you have obtained final recognition of the internship, you will be able to check the proper registration of the formative credits, also through the Calendar in the "My internships" area of JobGate, or in the Punto Blu in the "Career" section._ |
