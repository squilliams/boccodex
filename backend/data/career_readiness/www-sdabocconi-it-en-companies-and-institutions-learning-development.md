# Learning & Development

Learning opportunities designed to support the growth of individuals, teams, and organizations. From focused, agile programs to fully customized projects and in-depth degree paths, three approaches, one common goal: creating lasting value.

We work with companies and institutions to co-design learning projects that respond to specific needs and development goals. Each solution is shaped around your organizational, cultural, or operational context — with flexible formats, timing, and content.

### Choose the right program to grow people and skills

A diverse portfolio of short and medium-length programs designed to support the reskilling and upskilling of individual professionals or teams. Companies can select among a wide range of topics, industries, and seniority levels to activate effective, immediately applicable learning paths. Programs are delivered both online and on campus, encouraging cross-company exchange, new perspectives, and meaningful networking.

[Discover more](https://www.sdabocconi.it/en/on-campus-executive-education/) [open@sdabocconi.it](mailto:open@sdabocconi.it)

An investment in one individual that generates value across the entire organization. Our full-time and part-time MBAs and Specialized Masters offer a deep and structured growth journey — designed for professionals ready to take a leap forward, with the support of their company or institution.
