# MBA: Spotlight on our concentrations

General management and strategy

![Milan event - SDA Bocconi](https://www.sdabocconi.it/_next/image/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fsda-website-prod-sdawebsiteprodbucketpublic693d05b-w3mwb4fo9x9p.s3.eu-south-1.amazonaws.com%2Fs3fs-public%2F2025-01%2Fmilan-event-visual-desktop.jpg&w=3840&q=90&dpl=dpl_EgyAUeGYW1L1ERS2WGuDkM3HLa8o)

_Start_ 12 February 2025

07:00 - 08:00 New York City

New York City (-05.00)

_Language_ English

Milano

Online

Join us for an in-depth presentation of the [Full-Time MBA](https://www.sdabocconi.it/en/full-time-mba), where you’ll have the opportunity to interact with the Director as well as the Recruiting & Admissions Team. This event offers a unique opportunity to explore how the program can elevate your professional value and expand your network within a global context.

You will also have the chance to learn about the program’s five concentrations, designed to enhance the flexibility and customization of the MBA journey: **Finance and Private Investment**, **Innovation and Entrepreneurship**, **Customer Experience Management**, **Luxury Business Management, Digital Transformation and AI**. Each concentration allows to focus on a specific managerial subject that fits your preferences and skills.

We look forward to welcoming you and introducing you to the SDA Bocconi world.
