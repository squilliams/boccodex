PhD in Business Administration and Management

[Overview](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/phd/phd-business-administration-and-management) [Courses and Requirements](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/phd/phd-business-administration-and-management/courses-and-requirements) [Faculty](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/phd/phd-business-administration-and-management/faculty) [Current PhD Students in Business Administration and Management](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/phd/phd-business-administration-and-management/current-phd-students-business-administration-and-management) [Job Market Candidates](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/phd/phd-business-administration-and-management/job-market-candidates) [Placement](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/phd/phd-business-administration-and-management/placement) [Info for Current Students](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/phd/phd-business-administration-and-management/info-current-students)

In accordance with ministerial regulations, the Faculty Board is composed of at least twelve members (of whom at least 50% are full professors and associate professors).

The Faculty Board coordinates and supervises study and research activities carried out by PhD students; it monitors and assesses the performance of the program as a whole, and proposes possible improvement actions.
