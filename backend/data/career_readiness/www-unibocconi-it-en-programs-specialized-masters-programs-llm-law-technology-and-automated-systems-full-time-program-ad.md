For the **2025-2026 academic year,** the application calendar will be as follows:

|     |     |     |     |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Calendar | Online application (deadline 3pm Italian time) | Selection results | Enrollment (3pm Italian time) |
| **I Round** | November 28, 2024 -  <br>February 12, 2025 | March 6, 2025 | March 13, 2025 |
| **II Round** | February 13, 2025 - <br>9 June 2025 | June 26, 2025 | July 3, 2025 |

Please note that the timeline can be subject to some changes during the selection period.

Results will be released online approximately 3 weeks after the application deadlines.

In order to be evaluated, applicants must meet the following requirements:

- A degree in Law, i.e.:

a) a 5-year Integrated Master of Arts in Law or 3+2 years Master of Arts in Law from an Italian university,

or

b) equivalent degree in Law from a university abroad.

Please note that University studies in other fields may also be taken into consideration where significant experience in an LLM-related field is proved.\*
- English knowledge at least at the B2 level

The selection process is based on the evaluation of a **dossier** including:

- Academic curriculum (i.e. University transcript of records)
- Curriculum vitae
- Personal statement
- Reference letters (up to 2)
- Copy of an English language certificate (at least B2 level)

Interview (if needed). Applicants are encouraged to complete a video interview (in English). The video interview is recommended, though not compulsory. See all the [specific information about the procedure](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/specialized-masters/llm-law-internet-technology/video-interview) for the video interview (through the [Kira Talent Portal](https://app.kiratalent.com/n/assessment/4j5vgO)).

\* Final-year students, who have not completed their degree can apply and be admitted upon condition that they receive their degree within 90 days from the beginning of the program.
