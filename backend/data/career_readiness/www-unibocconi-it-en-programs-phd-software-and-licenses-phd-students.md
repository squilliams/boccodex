**PhD students have the chance to get some software installed on their personal laptops.** For the installation you can come to Via Gobbi 5 (Library building), 4th floor, room 409:

-Monday to Thursday from 9:00am-12:00pm or from 2:00-4:00pm

-Friday from 9:00am-12:30pm

**The software available is listed below.**

**Matlab**

**SAS  -**  not compatible with Windows 8 or MAC

**Scientific Workplace  -**  not compatible with MAC **SPSS**

**Stata (Intercooled version only) -** this licence lasts 6 months from the purchase date and is available for installation twice a year: end of January and beginning of July; there is a limited number of licenses available, thus an early installation request  is recommended **Latex -** can be downloaded for free in the internet

Please remember that desktop pcs placed in PhD students’ offices belong to the university and may be subject to adjustments and formatting without notice, thus no personal or work documents should be saved on them.
