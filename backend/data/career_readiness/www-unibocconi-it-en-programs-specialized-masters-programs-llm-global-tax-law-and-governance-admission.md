**Selection Criteria**

Candidates who meet the entry requirements (type of degree, working experience length and language proficiency) are selected on the basis of the following criteria, which aims to assess the background, competences (knowledge, skills and abilities) and motivation of the student:

- **Academic curriculum** (weight 50%)

- **Curriculum vitae** (weight 20%)

- **Motivational letter** (weight 30%)

- **Rating scale**: 1 – 100

Applications must be submitted via the " [Apply2B](https://admissions.unibocconi.it/admissions/ "MyApplication")" portal
