# Network Aziende Associate CERGAS

Opportunità di crescita strategica

##### Knowledge, strategy and innovation for healthcare organizations

Healthcare

CERGAS – Research Centre on Health and Social Care Management serves as a key reference point for member organizations operating in the healthcare and pharmaceutical sectors. Through advanced research and high-level training, the network provides a unique opportunity for dialogue, continuous learning, and the development of innovative strategies to address market challenges.

###### Network meetings

The meetings are structured to offer a variety of learning and interaction formats:

- **Informing**: Online sessions for strategic updates and scenario analysis.
- **Reflecting**: In-person meetings with experts for in-depth thematic exploration.
- **Experimenting**: Hands-on workshops to develop operational strategies.
- **Leadership** **Team**: Sessions dedicated to top executives to tackle complex scenarios.

![logo_cergas](https://www.sdabocconi.it/_next/image/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fsda-website-prod-sdawebsiteprodbucketpublic693d05b-w3mwb4fo9x9p.s3.eu-south-1.amazonaws.com%2Fs3fs-public%2F2025-09%2Flogo_cergas_grande.png%3Fv%3D1758617262&w=2048&q=90&dpl=dpl_EgyAUeGYW1L1ERS2WGuDkM3HLa8o)

## The Network offers for MEMBERS:

- ### 4 INFORMING meetings

A format including 4 live synchronous online sessions focused on strategic INFORMATION and updates on key topics related to the Italian National Health Service (SSN) and the healthcare industry, open to all employees of member organizations.

- ### 3 REFLECTING meetings

Opportunities to attend in-person sessions with experts for deeper thematic discussions.

- ### 1 EXPERIMENTING meeting

A hands-on experience of strategic and operational planning through the development of a prototype for collaboration or partnership with a public healthcare provider.

- ### 1 LEADERSHIP TEAM meeting

Designed to support executive teams in analyzing scenarios and shaping business strategies. Format: Half-day session with CERGAS experts. Target: Leadership team of member organizations (additional option).

## Program Advisory

Learning is never a linear journey—it is a path built together, combining specific training needs with practical tools tailored to individual challenges. Contact our team of Program Advisors, and together we will design the ideal learning path for you or your team, ensuring alignment with your goals.

**Your benefits:**

A dedicated orientation meeting, either in-person or remotely, to identify learning needs and create a customized plan. Throughout the course, you will experience a tailored welcome to introduce you to the program and foster networking between participants and instructors. Upon completion, your evaluation will become a valuable contribution for continuous improvement of the offering.

**How your organization benefits:**

Training consultation with HR managers to analyze organizational needs and develop personalized projects. Ongoing progress monitoring and a final follow-up to share results and gather feedback, ensuring constant alignment with pre-set goals.
