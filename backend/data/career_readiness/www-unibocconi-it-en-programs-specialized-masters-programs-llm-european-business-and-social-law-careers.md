This program prepares participants to be experts of European legal systems and institutions.

Students will be qualified to find a position within:

- **Firms operating in the European internal market** to cover different positions depending on their first degree. If they hold a law degree, they could work in the legal team, as in-house lawyers; if they hold other degrees, they could bring their legal knowledge to various business departments, such as those of data analysis, risk assessment, marketing, communications, or finance.
- **European research centers** and **institutions** to carry out policy-oriented research and impact assessment aiming at the development pf European policies.
- **NGOs or civil society organizations** to perform advocacy activities.

The LLM students of the last two editions have interned for example at institutions and organizations like the European Commission, the European Central Bank, the International Criminal Court, the International Bar Association; at companies like Deloitte, PWC, the Walt Disney Company, IBM; and at law firms like DLA Piper, Castaldi Partners and others.
