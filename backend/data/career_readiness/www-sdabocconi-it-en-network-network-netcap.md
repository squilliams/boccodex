# Network NETCAP

Network Accounts & Checks in Public Administration

##### In response to a concrete challenge, that of having discussions to improve, NETCAP Network Accounts & Checks in Public Administration was founded.

Public management

Planning, controlling and accounting for the activities carried out by public administrations is becoming increasingly complex due to the profound changes that are taking place. The implementation of reforms in recent years has shown a good ability by individual institutions to grasp the changes inspired by the many external stimuli, but it has also highlighted problems and critical issues which are not always adequately considered in institutional and academic circles.

To respond to this concrete challenge - that of having discussions to improve, NETCAP Network Accounts & Checks in Public Administration was founded.aggiungi uno spazio

The course was initially addressed to local governments, but the growing success has led us to extend participation to the Regions and central governments, with the aim of creating a synergistic collaboration.

The lively participation obtained in previous years confirmed the validity of the formula: we brought together the knowledge of our researchers and lecturers, dedicated specifically to the public sector, with the experience of the Associated Public Bodies, with the conviction that the discussion and deepening of experiences help to facilitate the renewal and improvement of the planning, control and public finance processes.

We have met, discussed and analyzed issues and experiences, providing innovative insight and concrete solutions.

We have created a privileged relationship with Associates through the virtual community and the support of current information on the topics discussed.

## The NETCAP Network offers:

- ### 5 meetings

NETCAP organizes **5** annual **discussion days**. The first day coincides with a conference with free participation.

- ### Providing COMPETENCES

Innovation and updates of ongoing reforms in accounting, planning, control and finance, through the contribution of SDA Bocconi teachers and operators of the sector

- ### Favoring DISCUSSIONS

and the sharing of concrete experiences (benchlearning) between public and private sector actors, through meetings, workshops, targeted workshops, case studies, research and with the creation of a real Community (distance learning)

- ### Giving voice to different INSTANCES

of the operators of the Public Administrations, highlighting original solutions to common problems and choosing the topics to be covered in the classroom.

## 5 discussion meetings

NETCAP organizes 5 annual comparison days. The first day coincides with a conference with free participation.

The other four meetings have the following structure:

- CHANGE MANAGEMENT section, which aims at describing the topic;
- TOOLS section, which takes place in one or more parallel classrooms, with discussions on experiences and identification of operational tools;
- PROBLEM SOLVING section, with plenary discussion and with the presence of relevant interlocutors, with respect to the subject matter. One day is dedicated to the Workshop, which provides a full immersion on a particularly relevant and highly topical issue, comparing ideas and experimenting with innovative solutions arising from the experience of the Associates.

## Program Advisory

Learning is never a linear journey—it is a path built together, combining specific training needs with practical tools tailored to individual challenges. Contact our team of Program Advisors, and together we will design the ideal learning path for you or your team, ensuring alignment with your goals.

**Your benefits:**

A dedicated orientation meeting, either in-person or remotely, to identify learning needs and create a customized plan. Throughout the course, you will experience a tailored welcome to introduce you to the program and foster networking between participants and instructors. Upon completion, your evaluation will become a valuable contribution for continuous improvement of the offering.

**How your organization benefits:**

Training consultation with HR managers to analyze organizational needs and develop personalized projects. Ongoing progress monitoring and a final follow-up to share results and gather feedback, ensuring constant alignment with pre-set goals.
