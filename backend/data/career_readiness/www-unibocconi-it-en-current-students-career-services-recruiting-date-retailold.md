# Recruiting Date Retail\_old

**14-20 February 2022 / Live on 16 February (1pm-6pm)**

The Digital Recruiting Date is your chance to meet a selected group of employers in the Retail industry, discover the career paths they offer and apply for available job and internship opportunities.

The

initiative lasts one full week, with the following agenda:

- 14 and 15 February: Preview Days - discover company

profiles of attending employers, read their corporate info, watch their videos,

click on their LinkedIn page;

- 16 February, from

1pm to 6pm: Live Day - join the networking afternoon and meet recruiters

online, have a one-to-one chat or a video call with company representatives, get

useful information about recruiting processes and apply for open positions;

- 16-20 February: Application

Days - apply for internship and job offers according to the instructions

shared by each company in their postings.

**To join the event, registration is mandatory (starting on 4 February 2022, 1PM) and will close on 13 February at midnight or upon reaching the maximum number of participants**: **access your** **yoU@B diary, click on "Career Service" and then on “Events Calendar”**.

Remember to **allow notifications in your yoU@B app, and also to flag <Appuntamenti> from the home page settings - Calendars Configuration Widget, if you wish to automatically import your registration in the Career Services activities within your meeting calendar**, together with classes, exams and other events.

More

info on how to participate will be shared with registered students and

graduates, on 14 February.

In the meanwhile, if you wish to get your profile ready for the event, prepare

and upload your CV in yoU@B/Career Service/CV@B (the event will be held on this

platform and you will need your CV uploaded here to interact with employers).

Please

note that the Digital Recruiting Date will be open and visible only to students

and graduates from Bocconi Master of Science Programs, Integrated Master of

Arts in Law, WBB and Pre-experience Masters (up to three years after completing

their studies).
