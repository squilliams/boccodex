# Orientation Meetings - Roma

![Rome](https://www.sdabocconi.it/_next/image/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fsda-website-prod-sdawebsiteprodbucketpublic693d05b-w3mwb4fo9x9p.s3.eu-south-1.amazonaws.com%2Fs3fs-public%2F2024-12%2Froma.jpg&w=3840&q=90&dpl=dpl_EgyAUeGYW1L1ERS2WGuDkM3HLa8o)

_Start_ 11 February 2025

_Time_ 08:00 - 13:00

_Language_ Italian, English

Roma

Ready to enjoy a new vision of your career? Eager to dive into an enriching and transformative learning experience at one of Europe's premier Business Schools?

Explore SDA Bocconi's **MBA** and **Master** Programs with us and request a personalized meeting with our recruiters. **Book your 30-minute one-on-one session and we will be contacting you soon in order to arrange your meeting. You will be requested to send us your CV in advance.**

_Please note: Due to high demand, the number of requests may exceed the available meeting slots. Consequently, some candidates may be placed on a waiting list, and a remote chat may be arranged as an alternative._

For any questions, please get in contact with: [**giorgia.bava@sdabocconi.it**](mailto:giorgia.bava@sdabocconi.it)

Do not miss this chance to discover the program that best fits your profile and professional goals. We are here to change your future and give voice to your aspirations. Join us.

We look forward to welcoming you and introducing the exciting opportunities awaiting at SDA Bocconi School of Management.
