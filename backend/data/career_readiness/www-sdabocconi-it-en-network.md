# Network

## The connections that matter

The networks represent **communities of practice** and **platforms for discussion** dedicated to specific executive and sectorial fields.

**They bring together professionals, companies, and institutions with the goal of fostering knowledge exchange, innovation, and professional growth** through research activities, experience sharing, and dialogue. Each network responds to the needs of its respective sector, supporting the individuals involved in addressing emerging challenges and promoting the evolution of skills and operational strategies.
