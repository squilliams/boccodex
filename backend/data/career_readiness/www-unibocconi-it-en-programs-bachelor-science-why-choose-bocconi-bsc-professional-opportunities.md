# Professional Opportunities

The University will be with you every step of the way as you make the transition into the workforce. You can leverage our extensive network of contacts with companies, firms and institutions around the world. If you have more of an entrepreneurial spirit, you will also have opportunities to test your creativity and learn about the world of startups.

#### Our Career Services

You will receive unparalleled and personalized support throughout the career orientation and job search processes. You will have access to customized tools to successfully enter the job market and meet employers in Italy and abroad.

_Recruiting Activities in 2024_

- **10,800+** internship and placement offers
- **750+** employers involved in recruiting events, including:

  - The **Bocconi&Jobs** career event in Milan: 2 editions
  - **Bocconi&Jobs Abroad** Benelux, France, Germany & Switzerland, UK&Ireland, Spain & Portugal, Middle East
  - **Tax & Legal Networking Event** on-campus networking and recruitment event dedicated to Partner Law Firms
  - **Investment Banking Days**, a career fair with leading financial institutions
  - **Recruiting Dates** dedicated to **Fashion, Luxury and Beauty; Financial Services & Fintech; Government, Public Services & International Organizations; Arts, Sport & Entertainment; Retail; Web Companies**
  - **Recruiting events** organized with individual companies
- **3,700+** individual meetings

#### B4i

To respond to the rapidly evolving job market, Bocconi also prepares and supports students and graduates looking to turn their innovative ideas into business opportunities. Our incubator, [B4i - Bocconi for innovation](https://www.b4i.unibocconi.it/), encourages young graduates to put their entrepreneurship skills to the test, in order to accelerate the growth of **innovative startups** in any market sector.
