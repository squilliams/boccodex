## **One year after graduation\***

- **98.8%** of graduates are employed
- **43.4%** of employed graduates work abroad

**\*** Data refers to a.y. 2021-2022 graduates

## **On graduation day\*\***

- **76.3%** of graduates are employed
- **60.8%** of employed graduates work abroad
- They have been called to an average of **4.7** job interviews
- They have received on average **1.8** job offers

**\\*\\*** Survey - graduation sessions a.y. 2022-2023

The **top recruiters** of graduates include, among others:

- AB InBev
- Accenture
- Amazon

## **Graduating students - satisfaction with the Study Program**

**86.6%** of students who graduated in the 2023-24 academic year declared they were satisfied with their Study Program

[**Outcomes of graduating students’ evaluation on the Study Program (in Italian only)**](https://www.unibocconi.it/it/chi-siamo/assicurazione-qualita/esiti-valutazioni)

## **Attending students - satisfaction with teaching**

**78.1%** of students who attended courses offered in 2024-25 academic year are satisfied with the way the courses were carried out (grades from 6 to 10 on a 1-10 scale)
