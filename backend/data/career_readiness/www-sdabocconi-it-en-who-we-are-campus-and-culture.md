# Campus and culture

## Different locations, same spirit

SDA Bocconi is more than a school – it’s an ecosystem where knowledge, dialogue, and innovation come to life.

Our campuses are designed to encourage the exchange of ideas, meaningful interaction, and a strong sense of community.

Each location – Milan, Rome, Mumbai – reflects this vision, offering spaces that support learning beyond the classroom through experiences, connections, and new perspectives.

MILAN

## The world’s most sustainable campus

The Milan campus is the flagship of our vision: a place where architecture, sustainability, and research come together to create a cutting-edge learning environment.

Built around the principles of Near Zero Energy, it was recognized by the **Financial Times** as the world’s most sustainable business school campus.

It’s a space that embodies our commitment to the future – set in the economic and cultural heart of one of Europe’s most international cities.

[Discover more](https://www.sdabocconi.it/en/who-we-are/campus-and-culture/milano/)

ROME

### A place of heritage, institutions, and connections

In the heart of the capital, the Rome campus blends historical charm with a forward-looking mindset.

Villa Morgagni is a space for dialogue and growth, where culture, institutions, and business converge to shape new ideas and perspectives.

It’s a welcoming environment for those seeking to connect with a network that extends beyond the city and region.

[Discover more](https://www.sdabocconi.it/en/who-we-are/campus-and-culture/roma/)

Mumbai

### A crossroads of cultures and opportunity

The Mumbai campus is a thriving hub of exchange and growth, where people, cultures, and ideas intersect within one of the world’s most dynamic economies.

Located in Hiranandani Gardens, it’s a meeting place for academic excellence and international outlook – a space that captures our ability to understand and anticipate shifts in the global market.

[Discover more](https://www.sdabocconi.it/en/who-we-are/campus-and-culture/mumbai/)
