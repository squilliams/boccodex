# Placement at a Glance

MAFINRISK - Master of Quantitative Finance and Risk Management

[Overview](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/specialized-masters-programs/mafinrisk-master-quantitative-finance-and-risk-management) [Program Structure](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/specialized-masters-programs/mafinrisk-master-quantitative-finance-and-risk-management/program-structure) [Admissions](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/specialized-masters-programs/mafinrisk-master-quantitative-finance-and-risk-management/admissions) [Contact us](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/specialized-masters-programs/mafinrisk-master-quantitative-finance-and-risk-management/contact-us)

The majority of Mafinrisk graduates enter specific roles in trading, risk management, asset management, sales, structuring within investment banks, commercial banks or other financial institutions.

Major recruiters of Mafinrisk graduates are among top investment banks and financial firms (see the list below).

Recruiters that offered internships or permanent positions to 2024-2025 students include Mediobanca, Axa Assicurazioni SpA, Mobius Technology (Switzerland), Unicredit, Intesa Sanpaolo, Commerzbank, Banor SIM SpA, CITIBANK EUROPE PLC, Morgan Stanley, NatWest Markets N.V, Amagis Capital Management Limited (Malta), RAIFFEISEN Bank SA, Generali Asset Management, KPMG Advisory, UBS Business Solutions, SOLARIS SE.

Recruiters that offered internships or permanent positions to 2023-2024 students include Intesa Sanpaolo (Italy), Amazon EU SARL (Luxembourg), Black Peak Capital (Bulgaria), FundRock Management Company (Luxembourg), DXT Commodities (Switzerland), ALANTRA, KPMG, Mediobanca (UK), Borsa Italiana, N M Rothschild & Sons (UK).

Employers of the 2022 and 2023 Mafinrisk graduates\* include Allianz (Italy), Aramco Trading Company (UK), Banca Sella (Italy), Blackrock (UK), BNP Paribas (Italy, UK, Belgium and Portugal), Ceresio Investors (Switzerland), Crédit Agricole (Italy), Deloitte (Italy), Edison (Italy), EFG International (Switzerland), ENI (Italy), EY (Luxembourg), Generali (Luxembourg), HQ Trust (Germany), ING (the Netherlands), Intesa Sanpaolo (Italy), Jefferies (UK), KPMG (Italy), Leonteq (Italy), Messier & Associés (USA), Morgan Stanley (Hungary and UK), Sabadell Asset Management (Spain), UBS (Switzerland and Poland), Unicredit (Italy).

\*placement survey administered one year after graduation

**Career Service**

The **Bocconi Career Service**, in collaboration with **Mafinrisk**, supports students in entering the job market - helping them get to know the job market, meet employers, find internship opportunities - through a range of dedicated initiatives, including:

- on-campus company presentations, career fairs and recruiting events (Bocconi&Jobs, Investment Banking Days, Recruiting Dates);
- JobGate, an online platform, updated daily with internship and job opportunities in Italy and abroad;
- the Cv Book, an annual publication featuring the résumés of Mafinrisk students and graduates.
