### LLM in European Business and Social Law – A chat with the students

Background: Bachelor of Laws

Thomaz Braga de Arruda

Brazil

Cohort: 2020/2021

This LLM has opened the doors for my professional life, allowing me to **build an international career** from the ground. I have never seen such an attentive faculty, willing to provide guidance throughout the entire program and to really invest time and energy into its students. Bocconi’s high standards and its tradition of excellence were crucial for my professional development.

![](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/styles/card_with_photo/public/media/images/torres.jpeg.webp?itok=Ze8OEuFG)

Background: Bachelor of International Business

Irene Roura Torres

Spain

Cohort: 2022/2023

The LLM allows students to develop **critical thinking**, and provides **uniqueness** to my profile by complementing my Business studies with valuable legal expertise, necessary for effective management. The methodology enables constant learning opportunities inside and outside the lectures, and the condensed number of students encourages the creation of **a community** in which sharing experiences and knowledge comes easy.

![](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/styles/card_with_photo/public/media/images/img_1.jpeg.webp?itok=BhS71-Po)

Background: Law Degree

Federica Del Gaiso

Italy

Cohort: 2021/2022

By attending **Bocconi’s Job Fair**, I had the chance to get interviews with HRs from some of the **best law firms worldwide**. I immediately found an internship afterwards and I’m currently working in the same law firm. Without this LLM, it wouldn’t have been so simple. The people you meet here are special! And the LLM staff will follow you on every step of your journey.

![](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/styles/card_with_photo/public/media/images/hugo.jpeg.webp?itok=5Aos6pDU)

Background: Bachelor of Laws

Hugo Sériès

France

Cohort: 2020/2021

This LLM is made up of **students from all over the world**. There is a true class spirit, which is perhaps more complicated to find in a master with a larger number of students. Also, why this program? Because the teaching staff is truly incredible. Indeed, they are always there to answer the slightest problems of the students but also to **help us in our recruitment process**.

After completing my Master’s in Political Sciences and International Relations, I felt like I needed more solid legal bases: this LLM greatly expanded my knowledge in international and EU law, especially thanks to the **practical approach**. The **international and diverse** environment of Bocconi is definitely a plus, enriching students from both a professional and personal point of view.
