# Intended Learning Outcomes

Economics and Management of Innovation and Technology

[Overview](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/master-science-program/economics-and-management-innovation-and-technology) [Annual Study Program Form](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/master-science-program/economics-and-management-innovation-and-technology/annual-study-program-form) [Governance](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/master-science-program/economics-and-management-innovation-and-technology/governance) [Placement and Program Performance](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/master-science-program/economics-and-management-innovation-and-technology/placement-and-program-performance) [Program structure](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/master-science-program/economics-and-management-innovation-and-technology/program-structure)

During the Master of Science program, students acquire specific knowledge and skills related to the management of innovation and technology in all business processes.

Through the teaching activities that characterize the course, they develop skills in

- making judgements autonomously
- improving their written and oral communication
- acquiring lifelong learning skills useful for both continuing their studies and their professional futures.
