#### Graduate Job Statistics

#### One year after graduation

- _**87.3%** of graduates are employed_
- _**45.2%** of employed graduates work abroad_

Data refers to a.y. 2022-2023 graduates interviewed

#### On graduation day

- _**57.9%** of graduates are employed_
- _**38.2%** of employed graduates work abroad_
- _They have been called to an average of **3.3** job interviews_
- _They have received on average **1.6** job offers_

Survey - graduation sessions a.y. 2023-2024
