**Master of Science Program Director**

Paola Dubini

The Program Director has the responsibility of managing the Program's operation and chairs the Program Committee.

Supported by the Committee, the Director plans educational activities for the Program, coordinates teaching activities and monitors the Program.

Director **IM Concentrations**: Paola Dubini

Director **IM CEMS**: Irene Dagnino

Director **IM CHINA MIM**: Gimede Gigante

Director **IM ESSEC**: Deborah Raccagni

Director **IM Global Experience**: Massimo Magni

**Assistant to the Director**

Laura Forti

The assistant collaborates with the Director in all aspects regarding teaching and management of student relationships; in addition, the assistant ensures a proper interaction with the University administrative services.

**Administrative Assistant**

Paola Rotelli

The administrative assistant  supports the Director by carrying out operative activities, manages the administrative matters of the Program and supports students, putting them in contact with central offices and departments if necessary.
