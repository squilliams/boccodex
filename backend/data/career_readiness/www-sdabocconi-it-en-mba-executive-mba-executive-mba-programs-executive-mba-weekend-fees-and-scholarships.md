## Fees and expenses

The tuition fee for the 2026-2028 EMBA Weekend is € 62,000 (VAT exempt), payable in five installments.

- First installment

**€ 10,000**

upon acceptance of admission

- Second installment

**€ 13,000**

by 30 June 2026

- Third installment

**€ 13,000**

by 30 November 2026

- Fourth installment

**€ 13,000**

by 31 May 2027

- Fifth installment

**€ 13,000**

by 30 September 2027

**Application fee: € 150**

The fees include:

  - Internal educational materials
  - Use of the school's facilities

**Criteria and assignment procedure:**

The number of tuition waivers available for each phase and the criteria for their assignment are determined by a Commission, whose decisions are final. Beneficiaries will be notified of the outcome by the start date of the Executive MBA.

**Requirements to apply for an exemption:**

- The completed and required documentation for the Executive MBA application must be submitted.
- The exemption request must be submitted in response to the call for applications, along with the specified documents, through the Admissions portal.
- The request must be submitted within the deadlines and in compliance with the requirements outlined in the call. Requests that do not meet the requirements will not be considered.

**Evaluation criteria:**

Exemption requests will be assessed based on the following factors:

- The candidate’s academic and professional background
- Motivation letter
- Requirements defined in the call
- Results of the Executive MBA selection tests

The tuition waivers will be assigned during the selection process, within the deadlines specified in the call. **The request may be submitted even before the completion of the selection process**.

**Admission to the Executive MBA is independent of the awarding of an exemption or scholarship.**

## Loans and funding

SDA Bocconi supports candidates in their search for funding by providing the necessary documentation for financial aid applications. For more information, you can refer to the funding section of the Bocconi University website ( [Italian students](https://www.unibocconi.it/it/studenti-iscritti/agevolazioni/finanziamenti-master-universitari-e-sda), [international students](https://www.unibocconi.eu/wps/wcm/connect/bocconi/sitopubblico_en/navigation+tree/home/programs/current+students/services/funding/loans+for+specialized+master+programs)). The funding application must be submitted to the school by the end of the month following the course start date.

Additionally, the University provides [international students](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/funding-country) with information on potential funding sources in various countries around the world.
