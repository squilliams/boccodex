# Investment Banking Days

## From 5 to 25 September 2025

_Investment Banking Days is **Bocconi University's event dedicated to the Investment Banking world** and it will be held in hybrid mode.It will be structured as follows:_

### 8, 9 and 10 September: Round Tables

Join Round Tables to hear about the experience of junior Alumni working in Investment Banking.

### 8 and 9 September: Webinars

Prepare to meet with employers by attending company webinars online.

### 11 September: Networking fair on campus

From 10am to 4pm, you'll have the chance to meet with company representatives in person, interact directly with recruiters, get useful information about the hiring processes and apply.

### 8 - 25 September: Digital Campaign

Access a digital area to find out about participating employers, their recruiting programs and available career opportunities.

_**Career guidance tools** are also available to help you prepare and get the most from the event._

_The seminar will be held **online, Friday 5 September, from 1.00pm to 2.00pm.**_

_How to register: YoU@B>Career Service> Events calendar, starting from 29 August at 1.00pm._

_**Online tools (available 24/7)**_

- _**E-learning courses:** the online resources  to improve your knowledge of the industry and to better understand how to approach the job market in this field. For the Investment Banking Days, you may focus on the two following:_

_Career Services – 05 – The Investment Banking: the internship recruiting process._
