# Faculty and Advisory Council

The **SDA Bocconi EMBE Professors** are chosen based on their academic and scientific prestige, communication skills, personal charisma, reputation in the business world, and professional experience. EMBE Professors will discuss a wide range of topics with **theoretical rigor as well as constant reference to management practice**.

State of the art research guarantees vitality of our education, and spurs development of international competitive ability of the participants.

## Advisory Council

Our **elite Advisory Council** gives participants the opportunity to build strong connections with leaders in different areas of the industry.

The Advisory Council offers valuable contributions to the program with its ideas, insights and in-class presentations. The Advisory Council holds an annual meeting in a hybrid format (in-presence and online) and takes part in at least one activity with participants during the program.
