# Finance and accounting

The banking, real estate, and financial sectors are undergoing profound changes, accelerated by global market dynamics and technological innovation. For entrepreneurs, managers, and consultants, it has therefore become crucial to master the tools needed to navigate complex scenarios and support informed decision-making.

In this section, you will find the School’s full educational portfolio dedicated to this topic, along with events, news, research activities, and in-depth content.

**Acquire Lab** \\
\\
The Acquire Lab (Acquisition and Capital Qualities in Responsible Enterprises) is a research center specializing in the analysis of acquisition processes and the role of human capital in post-transaction business management. The goal of the workshop is to provide analytical tools and training to improve the quality of M&A deals and support companies and investors in their decisions, with a focus on the central role of human capital.\\
\\
Discover](https://www.sdabocconi.it/en/faculty-research/research/acquire-lab/)

**Corporate Reporting and Performance Lab** \\
\\
With a clear commitment to anticipating future trends and creating a dynamic hub for discussion with the business community and regulators, SDA Bocconi's Corporate Reporting and Performance (CRP) Lab focuses on research into the creation, measurement and communication of corporate performance and business value, both from a traditional perspective and in ESG (environmental, social and governance) terms.\\

**PNRR Lab** \\
\\
The NRP Lab analyzes productivity dynamics and competitiveness challenges related to the implementation of the National Recovery and Resilience Plan ("NRP" or "Plan"). **Private Equity Lab (PE Lab)** \\
\\
SDA Bocconi's Private Equity Lab (PE Lab) is an independent reference point for companies and financial players in the acquisition industry. PE Lab's activities are primarily aimed at: Private Equity funds, banks, financial, legal and tax advisors, accountants, strategic and financial consulting firms, venture capitalists, professional investors and business angels.\\
