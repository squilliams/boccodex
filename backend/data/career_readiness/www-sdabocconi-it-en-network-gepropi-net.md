# GePROPI Net

Management of Implementation Processes of public works and infrastructure

##### We summarise more than 30 years of knowledge shared between GEPROPI and PREM Lab

Public management

**Specialized training course useful for qualification of contracting stations**

GePROPI, Gestione Processi Realizzativi di opere pubbliche e infrastrutture (Implementation Process Management of Public Works and Infrastructure) has a history of more than 20 years. It is an educational path that includes related courses, some of which date back to 1985. Ten years ago, the **Osservatorio PREM Lab**, Public Real Estate Management, was born, closely related to the GePROPI experience. The birth of the PREM Lab stemmed from the realization that **prolonged training, without direct experience** in research and assistance to public bodies, could create a gap between training and application needs. The goal was to go beyond mere training, yet recognizing its essentiality in the creation of an operational-application culture. Today, GePROPI is evolving into a wider "container" alongside the GePROPI courses (RUP, Project Manager oo.pp, Contract Code), ready to address the new complexities and challenges that have been emerging in recent years.

## Accreditamento SNA

È stata **approvata** da SNA l’istanza di accreditamento per le attività formative in materia di contratti pubblici, ai sensi del decreto del Presidente della Scuola Nazionale dell’Amministrazione n. 22 del 13 febbraio 2024.

## The GePROPI Net offers:

- ### 4 days of SPECIFIC TRAINING

designed to strengthen the application of quality (and expertise) in the areas of infrastructure, public works, PPP/project finance, assets and procurement

- ### 2 days of JOINT THEMATIC SESSIONS

Designed to strengthen the application of quality (and expertise) in the areas of infrastructure, public works, PPP/project finance, assets and procurement.

- ### 2 days of COACHING (individual support)

On ongoing or start-up projects in public bodies, focusing on issues such as public works, infrastructure, asset management and complex contract-tenders such as PPP and Project Finance.

### Who should apply

Il GePROPI Net è rivolto a professionisti provenienti da diversi ambiti della Pubblica Amministrazione — Enti Locali, Aziende Sanitarie, Università, altre stazioni appaltanti — e da operatori economici coinvolti nella realizzazione di opere e infrastrutture.

In particolare, si rivolge a:

- Dirigenti, quadri e funzionari pubblici.
- Responsabili Unici di Progetto (RUP) e RASA.
- Project manager pubblici e privati impegnati in contratti di lavori e servizi.
- Società di progetto operanti in partenariati pubblico-privato, concessioni e contratti a lungo termine.
- Consulenti di amministrazioni pubbliche e operatori economici.
- Partecipanti, passati, presenti e futuri, del percorso GePROPI.

Each participating institution or economic operator may involve a variable number of collaborators, depending on the chosen program:

L’adesione a GePROPI Net potrebbe avvenire con fondi ad hoc previsti dal Codice dei contratti D.lgs 36/2023 per dare seguito ai singoli progetti. I fondi sono quelli da prevedere obbligatoriamente (rif. art. 45, comma 2 del D.Lgs 36/2023) nel quadro economico di ciascuna opera pubblica ed infrastruttura (cfr. D.lgs 36/2023, art. 45 comma 5, comma 7 lettera a), b); Destinatari: allegato I.10; Voce del Quadro Economico: allegato I.7, art. 5 comma 1 punto 10) e, se non impiegati, potrebbero essere persi. Il D.lgs 36/2023, inoltre, rende obbligatorio ed auspicabile per il RUP e per il Project manager-RUP la formazione permanente e costante (art. 15 comma 5 e 7; art. 63 comma 7; allegato 1.2, art. 4 comma 1 e comma 4). Non si tratta quindi di fondi gestiti dal servizio formazione dell’ente, ma trovano - o potrebbero trovare - collocazione all’interno del quadro economico del singolo progetto di opera pubblica.

## Program Advisory

Learning is never a linear journey—it is a path built together, combining specific training needs with practical tools tailored to individual challenges. Contact our team of Program Advisors, and together we will design the ideal learning path for you or your team, ensuring alignment with your goals.

**Your benefits:**

A dedicated orientation meeting, either in-person or remotely, to identify learning needs and create a customized plan. Throughout the course, you will experience a tailored welcome to introduce you to the program and foster networking between participants and instructors. Upon completion, your evaluation will become a valuable contribution for continuous improvement of the offering.

**How your organization benefits:**

Training consultation with HR managers to analyze organizational needs and develop personalized projects. Ongoing progress monitoring and a final follow-up to share results and gather feedback, ensuring constant alignment with pre-set goals.
