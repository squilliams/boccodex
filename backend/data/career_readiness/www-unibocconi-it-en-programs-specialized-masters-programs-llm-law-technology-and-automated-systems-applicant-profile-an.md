The Master program is aimed at **law graduates**, **young professionals and current and future legal practitioners** who wish to learn an internationally acknowledged curriculum and obtain the skills to start or further develop a promising career in law and technology in the digital era.

The program prepares participants for careers in legal practice dealing with information technology, technological sectors and digital environments at:

- digital businesses, information technology corporations and companies relying on digital assets in general
- domestic and international law firms with specialized practices
- governments and public regulatory institutions
- universities and think tanks, as well as non-governmental organizations

Over 25% of graduates secure their first position through **professional networks** or **direct applications**, and many through JobGate and Bocconi recruiting events. A total of 40% work in law and tax firms, while more than one third enter innovative sectors such as ICT and energy. Other key areas include **data protection**, **commercial law** and **intellectual property**. Within just a few months, 40% obtain a permanent contract.
