# Fees and scholarships

**A challenging but high-return investment**

Attending the program entails a significant amount of financial commitment but always repays itself with a wide margin. The SDA Bocconi's MCF is an intensive program requiring a hefty investment in time and money.

**€10,000**

**€10,000**

by 05 January 2027

- Third installment

**€10,000**

by 25 February 2027

- Fourth installment

**€10,000**

The fees include:

Participants must provide the own laptop.

Each year, SDA Bocconi School of Management offers multiple scholarships and tuition waivers. These are awarded on the basis of merit to candidates with an outstanding profile. SDA Bocconi School of Management makes available partial merit-based tuition waivers to 2026-2027 candidates with an outstanding profile.

**Applications are evaluated according to the following criteria:**

- Analysis of the requirements indicated in the specific notice of competition
- Analysis of academic and professional records
- Results of the MCF selection process

**To be eligible for a tuition waiver, applicants must:**

- Have sent the application to the MCF 2026-2027
- Look at the pre-requisites for the tuition waiver
- Flag the tuition waiver option through the Application Portal by the deadline indicated

The tuition waivers will be awarded during the selection process and no later than the starting date of the MCF 2026-2027 Program.

**You can apply to the scholarship / tuition waivers even though your selection process is still in progress.**

In order **to be considered for a tuition waiver**, not only you must **choose the tuition waiver option** in the application, but also you **must submit your MCF application within the deadline indicated**.

Admission to the program is independent of the awarding of any scholarship or tuition waiver.

If you meet more than one requirement, you can apply to multiple scholarships / tuition waivers.

**Tuition waivers for candidate Athletes**

SDA Bocconi School of Management is also making available **2 merit-based tuition waivers** of **€ 20,000 each** to all **2026/27 Full-Time Specialised Masters**’ candidates with an outstanding athletic background who can offer clear evidence of **significant athletic activities**(preferably in one of these 3 sports: **basketball, volleyball and waterpolo**) **at a competitive level**, such as participation in or selection for competitions or tournaments organized at national or international level prior to the date of enrollment

## Loans and funding

The School assists candidates with their search for funding by supplying the necessary documentation, and encouraging them to explore all scholarship and loan possibilities. SDA Bocconi partners with a number of financial institutes offering low-interest loans to admitted and eligible students. Our partner institutes are:

- [Prodigy Finance](https://www.sdabocconi.it/en/prodigy-finance/) for international students (please refer to the eligibility criteria);
- [Lendwise](https://lendwise.com/borrow/education-funding/university/bocconi-university/) for international students;
- Italian Banks for [Italian](https://www.unibocconi.it/it/studenti-iscritti/agevolazioni/finanziamenti-master-universitari-e-sda) and [international students](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/student-loans) (please note that applications for loans issued by Italian Banks must be submitted by the end of the month following the starting date of your Master);
- Bocconi offers Support Programs to student-athletes who participate in sport competitions at regional, national and international level. Find out more [here](https://www.bocconisport.eu/en/support-programs-student-athletes)

Furthermore, the University provides information about [financing sources](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/funding-country) for international students from a large number of countries. For further information, please visit Bocconi University’s website.
