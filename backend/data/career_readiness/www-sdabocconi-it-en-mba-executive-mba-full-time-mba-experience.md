## A world of perspectives: the value of diversity

Diversity at SDA Bocconi is more than a value - it’s the driving force behind a transformative learning experience. Our classrooms bring together bright minds from across the globe, each contributing their unique perspectives, backgrounds, and expertise. This exchange fosters innovation, broadens horizons, and prepares our participants to navigate the complexities of a global business environment.

As part of this commitment, our [**Class Profile**](https://www.sdabocconi.it/en/mba-executive-mba/full-time-mba/class-profile/) reflects an extraordinary mix of nationalities, industries, and professional experiences, ensuring that every discussion is enriched by a variety of viewpoints. But we don’t stop there.

**International Student Care** is our way of ensuring that every participant, no matter where they’re from, feels at home at SDA Bocconi. Moving to a new country is exciting, but we know it comes with challenges. That’s why **Alice Dealessi** and **Valentina Grassi** are here to support you every step of the way, from visas and permits to stay, to cultural integration and daily life tips.

Beyond this, our **Program Management team** plays a fundamental role in ensuring that every student is guided and supported throughout their on-campus journey. Acting as dedicated points of reference, our Program Managers provide personalized assistance, fostering an environment where students feel heard, valued, and cared for. We’re not just shaping your education - we’re shaping an experience that feels like home.

Need more details? Reach out to Alice and Valentina at [studentcare@sdabocconi.it](mailto:studentcare@sdabocconi.it)

## Inside-out learning: an experience without borders

At SDA Bocconi, learning extends far beyond the classroom. We believe that the most impactful experiences happen when knowledge is applied, connections are made, and perspectives are broadened. That’s why our **extracurricular activities** are designed to bring the business world to life and help you build lasting relationships along the way.

The **architecture of our campus** also reflects this philosophy. Designed with **transparency, accessibility, and openness** in mind, our buildings feature vast glass facades, open spaces, and seamless indoor-outdoor integration. This unique structure symbolizes our commitment to breaking down barriers, creating a continuous exchange of ideas and knowledge that extends far beyond the classroom.

## Extracurricular activities

Every experience and challenge is an opportunity to refine your skills, expand your perspective, and step outside your comfort zone, deepening your understanding of diverse business landscapes. Here, you don’t just learn—you live the experience.

The MBA journey extends beyond the classroom, offering tailored **study tours** connected to specific concentrations. These immersive trips provide valuable hands-on exposure to global markets, innovative companies, and industry leaders, allowing students to apply their learning in real-world settings and explore business dynamics across different regions and sectors.

**Industry Days** further enrich the experience by bringing students face-to-face with top executives, entrepreneurs, and experts. These events offer the opportunity to engage in discussions, share insights, and explore current challenges and trends within various industries.

The **Leadership Series** and **Masterclass** provide students with access to cutting-edge knowledge and real-world leadership strategies. In the Leadership Series, influential executives share their perspectives on navigating complex business environments, decision-making, and innovation. Masterclasses, led by renowned experts, dive deep into emerging topics, allowing students to deepen their expertise in key areas that are shaping the future of business.

Led by students, they require strong management skills to secure funding, develop business plans, and handle relationships with the press and private firms, making them a dynamic platform for learning and collaboration.
