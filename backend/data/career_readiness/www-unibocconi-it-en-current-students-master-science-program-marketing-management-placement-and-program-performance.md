## **One year after graduation\***

- **93.5%** of graduates are employed
- **38.4%** of employed graduates work abroad

**\\*** Data refers to a.y. 2021-2022 graduates of the English class group

## **On graduation day\*\***

- **79.6%** of graduates are employed
- **44.2%** of employed graduates work abroad
- They have been called to an average of **4.1** job interviews
- They have received on average **2** job offers

**\*\*** Survey - graduation sessions a.y. 2022-2023 (English class group)

## **Graduating students - satisfaction with the Study Program**

**90.9%** of students who graduated in the 2023-24 academic year declared they were satisfied with their Study Program

## **Attending students - satisfaction with teaching**

**81.8%** of students who attended courses offered in 2024-25 academic year are satisfied with the way the courses were carried out (grades from 6 to 10 on a 1-10 scale)
