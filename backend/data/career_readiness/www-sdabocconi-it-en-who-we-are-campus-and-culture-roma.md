**Campus and culture**

# Roma

## A Campus where history meets business and institutions

SDA Bocconi’s Rome campus is a meeting point between the world of management, public institutions, and the entrepreneurial ecosystem of Central and Southern Italy.

Villa Morgagni spans 2,700 square meters, with classrooms, study areas, communal spaces, and offices designed to foster dialogue and development. Here, tradition and innovation come together in a dynamic learning environment – immersed in the culture of the city, but open to global transformation.

Every day, professionals, institutions, and companies gather on campus, creating connections and opportunities that reflect the School’s mission to advance management education across Italy.
