# Open Days at Bocconi

09 Nov 2016, by Susanna Della Vedova, translated by Jenna Walker

The event for high school students deciding on their university program will be held on Friday and Saturday 11 and 12 November, 8:30am to 2:30pm, in the building at Via Roentgen 1

For anyone who still hasn't decided, or who needs to clear up any reservations they may have on choosing a university, or who needs more information on the courses in the Bachelor of Science programs in Management, Economics, Finance, Political Science or Law: they can get answers to all their questions on Friday and Saturday 11-12 November at Bocconi.

From 8:30am until 2:30pm on both days, [Open Days](https://www.unibocconi.it/wps/wcm/connect/bocconi/sitopubblico_en/navigation+tree/home/campus+and+services/services/international+recruitment+services/undergraduate+recruitment+services/students+area/activities+for+prospective+students/open+days?key=slide) will be held in the Via Roentgen 1 building. This is a unique opportunity to meet faculty members, program directors and current students, as well as University staff, available to provide information on study tracks and university life in general.

The two-day program also includes two dedicated events: at 8:45-9:30am in the Aula Magna, "Getting to Know Bocconi – General Presentation of the University" and at 9:45-10:45am, the event "Economics at Bocconi – Bachelor of Science Programs in Business, Management, Finance and Economics."

There is also a meeting reserved just for parents on Saturday at 11:00am.

Informational areas, desks with info on student services and guided tours of the campus are also on the agenda, along with a practice run of the admissions test, held periodically during the two-day event.

There will be two dates for the undergraduate [admissions](https://www.unibocconi.it/wps/wcm/connect/bocconi/sitopubblico_en/navigation+tree/home/campus+and+services/services/application+and+admission/admission+-+bachelor+programs) test for the 2017-2018 academic year:

Winter session: 3 February at Bocconi and in Rome, Bari and Padua. Registration must be completed by 20 January 2017.

Spring session: 20 April at Bocconi. Registration can be completed between 16 February and 7 April 2017.

Prospective first-year students can choose between [8 Bachelor](https://www.unibocconi.it/wps/wcm/connect/bocconi/sitopubblico_en/navigation+tree/home/programs/undergraduate+school/prospective+students) of Science programs in Economics, Management and Finance (3 in Italian and 5 in English); [World Bachelor in Business](https://www.unibocconi.it/wps/wcm/connect/bocconi/sitopubblico_en/navigation+tree/home/programs/undergraduate+school/prospective+students/world+bachelor+in+business); one [Political Science](https://www.unibocconi.it/wps/wcm/connect/bocconi/sitopubblico_en/navigation+tree/home/programs/undergraduate+school/prospective+students/international+politics+and+government) program in English; and the [Law program](https://www.unibocconi.it/wps/wcm/connect/bocconi/sitopubblico_it/albero+di+navigazione/home/corsi/scuola+di+giurisprudenza/studenti+prospective/laurea+magistrale+in+giurisprudenza) in Italian (a 5-year Integrated Master of Arts).

See the Open Days [program here](https://www.unibocconi.it/wps/wcm/connect/bocconi/sitopubblico_en/navigation+tree/home/campus+and+services/services/international+recruitment+services/undergraduate+recruitment+services/students+area/activities+for+prospective+students/open+days?key=slide)
