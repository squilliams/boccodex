## **One year after graduation**\*

- **94.9%** of graduates are employed
- **29.3%** of employed graduates work abroad

**\*** Data refers to a.y. 2021-2022 graduates

## **On graduation day**\*\*

- **64.7%** of graduates are employed
- **40%** of employed graduates work abroad
- They have been called to an average of **4.2** job interviews
- They have received on average **1.8** job offers

**\*\*** Survey - graduation sessions a.y. 2022-2023

## **Graduating students - satisfaction with the Study Program**

**88.5%** of students who graduated in the 2023-24 academic year declared they were satisfied with their Study Program

## **Attending students - satisfaction with teaching**

**87.7%** of students who attended courses offered in 2024-25 academic year are satisfied with the way the courses were carried out (grades from 6 to 10 on a 1-10 scale)
