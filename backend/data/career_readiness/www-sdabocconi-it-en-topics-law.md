# Law

The growing complexity of regulatory systems, the globalization of markets, and the digitalization of services require legal professionals to constantly update their skills. Whether in private practice or within public and private organizations, it is essential to have the tools to address issues that demand technical specialization and managerial awareness of economic and social implications.

In this section, you will find the School’s full educational portfolio dedicated to this topic, along with events, news, research activities, and in-depth content.
