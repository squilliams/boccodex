# Evaluation

Bocconi provides support to its Faculty for the evaluation of their research outputs.

Its services include:

- Checking and validating the scientific outputs on the [IRIS institutional repository](https://www.unibocconi.it/wps/wcm/connect/bocconi/sitopubblico_en/navigation+tree/home/faculty+and+research/research/evaluation/iris+-+institutional+research+information+system)
- Promoting, developing and coordinating a number of activities, policies and tools to improve the evaluation of the university’s research products
- Monitoring national and international evaluation models
