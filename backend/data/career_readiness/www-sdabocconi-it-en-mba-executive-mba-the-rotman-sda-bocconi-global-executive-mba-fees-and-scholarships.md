## Fees and expenses

The tuition fee for the Rotman–SDA Bocconi GEMBA 2026-2027 is USD 110,000, payable in five installments

- First installment

**USD 10,000**

on acceptance of admission offer

- Second Installment

**USD 25,000**

by October 30, 2026

- Third Installment

**USD 25,000**

by February 26, 2027

- Fourth Installment

**USD 25,000**

by April 30, 2027

- Fifth Installment

**USD 25,000**

by June 26, 2027

**Application fee: € 130**

The fees include

  - Most teaching materials, access to the school's support facilities, class-day meals, and accommodation for modules held outside Italy and Canada.

Not included

  - Travel visas, travel expenses, accommodation in Italy and Canada and incidentals.

Participants must provide their own laptop.

## Scholarships and tuition waivers

_1st round_
31 March 2026

_2nd round_
7 May 2026

_3rd round_
24 June 2026

Each year, SDA Bocconi offers multiple merit-based scholarships and tuition waivers. Thanks to the contribution of the Bocconi Alumni Community, SDA Bocconi School of Management makes available 300,000 USD in merit-based Tuition Waiversfor 2026-2027 Rotman-SDA Bocconi GEMBA candidates.

**Tuition Waivers applications are evaluated according to the following criteria:**

- Analysis of academic and professional record
- Analysis of the requirements indicated in the specific notice of competition
- Results of the Rotman-SDA Bocconi GEMBA selection process

**Admission to the Rotman-SDA Bocconi Global EMBA is independent of the awarding of any scholarship or tuition waiver.**

**For the assignment of (at least) 2 Tuition Waivers priority will be given to Women with an outstanding profile**.

**To be eligible for a tuition waiver, you must:**

- Have submitted your application for the 2026-2027 Rotman-SDA Bocconi GEMBA program
- Review the pre-requisites for the tuition waiver
- Apply for the tuition waiver on the online Application Portal by the indicated deadline.

You can apply to the tuition waivers even if your GEMBA selection process is still in progress. The tuition waivers will be awarded in the rounds indicated in the notice of competition and within the Rotman-SDA Bocconi Global EMBA program's beginning.

We encourage candidates to apply early to increase their chances of a Tuition waiver.

## Loans and funding

The School assists candidates with their search for funding by supplying the necessary documentation, and encouraging them to explore all scholarship and loan possibilities. SDA Bocconi partners with a number of financial institutes offering low-interest loans to admitted and eligible students. Our partner institutes are:

- [Prodigy Finance](https://www.sdabocconi.it/en/prodigy-finance/) for international students (please refer to the eligibility criteria);
- [Lendwise](https://lendwise.com/borrow/education-funding/university/bocconi-university/) for international students;
- Italian Banks for [Italian](https://www.unibocconi.it/it/studenti-iscritti/agevolazioni/finanziamenti-master-universitari-e-sda) and [international students](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/student-loans) (please note that applications for loans issued by Italian Banks must be submitted by the end of the month following the starting date of your Master);
- Bocconi offers Support Programs to student-athletes who participate in sport competitions at regional, national and international level. Find out more [here](https://www.bocconisport.eu/en/support-programs-student-athletes)

Furthermore, the University provides information about [financing sources](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/funding-country) for international students from a large number of countries. For further information, please visit Bocconi University’s website.
