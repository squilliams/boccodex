# Your goal. Our guidance

SDA Bocconi offers a range of **international events designed to help prospective students explore its educational offer** by engaging with the Guidance and Recruitment team and Faculty, while **gaining insights into today’s most relevant managerial and economic topics.**

From thought-leadership meetups to personalized orientation meetings, these gatherings provide different ways to connect with the School, its programs and its Community.

## Orientation Meetings

**Explore SDA Bocconi's MBA and Specialized Masters** by requesting a personalized meeting with the Guidance & Recruitment team. Simply select the date and location that work best for you and register to **book your 30-minute one-on-one session**. Once you register, we will follow up to confirm the exact meeting time.

**Alternatively, we can arrange an online chat** if that is more convenient for you.
