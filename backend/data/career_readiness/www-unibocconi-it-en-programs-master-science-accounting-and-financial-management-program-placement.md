#### Graduate Job Statistics

#### One year after graduation

- _**95.7%** of graduates are employed_
- _**40.3%** of employed graduates work abroad_

Data refers to a.y. 2022-2023 graduates of the Accounting, Financial Management and Control English class group

#### On graduation day

- _**86%** of graduates are employed_
- _**46.3%** of employed graduates work abroad_
- _They have been called to an average of **6.9** job interviews_
- _They have received on average **2.3** job offers_

Survey - graduation sessions a.y. 2023-2024 (Accounting, Financial Management and Control English class group)
