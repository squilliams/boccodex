**First Year**

a) Suggested Law course:

- IP LAW, in English

**Second Year**

b) Compulsory concentration course:

- VALUE CHAINS AND BUSINESS MODELS IN FASHION & LUXURY, in English

c) 1 course chosen from among the following:

- DATA ANALYSIS FOR MANAGERIAL DECISION MAKING, in English
- PREDICTIVE ANALYTICS FOR DATA DRIVEN DECISIONS MAKING, in English
- BIG DATA FOR BUSINESS DECISIONS, in English
- PRICING ANALYTICS, in English

d) 1 course chosen from among the following:

- MANAGEMENT OF FASHION AND LUXURY COMPANIES, in English
- INTERNATIONAL MARKETING, in English
- SUPPLY CHAIN MANAGEMENT, in English
- EVENT AND MEGA EVENT MANAGEMENT AND CREATIVE INDUSTRIES, in English
- MANAGERIAL ISSUES IN MADE IN ITALY INDUSTRIES, in English
- INNOVATIVE RETAIL DESIGN, in English
- SOCIAL MEDIA MARKETING, in Italian and in English
- LUXURY COMPANIES IN THE GLOBAL LANDSCAPE, in English
- ECOMMERCE, in English

e) 1 course freely chosen from among the [electives](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/master-science/international-management/electives "Electives") offered by the Graduate School.

Please note that the list might be subject to changes
