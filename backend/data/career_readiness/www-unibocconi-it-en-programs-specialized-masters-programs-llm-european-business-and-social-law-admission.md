## **Admission Deadlines**

The admission process is articulated in two rounds, according to the following deadlines.

All the deadlines below are intended at **2 pm Italian time (UTC+1)**.

In order to start your application and to see the results, you must access the [Apply2B](https://apply.unibocconi.it/ "Apply2B") portal.

|     |     |     |     |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
|  | **Application Deadline** | **Results** | **Enrolment Deadline** |
| **First round** | 11 February 2026 | 18 March 2026 | 25 March 2026 |
| First round stand-by list |  | 25 June 2026 | 2 July 2026 |
| **Second round** | 27 May 2026 | 25 June 2026 | 2 July 2026 |
| Second round stand-by list |  | 9 July 2026 | 13 July 2026 |
