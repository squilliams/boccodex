PhD student offices are located in the [Via Roentgen Building (Grafton Building)](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/services/housing), while classes usually take place in other campus buildings. See the list of Bocconi [classrooms](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/campus/buildings-and-classrooms/classrooms "CLASSROOMS").

PhD students offices, like any office in the Grafton building, can be accessed during opening hours, available [here](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/media/attachments/Access%252BGrafton%252BBuilding%252Bupd%252B03%252B2020.pdf).

Study and reading spaces of the Library may be accessible till midnight and during weekends; please consult the [Bocconi Library website](https://lib.unibocconi.it/en/screens/opening-hours/) for detailed and updated information.

A study room is generally open from 8:00am. to 2:00 am., from Monday to Sunday, in Via Gobbi 1, basement,  entrance door at the left side of the S. Ferdinando church; opening times and closing days are posted at the entrance of the building and can be consulted online [here](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/campus/buildings-and-classrooms/classrooms "CLASSROOMS").
