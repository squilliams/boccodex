The Master in Healthcare Management (MiMS) is the 12-month undergraduate Master program that will enable talented young people to acquire and develop the skills and abilities needed for a successful career in public and private healthcare companies, technology provider companies, research and training centers, and the world of healthcare consulting.

## Why MiMS

- ### Quick placement

The MiMS course includes an internship in companies in the industry that often turns into or gives access to a professional opportunity. 97% of participants find employment within the first year, and of them 62% with permanent contracts.

- ### Action learning

MiMS not only provides you with theoretical knowledge, but also allows you to continuously put your skills into practice in a real professional setting. The second part of MiMS is in fact entirely dedicated to Action Learning: a unique educational experience that combines lectures, testimonials from companies and professionals, field research in collaboration with partner companies, site visits and study tours, and finally internship.

- ### Entrepreneurship

MiMS offers an innovative path towards working on your business idea in healthcare, starting with an elective Business Planning course and involvement with challengers and mentors from the MiMS Alumni Community and Bocconi Faculty.

- ### A Path to Self Management

MiMS provides a pathway to support you as you enter the job market by developing both the skills most in demand and awareness of your professional aptitudes. The self-management course includes individual and group counseling on CVs and job interviews.

[Discover more](https://www.sdabocconi.it/en/node/14549/)

- ### Solid experience and constant contact with the world of healthcare

MiMS is an in-house part of an executive school of the caliber of SDA Bocconi and has a dedicated faculty that has been working in the healthcare sector in Italy for about 50 years. Educational content is constantly updated through CERGAS research and training activities of the Government, Health and Non-Profit Division. Frequent participation of testimonials, field project partners and recruiters is provided for.

- ### Membership of the large SDA Bocconi network

MiMS offers you a wealth of contacts in the world of healthcare. Its 600 Alumni can be found in almost all healthcare companies and large enterprises in the industry. The MiMS network is a tool for relationship, information and development. It also means rightfully belonging to the historical community of those who have earned a Master degree or MBA from SDA Bocconi and therefore accessing a greater network of people and opportunities around the world.

## A comprehensive learning experience

- ### The transformative process: from models to practices

The MiMS learning model is composite, interactive and coherent. It includes:

  - Classroom lectures
  - Guided discussions of case studies
  - Testimonials from healthcare professionals
  - Exercises, case studies, simulations, group work
  - Field research
  - In-company field projects (internships)
  - Distance learning via a dedicated online platform
  - Examinations for each of the courses activated, with individual and group tests.

- ### Sector-wide Pathway: Self Management and Leadership in Healthcare (Feb - Sept 2027)

The objective of the course is to provide you with the tools to support your entry into the labor market by developing useful skills, increasing awareness of your professional aptitudes and knowledge of industry-specific characteristics. Individual and group counseling on CVs and job interviews are provided

- ### Study Tour

In an industry that is constantly changing and innovating, engaging with companies and professionals from different backgrounds becomes essential. The study tour pursues this goal by selecting the destination from a wide range of best practices and by means of a combination of lectures, testimonials and site visits.

- ### In-Company Project

The in-company project completes the program through a hands-on experience that allows students to join a company and develop a field-based training project. The final phase of MiMS is dedicated to Field Projects, in which each student applies and leverages the knowledge and skills developed during the Master’s program. The strong and long-standing relationships with partner companies enable MiMS to identify opportunities across a wide range of organizations (from public and private healthcare providers to technology suppliers and consulting firms) within the complex healthcare sector. For participants who already work in healthcare, it is possible to carry out the field project within their current organization.

- ### Field Research

Targeted research on a topic assigned based on individual preferences, carried out in groups of 5–6 participants under the supervision of an SDA Bocconi tutor. The research involves analyzing the solutions and management systems adopted by selected companies in the healthcare sector and developing proposals for improvement. qui il testo dello Study tour nel calendario: Direct exposure to best practices through a combination of lectures, guest testimonials, and site visits.

## Admissions

We are looking for candidates with well-defined personal qualities and strong motivation toward a field as specific as healthcare. Admission involves a rigorous selection process that assesses individual aptitudes and aspirations, ensuring that a stimulating, dynamic MiMS class is created each year.

**Requirements**:

- Master degree or single-cycle degree in any discipline. Those who are due to graduate within three months of the start of teaching activities (by April 2027) are also eligible for selection.
- Application form
- Specific interest in the health sector as a whole
- SDA Test
- Interview

The following are also considered:

- Good knowledge of the English language
- Professional experience gained both within the course of study and subsequently
- High motivation to study

## Fees and scholarships

Our MiMS course requires a significant commitment, both in terms of time and financial investment, but the return in personal growth and professional advancement is tangible.

The fee for MiMS 2027-28 is €24,000 (excluding VAT). Payment of the fee is to be made in four installments.

Based on exclusive merit criteria, each year SDA Bocconi makes several scholarships and waivers available to candidates and applicants.
