# Faculty

**Beyond expectations**

The MISA faculty at SDA Bocconi School of Management is made up of **professors and researchers** with strong, ongoing ties to the business community. They are joined by **entrepreneurs, managers, consultants, and experts** from international universities who bring valuable real-world insights into the classroom. A team capable of exceeding every expectation and giving your learning experience an extra edge.
