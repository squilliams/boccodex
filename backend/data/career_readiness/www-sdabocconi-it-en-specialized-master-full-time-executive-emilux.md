# Executive Master in Luxury Management

## The necessary luxury for your career development.

Fashion, design and luxury

_Start_ 11 January 2027

_Duration_ 13 months

_Where_ Paris / Milano / Dubai / London / Los Angeles & Silicon Valley / Lausanne / Roma

_Format_ Executive

_Language_ English

_Avg. working experience_ 11 years

_Credits_ 62 ECTS/CFU

The Executive Master in Luxury Management (EMiLUX) is an intense **post-experience modular program**, offering 62 ECTS/ CFU credits and taught over **13 months in Paris, Milano, Dubai, London, Los Angeles & Silicon Valley, Lausanne & Roma**.

EMiLUX offers a unique opportunity to enhance your career in the luxury business by exploring **diverse cultural perspectives and markets**. Designed with utmost care, the program equips professionals with the skills and abilities needed to successfully meet the challenges of luxury: managing modern aspirations while preserving timeless values, combining aesthetic sensitivity and creativity with robust business processes and models. Through real-life examples, participants experience a careful blend of commitment to heritage and passion for modernity, journeying from historic luxury capitals such as Milan and Paris to fast-growing markets like Dubai.

Without leaving your job, you can unlock a wealth of new opportunities and achieve new goals. Expand your global outlook, refine your strategic mindset, and gain in-depth insights into international luxury markets—all while enjoying exclusive **behind-the-scenes experiences with the world's leading luxury brands.**

## Why EMiLUX

- ### International environment in 6 different countries

Experience a truly global program through 8 international modules held in Paris, Milano, Dubai, Lausanne, Los Angeles & Silicon Valley, Roma, and London—luxury capitals that connect participants with key markets and iconic brands worldwide. Throughout the journey, you will benefit from the academic excellence and industry insight of an international faculty, while gaining exposure to diverse cultural environments, emerging markets, innovative business models, and new luxury brands shaping the future of the industry.

- ### Prestigious partnerships

Collaborate with top companies and institutions like Cartier, BCG, Dorchester Collection Academy, illy. These partnerships create exclusive content and tools to better support your future career in the luxury industry.

- ### Top Guest Speakers and Direct Brand experience

Gain the best and most up-to-date insights from industry leaders and directly experience luxury brands in stores, showrooms, events, and trade fairs.

- ### World-Class International Faculty

Benefit from the high level of academic preparation and close contact with the business world. EMiLUX professors are involved in research, publishing, teaching, and consultancy with some of the most famous luxury companies globally.

- ### Learn without leaving your job

Participate in a part-time program divided into 8 intensive modules during pre-established weeks. This structure allows you to take leave from your company for limited periods and apply new insights immediately in the workplace.

- ### Entrepreneurial and design thinking projects

Engage in the Entrepreneurial Project (EP) and Design Thinking Project (in partnership with illy), which run throughout the program. These projects allow you to exercise creativity, rigor, and tenacity in applying course principles to real business problems, nurturing your creativity and thinking out of the box.

- ### Broaden your network

Join the Bocconi community—one of Europe’s most prestigious and long-standing institutions for business education, with a global network of over 145,000 alumni. You will also benefit from the growing EMiLUX network of more than 250 ex participants, and gain access to an international professional ecosystem of the highest caliber, made up of SDA Bocconi graduates and strategic partners. It’s a valuable opportunity to expand your professional connections worldwide.

## A comprehensive learning experience

EMiLUX is a unique opportunity to empower your career in the luxury business, also looking at it from different cultural perspectives and in different markets.

- ### Learning model

Each course will start with a face-to-face residential session, continue online, and end with an exam in another face-to-face session. The first two modules are devoted to core courses that ensure a solid, common base. Other modules are dedicated to advanced courses.

- ### The face-to-face modules: features and advantages

**You will be able to interact** with accomplished executives outside the school and you will have the opportunity to get to know and work with the other participants and a great variety of nationalities, companies, and professional and personal backgrounds.

**You will gain deeper knowledge** on all the rules and dynamics of luxury business and brand management, through theory and practice.

**You can offer a valuable contribution to the class discussion** and in turn you will be able to gather ideas and opportunities to develop strategies that are extremely competitive.

- ### Distance learning

Distance learning is spread in several modules along 6 weeks and consists of specific topics (e.g. creativity and design thinking, sustainability, supply chain) as well as faculty advising on entrepreneurial projects.

Attendance is compulsory for both the face-to-face modules as well as the distance learning modules. This guarantees effective, balanced, consistent professional growth.

- ### Business Ethics & Diversity, Equity and Inclusion online seminar

A mandatory Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion course and exam will be integrated into the Program Curriculum.
