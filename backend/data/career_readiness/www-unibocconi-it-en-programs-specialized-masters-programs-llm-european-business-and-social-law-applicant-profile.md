## **Selection Criteria**

Applications can be submitted by candidates who:

- completed or are completing their **first level university studies**(obtaining their Degree within 90 days of the beginning of teaching activities)
- have a **B2 (upper intermediate) level** of competence in **English** certified by one of the recognized international English language certificates: Cambridge Assessment English ≥ 173  or  IELTS ≥ 6.5 with at least 6 in every section   or TEOFL IBT ≥ 88   or Duolingo English Test ≥ 110. Furthermore the English language requirement is considered satisfied for candidates who completed a degree taught only in English (they can upload their university qualification or secondary school diploma as proof of language) and for candidates who have an English exam at B2 level (or higher) passed in a university course (they can upload the certificate of passing the exam  as proof of language).

The assessment of the candidate is based on the evaluation of:

- **academic curriculum**
- **curriculum vitae**
- **motivational letter**

Besides the “dossier” evaluation, the Admission Board may request an interview in order to further assess the candidate's application.
