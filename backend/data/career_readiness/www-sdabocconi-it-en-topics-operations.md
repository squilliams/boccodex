# Operations

Strategic management of Operations and Supply Chain is crucial for competitiveness: flexibility, responsiveness, and innovation are essential for sustainable and responsible growth, in line with environmental and social standards. Professionals must develop skills to ensure efficient, sustainable, and ethical production processes and supply chains.

In this section, you will find the School’s full educational portfolio dedicated to this topic, along with events, news, research activities, and in-depth content.
