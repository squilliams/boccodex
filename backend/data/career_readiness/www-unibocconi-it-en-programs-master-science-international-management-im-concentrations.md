_Director **Paola Dubini**_

The **IM Concentrations** program features:

- several options for specializing your curriculum through various concentrations
- the opportunity to apply to Exchange or Double Degree programs
- behavioral soft skills seminars
- the chance to take some electives in Italian

IM Concentrations offers a learning path where the in-depth study of key corporate functions and processes equips graduates to take on managerial roles.

It aims to help students develop problem-solving skills, gaining a unique set of soft skills which are increasingly required for leadership responsibilities in challenging working environments.

The program offers job market oriented specializations (e.g. Luxury and Fashion Management, Consulting, Finance, Sport Management, Entrepreneurship, HR & Change Management and Performance Analysis and Management) with different courses to tailor students' curricula.

To reinforce the international background of our students, several study abroad opportunities are available, including Double Degree Programs – which will allow students to study at two top business and economic schools and receive two diplomas, one from each University – or shorter Exchange programs – which will allow students to study abroad for one term at a top partner university.

**Study Plan**

In the first year, you will be provided with the fundamental concepts of International Management through core courses and seminars that all feature highly interactive methods and are largely based on individual work as well as teamwork.

In the second year, you will complete your study program by choosinga **concentration** and, if you are interested, apply for a study abroad experience. You will also take part in a compulsory internship – in Italy or abroad – study foreign languages, and discuss your thesis to develop your research abilities.
