## **One year after graduation\***

- **96.7%** of graduates are employed
- **27.6%** of employed graduates work abroad

**\\*** Data refers to a.y. 2021-2022 graduates

## **On graduation day\*\***

- **94.6%** of graduates are employed
- **22.9%** of employed graduates work abroad
- They have been called to an average of **2.9** job interviews
- They received on average **2.3** job offers

**\\*\\*** Survey - graduation sessions a.y. 2022-2023

## **Graduating students - satisfaction with the Study Program**

**94.9%** of students who graduated in the 2023-24 academic year declared they were satisfied with their Study Program
