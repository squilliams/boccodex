## Why EMMAS

- ### Applied research in support of education

The value of EMMAS training and its innovative power are given by its close relationship with applied research. Since 1978, SDA Bocconi and [**CERGAS**](https://cergas.unibocconi.eu/)(Centre for Research on Health and Social Care Management) have jointly developed training and research/intervention to support healthcare management. And the EMMAS Faculty transfer methods and results into teaching.

- ### A broad and well-established network of former participants and partner companies.

Nearly 1,000 former participants make up the EMMAS Alumni network, which comes together during the Annual Conference and the annual presentation of the [OASI Report](https://cergas.unibocconi.eu/oasi-2025). In addition, participants meet around 30 companies and public and private institutions involved in each edition.

- ### Work and study formula

Comprehensive, integrated, modular, engaging and interactive training that does not interrupt your career path. EMMAS provides 50 days of training, with a 12-month commitment of 4 classroom days per month. A format that meets the needs of those who want to invest in training while continuing their work. For the participants, it is a great test of tenacity and organization. For EMMAS a challenge to make the pathway stimulating and effective in every step and activity.

- ### From knowing to doing

The didactic method is an essential component of EMMAS's training effectiveness, enabling you to develop your management skills in the context of situations typical of health and social welfare companies. Case study discussions, exercises, simulations and field projects (the well-known DSA-Diagnosis of Business Systems) are some of the classroom teaching tools that are accompanied by distance learning sessions designed to ensure continuity of learning, and activities designed to strengthen leadership (YourSELF - Empowerment Leadership Focus) and learn about international best practices (Study Tour)

- ### From awareness to improving your leadership

**YourSELF** (Self Empowerment Leadership Focus) is a leadership skills development program tailored to your specific needs. Leadership can be learned and trained. It implies personal and professional skills that can be developed from an awareness of one's strengths and weaknesses. It develops from reflection on the relationship between the starting intentions, the behaviors activated and the impacts generated. To support this process, SDA Bocconi provides you with two tools: assessment and coaching.

- ### Always in the forefront of training for the health and social welfare sector.

SDA Bocconi has historically been at the forefront of health and social welfare management education. EMMAS takes up the legacy of the **CORGESAN** and **COGEMSAN** programs, which have worked on skills development in these areas since the 1980s.

## A comprehensive learning experience

- ### The teaching methodology

The EMMAS learning model is composite, dynamic, interactive, and consistent with the executive format and thus calibrated to the managers' agenda. It includes:

  - **Classroom lectures**, analysis, case discussions and management games
  - Targeted **in-depth readings**
  - **Workshops**
  - Distance **learning sessions**
  - **Coaching session** s aimed at building a path to leadership empowerment ( **YourSELF** \- Empowerment Leadership Focus)
  - Group **field projects** (Learning Through Changing - Diagnosis of Business Systems)
  - A company **visit in Italy** and a study tour abroad that will enable participants to get to the heart of national and international best practices.

- ### Study Tour: discovering international best practices

In an industry that is constantly changing and permeated by scientific and management innovations, it becomes essential to engage with **international colleagues, professionals and business realities**. Despite local specificities, there are problems common to all advanced countries. But it is not only about identifying possible alternative answers to common problems, but also about recognizing and valuing similar answers or even critically evaluating some solutions.

The aim of the **Study Tour** is to open up to new frontiers while at the same time "discovering" the strengths of one's own organizations: fundamental requirements that a manager must possess in order to build a **solid vision of future healthcare**. The choice of destination is therefore made from within a narrow portfolio of **European best practices**, and the agenda includes a mix of lectures, testimonials and facility tours.

- ### YourSELF - Empowerment Leadership Focus

YourSELF is a leadership skills development program aimed at supporting master degree participants in a learning process about specific **individual needs**.  Leadership skills include not only innate aptitudes, but also **personal and professional skills**, which can be developed through becoming aware of one's strengths and weaknesses.

## Admissions

We are looking for participants with a solid professional background and strong motivation and cultural orientation toward health and social welfare system renewal. Admission involves a rigorous selection process that highlights personal motivation and aptitude and ensures the creation of a challenging and dynamic EMMAS class each year.

**Requirements**

- Master's or single-cycle degree in any discipline or equivalent degree recognized by SDA Bocconi
- Application for admission
- At least 5 years' experience in the health and social welfare sector or in service companies, private companies working in the field.
- SDA Test
- Interview

**Some individual characteristics are also taken into consideration:**

- High motivation
- High potential for professional development
- Tendency towards change and innovation
- Good interpersonal skills
- Leadership aptitude

## Fees and scholarships

Our EMMAS is a substantial personal and financial commitment, but the return in professional growth and advancement is real.

The tuition fee for participation in EMMAS 2025-2026 is € 25,000 (excluding VAT). The fee is to be paid in five installments.

Each year, SDA Bocconi School of Management makes multiple merit-based scholarships and tuition waivers available for outstanding candidates.

The School also participates in notices proposed by public administrations for scholarships/tuition waivers for EMMAS program applicants.
