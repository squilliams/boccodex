# Programs at the Leading Edge

Our MSc programs combine a strong theoretical background and an emphasis on real-world applications. They nurture critical thinking, an open-minded attitude and the ability to work in multi-cultural environments, all while promoting an attitude of responsible entrepreneurship. Indeed, the aim of our MSc programs is to contribute to the development of leading citizens of our globalized world.

#### MSc Program Structure

Our programs help you develop solid knowledge in all the core topics during your first year, while choosing from a wide list of electives in year two.

This allows you to design a flexible study plan that focuses on your main areas of interest and pursues them in depth.
