# The School

## Research, Rethink, Reimagine

Joining SDA Bocconi means transforming your path and shaping the world of business, organizations, and society.

At the heart of it all is research – the foundation that allows us to rethink scenarios, models, and strategies. And action – the way we turn knowledge into real-world impact.

Our mission is to guide people and organizations through continuous growth, with a practical, innovative approach to learning. It’s more than skill-building – it’s about opening new perspectives and reimagining what’s possible.

## Our values

Our community is united around core values:

### Social, ethical, and environmental responsibility

We believe the only real development is sustainable, and we cultivate responsible leaders.

### Independence

We uphold genuine freedom of thought, free from cultural, political, and economic influences.

### Critical Re-Thinking

We foster new perspectives for analysis, research, and the professionals who choose us.

### Scientific rigor

We evolve our knowledge alongside the world to consistently ensure its quality and reliability.

### Imagination and creative passion

We promote innovation through a pioneering spirit and the courage to change the world as we know it.

### Entrepreneurship

We not only educate but also stimulate the continuous pursuit of opportunities.

### Centrality of people

We support individuals at every step and cultivate a community made of lasting, valuable connections.

### Global vision

We embrace diversity and understand its value in a global context.

## SDA Bocconi’s Roots

Founded in 1971, SDA Bocconi is rooted in the long-standing tradition of Bocconi University. We draw from its academic excellence, entrepreneurial spirit, and research-driven approach.

The vision of Claudio Dematté, who played a pivotal role in the School’s founding and development, still inspires our mission today: to combine scientific rigor with practical application, meeting the evolving needs of a constantly changing world.
