The first part of the study plan includes mandatory courses in the students’ study program as well as seminars for the development of behavioral skills.

The second part of the study plan includes educational activities chosen by the student focusing on specific subject areas, e.g. entrepreneurship, management of technology and innovation and business analytics.

At Bocconi, Milan

In the first year, you will acquire a solid foundation in economic and management theory, analytical tools, and other methodologies to understand:

- **technology evolution and markets for technology**
- **innovation processes within and outside firms**
- **intellectual property rights and competition**
- **how innovative entrepreneurs may obtain financing**
- **how to develop, experiment with and choose among alternative strategies**
- **how firms use technology and innovation to position themselves strategically**

The courses in the first year touch on many different areas: microeconomics, management, strategy, organization theory, law, history, data management and data visualization and econometrics.

The study plan requires two European Union languages.

More specifically:

**1st language, English**

- for all students (regardless of their native language)
- the main aim is to foster the development of professional skills

**2nd language:**

- Italian for non-Italian native speakers
- French, Spanish, German for Italian native speakers
