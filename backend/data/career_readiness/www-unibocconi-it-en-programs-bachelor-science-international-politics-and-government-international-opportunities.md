International Opportunities

[After Graduation](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/bachelor-science/international-politics-and-government/after-graduation)

The BSc in International Politics and Government offers students different study abroad options, including Exchange Programs and a Double Degree. **Some of these options can be chosen after enrollment, while others have to be selected at the time of application.**

The program that must be **selected during your online application** is the track in **Data, Society and Organizations**, a Double Degree offered in collaboration with HEC Paris, which allows you to spend a total of 3 semesters at Bocconi and 3 semesters at HEC Paris.

Studying abroad can be a great opportunity for growth both academically and personally, allowing you to challenge yourself in a multicultural environment. The program's **Politics and Policy Making track** offers students different study abroad options through the Exchange Program, with **applications open after enrollment**.

_Exchange Programs_

One-semester programs with prestigious partner universities, including:

- Princeton University (US)
- Columbia University (US)
- Sciences Po (France)
- London School of Economics and Political Science (UK)
- Korea University, College of Political Science and Economics (Korea)
- The Australian National University (Australia)
