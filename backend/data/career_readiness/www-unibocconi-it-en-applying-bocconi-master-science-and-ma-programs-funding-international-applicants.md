# International applicants

Graduate Support Scheme

**Bocconi4Access to Education a.y. 2026-27**

Bocconi has always been committed to making higher education available to all motivated and talented students through a financial aid system, based on both merit and need.

In the section below, **funding opportunities for a.y. 2026-27** are summarized.

Merit based scholarships

Tuition waivers for students with specific features, regardless of the family's financial situation:

- [Bocconi merit award](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/master-science-and-ma-programs/funding/bocconi-graduate-merit-awards-ay-2026-27 "Bocconi Graduate merit Awards a.y. 2026-27");
- [Bocconi Award - Artificial Intelligence](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/master-science-and-ma-programs/funding/bocconi-award-artificial-intelligence-2026-2027 "Bocconi Award - Artificial Intelligence 2026-2027")
- [Women award;](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/master-science-and-ma-programs/funding/graduate-women-award-ay-2026-27 "Graduate Women award a.y. 2026-27")
- [Cyber scholarship](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/master-science-and-ma-programs/funding/bocconi-graduate-merit-awards-ay-2026-27 "Bocconi Graduate merit Awards a.y. 2026-27")

Status based scholarships

Tuition waivers for students with specific features, regardless of the family's financial situation:

- [Tuition waiver for students with disabilities equal or greater than 66%](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/master-science-and-ma-programs/funding/tuition-waiver-students-disabilities-equal-or-greater-66-master-science-programs "Tuition waiver for students with disabilities equal or greater than 66% - Master of Science programs a.y. 2026-27");
- [Partial tuition waiver for simultaneously enrolled siblings](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/master-science-and-ma-programs/funding/partial-tuition-waivers-simultaneously-enrolled-siblings-master-science-programs "Partial tuition waivers for simultaneously enrolled siblings - Master of Science programs a.y. 2026-27").

Other financial aid opportunities

- [ISU Bocconi Scholarship a.y. 2026-27](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/bachelor-and-law-programs/funding/introduction-isu-bocconi-scholarship-ay-2026-27-timeline "Introduction to ISU Bocconi Scholarship a.y. 2026-27 - Timeline");
- [Integrations for international mobility programs a.y. 2025-26](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/funding/international-mobility-grant-ay-2025-26 "International mobility grant a.y. 2025-26");

- [External opportunities](https://www.unibocconi.it/it/corsi-di-studio/area-studenti-iscritti/servizi/premi-di-laurea-bandi-esterni)

Loans and other opportunities

Our University, in agreement with major Italian banks, offers its students the possibility to finance their education through student loans, at particularly favorable interest rates with the repayment provision only after entering the job market.

- [Loans](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/funding/funding-master-science-programs/loans-master-science-programs);
- [Direct loans for US citizens](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/funding/us-direct-loans);
- [Funding by country](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/bachelor-and-law-programs/funding/funding-country "Funding by Country").
