# Leadership, HR and organization

People play a key role in organizations. This is why it is essential to gain a comprehensive understanding of the different dimensions of human resource management, while at the same time developing a leadership style that values diversity and is fair and responsible.

In this section, you will find the School’s full educational portfolio dedicated to this topic, along with events, news, research activities, and in-depth content.
