Welcome to the Bocconi PhD School Admissions pages.

**A new dedicated call for applications funded by the** _**Fondo Italiano per la Scienza (FIS)**_ **opens on April 17th within the PhD Program in Statistics and Computer Science – curriculum Computer Science. This call is part of the 42nd PhD cycle (a.y. 2026/27).**

We will be accepting new applications for a.y. 2026-27 starting from 17 April 2026 at 18:00 CET until **18 May 2026** at 23:59 (CET).

Please note that the ordinary Call for Applications to the PhD Programs of the 42nd cycle (a.y. 2026/27), which opened on 3rd October 2025, is now closed.

Before applying, we invite you to visit the [**PhD School official** **website**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/phd) for a wide and in-depth sight of the program. We encourage you to apply as early as possible.

Following the links on the left-hand side of this page, you will be able to access all info necessary to [**prepare**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/phd/admission-phd-programs/preparing-apply) and [**submit**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/phd/admission-phd-programs/submitting-your-application "Submitting your application")  your application to our PhD programs.

Our programs are designed to enable our PhD Alumni to move on to academic positions in top world class business schools and universities. You can get more info about the placement of our former students by clicking [**here**](https://www.unibocconi.eu/wps/wcm/connect/bocconi/sitopubblico_en/navigation+tree/home/programs/phd/placement).

Please find a list of [**Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)**](https://www.unibocconi.eu/wps/wcm/connect/bocconi/sitopubblico_en/navigation+tree/home/programs/phd/admission+-+phd+programs/preparing+to+apply/faq/faq)
