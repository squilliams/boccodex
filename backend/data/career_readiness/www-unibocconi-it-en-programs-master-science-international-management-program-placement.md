#### Graduate Job Statistics

#### One year after graduation

- _**94.6%** of graduates are employed_
- _**52.8%** of employed graduates work abroad_

Data refers to a.y. 2022-2023 graduates of the International Management class

#### On graduation day

- _**78.4%** of graduates are employed_
- _**71.7%** of employed graduates work abroad_
- _They have been called to an average of **7.5** job interviews_
- _They have received on average **1.9** job offers_

Data refers to a.y. 2023-2024 graduates of the International Management class
