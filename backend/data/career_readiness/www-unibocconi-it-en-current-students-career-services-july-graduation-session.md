# July graduation session

A

solution is proposed to facilitate graduation in the July session for students pursuing curricular internships

for credits in Italy or abroad in the period January/February 2023, when this

is necessary for access to a graduate-level

program.

Students

who at the end of the January-February 2023 exam session have not taken all the

exams for first semester courses will be able to take those exams during the

July session and, should they pass, will graduate in July.

This

opportunity is available to students:

- engaged

in curricular internships for credits

in Italy or abroad in the period January/February 2023; i.e., who begin their

internships before the end of the January/February exam session (4 February 2023) and attain the minimum

duration required for [credit recognition](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/services/curricular-internships-credit) not later than 26 May 2023 (included);
- who

submit a request to that purpose to the

Academic Services no later than 9 June 2023 together with documentation certifying admission to a

graduate-level program which requires possession of the degree by July 2023.

It is hereby specified that in order to be admitted to

the graduation session students must still complete the [recognition procedure](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/services/credit-recognition-procedure) for their internships by the date set for completion of the curriculum

of studies of the July session (21 June 2023).

For

further information on requirements for access to graduation, consult the Guide

to the University (Chapter 10. Final Paper and Graduation), the graduation

deadline calendar published on the [dedicated page](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/timetables-calendars-and-rooms/graduation/graduation-calendar-and-deadlines-3-y-degree-programs) of the site, and/or contact the l'Academic Services

through B in touch (topic Graduation > Graduation session and administrative

deadlines).
