**Campus and culture**

# Mumbai

## A strategic hub for management education

The SDA Bocconi Asia Center has been the School’s pan-Asian reference point since 2012 – a place where academic knowledge, research, and managerial training come together to support the growth of individuals and organizations.

By combining SDA Bocconi’s academic rigor with a global perspective, the Mumbai campus promotes the development of both managerial and cross-functional skills.

It’s an ecosystem that fosters innovation and dialogue across cultures, equipping professionals and businesses with the tools they need to tackle business challenges beyond borders.
