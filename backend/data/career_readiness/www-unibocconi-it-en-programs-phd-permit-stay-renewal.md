In this section you can find some basic information to help you with the **renewal of your Permit of Stay (for non-EU students).**

The **Permit of Stay** is generally valid for one year and must be renewed **at least 60 days before it expires**.

_We recommend starting the renewal process90 days before expiration **.**_

[HOW TO RENEW YOUR PERMIT OF STAY](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/media/attachments/HOW%20TO%20RENEW%20YOUR%20PERMIT%20OF%20STAY_2025.pdf "HOW TO RENEW YOUR PERMIT OF STAY")

**Non-EU PhD Students**

PhD students who are awaiting the renewal of their **Permit of Stay** may request a **temporary permit** ( _permesso di soggiorno provvisorio_) **only if**:

- They have a **valid reason** for leaving Italy (e.g., participation in conventions, conferences, summer schools, etc.); and
- They **return to Italy before** the issuance of the renewed permit.

The **temporary permit** is valid for a **maximum of 90 days**.

**How to Request a Temporary Permit**

PhD students must go to the **Police Station (Questura)** **at least 10 days before departure**, bringing the **original documents** listed below, along with **photocopies**:

- Receipt issued by the Italian Post Office certifying submission of the permit renewal application
- Expired permit of stay
- 4 passport-sized photographs
- Passport
- A **letter in Italian** explaining the reason for the request, issued by the **PhD Administrative Assistant** and signed by both the **PhD Director** and the **Head of the PhD School Office**
