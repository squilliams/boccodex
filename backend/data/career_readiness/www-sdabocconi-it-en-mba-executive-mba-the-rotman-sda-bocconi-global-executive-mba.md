# The Rotman SDA Bocconi Global Executive MBA

## Truly global, distinctly personal.

General management and strategy

_Start_ 26 September 2026

_Where_ Toronto / Milano / Dubai / Roma / Montreal / Shanghai

_Format_ Modular

The **Rotman-SDA Bocconi Global Executive MBA (Master in Business Administration) is a modular program** that combines the expertise and perspectives of a leading European and a leading North American business school in a transformative journey across international business hubs. Designed as a launchpad for future career growth and **senior management roles**, GEMBA offers a highly personalized approach to leadership development while connecting participants with a diverse network of exceptional managers, faculty, executives, and thought leaders—expanding their global outlook.

This unique 13-month program, spanning 6 cities across continents, expands your global perspective and leadership potential while enhancing leadership abilities and managerial skills from day one—without disrupting your professional and personal commitments. The Rotman-SDA Bocconi GEMBA transforms the way you think, network, and do business in some of the world’s biggest markets, providing you with a wider range of opportunities, contacts, and experiences. Offered in partnership with **the Rotman School of Management at the University of Toronto in Canada**, this program equips you with the skills and global exposure to advance your career.

## A comprehensive learning experience

- ### Grow your leadership on the global stage

The Rotman-SDA Bocconi GEMBA transforms the way you think, network, and do business in major global markets. With 24 courses - including core and non-core courses, Study Tour and optional Capstone Project on a real-world challenge - you'll master business fundamentals while gaining cutting-edge skills in areas where our schools excel - design thinking, luxury brand management, analytics, fintech, and more. Build a powerful international network and accelerate your career.

- ### Eight residential modules

The program is built around 8 residential modules. These take place at the Rotman School, at both SDA Bocconi Campuses in Milan and Rome, and on the campuses of our international afﬁliate academic partners, providing in-depth local perspectives on global management. Each module consists of several courses, and includes lectures, team discussions, and other dynamic, real-time learning opportunities.

- ### Real-world experiences and learning safaris

Classroom sessions are interspersed with real-world experiences - company visits, exchanges with local executives, and dynamic networking events - that we call 'learning safaris': an impactful formula blending standout site visits with direct interaction with top executives. It's the essence of experiential learning, where participants witness and engage firsthand.

Evenings are ﬁlled with group projects, individual assignments and time to explore the local culture and cities.

- ### Capstone Projects

The integrative Capstone Project allows students to apply GEMBA learnings to a real-world challenge, often aligned with their professional context. Students define a meaningful problem - individually or in small teams - and develop a viable solution with faculty guidance.

Projects run throughout the program (with some flexibility depending on scope) and are structured around a clear roadmap. They typically include problem definition, environmental and organizational analysis, a recommended solution, and an implementation plan.

- ### Online learning

Online learning predominates in the periods between modules: preparation begins for the next set of courses, with study materials delivered a month or two before class begins. This is also the time to complete assignments and team deliverables, participate in review sessions and write exams.

- ### The program by the numbers

**13** months

**8** residential modules

**61** in-class days

**2** 8-day modules

**6** 7.5-day modules

- ### Learning model

The Global EMBA is delivered through a variety of learning formats. The learning platform of the Global EMBA aims to make full use of your time by combining lectures, case studies, group projects, and business simulations with distance-learning sessions.

## Admissions

We seek highly motivated candidates with diverse backgrounds, strong professional experience, and a drive for global business transformation.

The strength of the Rotman-SDA Bocconi GEMBA lies in its talented, diverse, and ambitious students. We invest considerable effort in the selection and admissions process to ensure a dynamic and enriching learning environment.

**Requirements**:

- Degree certificate (in any discipline), transcript of the grades
- Application form
- Detailed CV/Resume
- 5+ years of full-time professional experience
- 2+ years of mid-to senior management experience (people,   project or budget experience)
- Current full-time employment
- Proficiency in English
- GMAT or GRE
- Two references
- Interview
- Strong motivation, good leadership and interpersonal skills

## Fees and scholarships

The Rotman-SDA Bocconi GEMBA represents a significant financial commitment, but it is a strategic investment in global business leadership with substantial long-term benefits.

The tuition fee for GEMBA 2026-2027 is USD 110,000, payable in five installments.

Thanks to the support of the Bocconi Alumni Community, 300,000 USD in merit-based tuition waivers are available for outstanding candidates.
