Participation in the 18-month EMBA program implies a fair financial commitment. But it is a strategic investment that certainly offers high future returns—both personally and professionally.

## Fees and expenses

The tuition fee for the 2026-2028 EMBA Modular is € 62,000 (VAT exempt), payable in five installments.

- First installment

**€ 10,000**

upon acceptance of admission

- Second installment

**€ 13,000**

by 18 December 2026

- Third installment

**€ 13,000**

by 30 April 2027

- Fourth installment

**€ 13,000**

by 30 September 2027

- Fifth installment

**€ 13,000**

by 4 January 2028

**Application fee: € 150**

The fees include:

  - Internal educational materials
  - Use of the school's facilities

Participants in the [PSM-Programma di Sviluppo Manageriale](https://www.sdabocconi.it/en/on-campus-executive-education/programma-di-sviluppo-manageriale/) of SDA Bocconi who are interested in the Executive MBA can access the program with a 20% discount on the registration fee.

**Criteria and assignment procedure:**

The number of tuition waivers available for each phase and the criteria for their assignment are determined by a Commission, whose decisions are final. Beneficiaries will be notified of the outcome by the start date of the Executive MBA.

**Requirements to apply for an exemption:**

- The completed and required documentation for the Executive MBA application must be submitted.
- The exemption request must be submitted in response to the call for applications, along with the specified documents, through the [Admissions portal](https://admissions.unibocconi.it/admissions/Students/OpenFromExtUrl?concourseId=12516&ordType=&_gl=1*1awzfzl*_gcl_aw*R0NMLjE3NDE3NzczNzIuQ2owS0NRanc0Y1MtQmhER0FSSXNBQmc0X0oxQlZsNWNxMWdGVTlZbzk3NmdOT1RJTUVIT0Ewb0phdWo2MDVnck8xZnNMSHlOM2EtMnBJNGFBdXJMRUFMd193Y0I.*_gcl_dc*R0NMLjE3NDE3NzczNzIuQ2owS0NRanc0Y1MtQmhER0FSSXNBQmc0X0oxQlZsNWNxMWdGVTlZbzk3NmdOT1RJTUVIT0Ewb0phdWo2MDVnck8xZnNMSHlOM2EtMnBJNGFBdXJMRUFMd193Y0I.*_gcl_au*MTA1NzYyMDQzNi4xNzM5OTgyOTg2*_ga*Nzg3MzM2NTg2LjE3MjEyMjEzMzM.*_ga_50JZS9XDXR*MTc0MjkwOTQ2MC40MDEuMS4xNzQyOTEwMDI1LjAuMC4w).
- The request must be submitted within the deadlines and in compliance with the requirements outlined in the call. Requests that do not meet the requirements will not be considered.

**Evaluation criteria**

Exemption requests will be assessed based on the following factors:

- The candidate’s academic and professional background
- Motivation letter
- Requirements defined in the call
- Results of the Executive MBA selection tests

The tuition waivers will be assigned during the selection process, within the deadlines specified in the call. **The request may be submitted even before the completion of the selection process.**

**Admission to the Executive MBA is independent of the awarding of an exemption or scholarship.**

## Loans and funding

The School assists candidates with their search for funding by supplying the necessary documentation, and encouraging them to explore all scholarship and loan possibilities. SDA Bocconi partners with a number of financial institutes offering low-interest loans to admitted and eligible students. Our partner institutes are:

- [Prodigy Finance](https://www.sdabocconi.it/en/prodigy-finance/) for international students (please refer to the eligibility criteria);
- [Lendwise](https://lendwise.com/borrow/education-funding/university/bocconi-university/) for international students;
- Italian Banks for [Italian](https://www.unibocconi.it/it/studenti-iscritti/agevolazioni/finanziamenti-master-universitari-e-sda) and [international students](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/student-loans) (please note that applications for loans issued by Italian Banks must be submitted by the end of the month following the starting date of your Master);
- Bocconi offers Support Programs to student-athletes who participate in sport competitions at regional, national and international level. Find out more [here](https://www.bocconisport.eu/en/support-programs-student-athletes)

Furthermore, the University provides information about [financing sources](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/funding-country) for international students from a large number of countries. For further information, please visit Bocconi University’s website.
