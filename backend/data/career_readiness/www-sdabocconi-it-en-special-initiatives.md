\\

**REInnovation Academy** \\
\\
REInnovation Academy is a management and economics-financial training program designed to develop junior professionals capable of being hired directly by Academy Partner Companies. \\
\\
The project is an integral part of the "REInnovation Lab," a research and training platform dedicated to the real estate sector, developed by SDA Bocconi School of Management and Assoimmobiliare.\\
\\
Discover more](https://www.sdabocconi.it/en/faculty-research/research/reinnovation-lab/reinnovation-lab-academy/)
- [**Best Performance Award** \\
\\
The Best Performance Award is the annual prize awarded to Italian companies that stand out for their excellence in sustainable development, understood in a broad sense as the ability to conduct business while ensuring business continuity (a necessary condition) while respecting: \\
\\
  - human and environmental dimensions (Green & Social) \\
  - innovation (Innovation & Technology) \\
  - economic management (Value Added)\\
\\
