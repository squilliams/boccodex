**BAR EXAMINATION (NOTARIAL PROFESSION)**

Thanks to an agreement between the University and the Milan Board of Notaries, Law students may advance 6 of the total of 18 months of training for access to profession of notary.

For further information about the necessary documentation and how to submit your application to the Board, please [click here](https://www.unibocconi.it/it/corsi-di-studio/esami-di-stato/esame-notaio-tirocinio-anticipato).

**BAR EXAMINATION (LEGAL PROFESSION)**

In the final year of studies, 6 of the total of 18 months of training prescribed by law for access to the Bar Exam may be effected.

For further information [click here](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/services/early-traineeship-bar-examination-legal-profession).

**EXAMINATION FOR CERTIFIED PUBLIC ACCOUNTANT AND ACCOUNTING EXPERT**

For information on State exam sessions enabling to the profession of Certified Public Accountant, to the profession of Accounting Expert and the additional tests enabling to the profession of legal audit [click here](https://www.unibocconi.it/it/corsi-di-studio/esami-di-stato) _(only in Italian)_.
