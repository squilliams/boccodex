# Healthcare

The healthcare sector is undergoing a major transformation, driven by demographic trends, technological innovation, and evolving organizational models. Healthcare and social care professionals are called upon to embrace new responsibilities and strengthen their strategic and managerial skills—key to ensuring both the effectiveness and the sustainability of healthcare systems, and to creating value for society.

In this section, you will find the School’s full educational portfolio dedicated to this topic, along with events, news, research activities, and in-depth content.
