## About

The Fourth Bocconi Assembly for Innovation and Cooperation

**2025 Edition**

The Assembly will take place on **26 and 27 June, 2025**, on the new campus of Bocconi University, a leading European institution committed to generating knowledge that matters and delivering cutting-edge business research. **SDA Bocconi School of Management will host the Conference on its brand new Campus**, an innovative urban space, a meeting point for excellence in research, education, and architecture.

We gratefully acknowledge the support to BAIC of **Romeo and Enrica Invernizzi Foundation**, devoted to inspiring the future of basic research, fostering progress, cooperation, and innovation and contribute to social progress.

## ICRIOS

Funded and supported by a donation from Milan’s **Romeo e Enrica Invernizzi Foundation, ICRIOS is a research center at Bocconi University** created to be a reference point for cutting-edge, multi-disciplinary and collaborative research on innovation, organization, strategy and entrepreneurship, with the explicit objective to influence in a significant way both the academic debate and management practice.

**ICRIOS promotes collaboration and knowledge** sharing at any stage of the research process, especially among scholars in diverse and complementary fields, based across nations, and working in different research traditions. ICRIOS fellows are selected among recognized experts in their field of study.

## Paper Submission

We seek submissions of rigorous academic papers that have the potential for publication in top management journals and that are aligned with the themes of the assembly. Presenters will have the opportunity to receive feedback from leading scholars and participate in the assembly discussions. By submitting a paper, you commit to presenting it in person in Milan upon invitation. The invitation is personal and cannot be transferred to a coauthor.

**Call for Papers**

**The Fourth Bocconi Assembly for Innovation and Cooperation**

In an era of rapid technological transformation, political shifts, environmental crises, and global conflicts, collaborative dialogue and debate offer hope. The Fourth Bocconi Assembly for Innovation and Cooperation (BAIC), organized by the Management and Technology Department at Bocconi University, invites you to explore these influential forces and their implications for business and society at large.

Join leading scholars and industry experts for two days of stimulating keynotes, panel discussions, competitive paper presentations, and interactive sessions. The upcoming BAIC conference will explore various facets of technology, societal and organizational shifts, as well as global dynamics, offering a platform for designing future research agendas and collaborations.

The conference will be hosted at the SDA Bocconi School of Management state-of-the-art new campus, a hub for excellence in research, education, and architectural innovation. This vibrant urban space offers the perfect backdrop for stimulating creative dialogue.

**Event Details:**

**Submission system open: December 2024**

**Paper Submission Deadline: February 16, 2025**

**Conference Date: June 26-27, 2025**

**Location: Milan, Italy**

Don't miss this opportunity to be part of a groundbreaking event that aims to shape the future of research in management! Save the date and we look forward to seeing you in Milan.

Acknowledgments:

We extend our sincere gratitude to the Romeo and Enrica Invernizzi Foundation for their unwavering support. Their dedication to advancing research, promoting innovation, and contributing to social progress has been instrumental in making this event possible.

### Life in Milano

You will discover its historical monuments and its landmarks, its financial and fashion districts, its renowned football clubs and its taste for food and wine, all in your everyday life. The city has long been the center where you can find the finest Italy has to offer in food & beverage and related industries. It is the hub of fashion, creativity, design, and culture. Thus, Milano is the ideal campus to discover Italian excellence, allowing SDA Bocconi to bring together business and innovation with the unparalleled wealth of the Italian cultural heritage.

**The new Campus**

SDA Bocconi’s campus buildings are in the center of Milano. In addition to being the business and financial capital of Italy, Milano is also ideally located in the heart of Europe.
