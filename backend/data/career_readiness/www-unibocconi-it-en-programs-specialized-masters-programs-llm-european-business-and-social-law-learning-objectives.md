The LLM in European Business and Social Law aims to allow students to:

- Develop a thorough comprehension of **EU law, EU institutions and internal market**

**dynamics**
- Enable specialization in one of the following legal areas: **Business Law; Digital Law; Public Law**

- Access the traditional **teaching of law enriched with the global dimension** needed today to enter the international market
- Combine the **theoretical**

**dimension** with a **hands-on approach**
- Understand legal scenarios through the **analysis of cases and rulings or via legal**

**competitions** (such as moot courts)
- Develop **analytic**

**reasoning and sound methodologies** to address new and challenging legal issues.
