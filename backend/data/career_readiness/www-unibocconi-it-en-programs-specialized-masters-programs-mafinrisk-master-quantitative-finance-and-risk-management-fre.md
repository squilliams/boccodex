# Frequently Asked Questions

MAFINRISK - Master of Quantitative Finance and Risk Management

[Overview](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/specialized-masters-programs/mafinrisk-master-quantitative-finance-and-risk-management) [Program Structure](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/specialized-masters-programs/mafinrisk-master-quantitative-finance-and-risk-management/program-structure) [Admissions](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/specialized-masters-programs/mafinrisk-master-quantitative-finance-and-risk-management/admissions) [Contact us](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/specialized-masters-programs/mafinrisk-master-quantitative-finance-and-risk-management/contact-us)

**Can I apply if I have not completed my undergraduate degree?**

You can submit your application prior to the conferral of your degree. We will accept and evaluate your dossier. **However, be aware that, in case of admission, you will have to receive your degree within 90 days from the beginning of the program**.

**Can I be exempted from taking the GMAT or GRE?**

All applicants - regardless of educational background or experience - must take either the [GMAT](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/specialized-masters/gmat-and-gre) (Graduate Management Admission Test/ [GMAT Focus Edition](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/specialized-masters/gmat-and-gre) (recently introduced by GMAC) or the [GRE](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/specialized-masters/gmat-and-gre) (Graduate Record Examinations) score or alternatively take the Bocconi online selection test. For further information regarding the GMAT test please visit [https://www.gmac.com/.](https://www.gmac.com/) For more information about GRE please consult [https://www.gre.org/](https://gre.org/).

**What is the Bocconi Test?**

The Bocconi Test is an aptitude test which consists of 70 multiple-choise questions, divided in 4 subject areas: verbal reasoning, numerical reasoning, critical reasoning and abstract reasoning/problem solving.The Bocconi online test is delivered through and external platform implemented with a proctoring system to monitor the applicant during the test. Within the platform, the candidate can view the dates available to take the test, book a test date, take the test online, view the results and download the score certificate. The candidate can select whether to take the test in English or in Italian.

**Can the Bocconi Test be taken online?**

The Bocconi test is an online test.

**Can I apply for MAFINRISK before taking the GMAT or the GRE?**

Candidates must take either the GMAT or the GRE prior to submitting their application. You need to upload your official GMAT/GRE score report into your online application upon submission, therefore by the application deadline. If you have not received your official score report when submitting your application we accept the unofficial score report as evidence of your GMAT/GRE on condition that the official one is provided upon admission to the program.

**What GMAT/GRE score do I need?**

It is very unusual for us to accept candidates with a total Gmat score below 650/a total Gmat Focus Edition score below 595 or a total Gre score below 158/160. The Gmat scores of admitted applicants vary across classes ranging from 650 to 750 (A Gmat score of 700+ or a Gre score of 165+ can add weight to your application). The Gmat Focus Edition scores of admitted candidates can vary ranging from 595 to 755.

**How do I report my GMAT/GRE scores to Bocconi?**

We recommend you not to ask the Test Center to send your score directly to Bocconi. **You are required to  upload your GMAT/GRE score report into your online application.**

**Can I submit both my Gmat (or Gre) score and my online Bocconi test score?**

If you have multiple test scores (e.g. Bocconi online test and GMAT), you can upload them to your application. The system will automatically take the most competitive score into account.

**Is the English language certificate compulsory?**

It is essential that all participants speak, write and understand English fluently. It is necessary to demonstrate an English language knowledge equal to B2 level. In particular applicants must meet one of the following requirements: university qualification (or secondary school diploma) awarded in English; one of the recognized international English language certificates (BEC Higher, BEC Vantage, CAE, CPE, FCE; IELTS; TOEFL; Duolingo English Test; English exam at B2 level (or higher) passed in a university course; English mother tongue.

**What kind of reference letters should I submit?**

You can submit one or two reference letters, academic or professional. Be sure that reference letters are written by someone familiar with your skills, character, and accomplishments at university or in the workplace. We do not require the reference letters to be in a standard format.

**What weighting do you place on the various different selection criteria?**

The Selection Committee will look at your academic records, your GMAT/GRE/Bocconi test score, your personal statement and motivation, your references, your CV as a whole and will make a careful, balanced assessment based on all these criteria.

**Are all applicants invited for an interview?**

We do not require all candidates to have an interview with us. In some  cases, after the initial application review, the Selection Committee may decide to invite a candidate for an interview.

**I have previously applied for MAFINRISK unsuccessfully. Can I reapply?**

You can reapply and submit a new application.

**Should I translate my degree?**

If you hold a degree issued by a non Italian Institution/University, the Bocconi Admissions Office can ask you to provide some additional documents aimed at verifying in detail the compatibility of your academic path with the Specialized Master program of interest.

You may be asked to provide the " **Declaration of Value**", which is an official document (written in Italian) - issued by the competent Italian Authorities (Embassy/Consulate/Italian Cultural Institute) in the country to which the educational system of your university refers - that confirms the validity of your Bachelor Diploma or Degree.

Candidates who are required to submit the Declaration of Value will be informed by the Bocconi Admissions Office upon notification of the selection results.

Candidates who already have a Declaration of Value of their Bachelor or Degree are advised to upload it into their online application.
