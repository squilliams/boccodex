# Introduction to ISU Bocconi Scholarship a.y. 2025-26 - Timeline

The Lombardy Region and the Ministry of the Universities and Research allocate, through the Universities, a cash benefit called the "Right to University Education Scholarship". For the Bocconi University, students enrolled in Bachelor of Science, Law programs, Master of Science and PhD programs, interested in applying for this benefit for the academic year 2025-2026, must submit an application for the "ISU Bocconi Scholarship a.y. 2025-2026".

In order to submit the application for the "ISU Bocconi Scholarship a.y. 2025-2026" ( **grant, related services and supplements**) economic requirements, enrollment requirements and merit requirements (minimum number of credits to be earned for confirmation of the benefit for each year of enrollment) must be met. These requirements are available on the dedicated pages.

The deadlines for submitting the application for the ISU grant are final and different for students enrolled in the first year of their BSc or Law programs, for students enrolled in the first year of their MSc or PhD, or students enrolled in years subsequent to the first (for all the study programs, BSc, Law programs, MSc and PhD). These deadlines are detailed below.

[**First-year students of Bachelor of Science or Law programs**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/funding/isu-bocconi-scholarship-first-year-students-ay-2025-26 "ISU Bocconi Scholarship - First year students a.y. 2025-26")

The application for the ISU Bocconi Scholarship a.y. 2025-2026 can be submitted by **first-year students** only after the submission of the single application " [**Bocconi4access to Education a.y. 2025-2026**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/bachelor-and-law-programs/funding "Funding")". For each admission session, a specific and corresponding time window exists for students to submit the funding application

Application deadlines and further deadlines are as follow:

- **Early Session and HEC 1st round**: January 7 - January 21, 2025
- **Winter Session and HEC 2nd round**: March 11 - April 3, 2025
- **Spring Session and HEC 3rd round**: May 20 - June 4, 2025

For the ISU Bocconi scholarship, applications will not be accepted outside of the above deadlines and from students who have not previously presented the "Bocconi4access to Education a.y. 2025-2026" application within the established deadlines.

In order to assess the economic conditions of applicants for the ISU Bocconi scholarship, the Office will verify that the documentation presented in the two applications is coherent and corresponds to the economic/financial situation declared by the family.

The deadline for submitting documentation concerning the non-resident or commuting status is available within the application. The final ranking will be published by the end of October 2025.

[**First-year students of Master of Science (Bocconi and Italian applicants)**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/funding/isu-bocconi-scholarship-first-year-students-ay-2025-26 "ISU Bocconi Scholarship - First year students a.y. 2025-26")

The application for the ISU Bocconi Scholarship a.y. 2025-2026 can be submitted by **first-year students** only after the submission of the single application " [**Bocconi4access to Education a.y. 2025-2026**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/master-science-and-ma-programs/funding "Funding")".

The online application for the ISU Bocconi Scholarship will be available during the following period:

- **Bocconi applicant 1st round and Italian applicants 1st round**: from January 15 to January 29, 2025
- **Bocconi applicant 2nd round and Italian applicants 2nd round \***: from May 29 to June 12, 2025

**\*** Students enrolled in the LSE-Bocconi and Sciences Po-Bocconi Double Degrees programs can participate in this selection session by contacting the Fees, Funding and Housing office.

In order to assess the economic conditions of applicants for the ISU Bocconi scholarship, the Office will verify that the documentation presented in the two applications is coherent and corresponds to the economic/financial situation declared by the family.

The deadline for submitting documentation concerning the non-resident or commuting status is available within the application. The final ranking will be published by the end of October 2025.

[**First-year students of Master of Science (International applicants)**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/funding/isu-bocconi-scholarship-first-year-students-ay-2025-26 "ISU Bocconi Scholarship - First year students a.y. 2025-26")

The online application for the ISU Bocconi Scholarship will be available during the following period:

- **1st round**: from January 15 to January 29, 2025
- **2nd round**: from March 12 to March 26, 2025
- **3rd round**: from April 17 to May 6, 2025
- **4th round**: from June 5 to June 16, 2025

In order to assess the economic conditions of applicants for the ISU Bocconi scholarship, the Office will verify that the documentation presented is coherent and corresponds to the economic/financial situation declared by the family.

The deadline for submitting documentation concerning the non-resident or commuting status is available within the application. The final ranking will be published by the end of October 2025.

International students admitted to 5th round will not be able to apply for ISU Bocconi Scholarship application a.y. 2025-26
