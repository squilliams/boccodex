The outstanding teaching and coaching methods, which have helped to establish Bocconi University’s international reputation in the business world, are applied throughout this new LL.M Program.

This professional program features prominently **hands-on activities**. Close connection with specialized **law firms**, **digital and high-tech companies and national or international regulatory institutions** is ensured, also in view of internships and clinical works.

The final goal of our LL.M Program is to prepare tomorrow’s professionals to find cutting-edge solutions to the legal questions emerging from fast-paced technological development and digital activities.
