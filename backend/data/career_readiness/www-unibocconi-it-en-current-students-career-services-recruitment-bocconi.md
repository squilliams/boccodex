# Recruitment at Bocconi

For more effective recruitment, the University offers potential employers a variety of **online** services -free of charge- to assist them in their process of research and selection.

- [Tailor-make your international recruitment at Bocconi](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/employers-and-partners/employers/online-recruitment/jobgate)
- [Start an internship](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/employers-and-partners/employers/setting-internship-universita-bocconi)
- [Get the CVs: recent undergraduates and graduates](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/employers-and-partners/employers/online-recruitment/cvs-recent-graduates)
- [Get the CVs: recent Master programs graduates](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/employers-and-partners/employers/online-recruitment/request-cv-book-master-holders)

The university places certain services at the disposal of employers, which enable them to offer personalized employer branding and recruitment activities **on-campus**.

- [On-Campus Company Presentations\*](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/employers-and-partners/employers/campus-recruitment/campus-company-presentations)
- [Participate in the Drop-In Sessions\*](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/employers-and-partners/employers/campus-recruitment/drop-sessions)
- [Participate in the Recruiting Dates](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/services/recruiting-dates)
- [Participate in the Investment Banking Week\*](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/employers-and-partners/employers/campus-recruitment/investment-banking-week)

\*These initiatives are reserved to those companies participating in the [Corporate Associates Program](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/media/attachments/PIA_CAE_SITO_last-update-11_1_24.pdf)
