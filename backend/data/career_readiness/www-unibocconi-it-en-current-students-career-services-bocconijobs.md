# BOCCONI&JOBS

Bocconi&Jobs is the Università Bocconi  career event dedicated to students, graduates and Specialized Master holders.

It’s an exclusive opportunity to get information, find out about opportunities and share experiences by meeting the companies, organizations and professional firms that best represent the Italian and international job markets.
