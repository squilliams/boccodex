#### Graduate Job Statistics

#### One year after graduation

- _**96.3%** are employed_
- _**88.5** are employed abroad_

Data refers to 2021-2022 a.y. graduates

#### On graduation day

- _**89.7%** of graduates are employed_
- _**84.6%** are employed abroad_
- _They have been called to an average of **8.6** job interviews_
- _They have received on average **2.2** job offers_

Survey - class graduating in October 2023 session

Graduate **employers** include, among others:

- Deloitte - USA, Canada, Italy, Ireland
- EY - USA
- Google - Ireland, Germany
- McKinsey & Co. - Italy, USA
- PIMCO - USA, United Kingdom
- PwC - USA, UAE
- The Boston Consulting Group - Italy, UAE
