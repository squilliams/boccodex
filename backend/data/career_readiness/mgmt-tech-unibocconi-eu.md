Highlight

Alessandro Iorio on the Editorial Review Board of Organization Science

Alessandro Iorio, Assistant Professor in the Department of Management and Technology, has been appointed as a member of the Editorial Review Board of Organization Science. His appointment is effective from October 1 until the end of October 2026.

_Organization Science_ publishes fundamental research about organizations and their processes, structures, technologies, identities, forms, and people. Its research portfolio includes papers grounded in a range of fields and disciplines that include organizational behavior and theory, psychology, sociology, economics, political science, strategic management, information systems, innovation, entrepreneurship, and cognitive science.
