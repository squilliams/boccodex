# System maintenance update (Monday, 24 November 10:00-11:00pm)

24 Nov 2025, by Library & Archives

From 10:00 to 11:00pm on Monday, 24 November 2025, a system maintenance operation is scheduled

During this time, some platforms and services will be temporarily unavailable, including yoU@B Diary, databases, and Badge and QR code management services.

We apologize for the temporary inconvenience.
