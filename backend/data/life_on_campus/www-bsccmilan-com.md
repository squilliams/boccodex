# Bocconi  Students  Consulting  Club

## Who We Are

![AdobeStock_278370910-scaled-1_edited.jpg](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/a4051a_5d497e65f541420a84244e454cee942e~mv2.jpg/v1/fill/w_309,h_356,al_c,q_80,usm_0.66_1.00_0.01,enc_avif,quality_auto/AdobeStock_278370910-scaled-1_edited.jpg)

Bocconi Students Consulting Club is the oldest and largest graduate consulting club in Italy. We are among the major consulting clubs in Europe and one of the most active associations at Bocconi University. BSCC mission is to offer its associates activities and instruments to support them in landing their dream job in consulting or related industries.

## What We Offer

![bscc consulting training_edited.jpg](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/a4051a_80c097055bbc491889b4f6a31f7bf562~mv2.jpg/v1/fill/w_309,h_310,al_c,q_80,usm_0.66_1.00_0.01,enc_avif,quality_auto/bscc%20consulting%20training_edited.jpg)

Our trainings are led by experienced BSCC associates and alumnis. Dive into the depths of consulting with practical tips, proven strategies, and real-world case studies that will not only prepare you for case interviews but also set you apart in the competitive world of consulting.

### Training Excellence

![bscc networking_edited.jpg](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/a4051a_bc9da68183844920be467a3d3ac243c1~mv2.jpg/v1/fill/w_309,h_310,al_c,q_80,usm_0.66_1.00_0.01,enc_avif,quality_auto/bscc%20networking_edited.jpg)

Networking at BSCC goes beyond the ordinary – it's your gateway to the consulting world. Each semester, we organize exclusive company visits with leading consulting firms.  Several times each year, BSCC alumni gather to offer you a real-world perspective, sharing their success stories and offering practical career guidance.
