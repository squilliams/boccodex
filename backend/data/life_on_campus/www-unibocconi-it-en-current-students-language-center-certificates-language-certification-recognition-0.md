# Language Certification Recognition

**Instructions to submit the request of recognition of a language certification for students enrolled in former cohorts** **(before the 2014-15 cohort in BSc Programs and CLMG and before 2018-19 cohort in MSc Programs):**

- Fill out entirely the **form** available at this [link](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/media/attachments/ING-form-registrazione-certificazioni-v2.pdf).
- Print the form filled out, sign it, specify the date and make a scan of it.
- Send a scan of it with date and original signature together with a scan of the certification, using the B in Touch procedure available in the yoU@B Diary, topic: "LANGUAGES > Discover language certifications".
- **Wait for the reply** by the Language Center which will indicate the outcome of the application submitted.

If the form has been completed correctly and the conversion request is accepted, the grade out of 30 resulting from the conversion will be recorded indicatively within two weeks than the request date.

If the form is completed incorrectly, the student will receive indication about it and he/she will have to submit a new request attaching again the form and scanned copy of the certification.

It is specified that the **date of submission of the complete and correct request** must be indicated on the form. In case of inconsistency, the certification will be recorded with the **date corresponding to the complete and correct request submission**.
