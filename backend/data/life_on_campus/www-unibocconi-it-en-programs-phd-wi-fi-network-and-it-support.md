Devices equipped with a Wi-Fi card can access the Bocconi wireless network on campus. PhD students can access the Wi-Fi network through the [yoU@B student Diary](https://agenda.unibocconi.it/) (“Wireless network access” section).Students who need support with the Wi-Fi network can refer to the B in Touch via [You@B](https://agenda.unibocconi.it/)
