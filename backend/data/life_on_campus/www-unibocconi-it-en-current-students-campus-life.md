# Campus Life

Make the most of your time!

Campus life is the point of reference for **cultural and artistic activities**, as well as **volunteer** and **social engagement** activities. Campus Life also coordinates the activities of the **counseling** service, which offers psychological support to students, and provides various courses and activities on **self-empowerment and wellbeing**. **Student association** activities are also coordinated by Campus Life.

Find out more about all the opportunities available so you can make the most of your university experience!
