# Counseling contact information

- In-person walk-ins on Mondays, Thursdays and Fridays from 3 to 7 p.m. at Room 3, Campus Life Space, via Bocconi 12, mezzanine floor
- E-mail: [counseling@unibocconi.it](mailto:counseling@unibocconi.it)
- Tel. 02 5836 2083 Monday to Friday from 9 a.m. to 1 p.m. and 2 to 7 p.m.
- Microsoft Teams Monday to Friday from 9 a.m. to 1 p.m. and 2 to 7 p.m. by writing to “counseling”

If you need support during days and hours not covered by the Bocconi Counseling Service or during holidays and University closures, you can contact International SOS at +33(0)155 633 155

Bocconi Counseling Service is not an emergency service.

If you don't know where to turn in an emergency or out of hours, here are a number of options.

**Emergency numbers**

- **112**: In case of a medical or psychological emergency, you can contact this number 24h/24, including to request an ambulance.
- **1522**: In cases of perceived violence, you can contact this number free of charge 24h/24. Specialized operators will take requests for help and support from victims of violence and stalking.

**Services**

- In case of medical or psychological emergency, it is possible to go to the local hospital emergency room free of charge without an appointment.
- In cases of perceived violence, in addition to contacting the 1522 number free of charge, in the main Italian cities you can also access the Anti-Violence Centers in person, where you will find specialized and trained staff for psychological interviews for help, not only in Italian.
