# New reserved seats for even more inclusive access

23 Jan 2026, by Library & Archives

Starting in January 2026, two reserved seats will be introduced in the Library’s reading rooms.

Thanks to booking through the **yoU@B PRO** app, access will be simple and immediate, ensuring maximum convenience for those who are entitled to use them.

In addition to these, five priority seats will be available on a non-exclusive basis: they can be booked and used by anyone, but must be vacated if requested by a person with disabilities. This is a simple act of consideration that fosters cooperation and mutual respect.

**This initiative reaffirms our commitment to making the Library an increasingly welcoming, accessible, and inclusive space, where everyone can feel at ease and find the ideal conditions for studying and working.**
