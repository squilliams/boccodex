# Preferential rate canteen service

All students **regularly enrolled** in a regular year of a degree program, master's degree program, or PhD program who have the requirements indicated in the table below can use the canteen service at a reduced rate at one of the designated **[dining areas](https://www.unibocconi.it/wps/wcm/connect/bocconi/sitopubblico_en/navigation+tree/home/programs/current+students/services/funding/2023-24+dining+areas)**:

|     |     |     |
| --- | --- | --- |
|  | Preferential rate meal | Application |
| ISU Bocconi eligible students | **V** | **X** |
| Residents in one of the Bocconi Residence Halls at preferential  rate | **V** | **X** |
| Students who request preferential  rate canteen service only\*. | **V** | **V** |

\\* Students who comply with the **[economic requirements](https://www.unibocconi.it/wps/wcm/connect/bocconi/sitopubblico_en/navigation+tree/home/programs/current+students/services/funding/2023-2024+economic+requirements)** and intend to request the preferential  rate canteen service **will** **have to submit the dedicated online application from** 19th to 29th

October 2023;

**[Preferential rate canteen service application](https://admissions.unibocconi.it/admissions/Students/OpenFromExtUrl?concourseId=10976&ordType=)**

The preferential  rate meal assistance will be valid starting **on December 1, 2023 until November 30, 2024.**

Preferential rate meals

The student who benefits from the preferential  rate canteen service can purchase a full meal at the rates indicated below, usually consisting of first course, second course, side dish, bread, fruit or drink.

The specific types of meal that can be purchased at a preferential  rate must be checked at the point of sale.

Students will be able to buy n. 2 meals per day, one for lunch (from 11:45 to 14:30) and one for dinner (from 19:00 to 20:30) at preferential  rate using their Bocconi ID card.

Once the meals have been purchased, they are loaded from the office onto the Bocconi card indicatively within the two working days following the purchase and in any case by the end of next month.

One or more meal packages consisting of 10 meals each for the amounts indicated below can be purchased.

\> 10 meals in 1st ISU BOCCONI bracket: € 27.5;

\> 10 meals in 2nd ISU BOCCONI bracket: € 34.00;

\> 10 meals in 3rd ISU BOCCONI bracket: € 41.00.

It should be noted that prices mentioned above refers to a.y. 2022-23.

**Starting from 1st decmber 2023 the following new prices will come into effect, increased on the basis of inflation:**

\> 10 meals in 1st ISU BOCCONI bracket: € 30.00;

\> 10 meals in 2nd ISU BOCCONI bracket: € 38.00;

\> 10 meals in 3rd ISU BOCCONI bracket: € 46.00.

Once the meals have been loaded onto the card, Meals can be used in a maximum of two per day, one for lunch and one for dinner (according to the periods foreseen for the individual stores) for the purchase of a complete meal at preferential  rate.

**Please note:** In the event of loss and simultaneous request for a new Bocconi badge, to access the canteen service at a reduced rate, you must send an email with the subject "Loss of Bocconi badge" to [funding@unibocconi.it](mailto:funding@unibocconi.it) to change the code of your own badge.

It is specified that following the **revocation** of the ISU Bocconi Scholarship, students who **meet** the economic requirements envisaged in the academic year following the one in which the revocation is incurred and intending to request **only the canteen service at a preferential rate**, they can submit a specific online **application** within the foreseen period, even if they are not eligible for the ISU Bocconi Scholarship.
