**Who Are We**

Founded in 2023, Bocconi Students for Alternative Investments (BSAI) has rapidly established itself as the largest association dedicated to alternative investments at Bocconi University. In just two years, we have grown to 72 members across 9 specialized divisions, published numerous analytical articles, and organized multiple signature events open to both our associates and the broader Bocconi community.

**Our Mission**

At BSAI, we are committed to:

- Connecting students with the alternative investments industry through networking events, guest lectures, and professional workshops with leading experts from private equity, hedge funds, venture capital, real estate, and other alternative asset classes

- Cultivating passion for alternative investments by providing comprehensive educational resources, hands-on experience, and mentorship opportunities that bridge theoretical knowledge with practical applications

- Developing essential competencies through division-specific training programs that equip our members with the technical and analytical skills needed to excel in the alternative investments sector

- Creating a collaborative community where students can explore career paths, share insights, and build meaningful professional relationships within this dynamic field

**Our Divisions**

**Hedge Funds:**

Exploring diverse investment strategies and market opportunities to generate superior risk-adjusted returns across financial markets.

**Private Equity:**

Analyzing leveraged buyouts, growth investments, and turnaround strategies to transform companies and create long-term value.

**Private Credit:**

Researching direct lending, mezzanine financing, and distressed debt strategies to understand how private capital is deployed outside public markets and generate insights on risk-adjusted returns.

**Real Estate:**

Evaluating property investments, development opportunities, and market trends across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.

**Venture Capital:**

Supporting innovation by identifying promising startups and emerging technologies with significant growth and disruption potential.

**Quant:**

Applying mathematical models, statistical analysis, and algorithmic strategies to identify investment opportunities and optimize portfolios.

**Energy and Infrastructure:**

Analyzing investments in renewable energy, utilities, transportation networks, and essential public services with long-term returns.

**Media:**

Creating compelling content and publications to showcase market insights, investment perspectives, and association activities.

**Operations:**

Coordinating association logistics, organizing professional networking opportunities, and delivering impactful educational programming.
