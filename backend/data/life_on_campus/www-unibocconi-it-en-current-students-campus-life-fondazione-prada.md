# Fondazione Prada

Since 1993, Fondazione Prada has been questioning the intentions and relevance of cultural engagement today, with a series of constantly evolving projects. There have been 'utopian' commissions to individual artists, conferences on contemporary philosophy, research exhibitions and film initiatives. The Foundation wants to offer new opportunities to broaden and deepen our ways of learning.

"What is a cultural institution for?" This is the fundamental question from which it springs. Culture is profoundly useful and necessary, as well as engaging and attractive. It must enrich our daily lives, help us understand the changes taking place in us and in the world. This conviction is the basis of the Foundation's activities.

The history of the Fondazione Prada is characterised by visual arts and architecture, cinema, performing arts and music, publications, conferences and meetings, digital and editorial activities.
