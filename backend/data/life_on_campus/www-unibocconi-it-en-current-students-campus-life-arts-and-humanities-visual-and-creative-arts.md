# Visual and creative arts

## Sculpture course

**Presentation of the course and introduction of the participants and their creative skills**

The goal of the course is to stimulate imagination in order to foster creative action. _"REBUS"_, like the previous edition _"ICEBERG"_, offers a journey of exploration that investigates the visible and the imperceptible—those unexplored territories where creative thought is born.

The skies and the oceans, reflecting each other, conceal depths invisible to our eyes and hold mysterious secrets that leave us speechless.

Participants will begin to familiarize themselves with the proposed materials through an individual approach, yet always oriented toward collective action.

In the first meetings, intuitions, emotions, and mysteries will emerge, helping to shape the participants' creative projects.

### Vincenzo Cabiati and Francesco La Fosca

**Vincenzo Cabiati** is a multidisciplinary artist who draws inspiration from various sources, including art, cinema, and architecture. He works with a wide range of mediums, such as painting, sculpture, photography, and ceramics. Cabiati's works often explore themes of sequence and the square, and he has exhibited internationally at prestigious galleries and museums.

**Francesco La Fosca** is an artist who lives and works in Milan. His work is characterized by a focus on light, reflection, and the dichotomy between strength and fragility. La Fosca often uses multiple materials to create his pieces, including drawings, sculptures, installations, photography, and video. He has exhibited widely and his works are included in several public collections.
