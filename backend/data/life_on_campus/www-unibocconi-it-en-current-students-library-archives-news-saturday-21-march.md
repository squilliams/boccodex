# Saturday, 21 March: Maintenance work

20 Mar 2026, by Library & Archives

Approximately from 7:00am to 2:00pm, maintenance work may generate noise

On the morning of **Saturday, 21 March**, approximately from 7:00am to 2:00pm, maintenance work may generate noise, particularly in the study seats overlooking via Sarfatti.

We apologize for the inconvenience and thank you for your understanding. We continue to work to make our spaces increasingly welcoming and inclusive.
