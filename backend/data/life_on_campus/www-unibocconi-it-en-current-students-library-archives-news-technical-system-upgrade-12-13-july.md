# Technical system upgrade (12 – 13 July)

11 Jul 2025, by Library & Archives

We're upgrading to serve you even better!

From **9:00am on Saturday, 12 July** to **9:00am on Sunday, 13 July. 2025**, the Library will take a short technical break: a system update **managed directly by our service provider** is scheduled to improve the performance of our systems.

During this time, the following services will be unavailable:

- Submitting book requests
- Using SearchLib for searches
- Accessing the **Library Course Reserves** tool in Blackboard

We apologize for the temporary inconvenience — we're working to make our services faster, more efficient, and easier to use.

We'll be back online in just 24 hours, ready to offer you an even better experience!

Please note that the Library will be closed on Saturday 12 and Sunday 13 July 2025. [More details on opening hours](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/library-archives/opening-hours)
