# CIVICA Open Science Conference 2026 – 20 May 2026

08 Apr 2026, by Library & Archives

The London School of Economics and Political Science (LSE) will host the CIVICA Open Science Conference 2026, focusing on key developments and practices in Open Science.

The conference will take place on **Wednesday, 20 May 2026** and will be held in a **hybrid format**, allowing both in-person and remote participation.

The event offers an opportunity for researchers and professionals to engage in discussions on the advancement and implementation of Open Science within academic institutions.

The full programme and registration details are available at the following [link](https://www.lse.ac.uk/asset-library/library/2026-05-civica-os-conference-programme.pdf).
