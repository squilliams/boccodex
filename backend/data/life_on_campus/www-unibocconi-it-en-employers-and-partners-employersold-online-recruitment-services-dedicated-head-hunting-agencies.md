# Services dedicated to Head Hunting Agencies

Services offered by Bocconi dedicated to head hunters are the following:

- **Access to CV Book** Bocconi’s CV Book is a summary of the personal data, academic background, work experience and language knowledge of Bocconi recent graduates. CVs are published after each graduation session and are available for one year.
- **Promotion of internship and placement opportunities** Bocconi University carefully checks the quality of opportunities posted on our portal and may opt not to offer job posting services to all those requesting it. In this regard, please consult our [Employer policies](https://www.unibocconi.it/it/employer-e-partner/employer/recruitment-online/employer-policies) which applies to both Head Hunters and its Client Companies.

Head Hunters may promote placement offers both for themselves and for their client companies, but may only promote internship opportunities for themselves. In this case, internships are to be formalized through Bocconi University.

Head Hunting agencies are allowed to promote up to:

- 12 offers a year;
- 2 offers at the same time.

**Please note** that such restrictions do not include senior placement offers posted by Head Hunters.
