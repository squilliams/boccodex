# Service Charter: Now Online!

18 Mar 2025, by Library & Archives

Library & Archives is pleased to announce the publication of the Service Charter.

Library & Archives is pleased to announce the publication of the **Service Charter**: a key tool to inform our users about the opportunities offered, how to access them, and the quality standards of the services provided.

#### What will you find in the Charter?

- Values that guide us
- Commitment to Service Quality
- Services for faculty
- Services for students
- Services for Alumni
- Third mission and social impact for civil society

#### Why is it important?

The Service Charter is a document designed to ensure transparent and accessible management of the services offered, responding to the needs of the community and facilitating access to knowledge.

#### How to read it?

The Service Charter is available online on our [website](https://www.unibocconi.it/it/studenti-iscritti/biblioteca-archivi) in the _About Us_ section under _Service Charter and Policies_.

We invite you to read and explore it. The Library team is available for any further information or clarification.
