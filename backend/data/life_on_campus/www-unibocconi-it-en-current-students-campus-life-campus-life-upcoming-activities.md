# Campus Life upcoming activities

#### **Welcome to Campus life!**

Develop and better understand your skills, discover new interests, reflect on current issues, discuss your university experience with classmates and much more.

A wide variety of activities have been organized for the I semester, designed to help you find unique ways of making the most of your academic life. The list you find below is currently being updated.
