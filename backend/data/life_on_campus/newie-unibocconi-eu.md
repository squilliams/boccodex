GenerazioneEU

Bocconi – as part of the activities for its Chair in European Studies funded by the Boroli Foundation – and La Repubblica – through its Repubblica@scuola platform – are launching a project for Italian high schools.
The project offers dissemination and engagement activities on European issues, in collaboration with the European Commission's Representation in Italy and the European Parliament Office in Italy.

[See more](https://newie.unibocconi.eu/high-school)

![Image of Our Mission](https://newie.unibocconi.eu/sites/default/files/styles/image_compression/public/media/heroimages/home_iStock-691595598.jpg?itok=RWrtyMOu)

Our Mission

NEWIE supports the production and dissemination of applied research on European studies within the framework of the Achille and Giulia Boroli Chair in European Studies at Bocconi University. It aims to develop closer and deeper relationships between academics, policy makers, professionals and the wider public, linking the scientific analysis with the challenges that people, firms and institutions face to make Europe work.

[See more](https://newie.unibocconi.eu/about-us)
