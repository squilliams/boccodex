### Museums always free in Milano

All these museums offer **free entry to their permanent collections**. Temporary exhibitions and events may require an additional ticket: please check the museums’ official websites before your visit for further information.

**Civic Museums**

- [Mudec - Museum of Cultures](https://www.yesmilano.it/en/see-and-do/venues/mudec-museum-cultures "Mudec - Museum of Cultures") (permanent collection)

- [Casa-Museo Boschi di Stefano](https://www.yesmilano.it/en/see-and-do/venues/casa-museo-boschi-di-stefano "Casa Museo Boschi-Di Stefano")

### Free museums on the first Sunday of the month

The first Sunday of every month is **Domenica al Museo (Sunday at the Museum),** a cultural initiative launched by the Ministry of Culture: the city’s state and civic museums are open free of charge.

**Civic Museums**

- [Museo del Novecento](https://www.yesmilano.it/en/see-and-do/venues/museo-del-novecento "Museo del Novecento")
- [Castello Sforzesco museums](https://www.yesmilano.it/en/see-and-do/venues/castello-sforzesco "Castello Sforzesco Museums")
- [Archaeological Museum](https://www.yesmilano.it/en/see-and-do/venues/archaeological-museum "Archaeological Museum")
- [Gallery of Modern Art](https://www.yesmilano.it/en/see-and-do/venues/galleria-arte-moderna "GAM Gallery of Modern Art")
- [Museum of Natural History](https://www.yesmilano.it/en/see-and-do/venues/natural-history-civic-museum "Museum of Natural History")
- [Civic Aquarium](https://www.yesmilano.it/en/see-and-do/venues/milanos-civic-aquarium "Civic Aquarium")
- Booking is mandatory: [Palazzo Marino](https://palazzomarinomilano.madeticket.it/it/shop/biglietti "Bok to visit Palazzo Marino")

**State Museums**

**Please note: booking is mandatory before your visit**

- [Brera Art Gallery](https://www.yesmilano.it/en/see-and-do/venues/pinacoteca-di-brera "Pinacoteca di Brera")
- [Leonardo's Last Supper](https://www.yesmilano.it/en/buy-your-ticket-leonardos-last-supper "Buy your ticket to Leonardo's Last Supper") (Please make your booking well in advance: visit the page for details)

**Other museums**

### Free entry to museums during the week

#### Every first and third Tuesday of the month from 2:00 p.m.

Every **first and third Tuesday of the month, from 2:00 pm onwards**, the **Milano Civic Museums** offer free entry, in addition to those that always offer free admission:

- [Museo del Novecento](https://www.yesmilano.it/en/see-and-do/venues/museo-del-novecento "Museo del Novecento")
- [Castello Sforzesco museums](https://www.yesmilano.it/en/see-and-do/venues/castello-sforzesco "Castello Sforzesco Museums")
- [Archaeological Museum](https://www.yesmilano.it/en/see-and-do/venues/archaeological-museum "Archaeological Museum")
- [Gallery of Modern Art](https://www.yesmilano.it/en/see-and-do/venues/galleria-arte-moderna "GAM Gallery of Modern Art")
- [Museum of Natural History](https://www.yesmilano.it/en/see-and-do/venues/natural-history-civic-museum "Museum of Natural History")
