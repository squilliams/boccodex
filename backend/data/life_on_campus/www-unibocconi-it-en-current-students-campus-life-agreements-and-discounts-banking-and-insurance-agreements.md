# Banking and Insurance Agreements

Allianz Global Assistance Gruppo

15% discount on all travel policies.

[You can calculate the discounted quote and purchase the policy by accessing this link](https://www.allianz-assistance.it/partner/bocconi-viaggi).

Assicurazione Travel Guard

Travel Guard insurance, developed by AIG with the support of broker Aon

15% discount on the different packages offered. In addition to the choice between 'single trip' and 'multiple trips', it is possible to choose three different levels of coverage ('Silver', 'Gold' and 'Platinum'), each with different conditions and costs. To calculate the quote and purchase the customized policy, simply access this link by indicating the discount code: 270.

Gruppo Europ Assistance

Europ Assistance Group offers discounts from 10% on the following Travel Study&Stage, Travel Nonstop Vacation, Outdoor Noproblem and Ski Noproblem policies:

- ViaggiStudy&Stage: The travel policy for a study trip or internship abroad. 24/7 health care anywhere in the world, medical expense reimbursement (including for Covid-19 or terrorist acts) up to 500k€, liability up to 250k, interpreter available in case of hospitalization abroad. May also cover any study course not enjoyed in case of hospitalization/medical return.
- Travel Nostop Vacanza: Travel insurance offering 24/7 health care and medical expense reimbursement (including for Covid-19 or terrorist acts) in Italy, Europe or the World. Traveling in Europe you also have the option to protect your vehicle (car, RV, motorcycle or bike) and have coverage in case of forced extension of stay for Covid-19.
- Outdoor Noproblem: Policy for those who do outdoor activities. In case of injury, assistance and medical expense reimbursement, plus coverage for rescue and search costs, third-party liability.
- Ski Noproblem: Policy designed for Ski/Snowboard/Skating enthusiasts, weekly or annual. Provides liability coverage, medical expense coverage in case of injury while skiing/snowboarding/skating, reimbursement of ski passes-ski lessons-rental of equipment following injury.

IMA Italia Assistance

Get a **30% discount on Imaway travel policies**, a brand of IMA Italia Assistance, the Italian subsidiary of France's leading assistance group Inter Mutuelles Assistance. While you're traveling, you can count on our assistance thanks to a health network of more than 12,700 affiliated medical centers and our 55,000 professionals worldwide.

**Imaway Student Travel**\- Imaway Student Travel Insurance is designed for young people up to age 45 who are traveling abroad for up to 365 days. It is the ideal policy for those who want to insure health care and payment of medical expenses during their study stays.

**Imaway Single Trip**\- insures your travel for up to 60 days. Choose travel insurance that offers you 24-hour health care, some of the highest maximums, direct payment of medical expenses, baggage protection and trip cancellation.

**Imaway Multivacation** \- Imaway Multivacation insures all the trips you take in a year, with a limit of 45 days per single trip. It is the ideal policy if you want to save money without giving up health care and payment of medical expenses during your travels.

The 30% discount will be applied by accessing [www.imaway.it](http://www.imaway.it/) via the following [link](https://www.imaway.it/?promo=AMICI).

Fineco

È attiva una convenzione tra l’Università Bocconi e Fineco, che consente di accedere a condizioni agevolate per l’apertura di un conto Fineco.

In allegato è disponibile un leaflet informativo che riassume le principali caratteristiche dell’offerta e le modalità di adesione.

All’interno del documento è presente un QR code che consente di consultare ulteriori dettagli direttamente online.

È inoltre indicato il Fineco Center di riferimento, da contattare in caso di apertura del conto con l’assistenza di un consulente (necessaria solo per i clienti non residenti in Italia).
