# Internal regulations

Welcome to the official page of the **Internal Regulations** for Tra i Leoni.

Welcome to the official page of the **Internal Regulations** for Tra i Leoni.

First published in may 2024, this document outlines the operational structure and roles within the newspaper’s team, ensuring clarity, efficiency, and collaboration among all members. It serves as a vital guide for maintaining the organization and quality of our publication, fostering a professional environment for aspiring journalists and editors.

The Internal Regulations detail:

- The responsibilities and duties of each member, from editors to contributors.
- The workflow and editorial process for content creation and publication.
- Guidelines for teamwork, communication, and conflict resolution.

The document is designed to support the smooth functioning of the newspaper and to uphold its commitment to excellence as a voice for Bocconi University students.

You can consult the **Internal Regulations** directly on this page or download a PDF version for your reference by clicking the link.
