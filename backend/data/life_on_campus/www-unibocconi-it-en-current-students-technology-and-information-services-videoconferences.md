# Videoconferences

The [Learning Technology Competence Centre](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/services/e-learning-and-technology-research) staff is available to support you in the use of videoconferencing for your work, teaching and research needs.

## **Your Virtual Room**

A personal Zoom room is available to all faculty, staff and students, which allows you to organize and manage online meetings and videoconferencing.

It can be accessed from any device at the address [myzoom.unibocconi.it](https://myzoom.unibocconi.it/) using university credentials.
