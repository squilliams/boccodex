#### In-Person Support (Ask a Librarian!)

If you're in the Library and need assistance, you can rely on the in-person reference service: Ask a Librarian! Visit the Information Desk in the reading rooms during the following hours:

\- Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM.

At the Information Desk, our specialized staff is available to assist you with:

\- Databases – Guidance and support for navigating online databases and using the Data Room 015

\- Bibliographic Information – Helping you find all the print and online resources you need, including textbooks and other Course Reserves

\- Loans – Borrowing and consulting Library resources, as well as requesting documents through Interlibrary Loan (ILL)

![Ask a librarian - online support](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/styles/highlight_slide/public/media/images/pc_0.jpg.webp?itok=_4DK9f6j)

#### Online support

If you're off-campus and need assistance, you can always reach out to the Library staff by emailing infobiblio@unibocconi.it or contacting us via B-in-Touch. You will receive an answer within 3 working days.

Our team can help with:

\- Guidance on using databases and support with bibliographic citation tools

\- Fixing access issues for Library resources

\- Finding print and online materials, including textbooks and other Course Reserves

\- Orientation on Library services

\- Purchase requests or document supply through Interlibrary Loan (ILL) and Document Delivery (DD)

External users are also welcome to contact the Library at infobiblio@unibocconi.it. However, priority will be given to requests from members of the Bocconi Community.

Please note that the Library staff does not:

\- Conduct bibliographic searches or perform database queries on behalf of users

\- Provide complete subject-specific bibliographies or repertories

![female student using a special laptop](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/styles/highlight_slide/public/media/images/_mg_5722.jpg.webp?itok=eiQ8RFC2)

#### Assistance by appointment (Library meetings)

Bocconi students and Faculty members can request one-to-one support through a Library Meeting.

Appointments are available in person at the Information Desk or online via Teams, depending on availability. Each session lasts 30 minutes.

\- Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM

To schedule a Library Meeting, email infobiblio@unibocconi.it, specifying your research needs.
