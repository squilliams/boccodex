# This book belongs to everyone. Please handle it with care.

21 Jul 2025, by Library & Archives

Textbooks in the Library are a shared resource: for you today, and for others tomorrow.

Textbooks in the Library are a shared resource: for you today, and for others tomorrow.

- **Do not highlight**
- **Do not underline**
- **Do not annotate**

Marked or damaged textbooks can become unreadable, especially for those who need clear and accessible reading. Preserving them intact is a matter of respect, responsibility, and inclusion.

📌In case of improper use (e.g., underlining, highlighting, writing...), **you may be required to reimburse the damaged item**.

💡 Library books belong to the whole community: keeping them intact is a simple gesture with great value.

🤝 Help us preserve them as a shared resource.

**Respect the books, respect those who use them.**
