# Tell us how you search for information!

05 Nov 2025, by Library & Archives

Take part in the CIVICA Information Skills Barometer from 3 November to 3 December

Have you ever thought about **how you look for information to study, do research, or prepare for an exam**?

From **3 November to 3 December**, you can share your experience by taking part in the **CIVICA Information Skills Barometer**, a survey involving more than **72,000 students** from the **CIVICA alliance universities**.

Your answers will help us **improve the services, resources, and tools** you use every day to study, search, and learn.
