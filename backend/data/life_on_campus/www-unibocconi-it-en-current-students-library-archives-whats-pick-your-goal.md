# Pick your Goal!

## A better and more sustainable world for all

### The 2020 UN Agenda for Sustainable Development

The 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development, adopted by all United Nations Member States in 2015, provides a shared blueprint for peace and prosperity for people and the planet, now and into the future. The core are the 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), which are an urgent call for action by all countries in a global partnership.

![](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/styles/container_100_lg/public/media/images/pickyourgoal_9.jpg.webp?itok=BxwdIS8F)

### Bocconi and the UN Agenda 2030 for sustainable development

Bocconi’s most direct contribution to the United Nations 2030 Agenda for sustainable development is in research and teaching, and in particular Quality Education (SDG 4), Gender Equality (SDG 5), Decent Work and Economic Growth (SDG 8), Reduced Inequalities (SDG 10), Sustainable Cities and Communities (SDG 11) and Climate Action (SDG 13). Bocconi University also contributes to the development of multiple fields of study that address, in a multidisciplinary manner, many other SDGs.

![](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/styles/container_100_lg/public/media/images/sustainability_1.jpg.webp?itok=L09V_0rc)

## Pick your goal!

### Bocconi Library and the UN Agenda 2030 for sustainable development

The Library promotes the 17 goals of the United Nations 2030 Agenda by ensuring inclusive services and environments in a stimulating climate that meets the needs of learning, teaching, research and interaction. Now the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) are even closer and visible among the collections in the reading rooms. All that it takes is to discover them together. Only the broadest and most crosscutting exchange enables the advancement of scientific research and understanding of the world: are you ready to go?
