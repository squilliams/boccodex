# Tutoring 2024-25 a.y.

## **Tutoring for all curricular languages**

The tutoring consists in **group sessions** of exercises on specific basic language topics.

It is addressed to students enrolled in curricular language preparatory courses (except for English) with no previous knowledge of the language or a low level.

Currently, some of the tutoring sessions are held in presence and some are online via Microsoft Teams.

Tutoring sessions are optional and students are admitted only upon reservation (via the “Appointments” procedure available on the yoU@B Diary).

Further information and the tutoring calendar will be available in the "Content" section of the preparatory course BBoard page.
