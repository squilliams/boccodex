# WiFi activation

Wi-Fi technology is now widespread, the best infrastructure to be able to access Internet services without a cable and logistic spaces (plugs, laboratories PC).

Bocconi offers a large list of applications available through Internet: undifferentiated browsing, email, administrative processes management, eLearning. Wi-Fi technology makes the use of computing resources and innovative educational models easier and increases work areas.

All the buildings are covered by wi-fi (standard 802.11ac).

![](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/styles/container_100_lg/public/media/images/retewireless_3.jpeg.webp?itok=jzWka4OS)

## Activation

The procedures for activating individual devices are personalized according to each user profile:

- **Students**

Access on the [yoU@B](https://agenda.unibocconi.it/ "Access your Diary") student Diary;
- **Faculty &** **Staff**

use the ICT request form available on the Intranet;
- **Participants in Conferences and Seminars**

receive temporary login and password for the duration of the event - no registration necessary.
