# Services for Prospective Students with Disabilities, Learning Disorders and Other Specific Needs

Bocconi University iscommitted to pursuing its main research and training objectives in an environment (both physical and intellectual) in which **everyone receives and offers to others equal opportunities, dignity and treatment.** This is why it is investing increasing resources so that all talented and deserving students can enroll in and attend its programs.

Customized support and services are available to students with temporary or permanent disabilities, while other forms of support and services are available to students with specific learning disorders (SLDs). These aim to ensure all students can participate in academic activities and be fully integrated into university life.

[Dedicated services available for current students](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/services-students-disabilities-mockup "Services for Students with Disabilities mockup") include personalized measures for lectures and exams; accessible accommodation; and digitized materials. They may also receive personalized assistance regarding exchange programs and career guidance.

## Your Application Journey

Admissions

Support measures may be provided during the admissions test to meet the needs of students with disabilities and learning disorders. These include adaption of the work station during the online test, additional time and other aids.

Complete information is available online on the pages dedicated to the [Bocconi admissions test](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/bachelor-and-law-programs/application-and-admissions/disabilities-and-learning-disorders "Disabilities and learning disorders").

Financial Aid

Bocconi University provides **a 100% tuition and fees waiver** for students with disabilities recognized in accordance with Italian Law no. 104/1992 or with a disability equal to or greater than 66%. It is available to students enrolled in the first year or in a year after the first with a certificate of disability or certificate of civil disability, issued by an Italian Medical Committee.

Complete information and specifics are published online for students enrolled in [Bachelor and Law programs](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/bachelor-and-law-programs/funding/tuition-waiver-students-disabilities-equal-or-greater-66-ay-2026-27 "Tuition waiver for students with disabilities equal or greater than 66% - A.Y. 2026-27") or [Master of Science and MA programs](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/master-science-and-ma-programs/funding/tuition-waiver-students-disabilities-equal-or-greater-66-master-science-programs "Tuition waiver for students with disabilities equal or greater than 66% - Master of Science programs a.y. 2026-27").

On-Campus Housing

52 rooms in Bocconi Residence Halls are designed to [accommodate students with disabilities](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/housing/life-bocconi-dorms "On campus dorms and disabilities"). In addition, students with disabilities equal to or greater than 66% are given priority over other students for housing assignments.
