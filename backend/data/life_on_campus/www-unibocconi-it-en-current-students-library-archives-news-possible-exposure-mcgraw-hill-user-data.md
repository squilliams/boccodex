# Possible exposure of McGraw Hill user data

17 Apr 2026, by Library & Archives

Information has been released about a security incident involving the education company McGraw Hill. Some users’ personal data may have been exposed.

**If you have used** McGraw Hill services, you are advised to remain alert for suspicious emails, unexpected messages, or unusual requests for personal information.

We recommend that users:

- avoid opening suspicious links or attachments;
- always verify the authenticity of communications received;
- update their password, especially if it is also used for other services.

If in doubt, please contact the appropriate support services.
