# Fondazione Arnaldo Pomodoro

![](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/media/images/pomodoro.jpg)

**Benefits for students and members of the Bocconi Community:**

The Arnaldo Pomodoro Foundation undertakes to grant to all students and employees of Università Commerciale Luigi Bocconi (upon presentation of an ID card)

\> reduced rates for participation in guided tours of the Entrance to the Labyrinth at Via Solari 35 in Milan (the reference rate is published on the website, in the [dedicated section](https://www.fondazionearnaldopomodoro.it/en/visite-e-laboratori/visite-e-laboratori-per-adulti/))

\> 10% discount on the cost of participation in other activities organised by the Foundation's Educational Section (the offer and the relevant fee schedule are published on the website, in the [dedicated section](https://www.fondazionearnaldopomodoro.it/en/visite-e-laboratori/visite-e-laboratori-per-adulti/))

\> reduced rate - where allowed - in the museum venues where the Foundation organises temporary exhibitions

\> free entry — current exhibitions in Arnaldo Pomodoro's study

For any further information please visit the Foundation's website: [www.fondazionearnaldopomodoro.it](https://www.fondazionearnaldopomodoro.it/)
