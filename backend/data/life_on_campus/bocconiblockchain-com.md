# WHO   _**ARE WE?**_

**BSBA** fosters a community-driven approach to the adoption of Blockchain. To inspire change, we need to motivate the youngest generations to study and understand the technological advancements around us. By consulting Web3 Companies, BSBA allows its members to aquire real-world **experience** and **work,** providing them with a solid breeding ground for their knowledge.

## Our Association

![](https://bocconiblockchain.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/THE-EYE-ICON.svg)

### EDUCATION & GROWTH

The Education&Growth division focuses on fostering growth throughout the whole Italian Blockchain ecosystem. It does this by providing high quality introductory material for newcomers and highly-technical research papers for experts. It also hosts numerous Events, which has fostered incredible growth within our community and the wider Italian Blockchain community at large. By pursuing these goals, we have successfully smoothed out the steep learning-curve for newcomers, whilst also engulfing them in a community that allows them to level up their skills in the blockchain space, with webinars, Q&A sessions and much more. Once members of this division have proven their skills and have acquired a solid grasp of the blockchain space, their next natural step is the market division.

### PROJECTS

The Projects division will focus on commissioned Research for our partners. The goal will be to deliver quality and value to startups and to the ecosystem in general.

The partnerships will be very specific, depending on the partner’s needs, and can and have taken the form of Market Research, Implementations of Use Cases, Competitor Analyses, Strategy, Governance Proposals, etc.

Each project manager will lead their team to ensure that analyses are carried out correctly and delivered in a timely fashion.

### MARKET

The Market Division analyzes trends and key movements within the crypto market. Our primary focus is tracking major tokens’ performance, identifying best and worst movers, and monitoring emerging launch pools to provide a comprehensive market overview. Through monthly market reports, we assess past trends and key indicators to anticipate potential future movements, offering valuable insights into the evolving crypto landscape. Our approach combines data-driven analysis with a broader perspective on market dynamics, ensuring that our research remains relevant and impactful. Each team member plays a crucial role in maintaining the quality and depth of our reports, contributing to a structured and insightful understanding of the market.

### DEV

The Dev Division focuses on building real-world internal projects using blockchain and Web3 technologies. Our goal is to develop innovative solutions while preparing teams to compete in top-tier hackathons worldwide. We follow a hands-on approach grounded in the fundamentals of blockchain, working to turn technical knowledge into practical applications. We value coding skills, especially in JavaScript and TypeScript, but above all, we look for passion, creativity, and teamwork.
