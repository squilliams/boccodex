# Workshops to discover the Library and tackle your final work

27 Jan 2026, by Library & Archives

Make the most out of Library collections and make sure your citations and bibliography are correct

Have you just started writing your final work or are you about to submit it and want to make sure that everything is correct?

Are you new to the Library and want to discover what’s available?

We have workshops for that!

_Monday 2 February: **Cite them right! Citation and bibliography**, in Italian, 10-11.15am, online_

_Tuesday 3 February: **Cite them right! Citation and bibliography**, in English, 2.30-3.45pm, in person_

_Friday 6 February: **Discover the Library**, in English, 2.30-3.30pm, in person_

Register via your [yoU@B Student Diary](https://agenda.unibocconi.it/), “Appointments” section.

This is just the beginning of a new Library Workshops season. The full calendar will be available soon on our [Library Workshops](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/library-archives/teaching-and-research-support/library-workshops "Library Workshops") webpage and keep an eye to the Appointments section!

See you there!
