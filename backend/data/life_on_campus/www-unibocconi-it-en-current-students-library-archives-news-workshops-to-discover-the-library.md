# Workshops to discover the Library and tackle your final work

22 Aug 2025, by Library & Archives

Upcoming workshops on Library tools and citation skills to learn everything you need

Are you new to Bocconi and want to discover how to find all the resources and data you need and get the most out of the Library?

Are you writing your final work and want to be sure you are writing your citations and bibliography correctly?

We have workshops for that!

_27/08: **Cite them right! Citation and bibliography**, in English, 10-11.15 am, online_

_28/08: **Discover the Library!**, in English, 2.30-3.30 pm, in English, online_

_02/09: **Discover the Library!**, in English, 2-3 pm, in person - FULLY BOOKED_

_02/09: **Discover the Library!**, in English, 3-4 pm, in English, in person_

_03/09: **Cite them right! Citation and bibliography**, in Italian, 10.30-11.45am, in person_

Register via your [yoU@B Student Diary](https://agenda.unibocconi.it/), “Appointments” section.

More information on our [Library Workshops](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/library-archives/teaching-and-research-support/library-workshops "Library Workshops") webpage.

See you there!
