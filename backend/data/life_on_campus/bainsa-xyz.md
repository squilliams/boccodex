Welcome to BAINSA

Our Mission

We are an independent research collective in AI and neuroscience, sharing our work through workshops, articles, and demos. Based near Bocconi in Milan, our community is mainly students but open to everyone.

### Why should I care?

AI is becoming a more and more prominent part of human life.

Are you interested in what the future might hold? That’s for you to say.

Methodology

BAINSA’s methodology of immersing individuals in the latest AI research is two-fold, in line with its core values:

### Strong research orientation

We connect talented individuals with researchers and AI companies to work on research projects, allowing members to gain practical experience, potentially secure internships with partner companies, and have opportunities

to publish research papers.

### A community of like-minded people

At BAINSA, team building is key to achieve great results and creating a strong community. Being a member means more than working on projects: it includes weekly meetings to discuss AI papers, trips and participation in international AI festivals, and building a professional network.

Divisions

BAINSA is structured into divisions dedicated to research, outreach, and community activities, each contributing to our mission of advancing and sharing knowledge.

[**Projects Division**\\
\\
We pursue rigorous, technical projects in AI, neuroscience, and robotics. Members can engage in vertical research depth and technological experimentation.](https://www.bainsa.xyz/projects/project)

[**Analysis Division**\\
\\
We engage with AI and neuroscience beyond disciplinary boundaries. The division conducts research reviews on AI applications and implications](https://www.bainsa.xyz/projects/analysis)

[**Culture Division**\\
\\
We bring ideas into real-life contexts, cultivating networking opportunities, events and meaningful connections between science, business and society](https://www.bainsa.xyz/projects/events)
