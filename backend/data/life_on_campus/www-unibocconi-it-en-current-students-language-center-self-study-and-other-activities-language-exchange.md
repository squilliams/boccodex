# Language Exchange

The Language Center organizes each semester a Language Exchange for students; it is a good chance to improve a language through talking.

## **What is it?**

The Language Exchange is a form of **autonomous language learning**. It is based on a mutual exchange between learners, speaking different languages in order to learn one from the other.

## **Who can participate in the Language Exchange?**

The initiative is open to students enrolled in Bachelor, Master of Science, Integrated Master of Arts in Law programs and Specialized Master Programs, Exchange incoming students who already have **at least an elementary level (A2)** in the language which they would like to improve. They must be at least B2 level or mother tongue for the other exchange language.

## **When and how is possible to take part in the Language Exchange?**

The Language Exchange is offered at the beginning of every semester. It will be possible to fill in the registration form on the yoU@B Student Diary, in the Language Center widget.

For 2nd semester 2025-26, registrations will be open **from January 26th to February 16th 2026**.

## **How will I be informed of the name and email address of my language partner?**

The information regarding your language partner, will be sent by **Bocconi email address**.

## **Is there a minimum duration for the Language Exchange?**

There is not but, for the effectiveness of the initiative, we recommend to schedule weekly meetings with a duration not inferior than three months.

## **When can I meet my partner?**

You and your partner can decide autonomously when to meet.

## **Who should we contact for help/support during the Language Exchange?**

You can request support from the Language Lab through the B in Touch procedure available on the yoU@B Student Diary (topic: LANGUAGES > Various).

## **Is there a test at the end of the Language Exchange?**

No, there is no test. At the end of the program each partner should fill in a feedback form.
