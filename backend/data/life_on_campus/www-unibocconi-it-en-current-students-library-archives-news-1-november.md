# Library opening hours 1 November

30 Oct 2025, by Library & Archives

**On Saturday, 1 November**, the Library will be **open from 8:30am to 4pm** (closing procedures start 30 minutes before). On Sunday, 2 November, the Library will be open during its usual hours (from 8:30am to midnight). Welcome November!
