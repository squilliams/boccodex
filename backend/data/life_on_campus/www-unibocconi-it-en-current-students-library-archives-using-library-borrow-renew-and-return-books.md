## Borrowing books

If you’re a member of the Bocconi Community (students, faculty, Bocconi Alumni Community, staff), you can borrow most items from the Library collections.

You just need:

- your Bocconi ID card or the QRcode created through the [yoU@B student Diary](https://agenda.unibocconi.it/)
- the books you’ve picked up from the shelves

If you need help, [ask a Librarian](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/library-archives/teaching-and-research-support/ask-librarian) at the Information Desk.

The external community can contact the Library team by writing to [infobiblio@unibocconi.it](mailto:infobiblio@unibocconi.it) for information about books.

### For how long can I keep a book on loan?

You can borrow books for **4 weeks**, except for textbooks, which can be borrowed for **2 weeks** (more information in the [dedicated libguide](https://unibocconi.libguides.com/coursereserves)).

## Renewing books

We automatically renew loaned books for up to 6 months, except for these specific cases:

- the book is a textbook: in this case, there is one automatic renewal of 1 week
- item has been requested by someone else and must be returned
- the due date would be after the expiration date of your Bocconi account

If we need you to return a book, we will e-mail you with a revised due date.

## Returning books

Return books using the self-service machines on the ground floor of the Library and put them in the Book return boxes.

If you don't want to enter the Library, place your books into the Book return box at the Library entrance.

We also accept postal returns to:

**Università Bocconi. Biblioteca e Archivi. Via Ulisse Gobbi, 5 - 20136 Milano (Italia)**

Please retain proof of posting until items are discharged from your Library account.

## Request books out on loan

To ensure the fair circulation of material we have a process where Bocconi Library users can request a book that is on loan to another reader.

When a book you have searched for is on loan it will show on [SearchLib](https://library.unibocconi.it/) as: On loan until ...

To place a request on the book:

1. Sign in
2. Search for a book
3. Click on the book to display the record of the item
4. In the 'Get it' section of the record click the request option
5. Fill in any relevant information in the short form
6. Submit your request by clicking the green request option

We will email you when the item is ready for you to collect.

### Access material not available: Can’t find it? service

Bocconi students and faculty can use the [Can't find it?](https://lib-unibocconi.primo.exlibrisgroup.com/discovery/blankIll?vid=39UCLB_INST%3AVU1) service to get access to material not available at Bocconi Library. Through Interlibrary Loan, it is possible to expand the search to other libraries’ collections and request the resources needed.
