# System maintenance update

17 Dec 2025, by Library & Archives

From Thursday, 18 December 10:00pm to Friday, 19 December 1:00am

From **10:00pm on Thursday, 18 December to 1:00am on Friday, 19 December**, a system maintenance operation is scheduled. During this time, some platforms and services will be temporarily unavailable, including yoU@B Diary, databases, and Badge and QR code management services.

We apologize for the temporary inconvenience.
