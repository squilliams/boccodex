# Tra i Leoni n. 108, April 2025

![Tra i leoni 108](https://i0.wp.com/traileoni.it/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/Tra-i-leoni-108.jpg?fit=318%2C451&ssl=1)

As we move closer to adulthood, the comforting idea that the world will one day simply be handed down to us begins to fade. Instead, we start to feel the weight of responsibility pressing in. Climate change, global conflict, inequality — these are no longer distant crises. They are shaping the very landscape we grow up in. And yet, within the chaos, there is also clarity: a generation that is no longer waiting its turn but stepping up, learning, adapting, and refusing to see the world only in black and white.

This issue reflects that journey. From European defence to Gen Z’s love for ’90s sitcoms, from global experiences to personal revelations, our writers offer a mosaic of perspectives that paint a picture of what it means to come of age now.
