# Library and Archives: online the Report 2024

12 May 2025, by Library & Archives

Library and Archives is pleased to announce the publication of the [**Annual Report**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/library-archives/about-us/annual-reports "Annual Reports"), a document that aims to gather data and information, to share the projects developed and the results achieved, and to narrate the road traveled so that it will be the basis for designing the future of our institution. If you would like to share suggestions, comments, ideas you can fill out the [Feedback form](https://forms.office.com/pages/responsepage.aspx?id=erXza7Sfwkea2lEVZRj1L6bDyXFMNdxOi2DioP2q8_NUMk80NVNVMDNKNFJQV0JaOVk4UFVSQUxCUi4u). Looking forward to hearing from you!
