### Bringing Macro to the Micro: Exploring Italy’s Real Economy

A student-driven initiative that turns macroeconomic insights into strategic action focused on Italian SMEs.

## Who We Are

Students for PMI was founded to fill a gap in the current student association landscape by offering a hands-on space for students passionate about real-world economics, macro analysis, and investment decisions tied to Italy’s productive fabric.

## What We Do

● We build virtual portfolios based on macroeconomic trends and SME analysis.● We organize strategic workshops focused on real companies.● We study case studies of under-the-radar Italian businesses.● We activate connections with entrepreneurs and investment funds specialized in SMEs.● We offer a practical and highly applicable learning environment.

## Value for Students

We provide a space where theory meets practice: our members develop professional skills, work on real-world case studies, and build direct relationships with the Italian SME ecosystem.
