# Virtual Exhibitions

![](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/styles/container_100_md/public/media/images/lea-sraffa-eng.png.webp?itok=lZMDO3pv)

### The many sides of Angelo Sraffa: jurist, cultural organizer and bibliophile

A new virtual exhibition dedicated to Angelo Sraffa, distinguished jurist, professor, key figure in the life of the University and refined bibliophile.

[The many sides of Angelo Sraffa: jurist, cultural organizer and bibliophile](https://virtualexhibitions.unibocconi.it/sraffa/en/1/home)

![](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/styles/container_100_md/public/media/images/lea_uffmodeng.jpg.webp?itok=dOWxMuqN)

### L'Ufficio moderno, 1926-1985

A journey through the history of an avant-garde magazine, among advertising, rationalization and business culture.

[L'Ufficio moderno, 1926-1985](https://virtualexhibitions.unibocconi.it/ufficio-moderno/en/1/l-ufficio-moderno-1926-1985?pageId=1)

![](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/styles/container_100_md/public/media/images/la_walteralbini.jpg.webp?itok=b70trWyI)

### Walter Albini and Sources on fashion at Bocconi

Discover sources preserved at Bocconi on fashion and the link with the city of Milan, together with archival and bibliographic materials that retrace the life path of the important designer Walter Albini who marked a turning point in Italian fashion history.

[Walter Albini and Sources on fashion at Bocconi](https://virtualexhibitions.unibocconi.it/albini/en/1/walter-albini-and-sources-on-fashion-at-bocconi)

### The Bocconi campus in Milan

A journey through time and space among the buildings and historical milestones of our University.
