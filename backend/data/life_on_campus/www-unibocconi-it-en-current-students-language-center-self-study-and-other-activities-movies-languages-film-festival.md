# Movies & Languages Film Festival

The Bocconi Language Center organizes a **film festival** devoted to movies **in original- language versions**. The festival is a good chance to broaden your language horizons, get to know other cultures, and enjoy yourself productively.

Viewers can read brief synopsis cards. **You can download synopses** from the Language Center website.

**Program**

Spring semester 2024-2025 a.y. Movies & Languages Film Festival program will be available in January 2025.

Event structure:

- Teacher's introduction (15 min.)
- Film
- Q&A

Entrance is **reserved to the Bocconi community** upon registration, according to the methods indicated for the single screening.
