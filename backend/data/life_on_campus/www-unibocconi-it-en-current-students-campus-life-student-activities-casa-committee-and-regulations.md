# The CASA Committee and the Regulations

Bocconi values students' active participation in making the life on campus richer and full of interesting opportunities. For this reason, the University promotes student initiatives and supports the organization of their activities and events with rooms, funds and means of communication.

All student activities are approved by the CASA Committee (or its delegates) that operates by principles collected and periodically adjourned.

CASA Committee composition

The Committee for the Coordination of Student and Group Activities ( **CASA**) is in charge for the evaluation of the student associations' activities.

The **CASA Committee** is representative of the whole Bocconi community, as its **8 members** are chosen from each component of the community. At the moment these are the members of the Committee:

- **faculty members**: prof. Paola Profeta (President).
- **staff members**: Paolo Cancelli, Anna Airoldi, Silvia Tracchi, Erika Zancan.
- **students**: Kevin Gjoni, Carlo Maria Pagliula, Sabino Andrea Rubino

The Committee meets **9 times per year**.

CASA Committee meeting schedule

The CASA Committee is in charge of evaluating the approval  of events and the requests of funding for activities organized by students and associations.
