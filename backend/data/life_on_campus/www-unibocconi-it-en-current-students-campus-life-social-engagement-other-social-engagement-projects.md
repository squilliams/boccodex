# Other Social Engagement Projects

**Bocconi University recognizes social engagement and responsibility as values that are integral to its institutional identity**.

At Bocconi there are several opportunities made available for students to do some volunteer activities, in Milan and beyond. In addition to the opportunities reported through the network managed by CSV and conveyed through the Volunteer Desk, there are several opportunities both inside and outside the university to donate your time.

#### **Activities organized by the University**

- [**Making Sense of Profits**](https://www.unibocconi.it/wps/wcm/connect/bocconi/sitopubblico_en/navigation+tree/home/programs/current+students/campus+life/social+engagement/dai+un+senso+al+tuo+profitto) **project**
- [**Learning Needs Peer to Peer tutoring project**](https://www.unibocconi.it/wps/wcm/connect/bocconi/sitopubblico_en/navigation+tree/home/programs/current+students/campus+life/counseling%2C+self-empowerment+and+wellbeing/self-empowerment/learning+needs+peer+to+peer+tutoring+project+tutor+eng) **for SpLDs students**
- **Volunteering opportunities in collaboration with Bocconi Inclusion Service** (for more information [inclusion@unibocconi.it](mailto:inclusion@unibocconi.it))

#### **Students for Humanity**

This student association has been active in the university for many years and promotes various social engagement activities open to students. [Find out more on their website!](https://studentsforhumanity.it/)

#### **Other Activities and External Opportunities**

- **AVIS**: [Blood donation on campus](https://www.unibocconi.it/wps/wcm/connect/bocconi/sitopubblico_en/navigation+tree/home/programs/current+students/campus+life/social+engagement/blood+drive+with+avis+milano-eng)
- **Pane Quotidiano**: Pane Quotidiano is a nonreligious and nonpolitical non-profit organization located only 200 meters from the Bocconi campus. Every day, it supports the poorest segments of the population free of charge, providing them with food and clothing. For additional information please click [here](https://www.unibocconi.it/wps/wcm/connect/bocconi/sitopubblico_en/navigation+tree/home/programs/current+students/campus+life/social+engagement/pane+quotidiano+initiative).
- **Sottovoce — IEO and Monzino Milano:** Sottovoce is a volunteer association that aims at involving volunteers in various departments. For additional information please check the [Italian website](https://www.unibocconi.it/wps/wcm/connect/bocconi/sitopubblico_it/albero+di+navigazione/home/corsi+di+studio/studenti+iscritti/campus+life/impegno+sociale/impegno+sociale_altre+iniziative).
