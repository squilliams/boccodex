# Fondazione Triennale Milano e CRT Teatro dell Arte

![](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/media/images/triennale.jpg)

**Discounts for students and members of the Bocconi Community**

For Triennale Milano's 2023 and 2024 performance and exhibition programme, Bocconi University students are entitled to the biggest reduction (approximately 50%) on all performances on the programme - Theatre Season and FOG Triennale Milano Performing Arts Festival and on all exhibitions (€7.50 instead of €15, €6 instead of €12) except for exhibitions with tickets costing €5 or less. The indicated rates do not apply during the Triennale Milano International Exhibition.

For Università Bocconi only, the 50% reduced price on all show tickets also extends to members of the Bocconi Community.

For students only, there is also a 25% discount on all types of membership (Starter, Explorer, Insider and Supporter), which include tickets to exhibitions and shows for 365 days, as well as discounts on food and beverage services and benefits.

More information can be found on the [official website](https://triennale.org/membership).

**How to purchase tickets:**

For tickets to exhibitions and shows and for membership: at the Triennale Milano ticket office by showing a document proving membership of Università Bocconi;

For tickets to exhibitions and shows: online at triennale.org, selecting the student rate.

In addition to the special rates for students and members of the community, Triennale Milano is also willing to organise meetings, open rehearsals and other in-depth discussions with guest artists, curators or other figures connected to the institution.

Excluded from the agreement are all JAZZMI concerts, events in collaboration with other partners and children's shows, as well as Discover initiatives and guided tours by appointment.
