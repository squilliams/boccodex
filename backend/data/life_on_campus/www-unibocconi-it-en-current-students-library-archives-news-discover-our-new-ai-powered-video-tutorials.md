# Discover our new AI-powered video tutorials

10 Feb 2026, by Library & Archives

Library & Archives is pleased to launch a new series of video tutorials created with the support of artificial intelligence, designed to make using library services easier and more accessible.

The first two videos, available in English, guide you step by step through:

- [how to borrow a book using the self-check service](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/library-archives/using-library/borrow-renew-and-return-books "Borrow, Renew and Return Books")
- [how to book a study seat with the yoU@B Pro app](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/library-archives/using-library/access-bocconi-library-archives "Access to Bocconi Library & Archives")

Whether you are visiting the Library for the first time or simply want to save time, these short tutorials help you get oriented quickly and make the most of the available services.

This is just the beginning: new videos about Library & Archives spaces, services and resources will be coming soon.

Do you have suggestions or ideas for new videos? You can contact the Library staff or write to us to propose new content and contribute to the development of the project.

Watch the videos and discover how easy it is to use Library services!
