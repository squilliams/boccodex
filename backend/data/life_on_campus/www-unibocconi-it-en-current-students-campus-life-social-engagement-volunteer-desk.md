# Volunteer Desk

Thanks to collaborations with [CSV Milano](https://www.ciessevi.org/) (the Volunteer Services center) the Desk **provides general information** on volunteering and **orients** students interested in dedicating themselves to social engagement.

There are hundreds of associations working in the Milan area, as well as summer and international opportunities.

If you’re interested in learning more about the opportunities offered by the various associations, please [**fill out this form**](https://forms.unibocconi.it/index.php?rif_quest=2451) that will help us understand which activities may fit your interests and availability.

Many activities promoted through the Volunteer Desk potentially qualify for the **Bocconi Social Impact Recognition project.** Find out more [on this page.](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/campus-life/social-engagement/bocconi-social-impact-recognition "Bocconi Social Impact Recognition")

#### **Types of activities**

The activities promoted by the volunteer desk are of different types and also involve varying commitment depending on the volunteer association of reference.

Some of the activities involve a commitment that is potentially compatible with the Bocconi Social Impact Recognition project. Others, on the other hand, require less time availability and therefore do not fall under the requirements of this project.

Thanks to the collaboration with CSV Milano and the associations in its network, you can find a wide range of occasional volunteering opportunities available both in Milan, throughout Italy and abroad. Discover all the occasional volunteering opportunities available for the upcoming weeks in this [webpage](https://www.unibocconi.it/it/studenti-iscritti/campus-life/impegno-sociale/opportunita-di-volontariato-occasionale-aperte "Opportunità di volontariato occasionale aperte").
