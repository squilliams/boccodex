# Towards Milan–Cortina 2026

02 Feb 2026, by Library & Archives

A bibliographic guide to the Olympic Games

In preparation for the Milan–Cortina 2026 Winter Olympic Games, the Library has prepared a **bibliographic guide** dedicated to the event, with the aim of supporting multidisciplinary academic research on the topic.

The guide provides a curated selection of books, reports, journals, and websites that examine the Olympic Games primarily from an **organizational** and **management perspective**, while also considering their **historical evolution** and the **legacy** that this mega-event leaves on host territories.

The guide is freely available online and includes both resources subscribed by the Library and open access material open to everyone.

[**Access the guide**](https://unibocconi.libguides.com/olympicgames)
