# Library Workshops Are Back!

17 Feb 2026, by Library & Archives

Find out where to access data and materials, manage your sources, and write citations for your thesis

Our workshops are back, designed to help you make the most of the Library resources for your exams, group projects, and thesis.

We offer workshops on:

- Using **financial databases** to find the data you need (Bloomberg, LSEG Workspace, FactSet, S&P Capital IQ Pro, Aida)
- Online tools for research and **finding materials**
- **Managing your thesis sources** effectively
- Writing **bibliographies** and **citations**
- **Law resources**

Check the full workshop schedule and register through the [_yoU@B Agenda_](https://agenda.unibocconi.it/), in the “ **Appointments**” section. Registration opens four weeks before each event, and you will receive a confirmation email with all the course details the day before the workshop.

If you have any questions, please visit B in Touch – Library section, or feel free to contact us at [infobiblio@unibocconi.it](mailto:infobiblio@unibocconi.it) — we’ll be happy to help!
