# Volleyball

## Are you a volleyball player and do you want to continue playing even during your university years?

Bocconi Sport gives you the possibility to join our teams:

- Male team – FIPAV Serie C
- Female team – FIPAV Serie D

On May 4th, 2023, **Bocconi Sport** received a financial contribution from the **Italian Volleyball Federation (FIPAV).**

Contribution Payment Statement [HERE](https://www.bocconisport.eu/sites/default/files/2023-06/Erogazione%20contributo%20finanziario%20bando%20Fipav.pdf)
