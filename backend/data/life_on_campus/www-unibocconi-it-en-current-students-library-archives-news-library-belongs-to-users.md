# The Library belongs to its users: let's treat it with care!

16 Sep 2025, by Library & Archives

The Library welcomes students for the new academic year, reminding everyone to follow simple rules that ensure a respectful, focused, and shared study environment.

As the new academic year begins, the Library once again welcomes students, offering study and research spaces designed to foster concentration, exchange, and personal growth.

To ensure a calm and efficient environment for everyone, it is important to keep in mind a few simple rules of coexistence:

- **Always book your study seat**, to guarantee fair access to spaces and services for all.
- **Keep your voice low**, to support both concentration and mutual respect.
- **Take care of the spaces and facilities**, as a sign of responsibility towards the whole community.

Please also note that any damage to University premises or facilities constitutes serious misconduct and may result in disciplinary action.

Respecting these simple essential rules helps improve everyone’s experience and makes the Library a welcoming place where we can study and grow together.
