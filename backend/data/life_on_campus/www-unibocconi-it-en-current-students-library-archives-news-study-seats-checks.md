# Use of Study Seats in the Library: Checks and Compliance with the Rules

26 Feb 2025, by Library & Archives

Starting on Monday 2nd March, Library staff will carry out checks in the Library to ensure the proper and fair use of study seats

Starting on Monday 2nd March, the Library staff will carry out checks in the Library to ensure the proper and fair use of study seats.

The aim is to allow everyone to access study spaces in an orderly way and in accordance with the shared rules.

Please remember to:

- activate the pause function when you temporarily step away
- release your seat when you finish using it
