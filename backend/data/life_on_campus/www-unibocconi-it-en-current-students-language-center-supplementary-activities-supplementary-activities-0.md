# Supplementary Activities

Before enrolling in a course, students are strongly advised to carefully read the Supplementary activities [**rules**](https://www.unibocconi.it/wps/wcm/connect/bocconi/sitopubblico_en/navigation+tree/home/programs/current+students/services/language+center/supplementary+activities/attivita+integrative+guidelines+new+ing).

Registration must be completed through the **yoU@B Student Diary**, by selecting the **‘Sign-up for various activities’** box.

Course places are limited. All students who correctly register will receive an acceptance or non-acceptance notification at the end of the registration period.
