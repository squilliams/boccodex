# Language Lab

**From 26 August 2024**, the Language Lab only hosts specific initiatives, including tutoring sessions reserved for students enrolled in the curricular language preparatory courses (except for English) and international certification exams.

Tools and resources for language self-study are available for Bocconi students in the yoU@B Diary ("Language Center" menu) and on the BBoard pages, including the ones for students behind normal academic progress.
