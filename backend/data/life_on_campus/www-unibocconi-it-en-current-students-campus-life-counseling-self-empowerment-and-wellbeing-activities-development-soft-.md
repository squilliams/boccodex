# Activities for the development of soft skills

If you would like to embark on a course to acquire or develop your soft skills, either to improve your **university course** or to **broaden your personal and interpersonal background**, we suggest you take part in the activities proposed on this page.

_**THE SKILLS WEEK**_

The [**Skills Week**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/campus-life/counseling-self-empowerment-and-wellbeing/skills-week) is a series of workshops and events (March 30 – April 3, 2026) focused on developing soft skills, offering students practical opportunities to recognize, strengthen, and acquire relational and behavioral competencies useful in their studies, daily life, and future careers.

_**ENHANCING EXPERIENCE ACTIVITIES**_

A number of skills courses are also offered to students enrolled in master's degree programs as ["enhancing experience activities"](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/campus-life/enhancing-experience-activities-offered-campus-life "Enhancing experience activities offered by Campus Life") . You can find more information in the dedicated section of the website.
