# Inclusion, Disabilities and Learning Disorders

The **Inclusion Service** is a point of reference that aims to guarantee the same opportunities to everyone: it collaborates with other offices on campus to create a connected and unified community where everyone – students, faculty, staff and alumni – can feel welcome and included.

## Multiculturalism

### Multifaith Space

A new Multifaith Space has opened, designed to ensure that all Bocconi community members have an appropriate place to pray, sit in silence or reflect.

The entrance is located at Piazza Sraffa 6 (door on the left, first floor.) The space is open from Monday through Saturday 7:30am–10pm, and on Sunday 9am–10pm.

### Student Associations

As of today, there are more than 100 student associations on campus, including ones representing various international communities.

[Associations](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/campus-life/student-activities/student-associations "Student Associations") offer an opportunity to engage with people from different backgrounds who share common interests, thus becoming a point of reference and mutual support.

### Ethical-Religious Meals

The offering of halal, kosher and vegan meals in dining areas underscores Bocconi's commitment to recognizing and supporting the dietary needs of the entire community.

Options may be related to religious, ethical and/or cultural beliefs. [Find out more about our dining areas](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/funding/dining-areas-0 "Dining areas").
