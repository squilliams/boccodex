# 4-6 April Library closed for holidays

30 Mar 2026, by Library & Archives

On Friday, 3 April, the Library will close at 8pm (closing operations starting 30 minutes earlier). From Saturday, 4 April to Monday 6 April inclusive, the Library will be closed for holidays. We look forward to seeing you on Tuesday 7 April at 8:30am!
