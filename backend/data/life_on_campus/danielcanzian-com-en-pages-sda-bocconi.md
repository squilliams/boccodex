Catering Campus SDA Bocconi School of Management

![Catering Campus SDA Bocconi School of Management](https://danielcanzian.com/cdn/shop/files/Campus_SDA_Bocconi_Daniel_Canzian_10x.jpg?v=1669822149)

From September 2020 I do collaborate with Compass Group Italia to take care of the gastronomic proposal of the new Campus SDA Bocconi, one of the higher Education centre and between the most important Universities of Europe. AD Arte Food, the company which offer catering service inside the new building is dislocated in three different halls. The architectural complex has been disigned by the Japanes Studio Sanaa; Compass Group Italia has decide to explore this new chance with the aim to offer students, professors and visitors, of SDA Bocconi Campus, an unique service. AD Arte Food’s know-how and my philosophy of Contemporary Italian Cuisine merge into a custom-made gastronomic experience in bidimensional spaces meant for axalting every single individual, with a cosy and functional atmosphere to the society, where food becomes a medium to promote relations of quality.

The project of SDA Bocconi Venues: La Centrale, Caffè Letterario e Materia

Since the very beginning of the project Compass Group Italia and I, have exalted operational functionality from the operator of food flow to costumer. That’s why today we have three different places, completely different which join the other locals of the Campus, managed by AD Arte Food, each of them bound to a different target of clients. From students, to professors and executives till external costumers.

La Centrale

![La Centrale](https://danielcanzian.com/cdn/shop/files/04_-_La_Centrale_10x.jpg?v=1669822961)

The venue owes its name to the famous “ Centrale del latte di Milano”, a special memory and place for all Milanesi. Till 2006’ was located in Via Castelbarco. So La Centrale become a tribute to a beloved placeof great value for Milan, always been epitome of high-qaulity. Inside a big counter of alluminum that reminds us the materials used by Sanaa for the buildings. Surrounded by forniture of design and wide windows, that call back openness-mind, which is the aim of Bocconi Campus. Here there’s a caffetteria service full day with the chance to have a quick and hearty lunch.

Caffè Letterario

![Caffè Letterario](https://danielcanzian.com/cdn/shop/files/03_-_Caffe_letterario_2_10x.jpg?v=1669823064)

In the heart of the Campus, food & drink hall, designed for SDA Bocconi students. An environment where food integrate in a friendly and sharing experience, essential on social level. Under the slogan of: “with one hand I read, with the other one I eat”. Caffè Letterario shows itself as a cosy modern lounge.

Materia

![Materia](https://danielcanzian.com/cdn/shop/files/02_-_Materia_1_10x.jpg?v=1669901954)

Is the restaurant dedicated to Bocconi University and SDA School of Management. The ideal place where to find the simplest receipes of Italian tradition: few ingredients of best quality, masterfully cooked. Seasonal ingredients balanced in plates so that the palate is able to call back memorial flavours.
