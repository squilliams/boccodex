# Student Media Center

Università Bocconi offers its students various tools to facilitate the debate and discussion of university issues, as well as to discuss ideas with other students and analyze current events.

**All the media are organizations created and managed by students.**

This is your opportunity to collaborate with and cultivate your interests!
