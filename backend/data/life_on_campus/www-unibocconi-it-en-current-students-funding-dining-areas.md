# Dining areas

Please note that for the Summer break:

**Pellegrini canteen Bocconi 12** will be closed from the evening of July 18 to August 23. Regular service will resume from August 25, 2025.

**Compass bar Sarfatti 25** will be closed from August 7 to 19 August. In the period from July 18 to August 6 and from August 20 to 29 August a reduced service will be provided.

Regular service will resume from September 1, 2025.
