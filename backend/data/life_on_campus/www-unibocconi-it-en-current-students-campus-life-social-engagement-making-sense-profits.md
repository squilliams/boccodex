# Making Sense of Profits

#### ON THE FIELD PROJECTS AND INTERNSHIPS AT SOCIAL ENTERPRISES AND IN THE THIRD SECTOR

Are you interested in working on a project you believe in while at the same time completing a meaningful personal and professional experience? Would you like to see your work make a tangible impact on the community?

Then **Making Sense of Profits is for you!**

This project is coordinated by the CERGAS Research Center. It offers students the opportunity to get first-hand professional experience, combining working in the field of economics with making a social impact.

**Dynamo Camp, Fondazione Cave Canem, ADMO, Fondazione Medici Senza Frontiere** are just some of the organizations involved in the upcoming edition.

The best projects will be recognized and, if they meet the requirements, may also be converted into an internship experience or be involved in the area of the [Social Impact Recognition](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/campus-life/social-engagement/bocconi-social-impact-recognition "Bocconi Social Impact Recognition").

**Application period:** extended deadline **by 11 May 2026**

More details on the 15th edition are available on the CERGAS Research Centre website [**at this link**](https://cergas.unibocconi.eu/research/social-policy-and-social-innovation/dai-un-senso-al-profitto) .

For more information: [cergasociale@unibocconi.it](mailto:cergassociale@unibocconi.it) — [campuslife@unibocconi.it](mailto:campuslife@unibocconi.it)
