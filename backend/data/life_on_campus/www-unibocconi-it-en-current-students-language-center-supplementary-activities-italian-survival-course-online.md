# Italian Survival Course Online

The Language Center offers a short intensive Italian language course in distance to help foreign students interact during their first days in Italy and facilitate their integration at Bocconi.

This course is addressed to 1st year students (BSc Programs and MSc Programs), University Master students and incoming Double Degree students.

It is entirely online asynchronous and is made up of five units, organized with explanatory videos and a series of related linguistic activities. It offers basic language skills and an introduction to Italian culture.

As the course is available online in a self-learning mode, **no attendance certificate is provided**.

## **Calendar**

The course will be available **from September, 4th to September 30th 2025**.

## **Enrollment**

Registrations will be opened **from August, 25th to August, 31st 2025,** via yoU@B Student Diary (“Sign up for various activities” box). **Unlimited places available.**
