# Agreements and Discounts

Università Bocconi is committed to offering **students, faculty and staff** the opportunity to participate first hand in the vibrant Milanese **cultural life** at **convenient prices** or to have discounts in **shops** or lab and institues for personal **health and wellness,** by providing your **Bocconi ID card.**
