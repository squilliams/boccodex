# Arts and Humanities

Bocconi believes it is fundamental to offer students the chance to complement their studies with activities related to culture and personal development.

Throughout the year, students can take part in activities such as interdisciplinary courses, events, conferences and meetings on cultural issues, concerts, musicals and shows put on by students, art exhibits and much more.

To coordinate and develop all this, the University has assigned the **Arts & Humanities Committee** the role of encouraging cultural and artistic development on campus.

[**Artistic workshops and cultural courses**\\
\\
Bocconi University organizes creative **workshops**, **cultural activities and extra-curricular courses** to give participants a chance to express their creativity and artistic abilities and to enrich their personal backgrounds.](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/campus-life/arts-and-humanities/cultural-courses-and-artistic-workshops-visual-and-performing-arts "Discover more")

[**Cultural agreements and guided tours**\\
\\
Università Bocconi is committed to offering students, faculty and staff the opportunity to participate first hand in the vibrant Milanese cultural life at convenient prices: **theater, music, cinema, museums and guided tours.**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/campus-life/agreements-and-discounts/agreements-cultural-institutions-and-guided-tours "Find out more")
