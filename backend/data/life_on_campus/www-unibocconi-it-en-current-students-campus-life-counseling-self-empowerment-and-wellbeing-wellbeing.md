# Wellbeing

Campus Life offers many opportunities to **improve your awareness of the relationship between mind and body** and to adopt a **healthy and balanced lifestyle**, thus achieving a psychophysical balance and all-around personal health.

#### **YOGA and MEDITATION**

During the 2nd semester 2025-26, Campus Life offers a series of **classes of yoga and meditation** to help you **improve your awareness of the relationship between mind and body.** Discover the complete program [**here**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/campus-life/counseling-self-empowerment-and-wellbeing/yoga-and-meditation "Yoga").

In addition, a **Mindfulness Area** is available on campus. This area is meant to be a space for reflection, and individual mindfulness and meditation practices. [**Find out more!**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/campus-life/counseling-self-empowerment-and-wellbeing/mindfulness-area "Mindfulness Area")

#### **HEALTHY HABITS WEEKS**

Growing as a student doesn’t just mean facing exams, classes, and projects: it also means taking care of yourself, both body and mind. Every year, Bocconi University promotes psychophysical wellbeing as an integral part of university life through initiatives that foster **awareness, prevention, and healthy practices.**

The **Healthy Habits Weeks** project was created to provide students with tools and concrete opportunities to embrace a balanced and sustainable lifestyle. Nutrition, sleep management, addictions, and relationship care are just some of the areas where small everyday choices can make a real difference in achieving long-lasting wellbeing.

For AY 2025-26, the Healthy Habits project will take place **from April 27 to May 15**.

Don’t miss the opportunity to book **one-on-one on-campus appointments** with experienced healthcare professionals, free of charge!
