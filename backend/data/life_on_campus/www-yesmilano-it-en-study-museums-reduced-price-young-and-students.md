# Discounted Museums for youth and students

[Museums and Galleries](https://www.yesmilano.it/en/study/museums-reduced-price-young-and-students#) [Attractions](https://www.yesmilano.it/en/study/museums-reduced-price-young-and-students#) [Students](https://www.yesmilano.it/en/study/museums-reduced-price-young-and-students#)

![](https://www.yesmilano.it/sites/default/files/styles/testata_full/public/articolo/copertina/6656/25861/musei-milano-ingresso-ridotto-studenti-giovani-pic%20by%20moniquec82-Instagram-cover.jpg?itok=GV6IZNpD)

**If you are under 25, or you are a university student**, you can visit numerous museums and cultural venues in Milano at a **reduced price**.

Here are the venues with reduced admission for young persons, and all the info needed for the visit. Don't forget: many museums permanently offer free admission or are open for free every first Sunday of the month.
