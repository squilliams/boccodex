# Library Services for Faculty

05 Sep 2025, by Library & Archives

Welcome to Bocconi Library! As a new academic year is rolling out, let us provide you with an updated overview of the Library services available to support your academic activity at Bocconi.

What’s there for you?

1. _**Access the Library 24/7**_

Bocconi Library is located in via Gobbi 5, but you can explore its collections and services directly from the Library website. On the [**Library homepage**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/library-archives) you will find [**SearchLib**](https://library.unibocconi.it/discovery/search?vid=39UCLB_INST:VU1&lang=en) **to** **access books, articles and more**, and the [**A-Z Databases list**](https://unibocconi.libguides.com/az.php?q=) **, the quickest way to browse and access databases**. The Library offers also access to leading international [**newspapers**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/library-archives/online-news-sources) and magazines, such as the Financial Times, The Economist and Il Sole 24 Ore, some of which are also available via **app**.

[**Libguides**](https://unibocconi.libguides.com/) are a great way to help your students find relevant resources for your discipline. They provide a selection of key databases, journals and books recommended for different subjects, topics (for example, how to use [financial databases](https://unibocconi.libguides.com/az.php?q=) or find specific data) and services.

Fill in the dedicated [form](https://lib-unibocconi.primo.exlibrisgroup.com/discovery/blankIll?vid=39UCLB_INST:VU1) for purchase and Inter-Library Loan requests: we shape our collections to match your research needs.

2. _**Course Reserves and teaching materials**_

The Library team can help you make required readings accessible to your students through the **Library Course Reserves** tool, integrated into your Blackboard course home page. Find out more about this [**service**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/library-archives/teaching-and-research-support/course-reserves-textbooks).

3. _**Get tailored support**_

Faculty members can get help and assistance from the Library team directly at the [**Information Desk**](https://bocconi.stackmap.com/explore/1?c=97) in the Library, or by scheduling an **online meeting** via MS Teams or Zoom. To schedule a Library meeting, please write to [infobiblio@unibocconi.it](mailto:infobiblio@unibocconi.it).

Each semester, the Library offers a series or [**workshops**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/library-archives/teaching-and-research-support/library-workshops) on databases, research strategies, managing citations, and much more. As a faculty member, you can write to us to request **on-demand workshops** designed specifically for your class or research needs.

Need to **purchase data for a research project** using your personal research funds? Contact the Library. We will support you during the whole acquisition process, from getting the quotation through the final agreement with the vendor.

4. _**Publish in Open Access**_

Bocconi University promotes **Open Access** as a key practice for making research freely available to all.  **Open Access publishing** is supported through **transformative agreements** with academic publishers, allowing Bocconi authors to publish in Open Access **at zero costs**. Discover more in our [**libguide dedicated to Open Access**](https://eur03.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Funibocconi.libguides.com%2Fc.php%3Fg%3D722521%26p%3D5248977&data=05%7C02%7Cfrancesca.bagaglia%40unibocconi.it%7Cd8b5ff1169a041bd4fc408dde475dd8a%7C6bf3b57a9fb447c29ada51156518f52f%7C1%7C0%7C638917917081075201%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJFbXB0eU1hcGkiOnRydWUsIlYiOiIwLjAuMDAwMCIsIlAiOiJXaW4zMiIsIkFOIjoiTWFpbCIsIldUIjoyfQ%3D%3D%7C0%7C%7C%7C&sdata=GO3EX381W40nSm3A2NNbB%2FnVYhdTUSIkYwSq1vioKo0%3D&reserved=0).

To find out further details about the Library services available to Bocconi Faculty, please consult the Library [**Service Charter**](https://unibocconi.libguides.com/servicecharter).

For any questions or further assistance, feel free to contact us writing to [infobiblio@unibocconi.it.](mailto:infobiblio@unibocconi.it)
