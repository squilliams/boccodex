# Cenacolo Vinciano

GUIDED TOUR

We are delighted to offer you this guided tour that matches the uniqueness of a world-renowned masterpiece like Leonardo da Vinci's Last Supper, protected by UNESCO.

The Last Supper occupies the entire northern wall of the refectory of the Dominican convent of Santa Maria delle Grazie, which served as the gathering place for friars to eat, pray, and meditate during meals. Since the 14th century, in the Tuscan tradition from which Leonardo drew inspiration, this place has commonly been referred to as the "Cenacolo," a term that from the early 16th century has also been used to denote the pictorial representation of the Last Supper that usually adorned such environments.

If you desire to witness this great masterpiece with your own eyes, we are thrilled to accompany you on an unforgettable guided tour.
