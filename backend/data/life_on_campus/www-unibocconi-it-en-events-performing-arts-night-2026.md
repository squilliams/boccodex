# Performing Arts Night 2026

11 May 2026

6:00pm - 11:30pm

Via Roentgen 1

#### An evening entirely devoted to performing arts at Bocconi!

A journey through the performing arts as a shared community experience, where every voice, every body, every image is a thread that binds us together.

Student participating in **theater, singing, dance and filmmaking** courses organized by Campus Life, together with students from the Association **Gruppo Teatro Studenti Bocconi,** will perform in an evening celebrating the performing arts on campus.

This will be followed by **the musical "Tonight We Gotta Footloose!" by BLPSA.**

The evening is organized by Campus Life **in collaboration with Teatro Franco Parenti** and coordinated byProf. **Alex Turrini**, Arts & Humanities Committee with responsibility for performing arts.

The Bocconi University community reveals itself through art as a living network of connections, passions, and presences.

### **Program**

From 6 pm, foyer Aula Magna Roentgen

### Performing Arts Showcase

The evening celebrates the journey of the workshops and student initiatives promoted by Campus Life: through distributed stages, video installations, and performance spaces, featuring theater, singing, and dance.

At the beginning and at the end of the evening, the Gruppo Teatro Studenti Bocconi - directed by Marco Brambini - will present readings from the works of Stefano Benni.

Each performance thus becomes a thread cast into the shared space of the community, capable of connecting stage and audience, performers and spectators, students and the public, making visible through art the network of bonds that already unites us.

The workshops were led by:

- **Theater**: Tobia Rossi  in collaboration with Claudia Grassi, artistic collaborator.
- **Choral singing**: Aleksander Zielinski
- **Dance**: Marco Pelle in collaboration with Gaia Maria Lorusso, artistic collaborator.
- **Filmmaking**: Tommaso Santambrogio, in collaboration with Astrid Ardenti, artistic collaborator.

_Free event, registration required._

### Musical "Tonight We Gotta Footloose!"

Get ready to dance, sing, and let yourself be swept away by the most infectious music ever. BLPSA is proud to present a rousing revival of one of the most beloved shows in the history of musical theater.

A show entirely produced by students, directed by Altea Russo, Resident Director of productions such as Sister Act and Pretty Woman at the Teatro Nazionale, and choreographed by GinevraGrossi, a Musical Performer and Dancer with experience in major international productions such as Così Fan Tutte at La Scala, West Side Story at the Rome Opera and Tootsie & Matilda the Musical with Peeparrow Entertainment.

The show will be held in Italian.

The Bocconi Live Performance Students Association is a student organization, active since 2014 and dedicated to live performances and staging a musical on campus every year.

The musical is an initiative funded by Bocconi University, organized as part of the Arts and Humanities activities of Campus Life.

_Paid event._
