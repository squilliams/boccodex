# HPC

**High Performance Computing**

Bocconi University provides access to **High Performance Computing (HPC)** resources to all faculty members, supporting the research community for those computations that cannot be performed on a local/personal machines.

The facility is currently built with 24 nodes interconnected with a special high-speed Infiniband network, with 752cores, 16 Nvidia Ampere A100 GPUs, 8 Nvidia H100 GPUs, 16 Nvidia H200 GPUs, 20TB of RAM and about 250TB of storage

Two nodes, each equipped with four NVIDIA A100 GPUs (80 GB VRAM each) and 512 GB of system RAM, are configured with MIG (Multi-Instance GPU) and dedicated to students.

The Technology team provides training workshops and support for researchers.

Access is granted by simple email request ( [hpc@unibocconi.it](mailto:hpc@unibocconi.it "mailto:hpc@unibocconi.it")).
