- **EUROMEDICA ASSISTANCE**

Bocconi University has activated an agreement with Euromedica Assistance, a health center located at Viale Bligny 11.  For further information please check the [Italian website](https://www.unibocconi.it/wps/wcm/connect/bocconi/sitopubblico_it/albero+di+navigazione/home/corsi+di+studio/studenti+iscritti/campus+life/convenzioni/convenzioni+salute) or click [here](https://www.euromedica-assistance.com/).

- **MEDICI IN FAMIGLIA**

Multi-specialty medical center with a focus on social issues, it reinvests its profits in free assistance for the neediest segments of the population.

For Bocconi students, faculty and staff, the first two appointments/services are offered at a discounted price of **20% off** listed prices. Services are also available in English; requests for English services must be made at the time of reservation (phone +39 02 8426 8020).

For further information: [www.medicinfamiglia.it](https://www.medicinfamiglia.it/)

- **ISTITUTO HUMANITAS**

Istituto Humanitasin Rozzano offers Bocconi students, faculty and staff a **20% discount** off the listed price of all diagnostic services and specialist appointments, with the exclusion of dental services. This discount will be available to anyone who shows their university ID.

Reservations can be made by calling the dedicated phone number +39 02 82242100 (medical personnel and staff also speak English).
