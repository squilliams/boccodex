# Bloomberg unavailable on Tuesday 3 February 2026

29 Jan 2026, by Library & Archives

Terminals won’t be available from 4.30pm until the end of the day

Due to a programmed technical intervention, it won’t be possible to book and access the Bloomberg terminals in the Library Data Room on Tuesday, 3 February, from 4.30pm onwards. The terminals will be available until 4.30, during the [Library opening hours](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/library-archives/opening-hours "Opening Hours").
