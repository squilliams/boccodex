# Cultural courses and artistic workshops in visual and performing arts

The University actively pursues the objective of creating opportunities and situations for **developing and expressing artistic abilities.**

Students, along with faculty and staff, have been able to express their creativity and skills for many years, to be appreciated by the entire community at performances and concerts that are open to the public.

### Acting

### Dance

"GuiDance" is a dance course open to all people Bocconi. Inclusiveness is the key word for us.

The course is held by Marco Pelle, resident choreographer of the New York Theatre Ballet, and Gaia Maria Lorusso.

[Discover more](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/campus-life/arts-and-humanities/dance-masterclass)

### Choral singing

The choral activity is open to all, without any distinction between those who are in tune and those who are not, and includes specific activities divided into various moments.

The course is offered in collaboration with Yamaha Music Europe.

[Find out more](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/campus-life/arts-and-humanities/choral-singing)

### Filmmaking

This course explores film direction through the critical thought and cinematic work of Pier Paolo Pasolini, combining theoretical analysis with hands-on documentary practice.

In collaboration with director Tommaso Santambrogio.

[Find out more](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/campus-life/arts-and-humanities/filmmaking)
