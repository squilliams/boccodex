# Bocconi Alumni

Here is a list of Library resources and services available to Bocconi Alumni:

- print collections (journals, books, theses, working papers, etc.)
- e-journals, e-books and a [selection of databases](https://unibocconi.libguides.com/az/alumni). You can access online resources from the Library and anywhere on-campus, but due to contractual limitations from the providers, off-campus access is not allowed. We remind you that access to online resources is authorised only for educational, research or personal use. [Terms of use](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/library-archives/using-library/using-online-resources)
- information and research assistance

Before entering the Library make sure you have your Bocconi ID card and wi-fi credentials with you. If you need assistance for them (the card and wi-fi credentials), please contact [alumni@unibocconi.it](mailto:alumni@unibocconi.it) at least 5 business days in advance.

**Special benefits for** [**Alumni who complied with the Community's Regulations and Values**](https://bocconialumni.it/en/community/exclusive-benefits/index.html)

- Borrowing: up to 5 books for 4 weeks, with a maximum of 5 renewals for extra 4 weeks each (except for textbooks)
