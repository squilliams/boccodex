# BOCCONI SPORT SCHOLARSHIPS 2026/2027

Ultimo aggiornamento: 26 February 2026

4 minuti di lettura

[Facebook](https://www.bocconisport.eu/#facebook) [Mastodon](https://www.bocconisport.eu/#mastodon) [Email](https://www.bocconisport.eu/#email) [Share](https://www.addtoany.com/share#url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.bocconisport.eu%2Fnews%2Fbocconi-sport-scholarships-20262027&title=BOCCONI%20SPORT%20SCHOLARSHIPS%202026%2F2027)

As part of the “Bocconi4Access to Education” program, the “ **Bocconi Sport Scholarships**” aim to support talented student-athletes by removing, where necessary, any socio-economic barriers that could prevent them from accessing university education.

This initiative stems from the University's belief that supporting high-level young athletes who are eager to commit their professional growth creates value for the community and for the country as a whole.

With the “ **Bocconi Sport Scholarships**”, the University grants a limited number of students (maximum 10 beneficiaries per year) exemption from university fees and contributions (between 60% and 100% depending on their verified financial situation) and the allocation of accommodation at a preferential rate of 50% of the full price.

Students who do not qualify for this support due to resource availability will be considered for one of the other benefits provided.

The "Bocconi4Access to Education" support scheme is highly detailed; the University will consider each application submitted for all benefits starting with the largest one up until all available resources are exhausted. The University will try and assign the one benefit that is most corresponding to the economic and asset situation assessed on the basis of the documentation submitted.

##### **1\. Eligible Applicants**

Applicants eligible for the “ **Bocconi Sport Scholarships**” for A.Y. 2026–27 include **students:**

- Italian or international applicants enrolled in the first year to a bachelor's degree program, master’s degree program, master's degree program in law, or university master’s degree program in the a.y. 2026-2027 and selected and registered with the main Bocconi Sport teams (Bocconi Sport Athletes Scholarships);

or

- Italian or international applicants enrolled in the first year to a bachelor's degree program, master's degree program, master's degree program in law, HEC-Bocconi Double Degree Program or university master’s degree program in the a.y. 2026-2027 who are involved in high-profile sports activities of national and international interest and are **members of national or foreign sports clubs or associations** (Bocconi Sport Talent Scholarships);

- **Priority will be given to****:**

Students with an **ISEE below €30,000** and a consistent financial situation based on Bocconi's family assessment method. However, students with higher ISEE values will also be considered.

Furthermore, it is specified that all students must submit the **application**: Bocconi4Access to Education a.y. 2026-2027 ( **please see below points 3 and 4**)

**Attention**:

The Fees, Funding and Housing Office will verify all documents presented and will assess the economic and asset condition of the Bocconi conventional family household of the student:

- [_**Economic and Asset Situation for Bachelor and Law**_](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/bachelor-and-law-programs/funding/economic-and-asset-situation-ay-2026-2027)
- [_**Economic and Asset Situation for Master of Science**_](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/bachelor-and-law-programs/funding/economic-and-asset-situation-ay-2026-2027)
- [_**Bocconi Conventional Family Household for Bachelor and Law**_](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/bachelor-and-law-programs/funding/bocconi-conventional-family-household-ay-2026-2027)
- [_**Bocconi Conventional Family Household for Master of Science**_](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/bachelor-and-law-programs/funding/bocconi-conventional-family-household-ay-2026-2027)

Based on this evaluation, the office will determine the entity of the support that can be attributed. In fact, it is not assigned any benefit upon a single requisite or element of evaluation.

The selection of profiles eligible from a sporting point of view will be carried out by a special **Sports Subcommittee**.

##### **2\. General rules**

The provisions outlined on the following pages constitute official publication of the regulations for the benefits provided under the “ **Bocconi4Access to Education**” support scheme, dedicated to students enrolled in the a.y. 2026-27:

- [_**Bachelor and Law**_](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/bachelor-and-law-programs/funding/general-rules-ay-2026-2027)
- [_**Master of Science**_](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/master-science-and-ma-programs/funding/general-rules-master-science-programs)

##### **3\. Single Application: Deadlines and Submission Procedures**

Interested students have to submit the single “ **Bocconi4access to Education A.Y. 2026–27**” application, according to the _**deadlines and procedure foreseen:**_

- [_**Bachelor and Law**_](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/bachelor-and-law-programs/funding/bocconi-sport-scholarship-ay-2026-27)
- [_**Master of Science**_](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/master-science-and-ma-programs/funding/bocconi-sport-scholarship-ay-2026-27-master-science-programs)

Students enrolled in a University Master’s program who are interested in submitting an application are invited to write to [**funding@unibocconi.it**](mailto:funding@unibocconi.it) _._

##### **4\. Required documentation**

The benefit can only be assigned after the detailed examination of all the required documentation submitted by the applicant in the “ **Bocconi4Access to Education A.Y. 2026–27**” application, as detailed:

- [_**Bachelor and Law**_](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/bachelor-and-law-programs/funding/required-documentation-funding-application-ay-2026-27)
- [_**Master of Science**_](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/master-science-and-ma-programs/funding/required-documentation-master-science-programs)

Please note that students applying for this type of benefit must also upload a **sports resume and/or presentation highlights** to their application.

Please be aware that **submitting an application does not automatically entitle** the student to receive the benefit, but is merely a condition for accessing the application assessment process.

##### **5\. Renewal**

All benefits assigned by Bocconi University as part of the “Bocconi4Access to Education” support scheme can be renewed year by year for the entire legal duration of the regular course of study, **if the merit requirements are met**, as specified:

- [_**Bachelor and Law**_](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/bachelor-and-law-programs/funding/renewal-ay-2026-2027)
- [_**Master of Science**_](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/master-science-and-ma-programs/funding/renewal-master-science)

Furthermore, sporting merit must be confirmed by the Sports Subcommittee.

Preferential rate housing renewal for the academic year 2027-28 is also subject to the above requirements being met.

Assigned students will be required to pay the first installment-advance each academic year. This amount, upon fulfillment of the benefit maintenance requirements, will be reimbursed each year by the end of December of the reference academic year.

##### **6\. Revocation and forfeiture**

Students may lose their benefit, or the benefit itself may be revoked, if one of the following conditions listed on the followingpages occurs:

- [_**Bachelor and Law**_](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/bachelor-and-law-programs/funding/revocation-and-forfeiture-ay-2026-27)
- [_**Master of Science**_](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/master-science-and-ma-programs/funding/revocation-and-forfeiture-master-science-programs)
