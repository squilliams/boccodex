## Newspapers

Here's a list of the most popular newspapers that you can access online. Some of them require to create an account, here you can find all the instructions:

- [**Corriere della Sera (Archivio Pro)**](https://go.openathens.net/redirector/unibocconi.eu?url=https%3A%2F%2Farchivio.corriere.it)

Includes all articles published on Corriere della Sera from 1876 up to now. The current edition is available from around 11 am (click on _Ricerca ultimi 7 giorni_)

- [**Il Sole 24 Ore**](https://du.ilsole24ore.com/utenti/authfiles/loginaziende.aspx?caller_id=CRUI.MYDESK&user_uniqueid=crui)

Access restricted to current Bocconi University and SDA members.

Enter _Universita Bocconi (OpenAthens)_ as the organization's name and login with your Bocconi/SDA credentials.

Scroll down the page and click on _Quotidiani_ within the section _I miei prodotti_. Registration required once a year through the dedicated form.

- [**Financial Times**](https://ft.com/Bocconi)

Access restricted to current Bocconi University and SDA members.

Once you've registered, you can also access the newspaper through the app.

Share links to articles exclusively by using any [sharing tools](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qPjJH4CRVng "https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qpjjh4crvng") made available by FT.

- [**The New York Times**](http://www.nytimes.com/)

Access restricted to current Bocconi University and SDA members.

You need a **NYT account** (academic pass) to access. Visit [accessnyt.com](http://accessnyt.com/), type in **Bocconi** as school name, click on the institution, and create your account to claim and activate your **one-year pass**. You must use your Bocconi/SDA e-mail address. Once your pass is active, you will be able to access nytimes.com from anywhere.

Once you've registered, you can also access the newspaper through the NYT app.

- [**The Wall Street Journal**](http://www.wsj.com/)

Access restricted to current Bocconi University and SDA members.

You need a **WSJ account** to access. [Register](https://go.openathens.net/redirector/unibocconi.eu?url=https%3A%2F%2Fpartner.wsj.com%2Fenter-redemption-code%2FUCLB8qrudjag) with your Bocconi/SDA e-mail address. Once your account is active, you will be able to access WSJ.com from anywhere. Access is valid for one year. After the expiration, you can [re-activate it](https://partner.wsj.com/enter-redemption-code/UCLB8qrudjag). **ATTENTION!** You must be **on-campus** to re-activate it.

Once you've registered, you can also access the newspaper through the WJS app.

- [**The Economist**](https://myaccount.economist.com/s/institution-login?entity=https://idp.unibocconi.eu/entity)

Access restricted to current Bocconi University and SDA members.

You can also access the newspaper through the app: once you have downloaded it, click on “Log In” in the top right corner, then sign in using your Bocconi/SDA email address and selecting “Università Bocconi (OpenAthens)".
