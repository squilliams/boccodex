# Esports

## **Are you a talent on video games and do you want to grow your interest in a healthy and competitive way?**

Bocconi Sport offers you the opportunity to join our Esports team!

Currently, the active teams are in the following titles:

\- League of Legends;

\- EA FC;

\- Rocket League;

\- NBA 2K25;

\- Gran Turismo 7

For those who join the team and wear the Bocconi colors, there will be weekly reserved spaces for training on consoles and computers in the **Red Bull Gaming Zone**.

In addition, all members who have submitted a valid medical certificate will also be able to access the gym of the Bocconi Sport Center dedicated to the Bocconi Sport teams.
