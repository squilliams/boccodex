# Library opening hours 7-8 December

28 Nov 2025, by Library & Archives

On Sunday, 7 December and Monday 8 December, the Library will be open from 8:30am to 4pm

On **Sunday,** **7 December and Monday 8 December**, the Library will be **open from 8:30am to 4pm** (closing procedures start 30 minutes earlier). On Tuesday, 9 December, the Library will be back to its usual opening hours, from 8:30am to midnight. Looking forward to seeing you there!
