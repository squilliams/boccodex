# 19-21 April Library closed for holidays

07 Apr 2025, by Library & Archives

_On Friday, 18 April, the Library will close at 8pm (closing operations starting 30 minutes earlier). From Saturday, 19 April to Monday 21 April inclusive, the Library will be closed for holidays. We look forward to seeing you on Tuesday 22 April at 8:30am_!
