# Single students' Initiatives

All student activities are approved by the CASA Committee (or its delegates), that operates by principles collected and constantly adjourned in a document available here. It is **FUNDAMENTAL** to know its contents in order to plan your activities in an achievable way: here you can download, read and consult the [**CASA Committee Regulations.**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/campus-life/student-activities/casa-committee-and-regulations "The CASA Committee and the Regulations")

#### **ORGANIZING AN EVENT**

To present you request you have to fill in this [FORM](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/media/attachments/FORM-rich-spazi-x-EVENTI.pdf) \- which will be evaluated by the CASA Committee - and deliver it to Campus Life, the Technical Secretariat of the CASA Committee.

Pay attention: in order to realize your events you need to submit your request in time! Check in the Regulations the deadlines you need to follow; you can also check online the next meetings of the CASA Committee.

After the Committee's approval you can interact with all the Bocconi offices that are involved in the event organization. Campus Life is available for information at each stage of the process.

#### **GETTING FINANCIAL SUPPORT**

Each year Bocconi provides funding for initiatives organized by students for students.

The request for funding has to be presented together with the request for event approval by the CASA Committee by filling out this [FORM](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/media/attachments/Domanda-di-finanziamento-delle-attivit%25C3%25A0-culturali-organizzate-dagli-studenti-2014.pdf) and delivering it to Campus Life paying attention to deadlines as above specified.

**Remember!**

- Consult the CASA Committee Regulations in order to verify the admissible costs
- Respect the deadlines for the presentation of the request to the CASA committee
- You cannot ask for funding after an event

**IN ORDER TO RECEIVE A FUNDING:**

When funding is granted, reimbursement of expenses will only be carried by submitting to the Campus Life office:

- proper invoices, headed to Università Bocconi:

Università Bocconi

via Sarfatti 25 - 20136 Milano

C.F. 80024610158

P. IVA 03628350153
- a detailed report of the activity carried out
- event poster; which needs to state that "The event is sponsored by Università Bocconi".

**ANY DOUBT?**

Contact Campus Life, the Technical Secretariat of the CASA Committee. It is available for information at each stage of the process, and it can help you to plan and organize your event.
