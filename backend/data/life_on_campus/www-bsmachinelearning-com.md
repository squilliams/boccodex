# Bocconi Students for Machine Learning

Connecting minds, not only layers

## Bocconi's first Machine Learning project-incubator

## A community centered around publishing projects, where

## members can nurture ideas, develop skills, and

## find collaborators to pursue their ambitions.

Our Values

#### Credibility

Our North Star. We build credible skills through projects that lead to internships and success.

#### Meritocracy

Open to all backgrounds. The best work stands out through outcomes.

#### Ownership

Members choose projects, set pace, and receive full recognition.

#### No-Fuffa Policy

Pragmatic work. Real projects. Concrete achievements over ego.

## Join us. Together we bring your ideas to life.

No idea yet? Contribute to ongoing projects.

Become a member by taking one of these roles

- **Personal Project**
- **Partnership**
- **Event**
- **Content**
- **Marketing**
- **Mentee**

#### Deliverable

Complete and publish your project with us.

#### Mission

Turn your idea into a project and publish it on our channels.

#### Description

Have an ML idea? Already building something and want peer feedback? Need help publishing your work?
BSML
offers support across coding, storytelling, and publishing.

#### Approximate workflow

1. Propose your project at an event or in the members’ chat.
2. Collaborate with associates to reach objectives and create content.
3. Publish on BSML channels with full recognition.

#### Examples

- You have a trading strategy and want help automating it and
testing ML models.
- You can publish work from an internship but need guidance to
write it up.
- You’re stuck on a thesis bug and want code debugging and
methodology feedback.

#### Requirements

- Personal project idea
- Openness to collaborators

**Your role brings fresh ideas and projects. It is BSML’s backbone.**

#### Deliverable

One company partnership per semester.

#### Mission

Connect BSML and its members with companies.

#### Description

Source prospects on LinkedIn and via your network. Set up partnerships such as talks, hackathons,
projects, or interviews.

#### Approximate workflow

1. Research Milan companies and ML/AI/Data Science teams. Identify openings and profiles.
2. Contact key profiles, understand needs, and propose BSML value.

#### Examples

- An open Data Analyst role. Offer a candidate selection via
competition, hackathon, or interview.
- A data science team with real problems. Offer a pro-bono BSML
project.

#### Requirements

- Concise communication
- Understanding of company operations
- LinkedIn and business outreach fluency
- Interest in social engineering

**This role networks BSML with real companies.**

#### Deliverable

One event per semester.

#### Mission

Turn partnerships and projects into events.

#### Description

Design and run events: research talks, company sessions, showcases, dinners with guests, and more.

#### Approximate workflow

1. Track association projects and external opportunities.
2. Organize logistics: venue, date, schedule, and marketing.

#### Examples

- Convert a professor interview into a public research talk.
- Launch a company-hosted hackathon with an info session.

#### Requirements

- Organization and time management
- Attention to detail

**This role enables BSML to meet and thrive in person.**

#### Deliverable

One piece of content per month.

#### Mission

Boost credibility by publishing tangible outcomes.

#### Description

Work with associates to turn projects into compelling content: articles, videos, presentations, and
more.

#### Approximate workflow

1. Track projects and motivate publishing.
2. Shape the story: problem, approach, outcome.
3. Produce the content.

#### Examples

- Turn a thesis project into a clear article.
- Capture an event and publish a recap video.

#### Requirements

- Content skills (video, images, or writing)
- Excellent English
- Creativity and execution

**This role builds BSML’s credibility.**

#### Deliverable

One social post per week.

#### Mission

Share timely updates and insights about BSML.

#### Description

Find the most relevant association news and package it into posts for social and web.

#### Approximate workflow

1. Stay current on association activity.
2. Create and publish assets across channels.

#### Examples

- Announce an upcoming company event with flyers and details.

- Recap a hackathon with photos and outcomes.

#### Requirements

- Social media marketing interest
- Storytelling and communication
- Image-editing proficiency

**This role nurtures BSML’s public image.**

None.

#### Mission

Reach _your_ goals.

#### Description

Get paired with a mentor in Quant Finance, Data Science, ML Research, PhD, or Master’s. Expect

#### Approximate workflow

Depends on your goals and mentor.

#### Examples

### Frequently Asked **Questions**

If you have other questions, reach out.

### I want to join but have no project idea. What can I do?

Join an ongoing project or wait for a new proposal and volunteer as a collaborator.

### I worry I won’t have time. What should I do?

We are full-time students. Academics come first. If you contribute, we are flexible and accommodate
needs.

### My idea feels trivial. Should I still apply?

Yes. We learn by doing. If it motivates you, it is worth pursuing.

### Is it hard to get in? Acceptance rate?

We are a student association focused on shared passion and outcomes, not gatekeeping. If you are
committed or curious, apply.
