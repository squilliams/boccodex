# DIPLÔME DE FRANÇAIS PROFESSIONNEL

Bocconi University is accredited test center for international certification of Business French, namely **DFP** **Affaires B1 and B2 level** issued by **CCI PARIS ILE-DE-FRANCE.**

Registration is open to students from Bocconi and other Universities or Schools.

Exam sessions are generally scheduled twice per year (Spring and Autumn Session).

Registration fees:

- DFP level B1, € 120
- DFP level B2, € 140

## **How to register for the exam**

The applicant must:

1\. pay the registration fee through the online procedure by the deadline for “Enrollment and payment period” scheduled for the session; registration is accepted only after payment;

2\. subimit the application (via the online procedure) during the "Enrollment and payment period" scheduled for the session

The registration fee will not be refunded in the case of absence on the date of examination.

## **Exam location**

Via Gobbi, 5 – Language Center

## **Exam format**

The exam consists of two parts :

- Written computer-based test (80’ for B1 level; 2 hours for B2 level)
- Oral test (Preparation: 20' for B1 level, 15' for B2 level; Presentation: 10’ for B1 level, 15' for B2 level)

[Further](https://www.lefrancaisdesaffaires.fr/tests-diplomes/diplomes-francais-professionnel-dfp/affaires/) [information](https://www.lefrancaisdesaffaires.fr/tests-diplomes/diplomes-francais-professionnel-dfp/affaires/)

## **Results**

Those who have passed the exam will receive an email from CCIP Paris Ile-de-France with a link to download the certification available in electronic format only.

Those who have not passed the exam will be notified by the Language Center.

## **Sessions**

Please note that the date of each session will be confirmed only upon reaching a minimum number of registrations. As soon as the number is reached, the session will be marked " **CONFIRMED**". Otherwise, it will not take place and the registration fee will be refunded to all registered candidates.

In addition, please note that **the registration fee will not be refunded in case of absence on the date of the exam**.
