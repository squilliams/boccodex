# Named study rooms

![Named Study Rooms](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/styles/container_100_lg/public/media/images/la_namedstudyrooms.png.webp?itok=u77ia_Qz)

![Named Study rooms 2](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/styles/container_100_lg/public/media/images/lea_salededicate2.jpg.webp?itok=XgTjStje)

The Bocconi Library is a place that metaphorically represents the meeting point between all generations of students at the University. For this reason, the initiative of Bocconi Donors who have chosen over the years to philanthropically name one of its spaces, to associate their own name or the name of a loved one with a place of great symbolic value, is particularly significant. The named spaces are recognisable by the presence of a plaque at the entrance, bearing the name of the Donors and the dedication - in memory or dedicated to - of those whose name or memory is associated with the room.
