# Our Collaborators

Soft skills and self-empowerment, psychological counseling and group coaching, courses to develop your creativity and courses aimed at personal wellbeing, health and proper nutrition: all this is part of Campus Life. To stay true to our mission, we collaborate closely with the best professionals in a variety of sectors.

Here are the professionals who collaborate with us, providing all students with a unique educational experience.

### Area Arts and Humanities

![](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/styles/highlight_slide/public/media/images/zielinski.jpg.webp?itok=7usC0i2_)

### Aleksandr Zielinski

Pianist and choral teacher at the Orsoline San Carlo Foundation in Milan.

Aleksandr has been collaborating with Yamaha Music Europe Gmbh since 2012. He specializes in children's choral teaching and has been the musical director of events at the Conservatory of Milan, Teatro da Verme, and other important theaters and historical sites.

![Marco pelle](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/styles/highlight_slide/public/media/images/marco_pelle.jpg.webp?itok=A9TJh9VA)

### Marco Pelle

He is an internationally renowned choreographer who began his dance training in Italy and continued in Monaco before moving to New York. He has been a resident choreographer for New York Theatre Ballet since 2012 and has collaborated with notable artists like Alessandra Ferri and Roberto Bolle. Pelle has also worked extensively in opera, choreographing productions in the US and China.

![](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/styles/highlight_slide/public/media/images/tommaso_santambrogio.png.webp?itok=ojEO19d5)

### Tommaso Santambrogio

He is a young Italian director; he has lived between Milan, Paris, Rome, Florence, and Havana, and has collaborated with several internationally renowned filmmakers. Oceans Are the Real Continents is his first feature film and had its world premiere in competition at Venice Days during the 80th Venice Film Festival.

[Find out more](https://www.tommasosantambrogio.com/)

![](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/styles/highlight_slide/public/media/images/ceresole_0.png.webp?itok=3w8cED-6)

### Anna Ceresole

Research Director in Theoretical Physics at the National Institute of Nuclear Physics, Turin Section.

Current Position: Permanent Delegation of Italy to UNESCO, Paris: Attachée 2024-2026

Main reasearch interests: Quantum field theories for gravity and the fundamental interactions: supergravity and string theory, applications to black holes and cosmology

[Find out more](https://www.strings.to.infn.it/personal/ceresole/)

### ISPI

ISPI is the only Italian institute - and among the very few in Europe - to combine its research activities with a significant commitment to training, conferences and activities of analysis and guidance on global risks and opportunities for businesses and institutions. All activity is characterized by an approach that combines geopolitical and geoeconomic analysis and by extensive collaboration with leading think tanks around the world.

![](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/styles/highlight_slide/public/media/images/paolo_grassi_logo.jpeg.webp?itok=73Ti7jFW)

### Civica Scuola di Teatro Paolo Grassi

The Civica Scuola di Teatro Paolo Grassi is part of the Fondazione Milano® and offers, unique in Italy, training courses for all the main professional figures in the field of theater and live performance.

For several years, Campus Life has been organizing in collaboration with the school a theater course that takes the form of a performance at the end of the year within the "Performing arts night".

### Skills and Self-Empowerment

![Simone Bandini Buti](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/styles/highlight_slide/public/media/images/bandini_buti_1.jpg.webp?itok=XMiUyoa3)

### Simone Bandini Buti

After a long professional experience in theater as an actor and director, he has been a trainer and communication consultant for about 25 years. He holds courses and interventions focused on empowerment, with special focus on issues related to Communication, Interpersonal Relationship and Leadership. Milan, 1959, linguist. Researcher and coach in communication techniques. Bachelor of Arts, years in journalism and advertising. Teaches at Iulm University, Scuola Sant'Anna in Pisa. Prevalent areas of research: medical and financial communication, public speaking and negotiation. Founded "La Palestra della Scrittura", a language research and training laboratory.

[Watch his videos on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=Alessandro%20Lucchini)

Scholar and researcher of linguistics and communication skills. He teaches at the University of Bolzano and at the Writing Gymnasium in Milan. ### Counseling

![](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/styles/highlight_slide/public/media/images/giulia_rancati_3_.jpg.webp?itok=BFoSKqJ6)

### Giulia Rancati

Cognitive and cognitive-behavioral psychologist and psychotherapist, she is a member of SITCC (Italian Society of Cognitive Behavioral Therapy). She has collaborated with Bocconi Counseling Service since 2007. She is also an expert in the treatment of eating disorders and obesity, and since 2006 has worked in a specialized department of nutritional rehabilitation. Member of SIDBT (Italian Society for Dialectical Behavior Therapy), she is specialized in dialectical behavior treatment for personality disorders, borderline disorder and self-injurious adolescents.

![](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/styles/highlight_slide/public/media/images/laura_rigobello_cut.jpeg.webp?itok=FObmLrtC)

### Laura Rigobello

PhD, psychologist and cognitive behavioral psychotherapist. She is also an individual DBT therapist and group skills trainer for patients with borderline personality disorder and their family members. She teaches at the School of Specialization in Cognitive Psychotherapy SPC in Verona. She is also a member of the Italian Society of Behavioral and Cognitive Therapy, SITCC.

![](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/styles/highlight_slide/public/media/images/camille.jpeg.webp?itok=T-AZO0j0)

### Camille-Annabelle Bertrand

Psychologist specializing in children and adolescents, she is a board-certified psychologist in Lombardy. In addition to working freelance mainly with expat families, she has been collaborating with Bocconi since 2007. As part of the University's Counseling Service, she works with Italian and international students (in Italian, English and French).

![Foto di chiara de falco](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/styles/highlight_slide/public/media/images/chiara_de_falco_0.jpeg.webp?itok=4G4dx6KT)

### Chiara De Falco

Bilingual psychologist and clinical sex therapist, currently a transcultural psychotherapy apprentice at GRT (Gruppo per le Relazioni Transculturali) in Milan. Since 2018, she has worked with young adults in multilingual clinical and socio-educational settings in Italy and abroad. Alongside her private practice, she delivers training in schools and social services on affectivity, DEA, and digital well-being, using interactive group-based methods and educational software. She is also an intern at CPS - Complex Operating Unit of Psychiatry 2, Department of Mental Health and Addictions, ASST Fatebenefratelli-Sacco.

![](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/styles/highlight_slide/public/media/images/kraslavski.jpg.webp?itok=2jte7_TZ)

### Alexandra Kraslavski

Clinical Psychologist and Psychotherapist, specialized in Cognitive Behavioral Therapy at the Milan school of Studi Cognitivi. An EMDR therapist and practitioner with expertise in psychotraumatology, she is trained in CBT-E for the treatment of eating disorders and obesity. She is a member of the EMDR Italy Association and the Italian Society of Cognitive and Behavioral Therapy (SITCC). Her clinical and research experience spans various settings, from hospital to outpatient care, integrating evidence-based protocols with a particular focus on the therapeutic relationship.

![](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/styles/highlight_slide/public/media/images/antonio_danzi_0.jpg.webp?itok=xXctzLGJ)

### Antonio Danzi

Bilingual psychotherapist and counselor. Trained in London at the Tavistock and Portman NHS Trust, he is registered with professional bodies in the UK and Italy. He has extensive experience supporting young adults in universities, public mental health services, and the non-profit sector. His approach is relational, psychodynamic, trauma-informed and psychoeducational. At Bocconi, he offers a safe and confidential space for students to explore issues such as stress, academic pressure, anxiety, relationships, cultural adjustment, grief, and questions of identity or sexuality (LGBTQI+).

![](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/styles/highlight_slide/public/media/images/karen_rigatti.jpg.webp?itok=a3o7-Duh)

### Karen Rigatti

Professional counselor and member of AssoCounseling Italia and the American Counseling Association in the United States. She works mainly with international students on issues related to cultural adaptation, study methods, improving communication and strategies for managing university life and cultural and interpersonal challenges.

![](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/styles/highlight_slide/public/media/images/milena_milani.jpg.webp?itok=S3iwq1RK)

### Milena Milani

Educational Psychologist and Psychotherapist. She is a Feuerstein mediator, a Theraplay practitioner and a member of the British Dyslexia Association. She has studied in both Italy and the US. Working mostly in English alongside a group of multilingual colleagues, she offers various services to the international and expat community living in Northern Italy and Ticino. She also teaches communication and bilingualism at several institutions.

![](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/styles/highlight_slide/public/media/images/lucrezia_islam_0.png.webp?itok=rQhDdNIM)

### Lucrezia Islam

MD, specialist in Psychotherapy. She studied in Italy at the University of Milan, the Institute of Gestalt HCC Italy Milan and the EMDR Association Italy, and in the US at the National Institutes of Health (NIH). She is a member of the Society for Neuroscience. Expert in psychiatric and psychotherapeutic treatment of the main psychological and psychiatric disorders.

### Cristina Rossetti

Psychologist and psychotherapist with a systemic-relational orientation (European Institute of Systemic-relational Therapies). Since 2006 she has been working at the local services of the Province of Milan dealing mainly with adoption, foster care and family protection, also responding to requests from the Judicial Authority.
