# Rugby

## **Are you a rugby player who wants to keep playing during your university years?**

Through a partnership with the prestigious AS Rugby Milano club, Bocconi Sport offers all students passionate about the sport the opportunity to train at one of Milan's best-equipped rugby facilities (G.B. Curioni Sports Center) and to compete across a wide range of levels.

In fact, AS Rugby Milano can guarantee participation in its senior competitive teams at three different levels, depending on the athlete's skill:

- **Serie A** – National Championship
- **Serie C1** – Regional Championship
- **UISP** – Regional Championship

The offer ensures that participation in any of the partnered activities, includes the opportunity:

- to take part in all team activities and competitions for your category, under the guidance of ASR Milano's coaching and athletic training staff.
- to have full access to the sports center, Clubhouse, restaurant, gyms, and changing rooms, as well as healthcare and physiotherapy services, and cultural and sporting events, with the same rights as all registered AS Rugby Milano members.

Are you interested in joining our rugby activities? Email us at [**rugby@unibocconi.it**](mailto:rugby@unibocconi.it) for all the details!
