Why BSQF

## What Makes Us Unique

Unlike other Bocconi associations, we focus on technical execution and hands-on experience, bridging the gap between quantitative theory and real-world application.

### Technical Focus

Hands-on coding with Python, R, and advanced quantitative tools, supported by a proprietary investment portfolio managed through systematic trading strategies

### Research Publications

Four specialized divisions producing five research papers per semester, complemented by a biweekly newsletter delivering market insights and quantitative analysis

### Industry Collaboration

A minimum of two institutional partnerships and industry events per year, providing direct exposure to leading financial institutions and boutique firms
