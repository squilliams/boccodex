## Revenue generation in the sports industry

Arts, sports and events

The program develops the skills needed to manage marketing and sponsorship activities, as well as to design and organize events in the sports sector. Through the analysis of best practices, discussions with industry experts, and practical application of acquired knowledge, participants will explore reference models, operational tools, and new insights to manage the business in an innovative way.

![fa per te se ondemand](https://www.sdabocconi.it/_next/image/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fsda-website-prod-sdawebsiteprodbucketpublic693d05b-w3mwb4fo9x9p.s3.eu-south-1.amazonaws.com%2Fs3fs-public%2F2025-03%2Ffa-per-te-se-ondemand-mb.png&w=3840&q=90&dpl=dpl_EgyAUeGYW1L1ERS2WGuDkM3HLa8o)

## Who should apply

The program is designed for:

- Former athletes looking to combine executive skills with their experience in the sports field
- Current and aspiring directors of sports organizations
- Marketing managers from companies with significant communication activities in sports sponsorship
- Professionals interested in an executive career in the sports industry

## Structure and contents

The “Sports Marketing & Sponsorship” Online Program can be attended whenever and however you wish. However, to maximize learning, we recommend spreading the learning commitment over 8 weeks with an average allocation of approximately 3 hours per week.

1st week

2nd week
