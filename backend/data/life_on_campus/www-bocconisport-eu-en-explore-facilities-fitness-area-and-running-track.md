# Fitness Area and Running Track

The gym area is equipped for fitness and wellness activities and includes an elevated athletics running track. The property is managed by our partner Virgin Active.

The club offers a great training experiences with the best professionals in the fitness industry, personalized workouts and the latest equipment. Available: gym floor, over 200 machines for cardio and functional training, Cycle studio, Boxing studio, Cross Active area, Grid area, Reformer Pilates studio, and Yoga studio. In addition, an exclusive 160m running track for great indoor running workouts.

It is possible to choose between functional training on Grid and the many courses scheduled every day (including Boxing, Cycling, Reformer Pilates, Yoga and much more) and relax in a comfortable and special relaxation area, with whirlpool, sauna, hammam, Mediterranean bath and sensory showers.
