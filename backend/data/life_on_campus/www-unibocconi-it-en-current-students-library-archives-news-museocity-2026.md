# Guided Tours of the Bocconi Historical Archives for MuseoCity 2026

19 Feb 2026, by Library & Archives

Bocconi Library & Archives is participating again this year in MuseoCity, an event featuring special openings, guided tours, workshops, meetings, and conferences promoted by 124 institutions across the city of Milan.

The [2026 edition](https://www.museocity.it/en/milano-museocity-2026) is entitled “Le imprese della cultura” (Cultural Enterprises) and the Bocconi Historical Archives have organized three guided tours to present, through documents, collections, and photographs, the history of the Bocconi Library as a cultural enterprise fostering knowledge.

We look forward to welcoming you on:

- **Saturday, 28 February** at 11:00am
- **Thursday, 5 March** at 1:00pm
- **Saturday, 7 March** at 11:00am

Reserve your place by writing to: [archivistorici@unibocconi.it](mailto:archivistorici@unibocconi.it)
