# English certificates

For all **students enrolled at Bocconi (including Master and PhD students)**, Bocconi University has entered the list of test venues for Cambridge Assessment English / British Council **IELTS  Academic** certification and Cambridge University / Anderson House **C1 Advanced and C2 Proficiency** certifications of general English.

Please note that the **test dates are defined in agreement with the British Council and Anderson House**.

**How to register for the exam**

It is possible to register directly on the [**yoU@B Student’s Diary**](https://agenda.unibocconi.it/)(Language Center widget) through the links to the British Council Milan and Anderson House registration platforms.

For any information on exam session registration (payments, invitation), exam results and certificates, please directly contact

- British Council ( [esami@britishcouncil.it](mailto:esami@britishcouncil.it)) or
- Anderson House ( [info@andersonhouse.it](mailto:info@andersonhouse.it))

IELTS terms and conditions at the following link: [https://www.britishcouncil.it/en/exam/ielts/terms-conditions](https://www.britishcouncil.it/en/exam/ielts/terms-conditions)

Cambridge certification terms and conditions can be viewed directly on the Anderson House platform at the time of registration.
