# IDEM Federation

Bocconi University has joined the [IDEM Federation](https://www.idem.garr.it/en/) (IDEntity Management per l’accesso federato), the Italian Authentication and Authorization infrastructures federation for the GAAR Network, the italian network for Research and Education. GAAR coordinates the various activities and provides the central infrastructure, the services and manages the applications.

IDEM is the first italian federation of Authentication and Authorization (AAI). Its goal is to allow the members of the scientific and academic community to easily access services and online content that's made available by various organizations.

![](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/styles/container_100_lg/public/media/images/logoidem.jpg.webp?itok=O5cCX30I)

Joining IDEM provides users at Università Bocconi with the convenience of using institutional login information to access all the resources available through the Federation.

Services that can be reached through the Federation are made available by [IDEM's partners and members](https://www.idem.garr.it/en/join/participants).

For any issues and information regarding the IDEM Federation, send an email to: [idem@unibocconi.it](mailto:idem@unibocconi.it).
