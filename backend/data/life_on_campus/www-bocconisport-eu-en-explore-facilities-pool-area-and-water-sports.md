# Pool Area and Water Sports

The space consists of an Olympic-size swimming pool and a 25-meter training pool.

Olympic-size swimming pool can host official competitions with a 500-seat grandstand.

View the [**25-meter training pool**](https://www.aquamore.it/bocconisportcenter/ambienti/vascafitnessedidattica/) page.

The facilities for water sports are managed by our partner Aquamore.
