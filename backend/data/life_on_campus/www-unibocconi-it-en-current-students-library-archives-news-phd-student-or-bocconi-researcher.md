# Are you a PhD student or a Bocconi researcher?

03 Apr 2025, by Library & Archives

Register for our online Open Science for Researchers workshop: learn the basics of Open Access agreements, predatory publishers and creative common licenses.

- What do you know about **Open Access** and how it drives transparency and accessibility in research?
- Did you know that with **Creative Commons licenses**, you can retain ownership of your work while sharing it widely?
- Are you aware of the risks posed by **predatory publishers** who might exploit researchers for profit?
- Did you know that you can **publish in Open Access at zero costs** with Elsevier, Springer and many more academic publishers?

Discover all of this and much more at our [_**Open Science for Researchers**_](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/library-archives/teaching-and-research-support/library-workshops "Library Workshops") workshop, book now for **15 April** or **27 May** (6.15 – 7.30 pm).

_**How to book?**_

Through your [yoU@B student diary](https://agenda.unibocconi.it/), “Appointments” section. Bookings open 4 weeks prior to each event.

For more information, write to [infobiblio@unibocconi.it](mailto:infobiblio@unibocconi.it)
