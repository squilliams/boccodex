First-month campus integration pack (Bocconi):

1. Join associations early
- Pick 2-3 associations aligned with your interests (academic, cultural, sports, volunteering, finance/media).
- Attend introductory meetings and join communication channels.

2. Attend orientation and starter events
- Use Welcome Days and initial campus events to build your network quickly.
- Prioritize events where you can meet student reps and association teams.

3. Use core services immediately
- Library access and study-seat routines.
- Student support channels (including inclusion/disability/SLD services when relevant).

4. Build social and career momentum
- Join student media/community initiatives if you want visibility and teamwork experience.
- Track campus talks/debates/workshops to meet peers beyond your class cohort.

5. Weekly execution routine
- Every week: attend at least one association activity and one university event.
- After 3-4 weeks: keep the groups where engagement is highest and drop low-signal activities.

Outcome target:
- By the end of month 1, you should have a stable peer network, 1-2 active communities, and a clear rhythm of campus participation.
