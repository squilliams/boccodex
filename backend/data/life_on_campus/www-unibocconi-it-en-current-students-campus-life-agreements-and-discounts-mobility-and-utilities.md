# Mobility and Utilities

## Mobility

Over the years, the University has entered into a number of agreements to encourage its community to make greater use of sustainable mobility options.

The full list of available opportunities and instructions on how to access them can be found by students in the **Mobility Agreements** section of the **yoU@B Agenda,** while staff and faculty can consult the **Intranet,** under **Human Resources > Welfare > Mobility.**

WETACOO

Update 2024

At Wetacoo we envision a world where managing objects is as simple as organizing files on the cloud.

Customers can request that their objects, furniture, or archives be picked up where they prefer, then eventually stored safely in one of our warehouses, and returned where and when needed. Specifically, storage services (and moving services with storage) are entirely configurable online. For direct removals, however, it is necessary to follow the directions provided by customer support, which can be reached by email at [customerservice@wetacoo.com](mailto:customerservice@wetacoo.com) or by calling 02-82944397.

Bocconi Students, Teachers and Employees can take advantage of the following benefits:

20% discount on the monthly deposit service plan.

20% discount on the final direct moving service quote.

For more info about the service click [here](https://wetacoo.com/).

The discount coupon (BOCCONI20) can be entered directly into the platform when placing the order, or communicated to customer service when requesting the quote.

Discounts cannot be combined with other active coupons or promotions.

Via Savoia 84, Rome - [info@wetacoo.com](mailto:info@wetacoo.com) \- P.IVA 15991751007
