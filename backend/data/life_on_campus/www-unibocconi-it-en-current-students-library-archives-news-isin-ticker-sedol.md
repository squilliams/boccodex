# ISIN, Ticker, Sedol... which one should I use?

02 Oct 2025, by Library & Archives

Finding your way through the financial identifiers maze

Library & Archives has just published a new library [guide](https://unibocconi.libguides.com/identifiers) to help navigate the complex landscape of company and equity identifiers. The guide, available to the entire Bocconi community, is designed especially for students engaged in empirical research involving the use of financial and accounting databases. Mastering identifiers is a key skill for downloading data effectively.

The [guide](https://unibocconi.libguides.com/identifiers) provides basic information on the main identifiers, along with **step-by-step instructions on how to obtain and use them in the Library databases**. To better understand the examples provided, it is helpful to already have some basic knowledge of financial databases. For a quick refresher, you can consult the [dedicated libguides](https://unibocconi.libguides.com/?group_id=15883 "https://unibocconi.libguides.com/?group_id=15883").

We hope you will find the guide useful and [ask a librarian](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/library-archives/teaching-and-research-support/ask-librarian "https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/library-archives/teaching-and-research-support/ask-librarian") should you need further support.
