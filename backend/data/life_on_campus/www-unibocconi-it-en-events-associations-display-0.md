# Associations on Display

09 Feb 2026 - 13 Feb 2026

10:00am - 6:30pm

Leonardo Del Vecchio Building

Bocconi student associations are welcoming our community!

Join us for the ultimate gathering of university student associations! Whether you're looking to meet like-minded peers, explore new interests, or get involved in campus life, this event is your gateway to a world of opportunities. Discover student-led organizations across culture, finance, economics, social impact, and more **below** and explore more details on the associations [**here**](https://www.unibocconi.it/it/studenti-iscritti/campus-life/attivita-studentesche/elenco-delle-associazioni).

**Don’t miss out—come explore, connect, and take the first step toward something great!**

In addition to the student associations, you can also discover at thr fair the **European University of Social Sciences (CIVICA)** an alliance of ten leading institutions with the goal to create an inter-university campus that fosters collaboration in education, research, and innovation.

It aspires to shape the future of social sciences while promoting inclusivity, sustainability, and societal impact.

You will also find a desk dedicated to all the Campus Life activities organized for the second semester.

**Swing by our desk and find out more!**
