#### Requirements for a valid medical certificate to participate in Bocconi Sport activities.

To register for Bocconi Sport, your medical certificate must fall into one of the following **two categories**:

1. Medical certificate for **non competitive** sports activities with a valid **ECG**
   - issued by a **Sports Medicine Center**, a **Specialist in Sports Medicine** or your **general practitioner/family doctor**

     **IN**:

     If issued by your general practitioner/family doctor, you must also provide a **signed self-declaration** (download template [**HERE**](https://www.bocconisport.eu/sites/default/files/2022-09/Dichiarazione%20Integrativa%20Cert.%20Medico%20di%20Famiglia.pdf)), confirming under your own responsibility that the doctor who issued the certificate is indeed your general practitioner/family doctor. Without this declaration, the certificate will **NOT** be accepted.

     Uploading the ECG tracing separately from the medical certificate is **NOT** valid. The term “ECG” must be explicitly stated on the certificate, as shown in the attached sample.

     Certificates for **recreational or motor activity** are **NOT** valid and will not be accepted.

     Certificates obtained by **Virgin Active CANNOT** **be accepted** for Bocconi Sport activities.

   - issued by an Italian doctor and written in Italian redatto da un medico italiano e in lingua italiana

     [**HERE**](https://www.bocconisport.eu/sites/default/files/2025-08/facsimilecertnonagon.pdf?VersionId=qieD07rI9naOre.y_5xQlxEL4WprwGLa) the example with all required elements highlighted in red.

     **SUBMISSION PROCEDURE**:

     The medical certificate must include the statement _“_ **copia conforme all’originale in mio possesso** _”_ with the **date** and **signature** of the certificate holder.

     It must be uploaded via the **you@B app**, in the widget **Bocconi Sport > Medical Certificate**.
2. medical certificate for **competitive sports activities** issued by a **Sports Medicine Center** (if you already have one, make sure it is written in Italian and is still valid)

**SUBMISSION PROCEDURE**:

Upload the certificate **online** via the **you@B app**, in the widget **Bocconi Sport > Medical Certificate.**

If you do not have either of these certificates, from September you can book a medical visit for a **non-competitive sports medical certificate with ECG**.

**Booking procedure:**

1. First, subscribe to the [**Bocconi Sport membership**](https://www.bocconisport.eu/news/bocconi-sport-membership).
2. Then, in the **“Membership”** section of the **“Sport”** widget in the **you@B agenda**, you will find the _Take Care_ booking link.

Medical visits are held on the **second floor of the Bocconi Sport Center**, Viale Toscana 30.

For any questions or issues, please email us at [**bocconisporteam@unibocconi.it**](mailto:bocconisporteam@unibocconi.it).
