# Boost your CV!

30 Oct 2025, by Library & Archives

Get Certified with Bloomberg, LSEG, and FactSet

**Relevance for finance students**

Leading financial data providers such as Bloomberg LSEG and FactSet offer certification programmes that help students build practical skills in market data, as well as prove their knowledge of financial databases to prospective employers.

**Recognition in the job market**

Your proficiency will be proven by a printable certificate and a badge that you can add to your LinkedIn profile.

**Flexible**

The interactive learning modules are available online with no live sessions or strict deadlines, allowing you to stop and resume the course, or review concepts, at your own pace.

**No charge**

All certification programmes are free to take for Bocconi students.

**How do I enroll?** You can register with your Bocconi e-mail and/or access the certification module within the database. For details, please check the Training & Certification tab of the relevant libguide:

[Bloomberg libguide](https://unibocconi.libguides.com/bloomberg)

[LSEG Workspace libguide](https://unibocconi.libguides.com/refinitiv)

[FactSet libguide](https://unibocconi.libguides.com/factset)

Please note: certifications are available in **English only**.

Don’t miss this opportunity to demonstrate your knowledge and skills with the most popular financial analysis products! For further information, please write to [infobiblio@unibocconi.it](mailto: infobiblio@unibocconi.it) or contact us via B in Touch – Library.
