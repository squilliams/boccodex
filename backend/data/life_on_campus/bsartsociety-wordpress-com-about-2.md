## ABOUT

![](https://bsartsociety.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/04/compil.png?w=1024)

Founded in January 2018 by Giuseppe Bongiovanni and Giada Ginevra Tagliabue, BSAS is a Bocconi University students association that aims to crate a bridge between the world of Bocconi and that of arts, in all their forms and shapes.

The association’s initiatives include the organization of events, guided tours, conferences with experts and also many other activities for the members.

![BSAS.png](https://bsartsociety.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/bsas.png)

We began this blog with the intent of it taking on two separate roles: one concerning the association and its activities, and the other rendering our little daily findings about the world of art and our point of view.

All of the writers and associate are students from bachelor or master programs at Bocconi University, or [Università Commerciale Luigi Bocconi di Milano](https://www.unibocconi.eu/wps/wcm/connect/Bocconi/SitoPubblico_EN/Navigation+Tree/Home/).
