# La Biennale di Venezia

**Biennale Architettura 2025**

On the occasion of **the 19th International Architecture Exhibition**, La Biennale di Venezia addresses universities, academies of fine arts and higher education institutions with a special project.

Thanks to an agreement with Biennale of Venice, a Special Pass for the Biennale Architettura 2025 (from 10 May to 23 November) can be purchased at the cost of 20€. It's valid for three consecutive days from the first day of use.

You can pay with your credit card **from May 5th to 20th, 2025** by accessing [**Bocconi online payment platform**](https://uni.bocconistore.it/buy_en/customer/account/login/referer/aHR0cHM6Ly91bmkuYm9jY29uaXN0b3JlLml0L2J1eV9lbi9jdXN0b21lci9hY2NvdW50L2luZGV4Lw%2C%2C/) with your Bocconi username and password, select SDA-Divisione Master and then choose Special Pass Biennale – Architettura 2025.

With reference to pass collection, the tickets will be sent to you by email not before the beginning of June.

For further information on the Biennale, please visit their official website: [Biennale Architettura 2025 \| Biennale Sessions](https://www.labiennale.org/en/architecture/2025/biennale-sessions)

This opportunity is offered in collaboration with SDA Bocconi School of Management.
