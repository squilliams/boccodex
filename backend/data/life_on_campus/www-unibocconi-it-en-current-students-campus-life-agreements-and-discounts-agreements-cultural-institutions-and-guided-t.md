# Agreements with cultural institutions and guided tours

Università Bocconi is committed to offering students, faculty and staff the opportunity to participate first hand in the vibrant Milanese cultural life at convenient prices: **theater, music, cinema, museums and guided tours.**

![](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/styles/highlight_slide/public/media/images/visita_guidata.jpg.webp?itok=neeS6rMB)

### Affiliated cultural institutions

Theatres, music schools, concert halls, museums and many other cultural institutions have an agreement with Bocconi University. As most opportunities are addressed to Italian speakers, further details and information are published on the Italian website. Discover them all!

[Find out more on the Italian website](https://www.unibocconi.it/it/studenti-iscritti/campus-life/convenzioni/convenzioni-culturali)
