# Meet our new Library & Archives website!

11 Mar 2025, by Library & Archives

Discover what has changed

With an image-focused design and a mobile-first approach, the new website offers a refreshed browsing experience - simple, intuitive, and perfectly integrated with the University's content. Explore the tools, services, and resources of Library & Archives: not just a website, but a tool always available to you anytime and anywhere.

![](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/styles/container_100_lg/public/media/images/lea-eng-new-website1.png.webp?itok=53YrwfHL)

#### Need books and articles?

[**SearchLib**](https://library.unibocconi.it/): Start here to search for books, articles, and much more. Enter a title or keywords to begin your search.

#### Need a database?

[**A-Z Database List**](https://unibocconi.libguides.com/az.php): Check which databases are available and access them quickly from here.

#### Need immediate support to find resources or use a database?

[**Libguides**](https://unibocconi.libguides.com/) **:** Browse over 40 guides on specific topics (such as startups and artificial intelligence), disciplines, and specific databases (like Bloomberg and LSEG Workspace).

#### Want to check the reading lists for your courses?

**Course Reserves:** Find your course reading list from the course homepage in Blackboard to get access to articles, case studies and adopted textbooks. If you need help, take a look at this [libguide](https://unibocconi.libguides.com/coursereserves).

- [**Using the Library**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/library-archives/using-library "Using the Library") for information on access to spaces and the use of physical and online collections
- [**Teaching and Search Support**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/library-archives/teaching-and-research-support "Teaching and Research Support") for more advanced support services for the Bocconi community
- [**What’s On**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/library-archives/whats "What's on") to discover our ongoing projects and activities
