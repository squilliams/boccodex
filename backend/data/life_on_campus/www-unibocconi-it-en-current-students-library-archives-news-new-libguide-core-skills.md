# New Libguide on Core Skills

03 Apr 2025, by Library & Archives

Library & Archives has just designed a new library guide on Core Skills in partnership with Bocconi Career Service, to support Bocconi community members in developing essential and transferable competencies for success in their professional career.

The [guide](https://unibocconi.libguides.com/coreskills) covers key skills such as **adaptability**, **communication**, **critical thinking, problem solving** and **teamwork**, offering **curated books and websites** to encourage independent learning and foster awareness on the importance of these soft skills that are increasingly valued by employers.

You can find more libguides dedicated to disciplines and specific topics on our [Libguides platform](https://unibocconi.libguides.com/) accessible through the [Library & Archives website](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/library-archives).

Hope you enjoy the recommend readings and [ask a librarian](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/library-archives/teaching-and-research-support/ask-librarian) should you need further support.
