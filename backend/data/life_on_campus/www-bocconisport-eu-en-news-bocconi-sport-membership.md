**Are you ready to join Bocconi Sport for the 2025/2026 sport season?**

We are glad to announce the beginning of a new sport season: **from August Monday 4th it will be possible to** join Bocconi Sport by **subscribing the Bocconi Sport Membership for the season 2025/26**, required to participate in all our activities.

_**HOW?**_

The subscription procedure is completely **online**.

Log in the shopping area of the website [https://sport.unibocconi.it](https://sport.unibocconi.it/), using you university’s credentials, and select your favorite membership.

_**WHY?**_

To be a member of Bocconi Sport for the season 2025/2026 gives **great advantages**, depending on the membership you will decide to subscribe.

It will be possible to choose among:

1. _**Student**_ **Membership Fee** – _27,00 euros_ (reserved to UB and SDA Bocconi Students)

To participare in the sport activities, it is mandatory the delivery of a valid **medical certificate**.

Click [**HERE**](https://www.bocconisport.eu/en/news/medical-certificate) to read all the compulsory characteristics and the possible deliveries of the certificate.

To participate in the Virgin Active (fitness area) and Aquamore (swimming area) activities, you can refer to their receptions to know characteristics and delivery methods of the required medical certificate.

If you haven't got a medical certificate with these characteristics, you can book a medical check at the Bocconi Sport Center using the link _Take Care_, that you will find in your widget "Sport" in the you@be Agenda, after subscribing the membership.

For any further information or problem do not hesiste to contact us at the e-mail address [bocconisporteam@unibocconi.it](mailto:bocconisporteam@unibocconi.it).
