# A New Season of Library Workshops is Here!

10 Oct 2025, by Library & Archives

Get ready to kick off our new series of Library Workshops — designed to help you make the most of the Library tools and resources throughout your academic journey.

Whether you’re preparing for exams, working on your final paper or conducting research for a group project, our sessions will guide you through:

• **Finding material** – learn how to quickly find the books, articles, and data.

• **Finding data** – gain practical experience using platforms like _LSEG Workspace_ and _FactSet_ to retrieve reliable company and financial information.

• **Writing citations and bibliography** – understand how to create accurate citations and bibliographies.

Browse the full [**workshop calendar**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/library-archives/teaching-and-research-support/library-workshops) and book your spot through your [yoU@B student diary](https://agenda.unibocconi.it/), **“Appointments”** section.

For any questions, you can check B in Touch – Library section or contact us at [infobiblio@unibocconi.it](mailto:infobiblio@unibocconi.it) — we’ll be happy to help!
