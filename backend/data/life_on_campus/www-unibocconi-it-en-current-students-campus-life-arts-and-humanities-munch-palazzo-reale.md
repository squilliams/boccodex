# Munch at Palazzo Reale

**GUIDED TOUR**

Palazzo Reale in Milan and Arthemisia, in collaboration with the MUNCH museum, present the major monographic exhibition dedicated to EDVARD MUNCH.

On the occasion of the 80th anniversary of the artist's death, the prestigious event, in the rooms of the Palazzo Reale, will see exhibition of 100 works, all from the MUNCH Museum in Oslo, including paintings, drawings and prints, in a complete, wide-ranging and profound account of the man and the artist.

**When: 16 October at 6:15pm**

\> 6:00pm: Meeting with the tour guide outside the building

\> 6:15pm: Beginning of the guided tour

\> Language: ENG

\> Available spots: 25

The activity is open also to Faculty and Staff Members, but priority will be given to students.

**Participation fee: 12 €**

### Palazzo Reale and Arthemisia

**Palazzo Reale** is the exhibition heart of central Milan, a structure of the Milan City Council that for years has been planning, designing and managing large art exhibitions for the general public.

**Arthemisia** is a nationwide leader in the production, organisation and realisation of temporary exhibitions and permanent artistic and cultural exhibition.

It supports cultural institutions, from museums to exhibition organizers, from public to private companies, in the realization of cultural projects: Picasso, Raphael, Michelangelo, Caravaggio, Leonardo are just some of the greats of art that they have made familiar to the non-expert public to work.
