# Funding

## **Bocconi4Access to Education a.y. 2025-26**

Bocconi has always been committed to making higher education available to all motivated and talented students through a financial aid system, based on both merit and need.

In the section below, **funding opportunities for a.y. 2025-26** are summarized. These opportunities offer a different kind of support to families and, in most cases, take into consideration the economic and asset situation of students' families.

Benefits are different based to the degree course to which you belong.

If you are a Bocconi applicants, check the information available here
