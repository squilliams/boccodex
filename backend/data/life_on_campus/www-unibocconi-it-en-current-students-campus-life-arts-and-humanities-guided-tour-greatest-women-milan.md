# Guided Tour: The Greatest Women of Milan

Join us in this wonderful journey to explore the stories of the great women who made Milan the city that we love and admire today.

Among fictional characters and great historical figures, participants will discover extraordinary characters that shaped the social, scientific and cultural spheres of the city.

The stories of the great women of the Visconti-Sforzesco period will be illustrated, such as Beatrice d'Este and Cecilia Gallerani, figures of the Risorgimento era, such as Clara Maffei or the Manzoni woman Marianna de Leyva, without forgetting the world of art with Maria Callas and Fernanda Wittgens.

**The meeting point for the tour is in Piazza Castello, at the entrance of Torre del Filarete, at 6 PM.**

**When: 5 March 2025 at 6.15 PM**

**Language: English**

**Available spots: 25**

The activity is open also to Faculty and Staff Members, but priority will be given to students.

**Participation fee: 9 €**
