Suggestions and Complaints

## Let us know what you think!

We work every day to ensure that Bocconi library users are satisfied with the services offered. Our aim is to provide a quality experience for all users of our services and we welcome feedback, whether positive or negative. Together we want to build the Library of today and tomorrow, one that increasingly meets your dreams and desires.

Would you like to tell us what you think?

If you would like to send **suggestions, comments and ideas** on how to improve services or complaints to help us improve, you can fill in the [Feedback form](https://forms.office.com/pages/responsepage.aspx?id=erXza7Sfwkea2lEVZRj1L6bDyXFMNdxOi2DioP2q8_NUMk80NVNVMDNKNFJQV0JaOVk4UFVSQUxCUi4u)

If you would like to **propose** the **purchase** of a title that you cannot find among the Library's collections, you can share all the details of your proposal by carefully filling in the [Can't find it?](https://library.unibocconi.it/discovery/blankIll?vid=39UCLB_INST:VU1) form.

Thank you, we look forward to hearing from you!
