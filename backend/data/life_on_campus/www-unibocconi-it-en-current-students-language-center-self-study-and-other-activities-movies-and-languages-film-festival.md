# Movies and Languages Film Festival - Spring 2026

The Bocconi Language Center presents a **film festival** showcasing movies in their **original-language versions**. This event offers a fantastic opportunity to enhance your language skills, explore diverse cultures, and enjoy a meaningful and engaging experience.

For each film, attendees will be able to download a brief synopsis and glossary once the registration opens (available in the "About the Movies" section below).

Event structure:

- Teacher's introduction (15 min.)
- Film
- Q&A

Entrance is **reserved to the Bocconi community** and admission is free **.** To join a screening, registration is required via the **online form**, accessible either through the Postmaster email or the link provided below each film title.
