# Open Science for Researchers

13 Nov 2025, by Library & Archives

A workshop to boost your research career

**Are you a PhD student in your second year or beyond?**

Starting this year, you can include the Open Science for Researchers workshop in your [study plan](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/phd/phd-business-administration-and-management/courses-and-requirements).

**When:** November 27, 6:15–7:30 p.m.

**Where:** Online on Blackboard

**Assessment:** A short final quiz will certify attendance

**What you will learn**

- The fundamentals of **Open Science** and **Open Access**, essential tools for anyone pursuing an academic career.
- How Open Science practices promote **transparency** and **accessibility** in research.
- How to use **Creative Commons licences** to share your work while retaining intellectual property rights.
- How to identify and avoid **predatory publishers** that exploit researchers and their work.
- How to **publish in Open Access at no extra cost** with Elsevier, Springer, and other participating academic publishers.
