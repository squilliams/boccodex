# Inclusion Service

The **Inclusion Service** is a point of reference that aims to guarantee the same opportunities to everyone: it collaborates with other offices on campus to create a connected and unified community where everyone – students, faculty, staff and alumni – can feel welcome and included.

**Contact Us**

The Inclusion Service is the first point of contact for the Bocconi community regarding issues such as:

- Physical and digital accessibility
- Support for students with disabilities and other specific needs
- Multiculturalism
- Gender
- Inclusion and non-discrimination

Email: [**inclusion@unibocconi.it**](mailto:inclusion@unibocconi.it)

**Bocconi Students**

Students can contact the Inclusion Service on **B in Touch: Inclusion, Disability and Wellbeing** section, or in person at the **B in Touch Point** at the address **Piazza Sraffa 11**.
