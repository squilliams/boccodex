# Phone Booths arrive at the Library: dedicated spaces for calls and online Meetings

01 Dec 2025, by Library & Archives

Starting today, the Library offers new Phone Booths, soundproof boxes designed to provide a quiet and private space for your calls and online meetings.

Starting today, the Library offers new Phone Booths, soundproof boxes designed to provide a quiet and private space for your calls and online meetings.

The Phone Booths are located on the First Floor of the Library, in rooms 109 and 110, and can be booked via the yoU@B Pro app during the Library’s opening hours. Each booking can last up to one hour, with the option to reserve up to two slots per day.

Check out the [Usage Rules](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/media/attachments/Phone_booths_ENG_251125.pdf "RULES FOR UTILIZING PHONE BOOTHS IN THE LIBRARY") for more information!
