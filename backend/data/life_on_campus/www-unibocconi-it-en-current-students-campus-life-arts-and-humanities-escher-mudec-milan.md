# Escher at Mudec in Milan

**13 NOVEMBER 2025: GUIDED TOUR AND WORKSHOP**

_**“M.C. Escher. Between Art and Science” at Mudec, Milan**_

_**“Paradox as a Critical Lens: Breaking Symmetry”**_

The exhibition **M.C. Escher. Between Art and Science**, at MUDEC in Milan from September 25 to February 8, 2026, brings back to the city, after a ten-year absence, one of the most fascinating and recognizable artists of the twentieth century, **Maurits Cornelis Escher** (Netherlands, 1898–1972).

Through a project entirely dedicated to him, the exhibition offers a fresh perspective on his artistic journey.

Known for his impossible architectures, optical illusions, tessellations, and metamorphoses, **M.C. Escher** created a unique visual language that bridges art and mathematics. A mathematics not made of the abstract reasoning of professional academics, but of long, meticulous, and passionate work, rooted in a more intuitive and perceptive approach.

In **Escher**, art and science merge into a vision that is rigorous, inventive, and deeply personal, a guiding thread that strongly defines the exhibition.

**The workshop**, inspired by Escher’s works such as _Circle Limit III_, _Order and Chaos_, and _Development_, invites students to reflect on order, symmetry, norms, and perception through a guided analysis of the exhibition.

During the workshop, students, divided into groups of four, create a visual map of social paradoxes. Starting from a regular graphic pattern representing order, they choose a social theme such as marginalization, gender stereotypes, inequalities, conflicts between conformity and identity, or discrimination, and express it by intentionally altering the pattern: interrupting, deforming, or contaminating it with colors, marks, and words—just as Escher moves from order to chaos.

The workshop concludes with a collective presentation, where each group shares their work and reflects on how change can challenge the apparent order we usually observe.

### 24 ORE Cultura at Mudec

**24 ORE Cultura**, part of the **24 ORE Group**, is involved in the production and organization of major art exhibitions and cultural projects in Italy and abroad, with a specialization in the enhancement of public and private cultural heritage and in museum management.

It also provides consulting services in education, communication and press office, retail, and corporate event organization, working with institutions and companies to develop tailor-made projects.

Among its main activities is the management of **MUDEC — the Museum of Cultures in Milan,** an intercultural and multidisciplinary hub that brings together the public and private sectors in a unique governance model.
