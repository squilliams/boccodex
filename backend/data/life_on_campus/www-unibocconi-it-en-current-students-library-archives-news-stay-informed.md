# Stay Informed, Every Day

12 Jun 2025, by Library & Archives

Explore Digital Newspapers and Magazines from Bocconi Library

Studying social sciences means developing strong critical thinking skills, also being up-to-date on what’s happening in the world.

Did you know you can read top publications like Il Sole 24 Ore, Financial Times, The Economist, Harvard Business Review and The New York Times every day in digital format, wherever you are?

Whether you’re researching global market trends, looking for real-world cases for an assignment, or simply trying to understand today’s headlines, the Library’s resources are there for you.

You can read **digital newspapers** and **magazines** from your laptop or smartphone, **on campus** or **remotely**. Some of them are also available via **app**. Access is free for Bocconi students, faculty and staff!

**Where to find them:**

- Go to [SearchLib](https://library.unibocconi.it/)
- Login with your Bocconi credentials
- Search by title or browse the “Journals” section

**Please note!** Some resources (like _Financial Times_) require free registration with your Bocconi email address. Detailed instructions can be found in the resource description or in the [Online news sources](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/library-archives/online-news-sources "Online News Sources") webpage.
