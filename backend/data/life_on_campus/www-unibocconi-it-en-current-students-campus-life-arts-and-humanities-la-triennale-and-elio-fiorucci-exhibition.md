# La Triennale and Elio Fiorucci exhibition

_**GUIDED TOUR: Mostre del Museo del Design Italiano ed Elio Fiorucci**_

The exhibition seeks to represent the creative interests of Elio Fiorucci (Milan, 1935–2015), Italian designer, cool hunter and entrepreneur. From the 1960s on, the Fiorucci brand revolutionized lifestyle, fashion and the contemporary art scene in Italy

[Discover more on La Triennale website.](https://triennale.org/en/events/elio-fiorucci)

_**When: 26 February 2025 at 6pm**_

\> Language: ENG

\> Available spots: 25

The activity is open also to Faculty and Staff Members, but priority will be given to students.

**Participation fee: 12 €**
