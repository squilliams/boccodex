# Sport Discounts

Bocconi University offers all members of its community the opportunity to access various conventions with sports and wellness establishments and businesses at reduced prices.

Inclusion in the list is made on the sole basis of verification of the discounted economic conditions offered and does not represent a qualitative selection.
