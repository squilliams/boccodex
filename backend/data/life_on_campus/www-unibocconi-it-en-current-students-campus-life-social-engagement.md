# Social Engagement

**Bocconi University recognizes social engagement and responsibility as values that are integral to its institutional identity**.

Because of this social commitment, the **Community and Social Engagement** project has been launched, with the aim of contributing to the personal development of the students, faculty and staff that make up the Bocconi community.

The University offers a number of ideas and suggestions on these issues, by creating not only moments for reflection, but also concrete opportunities for personal engagement.

## Why volunteer?

The volunteer experience gives you the opportunity to give freely of your time, energy and skills to others and at the same time enrich yourself. But how?

- By gaining self-confidence and feeling useful by collaborating in a cause you care about;
- By putting your human and professional skills on the line. By donating your skills to receive more, experiencing new situations and opportunities for growth;
- By meeting new people who share the same goals with you;
- By enriching your resume with social impact activities;
- By improving your organizational, time management and communication skills.

In addition, starting from A.Y. 2023-2024, the university has also decided to formally recognize student engagement in social impact activities.
