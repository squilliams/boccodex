# Football

## Are you a football/futsal player and you would like to keep on playing even during your university years?

Bocconi Sport gives you the possibility to join our teams:

- men’s 11-a-side football – university championship CUS
- women’s futsal – university championship CUS
- men’s futsal – FIGC Serie C1
