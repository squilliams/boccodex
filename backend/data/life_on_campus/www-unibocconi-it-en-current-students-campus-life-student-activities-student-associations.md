# Student Associations

Bocconi values the active participation of students in making life on campus richer and full of interesting opportunities. For this reason, the University promotes student initiatives and supports the organization of their activities and events with access to rooms, funds and means of communication.

All student activities are approved by the CASA Committee (or its delegates), which operates based on periodically updated shared principles.
