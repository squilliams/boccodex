# CIVICA Student Engagement Fund

Selected by the **European Commission** as one of the pilot [European Universities](https://education.ec.europa.eu/education-levels/higher-education/european-universities-initiative) in 2019 and confirmed as successful alliance in 2022 under the Erasmus+ programme, [**CIVICA**](https://www.civica.eu/) brings together **10 leading European higher education institutions** in the social sciences, humanities, business management and public policy.

Under the leadership of SGH Warsaw School of Economics, CIVICA partners are developing a framework to **foster students’ engagement within the CIVICA alliance** while strengthening their **sense of belonging and** **shared identity**, deeply rooted in European values and heritage.

The [**CIVICA Student Engagement Fund (SEF)**](https://www.civica.eu/ambassadors/student-initiatives/student-engagement-fund) is introduced as one of the initiatives facilitating **bridge-building between nations, cultures, and languages**.

### Important information for Bocconi students and registered student associations

**The SEF is a** **mini-grant funding scheme aimed at supporting student-led joint initiatives within the CIVICA alliance.** It aims to foster **cross-campus collaboration among students** **and registered student associations** from different disciplines and backgrounds in tackling the important issues facing Europe today, and to further enhance the students’ experience within the alliance.

The SEF will support **up to 10 joint initiatives** per academic year across the alliance, to be implemented during the **Spring semester**, and foresees **one centralized call for application** in **Academic Year 2025/2026**.

_Before starting your application, please make sure **to read carefully** all information indicated in the **Call for Proposals AY 25/26** and in the **SEF Project selection and implementation rules:**_

- [_**Call for Proposals AY 25/26**_](https://www.civica.eu/files/user/Application_forms/SEF_Call_for_proposals_2025_26.pdf)

- [_**SEF Project selection and implementation rules**_](https://www.civica.eu/files/user/Application_forms/CIVICA_SEF_Regulations_2025_26.pdf)

### Preparation of joint project proposals – process overview

The full process for Bocconi students is detailed here

- **STEP 1 - Form a project team and prepare the joint proposal:** team up with other CIVICA students/registered student associations, form a project team (and identify a team leader) and **prepare a detailed concept of a joint project/initiative** by completing the following **Application form template**:

  - [_**Application form**_](https://view.officeapps.live.com/op/view.aspx?src=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.civica.eu%2Ffiles%2Fuser%2FApplication_forms%2FSEF_Application_Form_2025.docx&wdOrigin=BROWSELINK) \- please read the instructions carefully
- **STEP 2 - Prepare the budget of the joint proposal:** prepare a cumulated budget for the joint project/initiative **(max total value 10,000 EUR)** and the partial budget **(max 3,000 EUR)** detailing the expenses to be covered by Bocconi and **fill out the Budget form template** here below:

  - [_**Budget form**_](https://view.officeapps.live.com/op/view.aspx?src=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.civica.eu%2Ffiles%2Fuser%2FApplication_forms%2FProject_Budget_Excel_SEF.xlsx&wdOrigin=BROWSELINK) \- please read the instructions carefully
- **STEP 3  - Obtain pre-approval on the initiative and budget by the CASA Committee @ Bocconi:** in order to obtain the required pre-approval by the CASA Committee, Bocconi students associations **must send via email to** [**civica@unibocconi.it**](mailto:civica@unibocconi.it) **AND** [**comitatocasa@unibocconi.it**](mailto:comitatocasa@unibocconi.it) the **Application form and the Budget form** duly filled **no later than 27 October 2025 at 1:00pm** (Milano time).

**- Please note: all proposals will be thoroughly discussed and formally evaluated during a dedicated extraordinary session of the CASA Committee scheduled for 3 November 2025.**

- **STEP 4 - Submit the joint proposal to the centralised SEF Committee:** after obtaining the official pre-approval of the CASA Committee, formally submit **the joint proposal (Application form and Budget form) AND proof of pre-approval** **by the CASA Committee** to the centralised SEF Committee **no later than 12 November 2025 at 1:00pm** as per the following instructions:

**a)** Access [**my.civica.eu**](https://my.civica.eu/) with the credentials from your home institution;

**b)** Open the link [**https://whaller.civica.eu/org/15773/forms/201**](https://whaller.civica.eu/org/15773/forms/201) still using the same browser and complete the online form.
