# People

The Sport and Entertainment Knowledge Center has a dedicated faculty able to carry out research, innovation and education with an international profile.

The team includes academics and professionals who have worked in the sports and entertainment worlds for several years, covering different areas of expertise, allowing for a comprehensive and in-depth perspective of the two ecosystems and their actors.
