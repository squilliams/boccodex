# Access to Bocconi Library & Archives

## Welcome!

Library & Archives supports the Bocconi community and works every day to make information accessible and to offer services for teaching, learning, research, and inspiration.

The Library is open from [Monday to Sunday](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/library-archives/opening-hours). You can discover the world of the online Library 24 hours a day also off-campus and access all the services dedicated to you, entering into your personal area [sign in](https://library.unibocconi.it/discovery/login?vid=39UCLB_INST:VU1).

## Library:

- collections (print and digital): books, periodicals, statistics and research databases focusing on economics, law and social sciences
- individual study seats in the reading rooms
- Phone Booths: soundproof booths dedicated to online calls and meetings. For more details, you can refer to the [**Rules for utilizing Phone Booths in the Library**](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/media/attachments/Phone_booths_ENG_251125.pdf "RULES FOR UTILIZING PHONE BOOTHS IN THE LIBRARY")

From the [yoU@B student diary](https://agenda.unibocconi.it/) you can:

1. generate a temporary QrCode to access, as an alternative to the Bocconi ID Card
2. view the [**Rules for utilizing study seats in the Library rooms and conduct to be observed**](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/media/attachments/Rules%20Seat%20management%20_27-03-25.pdf?VersionId=1pE.Aom6eTIkLayJ9qgLL9ZCaQ4N26Cr): their subscription is necessary to access the Library; you must comply with these rules throughout your stay
3. reserve your study seat or a Phone Booth through the **Study Places/Desk Finder** section: reservation is **mandatory**

- dedicated services [(Accessibility and inclusion)](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/library-archives/accessibility-and-inclusion)
- [ask a librarian!](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/library-archives/teaching-and-research-support/ask-librarian)

### Data Room

In the Data Room you can find the following databases, available upon reservation:

- Bloomberg
- LSEG Workspace

[Reserve a slot](https://libdatabases.unibocconi.it/)

[Read the reservation policy](https://libdatabases.unibocconi.it/modules/policies.php)

All other databases are accessible [online](https://lib.unibocconi.it/), including [off-campus](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/current-students/library-archives/using-library/using-online-resources).

## Archives:

- the documents (letters, photos, registers, etc.) produced by Bocconi University during the course of its activity and the historical fonds acquired by the University over the years
- dedicated consultation room
- dedicated portal [ASBOC](https://asboc.unibocconi.it/?l=en)
- or consultation **it is necessary to schedule an appointment** by contacting the [Archives team](https://asboc.unibocconi.it/contatti/?l=en)
