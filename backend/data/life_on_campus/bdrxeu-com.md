# BDRX

Europe's First Undergraduate Club Focused on Restructuring and Distressed Investments

[Join Us](https://bdrxeu.com/join) [Learn More](https://bdrxeu.com/#mission)

## Our Mission

Our mission at BDRX is to provide a forum for students interested in distressed assets, credit, and restructuring. We give our members a platform to exchange ideas, develop crucial career skills, and network with investment professionals through external events.

## Our Activities

### Training Sessions

Technical training sessions led by division heads to build interview skills and market knowledge. Key insights shared via our social media channels.

### Mock Interviews

Our board members conduct mock interviews with associates to help prepare them for real-world interviews

### Company Workshops

Hands-on sessions with leading firms to develop practical skills in restructuring and credit analysis

### Networking Events

Regular opportunities to connect with professionals and peers in the industry, including corporate firms and industry leaders

### Research & Resources

Access to primers, case studies, and industry analysis to build knowledge in distressed investing

## Our Divisions

### Corporate Finance

Covering capital markets, M&A, and financial decision making. We explore valuation, deal structuring, and financial advisory, equipping members with skills to analyze complex transactions.

### Special Situations & Private Equity

Focusing on high stakes investing in distressed opportunities and alternative assets. Members gain exposure to leveraged buyouts, turnaround strategies, and asset restructuring.

### Credit Investments

Exploring fixed income from a fundamental credit perspective, focusing on leveraged finance, distressed debt, and credit risk assessment.

As the first undergraduate club in Europe focused on restructuring, distressed investments, and credit, we provide unique opportunities for ambitious students.
