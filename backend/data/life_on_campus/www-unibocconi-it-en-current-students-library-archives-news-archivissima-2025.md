# Looking to the future during the Night of the Archives!

09 Jun 2025, by Library & Archives

From 5 to 8 June, Italian Archives are looking to the future for the 2025 edition of [Archivissima](https://www.archivissima.it/en), the national festival of archives. Just to celebrate Archives Night, a video prepared by the **Bocconi Historical Archives** to tell the story of the birth of our University through documents and photographs of the time has been available [online](https://archivissima.it/it/oggetti/5256-alle-radici-dell-universita-bocconi-il-sogno-di-un-futuro-realizzato) since June 7. The title of our video is “At the roots of Bocconi University: the dream of a future realized.”
