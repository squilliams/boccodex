# Full rate a.y. 2026-27

Staying in one of the 7 university residences provided by the University is the best way to fully experience your university life. You will easily meet other students and share your daily life with students from all over the world, in a multicultural, lively, and stimulating environment.

The residences offer **well-maintained spaces, practical services, and an international community**. They are all located in the **city center**, within a maximum walking distance of 10–15 minutes from the classrooms where lectures take place. All our residences offer single bedrooms, with either a private bathroom or one shared with at most one other student, and access to a shared kitchen.

On this webpage, all the details about requirements, methods and deadlines of application, results and requirements needed to use the accommodation are explained. Therefore, **before applying**, be sure to carefully read the contents of **paragraphs 1-9** on this page.

You can access the application you are interested in through the boxes at the top of the page; links will be available during the application period.

1\. Requirements

A full rate assignment may be requested only by students who:

- **are non-resident**, meaning living in a municipality from which it is possible to reach the location of the study course attended by public transportation, with travel times exceeding 90 minutes(\*)

The only exception are students interested in applying during the Open reservation, for whom this requirement does not have to be strictly fulfilled.

- **Not fall under any of the** [**grounds for exclusion**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/bachelor-and-law-programs/housing/grounds-exclusion-full-rate-housing-ay-2026-27 "Grounds for exclusion - full rate housing a.y. 2026-27")

- **having earned the minimum number of credits** as per paragraph 2 "Credits required to submit the application"

- **enrolled for the a.y. 2026-27:**

>in the first, second, or third year of a Bachelor of Science program

>in the first, second, third, fourth, or fifth year of the Integrated Master of Arts in Law

>in the first, or second year of a Master of Science program.

**Students who are recipient of financial aid that includes** free **accommodation**(e.g., Una Scelta Possibile) or accommodation at reduced rate (e.g., Moving to Milan), upon acceptance of the benefit, renounce the possibility of receiving any another type of housing assignment (preferential rate, full rate) for the academic year of the first assignment and subsequent years (if the financial aid is renewed). Acceptance will result in the automatic cancellation of any housing request submitted and/or of any housing assignment received. It is also specified that after acceptance, no further housing requests of any type can be submitted.

The number of rooms available at full rate is usually lower than the applications submitted. Regularly enrolled students will be considered as a priority: applications submitted by students transferring from other Universities or repeating any year of course will not be accepted.

(\*) The travel times to be calculated are the minimum times required, based on the public transport route, between the departure station/stop from the place of residence and the arrival station/stop at the study location, including waiting times at intermediate stops. Walking routes or private transportation are excluded. These times can be verified on the regional website [www.muoversi.regione.lombardia.it](http://www.muoversi.regione.lombardia.it/) and, for cities outside the Lombardy Region, through the Google Maps search engine at [www.google.it/maps](http://www.google.it/maps)

2\. Credits required to submit the application

Exclusively for applications for full rate housing renewal applications, a minimum number of credits —detailed below— will be verified, which the student must have in order to apply. However, please note that **no** minimum number of credits is required for:

>First year students in a.y. 2026-27 of the Bachelor or Integrated Master of Arts in Law programs are not required to reach this minimum number of credits if they wish to apply for the "Full rate new admission application (for 1st year students")

>Students who wish to apply for the "Full rate new admission application (for all year students) application.

>Students who wish to apply for the Open reservation application.

In the " **Full rate housing renewal**" application session, only applications from students who have obtained the number of credits specified below by the following deadlines will be considered: **February 3, 2026** for bachelor and master of science, and **March 25, 2026** for integrated master of arts in law programs

**Minimum number of credits required**

**11 credits** for those enrolled in the first year (bachelor, master of science and integrated master of arts in law programs) in a.y. 2025-26

**60 credits** for those enrolled in the second year (bachelor and integrated master of arts in law programs) in a.y. 2025-26

**120 credits** for those enrolled in the third year of a bachelor programs in a.y. 2025-26and intending to enroll in the first year of a master of science program in Bocconi University for a.y. 2026-27, or for those enrolled in the third year of an integrated master of arts in law program in a.y. 2025-26

**180 credits** for those enrolled in the fourth year of an integrated master of arts in law program in a.y. 2025-26

It is specified that for the achievement of the minimum number of credits required at the end of the winter session there is NO use of bonus credits in addition to the credits actually earned by the student.

For students who have participated in an Exchange program during the first semester, the timing of recognition of exams taken abroad will be considered. Such students are invited to still apply for housing renewal if interested and to monitor the credit recognition in order to complete it as soon as possible. Please note that no flexibility can be applied in relation to the achievement of the merit requirement for accommodation - credits to be achieved by August 10, 2026.

It is also specified that for students with disability pursuant to Italian Law n.104/1992 or with civil disability equal or greater than 66%, the number of minimum credits may deviate up to a maximum of 40% from the number of credits indicated above.

3\. Application submission

The housing assignment for a.y. 2026-27 is granted for the academic year of application only, therefore, it is not automatically renewed for subsequent academic years, but it is the student's responsibility to check whether it can be renewed and to make subsequent applications for assignment in the ways that will be provided and detailed on the website for each academic year. Throughout the period in which the student is a resident in a Bocconi residence hall, room changes are not permitted. Instead, it is the right of Bocconi University to transfer the student, even during the year, to another residence hall at the rate provided for the new residence.

4\. Results and acceptance

**Result housing renewal application**

The result will be made available within early April 2026 on MyApplication. For the renewal application, no subsequent steps of acceptance are required, the room is assigned.

**Result housing new admission application and acceptance**

The information in the following paragraph is valid for all the new admission application sessions (1st year students and subsequent years students) and for the monthly open reservations.

The result of a housing new admission application is published on MyApplication usually around 3 working days after the session.

If the result is positive, you will receive a full rate housing assignment for a.y. 2026-27. On the result, you will be notified the residence assigned, the room type, the period of assignment and the fee applied. The accommodation offer received is not changeable and no room changes are foreseen.

If you wish to accept the offer of accommodation received, proceed to pay the security deposit of 600 euros and upload all the required documents by the date that will be indicated in the result. If you fail to complete all the steps required to accept the housing assignment received by that deadline, your assignment will automatically be cancelled.

5\. Requirements needed to use the accommodation

Students who will result housing assignees for a.y. 2026-27 following the procedure described above will be able to use the assigned accommodation only in presence of the following conditions:

**1) REGULAR ENROLLMENT for a.y. 2026-27**

\> regular enrollment for the absolute first time for a.y. 2026-27 in the first regular year of a Master of Science (i.e. bachelor Bocconi students going to the Master of Science program)

or

\> regular enrollment for a.y. 2026-27 for the absolute first time in a regular year subsequent to the first of a Bachelor of Science, Master of Science, Integrated Master of arts in Law or PhD program.

It is specified that the years of enrollment are counted for each study cycle starting from the absolute first year of enrollment\*.

It is specified that for students with a disability pursuant to Italian Law n.104/1992 or with civil disability equal or greater than 66% can reside in the Bocconi Residence Halls at a preferential rate also if enrolled for a.y. 2025-26 in the 2nd year “fuori corso” of a Bachelor of Science, Master of Science, Integrated Master of Arts in Law program.

\*The absolute first year of enrollment shall be understood as any academic year of first enrollment in any kind of degree program (even if different from the one attended in the a.y. 2026-27) which required the same education qualification as the one needed to access the attended program in the a.y. 2026-27, in any University in Italy or abroad, even if the program has not been completed.

**2) MERIT REQUIREMENT: CREDITS TO BE OBTAINED AND REGISTERED BY AUGUST 10, 2026**

It is specified that for the "New admission" and the "Monthly Open reservation" application sessions, the fulfillment of this requirements is not required.

For the purpose of eligibility to use the accommodation, the rules established in terms of merit requirements and detailed on the [**dedicated webpage**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/bachelor-and-law-programs/housing/merit-requirements-ay-2026-27 "Merit requirements a.y. 2026-27") for the different types of housing applications apply.

6\. Payment

Before your arrival at the residence for a.y. 2026-27 you will have to pay the **housing 1st installment**: the deadline will be August 5th, 2026. You will see the amount due on your you@B once you'll have completed your enrollment for a.y. 2026-27. We recommend you to make the payment through the **Flywire tool** system.

[**Fees, deadlines and methods of payment a.y. 2026-27**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/bachelor-and-law-programs/housing/fees-deadlines-and-methods-payment-ay-2026-27 "Fees, deadlines and methods of payment a.y. 2026-27").

Please consider that the calculation of the housing installment is carried out at the beginning of July for all housing assignees, regardless of their situation in terms of reaching the merit and the enrollment requirement (see point 5) that the office cannot verify before August 10. The possibility of seeing the due payment on you@B does **not** entail an unconditional room assignment, which is always subject to the possession of the enrollment and merit requirements. In absence of the requirements needed to use the assigned room, the office will proceed with the cancellation of your housing assignment, but you will still be required to pay the first installment (it will not be possible to cancel the due amount).

In the event you will pay the amount due but after a subsequent verification the merit and enrollment requirement will not be met, the amount you paid will not be refunded and the housing assignment will be cancelled in any case.

Please remember that the University grants the right to leave the accommodation early and to withdraw from the commitment to such payment, exclusively in the foreseen cases of renunciation of the accommodation, proven by the specific academic reasons required for the acceptance of the renunciation request, and withdrawal from the accommodation, according to what detailed on the dedicated website pages.

7\. Cancellation in case of lack of the requirements

Students assigned housing for whom it is subsequently determined that they did not regularly enroll for the a.y. 2026-27 or did not meet the merit requirements will lose their right (eligibility) to use the Housing Service for the a.y. 2026-27. The assignment will be cancelled by the office and students will be required to leave the assigned accommodation immediately. It is specified that the payments due will remain the responsibility of the student even in the event of cancellation of the assignment. For this reason, before submitting a housing application, students are invited to pay particular attention to the provisions regarding the [**cancellation**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/bachelor-and-law-programs/housing/cancellation-and-revocation-ay-2026-27 "Cancellation and Revocation a.y. 2026-27") of the housing assignment, specified on the dedicated webpage.

8\. Residence halls calendar and extraordinary openings

The Bocconi residence halls will be open from the end of August 2026 to the end of June 2027. The standard assignment period for the 2026-27 academic year will meet the academic needs of most students. To stay during the special opening periods (winter break 2026 and July 2027), you will need to submit a separate request (information available soon).

There are no time restrictions on entry or exit for residence hall guests. There are restrictions, however, for external guests, whose presence in the residence hall is allowed only between 7:00 a.m. and midnight.

**Important note regarding check-out**

Regardless of the end date of your housing assignment, we remind you that **at the end of your housing assignment period, you must completely clear your room and apartment of all your belonging** s, even if you will return to the same room the following year. In fact, as specified in the Bocconi Residences Regulations:

_At the time of check-out for the end of the assignment period or during the year, the student is required to completely empty their room and other areas of their accommodation (bathroom, kitchen, living room, where present), including completely emptying refrigerators and freezers. Bocconi University is not responsible for goods left in the apartment/room after check-out._

Attention: rooms are reassigned to Summer School students during the summer, so it is not possible to leave anything inside your room or apartment. **Any issues related to missing or deteriorated items during the summer, a period when the room is not assigned, cannot be considered by Bocconi University or the residence management.**

9\. Students with disabilities

Bocconi University has accommodations in each residence hall that are designed to accommodate students with disabilities in order to offer them the possibility to easily live their university experience in a collective context that meets their needs. Students with disabilities who require special housing needs may contact the Office of Fees, Funding and Housing, which will evaluate, with each student, which housing is best suited to their specific needs. All reported issues and provided documentation will be treated confidentially and subject to applicable privacy regulations. In order for the Office to be able to satisfy the requests received, it is best to clarify your needs in advance. Interested students are advised to contact the Fees, Funding and Housing Office before submitting their housing application.
