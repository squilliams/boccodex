# Faculty and Staff

University mission and strategic objectives may require Faculty and Staff movements at a global level on work related activities such as conducting a research, attending a conference, meeting, students fairs and teaching.

These activities frequently involve travel abroad and be an enriching experience that can also involve exposure to certain risks.

Bocconi travel abroad policy establishes a set of guidelines and requirements for planning **international travel**, careful planning must precede international travel, even for seasoned travelers. Each travel destination requires a different strategy for responding to small inconveniences and serious emergencies.

Bocconi fundamental approach to managing the risks associated with travel is **to anticipate any possible risk** and take steps to prevent or at least reduce harm and protect travellers from exposure to undue risk. Key to this is **good preparation and prevention**. Pre-travel risk assement is therefore a crucial step of the travel itinerary plan.

Bocconi has established a Risk Rating System and contracted with a **travel assistance company** ( [ISOS - International SOS](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/campus-and-services/services/international-sos-faculty-and-satff)) to provide 24-hour worldwide travel security and medical assistance to its travelling employees.
