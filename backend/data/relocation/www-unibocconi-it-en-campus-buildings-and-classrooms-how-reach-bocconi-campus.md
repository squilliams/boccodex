# How to Reach Bocconi Campus

#### By Plane

From Linate Airport

- By taxi it takes about 20 minutes: more info on the [Linate airport website](https://milanolinate-airport.com/en/from-to/by-taxi).
- Subway line 4 (blue line) in the San Babila direction to Piazza Tricolore, then tram 9 (Porta Genova direction) to Via Bocconi.

From Malpensa Airport

- By taxi it takes about 50 minutes: more info on the [Malpensa airport website](https://www.milanomalpensa-airport.com/en/from-to/by-taxi).
- [Malpensa Express](https://www.malpensaexpress.it/en/) train to Cadorna Station, then subway line 2 (Abbiategrasso Chiesa Rossa or Assago Milanofiori Forum direction) to the Porta Genova stop, then tram 9 to Via Bocconi.
- [Malpensa Shuttle](https://www.malpensashuttle.it/) bus to Centrale Station, then subway line 3 (San Donato direction) to the Porta Romana stop, then tram 9 (Porta Genova direction) to Via Bocconi.

From Orio al Serio International Airport (Bergamo)

Information on the [Orio al Serio website](https://www.sacbo.it/Editorial/newsCategoryViewProcess.jsp?editorialID=171).

#### **By Train**

From Centrale Station

- By taxi it takes about 20 minutes.
- Subway line 3 in the San Donato direction to the Porta Romana stop, then tram 9 (Porta Genova direction) to Via Bocconi.

Suburban Rail Line S9 (Tibaldi stop)

- Signs for Bocconi are inside the station.
- Exit on Via Bazzi, cross the street to reach Via Castelbarco: access to the Bocconi Campus on Via Castelbarco and Viale Toscana, crossing the New Campus green.

#### **Taxi Service**

In Milan, taxi service is available 24 hours a day. Taxis can be the quickest way to get around the city because they often travel in dedicated lanes. You can request a taxi at the Bocconi reception, or call the numbers +39 02 4040 and +39 02 8585.

#### **Public Transport (Bus, Tram and Subway)**

Tickets for public transport cost €2.20 and are good for travel in the urban area and on the [Trenord](https://www.trenord.it/en/?_gl=1*dybndh*_up*MQ..*_ga*MTQ5OTA4Nzg0Ny4xNzQ2NTI2NDYx*_ga_CFQRRL9CP6*czE3NDY1MjY0NjAkbzEkZzAkdDE3NDY1MjY0NjAkajAkbDAkaDA.) urban rail network for 90 minutes after validation. Transfers are included, but with only one ride on subway and rail lines per ticket. For more information, visit the [ATM](https://www.atm.it/it/Pagine/default.aspx) (Azienda Trasporti Milanesi) website.

#### **By Car**

From the highway: Tangenziale Ovest Milano, exit Viale Liguria.
