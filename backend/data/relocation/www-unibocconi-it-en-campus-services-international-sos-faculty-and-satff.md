# International SOS for Faculty and Satff

**Travel Safely**

Bocconi has partnered with International SOS to offer medical, safety and security advice to travelling faculty and staff. Services are designed to help with medical, personal, travel, security, logistical and legal problems that may arise when away from home.

International SOS services include:

**Before leaving**

- **information** about the country of destination

**While abroad**

- **advice** on health and security issues

**In case of emergency**

- **assistance** in case of medical or security emergency
