# Housing application full rate a.y. 2026-27

a.y. 2026-27

page being updated

Living in a university residence is the best way to fully experience your university life. You will easily meet other students and share your daily life with people from all over the world, in a multicultural, lively, and stimulating environment. In recent years, we have made strong investments to expand and enhance campus life, significantly increasing the housing options available to our students. This allows you to relocate to Milan comfortably and with peace of mind, build your network, and enjoy your university experience to the fullest.

We look forward to welcoming you!

For the 2026–27 academic year, the following full-rate application opportunities are available:

- [**Dedicated to Freshmen – Olympic Village (starting from April 9, 2026)**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/bachelor-and-law-programs/housing/dedicated-freshmen "Dedicated to Freshmen")

- [**Full rate – Bocconi Residences (starting from April 14, 2026)**](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/bachelor-and-law-programs/housing/full-rate-housing-new-admission-application-ay-2026-27 "Full rate housing new admission application a.y. 2026-27")
