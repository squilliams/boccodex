FAQ

How do I choose the right Malpensa Shuttle bus for my flight?

We recommend booking the bus so that you arrive at the airport at least 4 hours before your flight. In most cases, your ticket can also be used on earlier or later trips on the same date, subject to service availability and free seats.

Where are the Malpensa Shuttle bus stops located?

The addresses of the stops and maps to find their exact location are available on the "Stops" page of our website.

What are the Malpensa Shuttle bus timetables?

All bus timetables are available on the "Stops" page of our website.
