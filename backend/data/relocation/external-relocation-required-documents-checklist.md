Relocation first-month checklist for Bocconi students (practical order):

1. Enrollment and credentials
- Complete Bocconi enrollment steps and keep university credentials active.
- Keep copies of admission/enrollment confirmations for admin requests.

2. Housing and contract paperwork
- Prepare passport/ID, enrollment proof, and any landlord/residence forms requested.
- Keep rental contract and payment receipts for residence permit or municipal requests.

3. Immigration documentation (non-EU in particular)
- Follow Bocconi immigration guidance for visa/residence permit workflow and deadlines.
- Track appointment receipts and submission proofs.

4. Tax and identity admin
- Request/confirm Italian fiscal code (codice fiscale) when required for contracts/services.

5. Healthcare setup
- Verify health coverage pathway (EHIC/private/Italian national health service eligibility).
- Keep insurance certificates and payment/registration confirmations.

6. Mobility setup
- Activate local transport options (ATM tickets/passes) and map your daily campus route.

Use official pages as the source of truth, because required forms and timelines can change by intake and student profile.
