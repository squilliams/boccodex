### International Student Care

Moving to a foreign country to study can be an unfamiliar and stressful experience, but we want to make the transition as smooth as possible and your time at SDA Bocconi enjoyable and worry-free.

With an ever-greater awareness of the importance of student wellbeing and engagement, SDA Bocconi makes its **Student Care Service** available to anyone enrolled in a master program.

We will continue to expand our services as we grow, to further provide everyone with the guidance they need to feel safe, protected and fulfilled during their time at SDA Bocconi.

Our purpose is to give you a positive experience: we are particularly aware of the challenges for international students and therefore offer guidance throughout all of the processes involved in studying at SDA Bocconi.

We provide services to help with a variety of bureaucratic and practical matters as well as help in the event of unexpected and difficult situations.

To accompany you throughout these steps, we strongly encourage you to consult our **Roadmap**, a comprehensive and easy-to-follow guide designed to help you navigate essential steps both before and upon your arrival. The roadmap outlines key procedures, such as visas, tax codes, health insurance, accommodation, and residency requirements and offers useful resources to help you settle into Milan and fully prepare for your journey at SDA Bocconi. Just read it carefully, as it contains valuable information that will help make your transition smoother and more informed.
