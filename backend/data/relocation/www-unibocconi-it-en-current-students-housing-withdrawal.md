# Withdrawal

You can withdraw from the housing assignment, regardless of any reason, by applying for withdrawal **exclusively** **from 4 to 8 November 2024**.

Applying for withdrawal in this way will result in the cancellation of the housing **assignment as of 24 December, 2024**. If you fall into this category, you will not be required to pay subsequent installments, but **you will not get a refund** **of the** **security deposit**, which will be retained as a penalty deposit.
