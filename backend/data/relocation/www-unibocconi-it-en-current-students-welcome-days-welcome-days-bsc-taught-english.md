# Welcome Days - Bsc taught in English

_Your Bocconi learning experience starts at the end of August with the welcome activities._

_Meet faculty, make friends and have fun!_

If you haven't picked up your Welcome Kit yet, you will be able to request it at the B in Touch Point starting from September 15, from Monday to Friday (from 9.00am to 5.00pm).

Math crash course

This course consolidates several topics in pre-undergraduate math that will help you **begin university courses with more self-confidence** and stable notions. These crash courses follow the “blended” courses delivered online from the beginning of August accessible through your YoU@B Student Diary.

No enrollment required; timetables and course profile are available [here](https://didattica.unibocconi.eu/ts/precorsi.php?ord=3).

YESMilano Desk and Workshop

YesMilano, the Milanese Chamber of Commerce agency for student internationalization, will be on campus:

- on **August 28th**( **10:00am-5:00pm**) and **August 29th** ( **10:00am-3:30pm**) with a **dedicated desk**, to provide first-year students with information on relocating to Milan and life in the city.
- on **August 28th** ( **1:00pm-2:00pm**, Aula Franceschi) with a **dedicated workshop** on the **Italian national healthcare** **system, transportation, housing and tips** on living in Milan.

### Associations on Display

From **8 September**, a fair takes place where student associations present themselves to newcomers.

At Bocconi we have **more than 100 student associations**, which enliven the campus with their many activities. Attend the event and meet members of the associations directly and gather useful information about their activities and how you too can become an active association member yourself.
