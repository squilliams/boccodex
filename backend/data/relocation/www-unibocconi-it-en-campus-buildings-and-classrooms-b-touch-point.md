# B in Touch Point

The **B in Touch Point** (former One-Stop Service Center) is a welcome point where staff are available who can offer you support to get started and direct you to the correct offices for your requests. In addition, at B in Touch Point you can pick up materials useful for your academic and campus life.

**Opening hours**

Mondays through Fridays from 8am to 5pm.

**How to reach us**

B in Touch Point is located in the Piazza Sraffa 11 building, with entrance at the back from Piazza Sraffa 13.

Information for prospective students

The Guidance and Recruitment Office is available for information on Bachelor and MSc programs and admissions procedures during On-Campus Infosessions:

- if you are interested in undergraduate programs, [click here](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/bachelor-science/guidance-activities/campus-visits)
- if you are interested in graduate programs, [click here](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/master-science-programs/guidance-activities/campus-visits)

Information for current students

The **B in Touch** online service is a point of contact between students and University offices, and it is accessible directly from your **yoU@B student diary**. There you can find dedicated content for students and send requests to the various offices. [At this link](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=43ON-FlKBe4) you can watch a short clip on how to use the tool.
