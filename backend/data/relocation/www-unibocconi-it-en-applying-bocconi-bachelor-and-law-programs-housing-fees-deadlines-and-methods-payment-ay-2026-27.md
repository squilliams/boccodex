# Fees, deadlines and methods of payment a.y. 2026-27

Bocconi Residence Halls

_The content of this page refers exclusively to the 7 Bocconi Residence Halls. For fees, installments, and deadlines related to the Olympic Village, please refer to the dedicated page (Dedicated to Freshmen)._

**General information**

By accepting the housing assignment, the student **commits to paying the housing fee for the entire agreed period and the security deposit**, which will only be returned at the end of the agreed period. The housing rates in Bocconi university residences vary according to the type of residence and accommodation and are divided into “ **full rate**” and “ **preferential rate**”.

The preferential rate of the accommodation service is applied to students who meet the requirements defined by the Right to University Education, based on the economic condition of the applicant's family evaluated on the basis of the value of the ISEE/ISSEU parificato indicator for services for the Right to University Education and the ISPE/ISPEU parificato value.

Students who meet the economic requirements (ISEE/ISEEU parificato and ISPE/ISPEU parificato parameters) foreseen for a.y. 2026-27 may request the assignment of a room at preferential rate. For the preferential rate, 3 different fee levels based on the ISU brackets (1st, 2nd and 3rd ISU bracket) are foreseen, depending on the values indicated on the ISEE/ISEEU Parificato Certificate submitted at the moment of the application. For all other students the full rate is applied.

The students contemporary enrolled or who intend to contemporary enroll for a.y. 2026-27 also to another bachelor of science, master of science, integrated master of arts, AFAM (Institution of Higher Education for Fine Arts, Music and Dance) course of 1° or 2° degree of study, pursuant to Italian Law n. 33/2022, at a different Italian or foreign University or AFAM, who submit or have submitted the application for the benefit connected with the Right to University Education Assistance at the other University or AFAM, cannot benefit of the preferential rate housing assignment. These students will be able to submit the application only for full rate housing.

The students assigned with a preferential rate accommodation for whom the contemporary enrollment with the request of the benefit connected with the Right to University Education Assistance at the other University or AFAM is verified, will be requested to pay full rate. The new fee will be applied from the first installment.

The standard assignment period goes **from 20 of August 2026 to 27 of June 2027** **(10 months)**  and meets the academic needs of most students. The assignment for the month of July 2027 can be specifically requested and paid separately by those who need it. A special application to request the assignment for the month of July 2027 will be available: please check all the information on the [dedicated webpage.](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/applying-bocconi/bachelor-and-law-programs/housing/july-2027 "July 2027")

Housing rates may change according to the rate of inflation and any updates.

The fees published here refer exclusively to the academic year 2026-27, for which they will apply. For subsequent academic years, housing fees may be subject to change according to the inflation rate and any updates.

- **1st installment** (covering the period September-December 2026) by **July 30, 2026 - tbc**
- **2nd installment** (covering the period January-March 2027) by **December 19, 2026 - tbc**
- **3rd installment** (covering the period April-June 2027) by **March 10, 2027 - tbc**

Exceptions are open reservations for periods shorter than one year, which require payments with specific deadlines communicated at the time of assignment.

**Important note - first installment for accommodation**

Please note that the option to view the payment due for the first installment for accommodation will be available to all students who are assigned accommodation at the beginning of July 2026, regardless of their status in terms of meeting the merit or enrollment requirements. In fact, viewing the payment due in the you@B calendar does not equate to an unconditional assignment of accommodation, which remains subject to enrollment and merit requirements, verified by the Office after the payment deadline (July 30, 2026). Please note that **if the requirements for accommodation are not met, the assignment will be automatically canceled and the payments due will remain the responsibility of the student; the amount due cannot be canceled.**

### Housing fees a.y. 2026-27

- [Housing+fees+2026-27.pdf](https://www.unibocconi.it/sites/default/files/Housing%2Bfees%2B2026-27.pdf)

**Tuition fees are paid via PagoPA** by clicking on the “payment code” and then on “Pay with PagoPA.” The transaction is completed online (credit card, bank account, PayPal, etc.) and is registered in a few minutes: the sticker changes from red to green.

**Alternatively, you can print the MAV and pay via ScrignoPagoFacile** ( [www.scrignopagofacile.it](http://www.scrignopagofacile.it/)) or home banking. In this case, registration takes 3-5 working days.

**For non-SEPA payments (currencies other than the euro), the Flywire system is also available**, which can be selected in the Punto Blu. Registration takes approximately 5 working days.

To avoid penalties, we recommend paying in advance of the due date. A complete guide is available on B in touch.

Accommodation fees are published a few weeks before the deadline (immediately after confirmation of availability in the case of open reservations). The first accommodation installment will be available:

- after enrollment for the 2026-27 academic year (by July 31) for students already enrolled.
- approximately from mid-July for newly enrolled students.

Important: the tuition fee calculation process automatically sends users an electronic invoice containing an IBAN that must not be used to make the payment. **Payments made in a manner other than that specified will cause interruptions and delays in the normal payment registration process**.

If the payment is not made nor registered in agenda **within 15 days** from the deadline, the student will incur: :

• the **cancellation of the housing assignment** is applied with immediate expulsion from his/her assigned accommodation;

• the **suspension of the academic and administrative position** of the student is applied until proof of completed payment regarding the amounts due is submitted.

The student will in any case and still be subject to the payment in full of all pending amounts and will not have the right to the refund of amounts already paid. In addition, the deposit will not be refunded.

The Fees, Funding and Housing Office, in addition, reserves the right to determine the non eligibility of the student for the Housing Service for the whole study cycle.

**ATTENTION**

A student who receives a disciplinary sanction of suspension from exams and partial exams during the year will not incur revocation of the housing assignment, however, if they have an assignment at preferential rate, they will undergo the **change of rate from preferential to full rate**, and will therefore be required to pay the housing fee at the full rate starting from the first installment due after the sanction is communicated.
