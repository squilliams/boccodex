# Buildings

Recent campus development projects bring together a variety of different buildings that have been constructed over the years starting in 1941, the year in which the Via Sarfatti building was inaugurated.

All campus offices, classrooms and services are permanently gathered around the area near our oldest building. This allows the Bocconi community to be fully integrated into the surrounding Porta Ludovica neighborhood of Milan.
