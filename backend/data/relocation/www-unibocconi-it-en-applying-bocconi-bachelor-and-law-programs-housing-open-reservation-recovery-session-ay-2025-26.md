# Open reservation - recovery session a.y 2025-26

In order to satisfy all the need of students of the Right to University, according to the housing application timeline for a.y. 2025-26, a new "recovery" session of Open reservation has been introduced. This session will allow students who will result eligible for the ISU Bocconi Scholarship a.y. 2025-26 during the autumn 2025 to request the housing rate change with respect to the rate already applied for a.y. 2025-26 (if already assignees of a room at full rate) or to request a housing new admission at preferential rate for a.y. 2025-26 (if not yet assignees of a room).

|     |
| --- |
| Only students who will result eligible for the ISU Bocconi Scholarship a.y. 2025-26 will be entitled to participate to the Open reservation — recovery session (preferential rate) |

By participating to the Open reservation — recovery session, one of the following requests may be submitted:

**1\. Housing rate change for a.y. 2025-26**

This request may be submitted only by students who:

- are assignees of a room at full rate for a.y. 2025-26 (in any previous assignment session);
- resulted eligible for the ISU Bocconi Scholarship a.y. 2025-26.

In case the request will be accepted:

- the preferential rate will be applied starting from the second housing installment and there will be no adjustment of the first housing installment already paid;
- the rate change will be applied only if the student who is requesting it will be eligible for the ISU Bocconi Scholarship a.y. 2025-26;
- the rate change will be applied only if the room occupied by the student for a.y. 2025-26 is one of the room types available also at preferential rate.

It is specified that the rate change will be subject to availability of rooms that can be assigned at preferential rate possibly left after all the previous preferential rate applications, or after the "Renunciation/ Withdrawal" period, and always up to the number of rooms defined in the Housing Application Requirements and Regulations a.y. 2025-26.

**2\. Preferential rate new admission housing application for a.y. 2025-26**

This request may be submitted only by students who:

- are NOT assignees of a room at full rate for a.y. 2025-26
- resulted eligible for the ISU Bocconi Scholarship a.y. 2025-26

The housing assignment, valid from January 2026, will be subject to availability of rooms that can be assigned at preferential rate and up to the number of rooms defined in the Housing Application Requirements and Regulations a.y. 2025-26. For these room assignments, all the preferential rate rooms possibly left after all the previous preferential rate applications (included the housing rate change requests) or after the "Renunciation/ Withdrawal" period, will be used.

In the event that the number of applicants will exceed the number of rooms available, the room assignments will happen by giving priority to:

- non resident students
- 1st year students
- increasing amount of the ISEE/ISEEU parameter

**HOW TO SUBMIT THE APPLICATION**

This application may be submitted in the period **from 5 to 9 November, 2025** through the dedicated link that will be published on this page of our website.

[**Online application Open Reservation Recovery Session**](https://admissions.unibocconi.it/admissions/Students/OpenFromExtUrl?concourseId=14139&ordType=)

Before accessing the application, **please see the useful documents** (Provisional Housing Application requirements and regulations a.y. 2025-26, Bocconi residence regulations a.y. 2025-26, Privacy policy) available at the following [link](https://www.unibocconi.it/en/programs/current-students/services/useful-documents-0).

Results

The results of the Open reservation - recovery session application for the 2025-26 a.y. will be communicated on MyApplication.

In the event of a **request of housing rate change** with a negative result due to non-availability of assignable housing at preferential rate (as specified above), the student may apply for Early withdrawal by and no later than **November  2025**.

In the event of a **preferential rate new admission housing application** with a negative result due to non-availability of assignable housing at preferential rate, the student may apply for the Open reservation at full rate (during the first session in **December 2025**).
