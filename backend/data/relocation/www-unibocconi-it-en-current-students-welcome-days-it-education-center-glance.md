# IT Education Center at a Glance

The IT Education Center (ITEC) is the center that manages ICT education at Bocconi University, offering supplementary courses and ICT certifications.

Faculty members are chosen among the best professionals in the field of ICT education and tutors are selected among the best students at Bocconi.

Supplementary Activities

Supplementary courses complete the University teaching offer, allowing students to enrich their profiles.

ITEC offers several courses on a wide range of ICT topics in order to introduce students to new technologies and software, deepen their knowledge of the most useful ones to their academic and career paths (e.g. Excel, PowerPoint, coding with Python, Stata, R, Business intelligence, Big Data, Bitcoin and Blockchain, SQL, Photoshop and Generative Artificial Intelligence).

Curricular Supplementary Activities

In addition to the extracurricular activities, the IT Education Center offers curricular supplementary activities for Master of Science students.

These activities can be included in the study plan as supplementary activities with the value of two credit points (except for the MSc in Data Science and Business Analytics, Cyber Risk Strategy and Governance and Transformative Sustainability programs whose students can only register the two credit points as additional courses).

ICT  Certifications

ICDL (International Certification of Digital Literacy) is an internationally recognized certification attesting the ability to use the main IT tools.

ITEC is a Test Center for ICDL certifications, offering weekly exam sessions and discounted prices for Bocconi students. Completion of the ICDL certification (or another recognized certification) is a requirement in order to take the Computer science and Computer skills for law exams.

ITEC is also Test Center for the following ICT Certifications:

- ICDL Advanced (Word Processing, Presentation, Spreadsheets, Database)
- Digital Marketing
- EPM (European Project Management)
- MOS (Microsoft Office Specialist).
