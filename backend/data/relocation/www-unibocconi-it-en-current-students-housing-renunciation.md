# Renunciation

a.y. 2024-25

It is possible to renounce from the housing assignment exclusively for expected academic reasons and only within the time windows detailed below.

## **Renunciation June 5-9, 2024 – only for students who:**

Have received confirmation of departure for exchange or internship for a.y. 2024-25.

**The application will be immediately accepted and the room assigned for 2024-25 will be cancelled.**

The office will verify afterwards with the competent offices the truthfulness of the statement, in case of substantiated statements the security deposit will be refunded and the student will not be burdened with penalties or further payments.

**Please note: In case of declarations NOT substantiated by the specific academic reasons provided for the acceptance of the renunciation request, the student will not be refunded the security deposit (500 euros), will still have the obligation to pay one month's fees and will also be subject to the payment of 200 euros for administrative management charges**.

ONLINE APPLICATION HOUSING RENUNCIATION 5-9 JUNE 2024 (application expired)

## **Renunciation June 19-23, 2024 – only for students enrolled in a Bachelor or Master’s program who:**

a) Have realized that they will not achieve the credits required for the merit requirement by 10 August, 2024.

b) Withdraw from their studies or transfer to another university

c) They are Bachelor students and, despite having participated in the selections for admission to a Master’s program for the 2024-2025 a.y., were not admitted. Please note: Bachelor students who are guests at a preferential rate and are not admitted to a Master’s program, but will enroll in the first year fuori corso, will not be able to submit a renunciation request.

**The application will be immediately accepted and the room assigned for 2024-25 will be cancelled.**

The office will verify afterwards with the competent offices the truthfulness of the statement, in case of substantiated statements the security deposit will be refunded and the student will not be burdened with penalties or further payments.

**Please note: In case of declarations NOT substantiated by the specific academic reasons provided for the acceptance of the renunciation request, the student will not be refunded the security deposit (500 euros), will still have the obligation to pay one month's fees and will also be subject to the payment of 200 euros for administrative management charges**.

ONLINE APPLICATION HOUSING RENUNCIATION 19-23 JUNE 2024 (application expired)
