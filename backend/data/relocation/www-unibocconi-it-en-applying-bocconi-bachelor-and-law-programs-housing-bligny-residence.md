# Bligny Residence

[VIA BLIGNY 22](https://www.google.it/maps/place/Residenza+Bligny/@45.4509665,9.1874869,17z/data=%213m1%214b1%214m5%213m4%211s0x4786c404dfbf427f:0xf157cc6b887a8008%218m2%213d45.4509628%214d9.1896756)

Inaugurated in September 2016, the **Bligny Residence** is located on campus.

The Residence has:

**A.** 4 single rooms with a private bathroom and shared kitchen;

**B.** 60 single rooms with a gender - specific bathroom shared with another student and shared kitchen;

**C.** 112 bedrooms in apartments with 4 single rooms, 3 bathrooms and a shared living room/kitchen area.

**Description of the spaces**

All rooms are furnished with a bed, a desk, a wardrobe and are equipped with WiFi internet. Blankets, sheets, pillowcases and towels are provided. Linens are changed and rooms are cleaned once a week. Guests in Bligny Residence may use shared kitchens located in each floor or the kitchen located in the common areas of the apartments. In addition, there are study rooms, break rooms, a coin laundry area and a relax area.

Why live in University housing?

First of all, **location**: university residence halls are on or near campus, close to classes and the university facilities, and just minutes from the center of town.

Another important advantage of dorm life is constant **interaction** with your fellow students, Italian and international alike. You'll meet lots of different people with whom you'll have many things in common - interests, ambitions and dreams - in addition to your studies. It will be friendly environment where you'll soon feel at home.

Only students who are attending Bocconi University or participating in exchange programs are allowed to access the residence halls.

Plus, living in one of the university residence halls is both **safe and convenient**. All our residence halls have a doorman ready to help you 24/7, and the services included in your lodging fee will allow you to maintain your busy schedule without worrying about the electric bill or changing the sheets.

All Bocconi residence halls are closed during summer period, according to the period of suspension of courses and exams.

What the residents love about the Bligny Residence

- The Bligny Residence is located inside the campus;
- It is provided with large study rooms;
- It is located in a central area and well served by public transport.

How to book a room?

**Booking a room in a Bocconi residence is simple**: participate in one of the online applications planned for the 2026-27 academic year, at preferential or full rate.
