# Bocconi Residence Halls

Bocconi University offers you the chance to make the most of student life by staying in one of our **7 university residence halls**.

The halls are located in different areas of the campus and differ for room types and other characteristics. On the webpages dedicated to each residence hall you will find more relevant information.
