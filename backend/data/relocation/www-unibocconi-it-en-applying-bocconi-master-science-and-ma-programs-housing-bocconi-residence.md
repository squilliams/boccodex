# Bocconi Residence

[VIA BOCCONI 12](https://www.google.it/maps/place/Residenza+Bocconi/@45.4496371,9.1881085,17z/data=%214m13%211m7%213m6%211s0x4786c4054a082637:0x4c1fc250b2b0f52a%212sVia+Ferdinando+Bocconi,+12,+20136+Milano+MI%213b1%218m2%213d45.4496371%214d9.1902972%213m4%211s0x4786c4054a082637:0xf7c86a8f47ecfde6%218m2%213d45.4496371%214d9.1902972)

The Bocconi Residence is located right on campus.

It has single rooms with a private bathroom or a bathroom shared with another student.

**Description of the spaces**

All rooms are furnished with a bed, a desk, a wardrobe and are equipped with a mini-bar and WiFi internet. Blankets, sheets, pillowcases and towels are provided. Linens are changed and rooms are cleaned once a week. Guests in Bocconi Residence may use shared kitchens located in each floor. In addition, there are study rooms, break rooms, a coin laundry area and a relax area.

Why live in University housing?

First of all, **location**: university residence halls are on or near campus, close to classes and the university facilities, and just minutes from the center of town.

Another important advantage of dorm life is constant **interaction** with your fellow students, Italian and international alike. You'll meet lots of different people with whom you'll have many things in common - interests, ambitions and dreams - in addition to your studies. It will be friendly environment where you'll soon feel at home.

Only students who are attending Bocconi University or participating in exchange programs are allowed to access the residence halls.

Plus, living in one of the university residence halls is both **safe and convenient**. All our residence halls have a doorman ready to help you 24/7, and the services included in your lodging fee will allow you to maintain your busy schedule without worrying about the electric bill or changing the sheets.

All Bocconi residence halls are closed during summer period, according to the period of suspension of courses and exams.

What the residents love about the Bocconi residence

- The Bocconi residence is the oldest one and in it you can breathe the past 60 years of Italian history and a strong sense of community;

- Students who live here consider this residence the beating heart of the University. Home away from home;

- It is located inside the campus;

- At the ground floor there is the university canteen;

- The next soccer field and the interior spaces for extracurricular activities.

How to book a room?

**Booking a room in a Bocconi residence is simple**: participate in one of the online applications planned for the 2026-27 academic year, at preferential or full rate.
