Milan transport practical facts for students:

- Main local operator: ATM (Azienda Trasporti Milanesi).
- Typical products for students: monthly and yearly city passes, plus pay-as-you-go options.
- Coverage: metro, tram, bus and selected suburban integrations under STIBM fare system.
- For arrivals from airports (LIN/MXP/BGY), use dedicated shuttle/train options and then connect to ATM network.
- Always verify current prices and eligibility categories directly on official pages before purchase.

Useful official references:

- ATM passes and tickets: https://www.atm.it/en/ViaggiaConNoi/abbonamenti/Pages/default.aspx
- ATM tickets: https://www.atm.it/en/ViaggiaConNoi/biglietti/Pages/default.aspx
- Milan open GTFS dataset: https://dati.comune.milano.it/dataset/ds929-orari-del-trasporto-pubblico-locale-nel-comune-di-milano-in-formato-gtfs

- Distinguish daily mobility inside Milan from airport transfer.
