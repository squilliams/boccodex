# Housing

a.y. 2025-26

**Welcome!**

Thank you for choosing Bocconi Residences.

Staying in one of the residence halls provided by the University is the best way to fully experience your university life. You will easily get to know other students and share your daily life with students from all over the world, in a multicultural, vibrant, and stimulating environment. The residences offer well-maintained spaces, practical services, and an international community.

**Useful information for students enrolled in a.y. 2025-26**

In this section of the website, you will find detailed information about all housing opportunities planned for the academic year, such as: Monthly Open reservations if you want to book a room during the year; Temporary sublease, if you want to temporarily transfer it to another student during your exchange period or internship abroad; all payment deadlines and information on how to make them; final check-out and summer period.

Consult on this page:

- **Highlights**: upcoming deadlines and opportunities for guests
- **All contents a.y. 2025-26**: comprehensive overview of topics
