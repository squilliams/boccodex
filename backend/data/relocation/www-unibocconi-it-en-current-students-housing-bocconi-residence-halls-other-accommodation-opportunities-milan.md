# Other accommodation opportunities in Milan

Life in one of the Bocconi Residence Halls is characterized by many advantages in terms of **security and opportunities to socialize**. However, it also requires the ability to adapt to certain limitations, including the impossibility to choose your flatmates and of guests overnight stay. Should you wish an accommodation alternative option, that may guarantee a greater degree of freedom, looking for an apartment might be a good solution.

Please find below a list of Residences and Housing opportunities in Milan **not** **managed and/or guaranteed** by Bocconi University (m - male residences \| f - female residences). For detailed information, please contact the housing  facility directly.
