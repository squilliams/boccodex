# Welcome Days — MSc programs

_Your Bocconi learning experience starts at the end of August with the welcome activities._

_Meet faculty, make friends and have fun!_

If you haven't picked up your Welcome Kit yet, you will be able to request it at the B in Touch Point starting from September 15, from Monday to Friday (from 9.00am to 5.00pm).

Know your program

**Welcome from Directors and Kick-off Meeting**

**September 4**

Meet your Program Director and the related career service consultant and listen to testimonies from your MSc program. Program Directors will introduce faculty members, describe the teaching model, the structure of the specific graduate degree program and will highlight important opportunities dedicated to MSc students.

Your Career Service consultant will provide an overview of the dedicated services and activities and will explain how you can actively plan your career since the beginning of your experience at Bocconi.

[Timetable and classrooms are available here](https://www.unibocconi.it/it/studenti-iscritti/welcome-days/lauree-magistrali/welcome-directors-benvenuto-dei-direttori) and in the yoU@B Agenda.

Preparatory courses

These courses consolidate several topics covered during the first year of your degree program and will help you start your Bocconi experience with more self-confidence and stable notions.

The preparatory courses are available **online**, in **asynchronous mode**.

You can access **preparatory courses** available for your MSc Program through BlackBoard, starting from June. The timing for accessing the materials may vary depending on the course.​

Check [here](https://didattica.unibocconi.eu/ts/precorsi.php?ord=2) or through your yoU@B for all the details.

YesMilano Desk and Workshop

YesMilano, the Milanese Chamber of Commerce agency for student internationalization, will be on campus:

- on **August 28th**( **10:00am-5:00pm**) and **August 29th** ( **10:00am-3:30pm**) with a **dedicated desk**, to provide first-year students with information on relocating to Milan and life in the city.
- on **August 28th** ( **1:00pm-2:00pm**, Aula Franceschi) with a **dedicated workshop** on the **Italian national healthcare** **system, transportation, housing and tips** on living in Milan.

### Campus Life and Bocconi Sport Team

From **28 August**, meetings and events to discover all the cultural and sports activities on campus, to visit the spaces available to students, and the Bocconi Sport Center.

**Bocconi Sport Team** is recruiting new athletes to join our teams and also organizes guided tour of the Bocconi Sport Center.

### Associations on Display

From **8 September**, a fair takes place where student associations present themselves to newcomers.

At Bocconi we have **more than 100 student associations**, which enliven the campus with their many activities. Attend the event and meet members of the associations directly and gather useful information about their activities and how you too can become an active association member yourself.
