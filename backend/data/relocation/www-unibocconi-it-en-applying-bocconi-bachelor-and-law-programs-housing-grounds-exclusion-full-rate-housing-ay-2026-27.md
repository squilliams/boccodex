# Grounds for exclusion - full rate housing a.y. 2026-27

a.y. 2026-27

\- page being updated -

**Full rate housing renewal application**

The full rate housing renewal will exclude students who, in the a.y. 2026-2027:

- during a.y. 2025-2026 occupy a room on temporary sublease (as incoming student);

- are not “non-resident students”;\*

- are already assignees of housing upon payment, or free of charge, or at a reduced rate for the academic year 2026-2027;

- are recipients of housing assignment free of charge or at a reduced rate as beneficiaries of financial assistance that includes housing for the academic year 2026-2027;

- are already in possession of a qualification at equivalent or higher level than the program for which the full rate housing is requested for the a.y. 2026-2027, achieved in Italy or abroad (including qualifications issued prior to the implementation of Italian Ministerial Decree 509/1999);

- when enrolling in a Master of Science program, have already obtained a four-year qualification higher than Bachelor level;

- were already enrolled in other degree programs of the same level or higher than the program for which benefits or services are requested, even in faculties other than those offered by Bocconi University and in years previous to a.y. 2026-2027;

- will enroll in the first year “fuori corso” or subsequent starting from the absolute first year of enrollment, after the regular study program, except for students in a condition of disability pursuant to Italian Law n.104/1992 or civil disability equal or greater than 66%, who will be allowed to apply for preferential rate renewal even if they will enroll in the first year “fuori corso”;

- repeat or have repeated the enrollment in an academic year of study already attended, even in case of withdrawal from studies or transfer from another University, when transferring Bachelor of Science, Integrated Master of Arts in Law, Master of Science or PhD program or in case of change of faculty and/or University;

- incur or have incurred in the disciplinary sanction of expulsion from Bocconi University;

- are responsible for serious violations of the Bocconi Residence Regulations. Are also excluded all students who in any academic year have incurred a cancellation due to delayed payment or a revocation due to violation of the Bocconi Residence Regulations and in which cases the University has ordered the ineligibility to use the Housing Service for the entire duration of the study program;

- do not reach the minimum number of credits required to submit the renewal application by the winter exam session a.y. 2025-2026, considering that such credits will be calculated starting from the absolute first year of enrollment;

- participate in double degree programs, selected or enrolled in the program by the partner University (DDIB CEU-Bocconi program, Double/Joint Degree programs), as they are not in possession of the requirements provided by the Housing Application Requirements and Regulations;

**Full rate new admission application (FIRST YEAR Bachelor of Science of Integrated Master of Arts in law session)**

Assignment of full rate housing will exclude students who, in the a.y. 2026-2027:

- are not “non-resident students”;\*

- are already assignees of housing upon payment, or free of charge, or at a reduced rate for the academic year 2026-2027;

- are recipients of housing assignment free of charge or at a reduced rate as beneficiaries of financial assistance that includes housing for the academic year 2026-2027;

- are already in possession of a qualification at equivalent or higher level than the program for which the preferential rate housing is requested for the a.y. 2026-2027, achieved in Italy or abroad (including qualifications issued prior to the implementation of Italian Ministerial Decree 509/1999);

- repeat or have repeated the enrollment in a program year already attended, even for transfers of Bachelor of Science, Integrated Master of Arts, Master of Science or PhD programs or for changes of faculty and/or university campus;

- were already enrolled in other degree programs of the same level or higher than the program for which benefits or services are requested, even in faculties other than those offered by Bocconi University and in years previous to 2026-2027;

- incur or have incurred in the disciplinary sanction of expulsion from Bocconi University;

- are responsible for serious violations of the Bocconi Residence Regulations. Are also excluded all students who in any academic year have incurred a cancellation due to delayed payment or a revocation due to violation of the Bocconi Residence Regulations and in which cases the University has ordered the ineligibility to use the Housing Service for the entire duration of the study program;

- participate in double degree programs, selected or enrolled in the program by the partner University (DDIB CEU-Bocconi program, Double/Joint Degree programs), as they are not in possession of the requirements provided by this Housing Application Requirements and Regulations;

- enroll for the a.y. 2026-2027 in the first year at Bocconi University to a Bachelor or Integrated Master of Arts in Law program who have withdrawn from studies in other Universities or who are transferring from another University (with or without credit recognition request) independently from the number of credits that will be recognized by Bocconi University.

\*Non-resident students are students residents in a municipality from which it is possible to reach the location of the study course attended by public transportation, with travel times exceeding 90 minutes. The travel times to be calculated are the minimum times required, based on the public transport route, between the departure station/stop from the place of residence and the arrival station/stop at the study location, including waiting times at intermediate stops. Walking routes or private transportation are excluded. These times can be verified on the regional website [www.muoversi.regione.lombardia.it](http://www.muoversi.regione.lombardia.it/) and, for cities outside the Lombardy Region, through the Google Maps search engine at [www.google.it/maps](http://www.google.it/maps)
