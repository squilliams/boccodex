# Withdrawal a.y. 2025-26

You may withdraw from the housing assignment by submitting a withdrawal application from **November 5 - 9, 2025**, and **exclusively if you fall into one of the two cases** listed below:

\> during the second semester of a.y. 2025-26 you will participate in an international **mobility** experience (exchange, free mover) or an **internship** recognized by Bocconi University, in a location other than Milan;

\> you participated in the Open reservation - recovery session because you were already assigned a full rate accommodation and you wanted to **change the rate**, switching to the preferential rate, but you received a **negative result**. In this case, the deadline for submitting the withdrawal request will be communicated to you in the result of the Open reservation - recovery session.

There are no other cases of withdrawal; in all other cases, the student will keep the housing assignment for the entire agreed period and will be required to pay all the scheduled housing installments.

Application for withdrawal results in cancellation of the housing assignment **starting from December 24, 2025**. If you fall into this category, you will not be required to pay subsequent installments and will get a refund of the security deposit.

**WITHDRAWAL APPLICATION**

Available from 5 to 9 November 2025
