# Isonzo Residence

[VIALE ISONZO 23](https://www.google.it/maps/place/Residenza+Isonzo/@45.4464294,9.198049,17z/data=%213m1%214b1%214m5%213m4%211s0x4786c417c271c295:0x2638fba69944e8cf%218m2%213d45.4464257%214d9.2002377)

The Isonzo Residence is located a 15-minute walk from campus and close to the M3 subway line. It was inaugurated in September 2014 and it has two units: Isonzo 23 and Torre Isonzo.

The **Isonzo Residence** has:

**A.** 11 single rooms with a private bathroom;

**B.** 86 single rooms with a gender-specific bathroom shared with another student;

The **Torre Isonzo Residence** has:

**C.** 116 rooms in 4-bedroom apartments with 2 bathrooms and a shared living room/kitchen area.

**Description of the spaces**

All rooms are furnished with a bed, a desk, a wardrobe and are equipped with WiFi internet. Blankets, sheets, pillowcases and towels are provided. Linens are changed and rooms are cleaned once a week. Guests in Isonzo Residence may use shared kitchens located on each floor or kitchen located in the common areas of the apartments. In addition, there are study rooms, break rooms, a coin laundry area and a relax area.

Why live in University housing?

First of all, **location**: university residence halls are on or near campus, close to classes and the university facilities, and just minutes from the center of town.

Another important advantage of dorm life is constant **interaction** with your fellow students, Italian and international alike. You'll meet lots of different people with whom you'll have many things in common - interests, ambitions and dreams - in addition to your studies. It will be friendly environment where you'll soon feel at home.

Only students who are attending Bocconi University or participating in exchange programs are allowed to access the residence halls.

Plus, living in one of the university residence halls is both **safe and convenient**. All our residence halls have a doorman ready to help you 24/7, and the services included in your lodging fee will allow you to maintain your busy schedule without worrying about the electric bill or changing the sheets.

All Bocconi residence halls are closed during summer period, according to the period of suspension of courses and exams.

What the residents love about the Isonzo Residence

- It is located close to the Metro Station.
